The **Jedi-Sith War** (ended [1032 BBY](/wiki/1032_BBY "1032 BBY")) was the last of the [wars](/wiki/Sith_Wars "Sith Wars") fought between the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") and the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") during the [fall of the Old Republic](/wiki/Fall_of_the_Old_Republic "Fall of the Old Republic"). The war included a [battle](/wiki/Battle_of_Coruscant_(Jedi-Sith_War) "Battle of Coruscant (Jedi-Sith War)") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), and its subsequent [liberation](/wiki/Liberation_of_Coruscant "Liberation of Coruscant").

The war ended with the [presumed destruction](/wiki/Defeat_of_the_Sith "Defeat of the Sith") of the Sith on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Ruusan](/wiki/Ruusan "Ruusan"), though one Sith, [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane"), had actually survived and reformed the Sith via the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two") which allowed them to exist in hiding for the next [thousand years](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") until they [toppled](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") the Jedi Order.

## Prelude[]

:   "*The Jedi and Sith battled each other over countless generations.*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)

During a period of [time](/wiki/Time "Time") known as the [Hundred-Year Darkness](/wiki/Hundred-Year_Darkness "Hundred-Year Darkness"), a [rogue Jedi](/wiki/Unidentified_rogue_Jedi "Unidentified rogue Jedi") came to believe that the true power of [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") lay not through [contemplation and passivity](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force"), but that only by tapping into the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") could the true [potential](/wiki/Force_power "Force power") of the Force be reached. The [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") rebuked this and [cast](/wiki/Exile_(punishment) "Exile (punishment)") the fallen [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") out of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"); however, the rogue Jedi gained followers and formed a new order[[14]](#cite_note-Sith-14) that eventually became the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith").[[15]](#cite_note-Star_Wars_9-15) Eventually, [war](/wiki/Sith_war_(Old_Republic) "Sith war (Old Republic)") broke out between the Jedi and the Sith,[[5]](#cite_note-Tarkin-5) during which time the Sith built [empires](/wiki/Sith_Empire "Sith Empire") which were sustained through [slavery](/wiki/Slavery "Slavery"),[[16]](#cite_note-Death_Star_Superlaser-16)[[17]](#cite_note-Big_Bang-17) but it would be the Jedi and the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") that ultimately achieved victory.[[18]](#cite_note-Nexus_of_Power-18) Even so, the Jedi and the Sith continued to do [battle](/wiki/Sith_Wars "Sith Wars") over the [millennia](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium").[[19]](#cite_note-Helmet-19)

The Jedi came into [conflict](/wiki/Mandalorian-Jedi_War "Mandalorian-Jedi War") with the [Mandalorians](/wiki/Mandalorian "Mandalorian"),[[20]](#cite_note-The_Mandalore_Plot-20) but the Old Republic continued to fend off the onslaught of the Sith as the [Old Republic fell](/wiki/Fall_of_the_Old_Republic "Fall of the Old Republic") and a dark age began.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

## The war[]

:   "*The Sith were eventually exterminated—or so it seemed—and order was restored.*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)

 [![The Jedi-Sith War ended with Darth Bane as the sole surviving Dark Lord of the Sith.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/df/DarthBaneHS-CM.png/revision/latest?cb=20200706141738) [![The Jedi-Sith War ended with Darth Bane as the sole surviving Dark Lord of the Sith.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/df/DarthBaneHS-CM.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200706141738)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/df/DarthBaneHS-CM.png/revision/latest?cb=20200706141738) 

The Jedi-Sith War ended with Darth Bane as the sole surviving Dark Lord of the Sith.

 

During the Jedi-Sith War, the Jedi's [Army of Light](/wiki/Army_of_Light "Army of Light") conducted crusades against the Sith [Brotherhood of Darkness](/wiki/Brotherhood_of_Darkness "Brotherhood of Darkness").[[12]](#cite_note-Endless-12) During the war, the Sith used a [dark side stronghold](/wiki/Ando_Sith_temple "Ando Sith temple") on [Ando](/wiki/Ando "Ando").[[21]](#cite_note-Obi-Wan_4-21) Within the conflict, the Sith had [taken over](/wiki/Battle_of_Coruscant_(Jedi-Sith_War) "Battle of Coruscant (Jedi-Sith War)") [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), and later the Jedi [liberated](/wiki/Liberation_of_Coruscant "Liberation of Coruscant") the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet").[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) Though the Sith nearly succeeded in conquering the Republic,[[22]](#cite_note-TSWB-22) they were [defeated](/wiki/Defeat_of_the_Sith "Defeat of the Sith") at [Ruusan](/wiki/Ruusan "Ruusan")[[3]](#cite_note-Helmet_Collection_29-3) in the [year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") [1032 BBY](/wiki/1032_BBY "1032 BBY").[[2]](#cite_note-Date-2)

The ancient Sith Order's ranks were culled through "unchecked ambitions and infighting" as ambitious Sith Lords vied for power and dominance, which, in turn, created an opportunity for the Jedi[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) to remove the Sith as a "viable threat" to the Republic. The result was the near-complete extermination of the Sith;[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13) however, the extinction of the Sith was averted by the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane"),[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) who survived and emerged from the Jedi-Sith War as the last survivor of his Order.[[7]](#cite_note-Sacrifice-7) To Bane, the Sith Order had grown weak from infighting[[24]](#cite_note-Dark_Side-24) and needed to be reforged.[[7]](#cite_note-Sacrifice-7)

## Aftermath[]

:   "*The Republic victory was so definitive that it reinvented itself, resettling its capital on Coruscant after a devastating ouster. History reset its chrono on this date, and from that moment, the modern Galactic Republic was born.*"
:   ―Janyor[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

 [![History began anew with Coruscant as the capital world of the modern Galactic Republic.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Galactic_Senate.png/revision/latest?cb=20130118044253) [![History began anew with Coruscant as the capital world of the modern Galactic Republic.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Galactic_Senate.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20130118044253)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Galactic_Senate.png/revision/latest?cb=20130118044253) 

History began anew with Coruscant as the capital world of the modern Galactic Republic.

 

Despite the fall of Coruscant having a devastating effect on the Republic, it resettled its [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") on the [ecumenopolis](/wiki/Ecumenopolis "Ecumenopolis") by the end of the Jedi-Sith War. Years of full-scale war with the Sith brought about the decline and fall of the Republic's pre-modern incarnation. Following the near destruction of the Sith, the Republic was not only restored but reinvented as the "modern Galactic Republic."[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

Coruscant's restoration as the Republic's seat of power marked the official reset of [galactic history](/wiki/Timeline_of_galactic_history "Timeline of galactic history"), which thenceforth began afresh with the re-founding of the Republic in its modern form. While this version of the Republic was considered the "latest evolution" of the government that had existed for millennia, the history prior to the Republic's rebirth, including the Jedi-Sith War, became a forgotten dark age collectively known as the "Old Republic."[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) Around [972 BBY](/wiki/972_BBY "972 BBY"), the [Galactic Accord of Systems](/wiki/Galactic_Accord_of_Systems "Galactic Accord of Systems") was established should another war break out.[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11)

:   ―Janyor[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

 [![The term "Old Republic" referred to an all-but-forgotten age predating the Republic's rebirth.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/24/Great_Scourge_of_Malachor_aftermath.png/revision/latest?cb=20201027143624) [![The term "Old Republic" referred to an all-but-forgotten age predating the Republic's rebirth.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/24/Great_Scourge_of_Malachor_aftermath.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20201027143624)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/24/Great_Scourge_of_Malachor_aftermath.png/revision/latest?cb=20201027143624) 

The term "Old Republic" referred to an all-but-forgotten age predating the Republic's rebirth.

 

The Jedi-Sith War was remembered as the final war in the history of the Old Republic prior to the Republic's rebirth as well as [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Bane's creation of the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two"). The accounts that chronicled the war included *[A History of Persuasive Art in the Galaxy](/wiki/A_History_of_Persuasive_Art_in_the_Galaxy "A History of Persuasive Art in the Galaxy")*, a [book](/wiki/Book "Book") on the history of [propaganda](/wiki/Propaganda "Propaganda"), which also featured the [works](/wiki/Painting "Painting") of the [Bith](/wiki/Bith "Bith") [artist](/wiki/Artist "Artist") [Janyor](/wiki/Janyor "Janyor");[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) *[The Secrets of the Jedi](/wiki/The_Secrets_of_the_Jedi "The Secrets of the Jedi")* by [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker");[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13) and *[The Secrets of the Sith](/wiki/The_Secrets_of_the_Sith "The Secrets of the Sith")* by the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious").[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) However, these works focused more on the long-term results of the war rather than its events.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23)

### The age of the Republic[]

:   [![icon](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/04/Blue_Glass_Arrow.svg/revision/latest?cb=20090726033133) Main article: [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic")

:   "*From the ashes of that conflict, the Galactic Republic was born.*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)

 [![The Jedi-Sith War was succeeded by a millennium of peace under the Galactic Republic.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest?cb=20180202010839) [![The Jedi-Sith War was succeeded by a millennium of peace under the Galactic Republic.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20180202010839)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest?cb=20180202010839) 

The Jedi-Sith War was succeeded by a millennium of peace under the Galactic Republic.

 

Under the Jedi Order's protection, the Republic succeeded in uniting much of [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") under the rule of the [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") on Coruscant.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) The [Republic Military](/wiki/Republic_Military "Republic Military") was demobilized following the Jedi-Sith War,[[5]](#cite_note-Tarkin-5) leaving the Republic in a demilitarized state until the formation of the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") at the end of the [Separatist Crisis](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis").[[25]](#cite_note-Episode_II-25) The [Republic Era](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") was, for the most part, devoid of armed conflicts with the exception of a few isolated incidents that were quelled by the peacekeeping efforts of the Jedi.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) As such, it was also referred to as the "Great Peace."[[6]](#cite_note-Rise-6)

The Republic rose to the pinnacle of its power during the time of the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"),[[26]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-26) a sub-era within the overall Republic Era that lasted roughly a thousand years. Driven by a sense of manifest destiny, the Republic adopted an expansionist policy that aimed to unite the entire galaxy under the [Republic banner](/wiki/Republic_crest "Republic crest").[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

The age of [democratic rule](/wiki/Democracy "Democracy") endured for a millennium[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) until the Republic was [replaced](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") by the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") at the end of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars")[[27]](#cite_note-Episode_III-27) in [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY").[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11) According to the book *A History of Persuasive Art in the Galaxy*, the Republic Era was "the last era of peace" in galactic history.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4)

### The light of the Jedi[]

:   [![icon](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/04/Blue_Glass_Arrow.svg/revision/latest?cb=20090726033133) Main article: [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order")

:   "*With the Sith no longer a viable threat, the Jedi expanded their role, dedicating themselves to maintaining justice and preserving harmony across the known galaxy.*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)

 [![The Jedi Order achieved the pinnacle of their power while serving as guardians of the Galactic Republic.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e9/Jedi-of-the-High-Republic.png/revision/latest?cb=20200227023613) [![The Jedi Order achieved the pinnacle of their power while serving as guardians of the Galactic Republic.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e9/Jedi-of-the-High-Republic.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200227023613)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e9/Jedi-of-the-High-Republic.png/revision/latest?cb=20200227023613) 

The Jedi Order achieved the pinnacle of their power while serving as guardians of the Galactic Republic.

 

Having defeated the Sith, the Jedi expanded their role in the galaxy, closely working with the Senate[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13) of the reborn Galactic Republic in the pursuit of peace.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) Generations of Jedi dedicated themselves to the cause of peace, promoting justice and harmony across the known galaxy[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13) as peacekeepers of the Republic.[[25]](#cite_note-Episode_II-25) Assisted by the Republic [Judicials](/wiki/Judicial "Judicial"), the [Jedi Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") were responsible for maintaining law and order, namely by mediating conflicts to bring about peaceful solutions between various rival factions and planets, as well as serving as interplanetary [law enforcement](/wiki/Law_enforcement_agency "Law enforcement agency") by pursuing wanted and dangerous criminals, [pirates](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate"), and [smugglers](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler").[[5]](#cite_note-Tarkin-5)

The Jedi Order reached the apex of its power during the High Republic Era,[[26]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-26) a time which saw the Order expanding its influence to the galaxy's [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories").[[28]](#cite_note-Kylo_2-28) In addition, the Order maintained its presence on Coruscant, the galactic [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") of the Republic,[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13) using the [Jedi Grand Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace")[[22]](#cite_note-TSWB-22) as its headquarters as well as a [training center](/wiki/Jedi_temple "Jedi temple") for the [education](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") of [future generations of Jedi](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") in the ways of the Force.[[6]](#cite_note-Rise-6) The Jedi swore their allegiance to the Senate, and the Republic overall,[[27]](#cite_note-Episode_III-27) officially serving as the Senate's [emissaries](/wiki/Ambassador "Ambassador")[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_I-10) and enforcers when necessary.[[25]](#cite_note-Episode_II-25) Nevertheless, the Order retained its autonomy and was independently governed by the Jedi High [Council](/wiki/Jedi_Council "Jedi Council").[[13]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-13)

### The return of the Sith[]

:   [![icon](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/04/Blue_Glass_Arrow.svg/revision/latest?cb=20090726033133) Main article: [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith")

:   "*After thousands of years of war, the Sith were pushed to the edge of extinction. From the darkness, those who survived laid the foundations for our Order's return…and my rise to power.*"
:   ―Darth Sidious[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23)

 [![The Rule of Two was created by Darth Bane, spawning a thousand-year Sith lineage that culminated in the rise of Darth Sidious.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/a3/SithOrder-SOTJ.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200113231523) [![The Rule of Two was created by Darth Bane, spawning a thousand-year Sith lineage that culminated in the rise of Darth Sidious.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/a3/SithOrder-SOTJ.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20200113231523)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/a3/SithOrder-SOTJ.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200113231523) 

The Rule of Two was created by Darth Bane, spawning a thousand-year Sith lineage that culminated in the rise of Darth Sidious.

 

Realizing that his fellow Sith [Lords](/wiki/Lord "Lord") destroyed themselves with their greed for power, Darth Bane reformed the Order by establishing the Rule of Two.[[7]](#cite_note-Sacrifice-7) The successor of the [Doctrine of the Dyad](/wiki/Doctrine_of_the_Dyad "Doctrine of the Dyad"),[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) the Rule of Two mandated that only two Sith Lords—a [master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") and an [apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice")—could exist at any given time, lest they fall prey to infighting.[[7]](#cite_note-Sacrifice-7) Another reason he created the Rule of Two was so that the Sith could operate in secret, working from the shadows and favoring guile and conspiracy rather than brute force until it would be their time to take revenge,[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) rise again, and subjugate the galaxy.[[29]](#cite_note-Bane-DB-29)

Although the Jedi discovered Bane and also learned about his creation of the Rule of Two, the Sith Lord's defeat convinced the Jedi that his plans to restore the Sith to power had been aborted.[[30]](#cite_note-Bane-SWE-30) Unbeknownst to the Jedi, the reformed Sith Order survived the death of Bane, who was killed by his apprentice,[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) [Darth Zannah](/wiki/Darth_Zannah "Darth Zannah").[[31]](#cite_note-Helmet2-31) As a consequence, the Sith survived for another millennium, cycling through generations of Sith Lords who completed their ascendency in the dark side by supplanting the masters who trained them.[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23)

 [![Darth Sidious (pictured) restored the Sith to power by purging the Jedi and ruling over the galaxy as Emperor.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/37/Insider224-Palpatinecover.png/revision/latest?cb=20240630020300) [![Darth Sidious (pictured) restored the Sith to power by purging the Jedi and ruling over the galaxy as Emperor.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/37/Insider224-Palpatinecover.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20240630020300)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/37/Insider224-Palpatinecover.png/revision/latest?cb=20240630020300) 

Darth Sidious (pictured) restored the Sith to power by purging the Jedi and ruling over the galaxy as Emperor.

 

Bane was the last Sith Lord to be [entombed](/wiki/Tomb_of_Darth_Bane "Tomb of Darth Bane") in the [Valley of the Dark Lords](/wiki/Valley_of_the_Dark_Lords "Valley of the Dark Lords") on [Moraband](/wiki/Moraband "Moraband"),[[30]](#cite_note-Bane-SWE-30) the traditional [homeworld](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") of the Sith which they abandoned after it was scarred by many wars.[[32]](#cite_note-Moraband-DB-32) The Sith stayed in hiding for a thousand years, their survival unknown to the Jedi, as they plotted their revenge against the Jedi Order. The Sith remained a distant memory until the rise of Darth Sidious, a Dark Lord of the Sith, and his apprentice, [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul"), in the waning years of the Galactic Republic.[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_I-10) Sidious' machinations achieved the fulfillment of the [Grand Plan](/wiki/Grand_Plan "Grand Plan") that began with Bane;[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) the Jedi Order was [purged](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") and the Republic became the Empire, with Sidious as the [reigning](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor").[[27]](#cite_note-Episode_III-27) Sidious ruled the galaxy for slightly over twenty years[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11) with his final Sith apprentice, [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"),[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) by his side as his right-hand man[[33]](#cite_note-Bust_Collection-33) and personal enforcer.[[34]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-34)

Vader had once been known as Anakin Skywalker, a Jedi Knight prophesied to be his Order's [Chosen One](/wiki/Chosen_One "Chosen One") in order to bring balance to the Force.[[27]](#cite_note-Episode_III-27) After years of living as a Sith Lord,[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11) however, Vader would redeem himself and return to the light side thanks to the compassion of his son, the Jedi Knight [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), during the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor")[[35]](#cite_note-RotJ-35) of [4 ABY](/wiki/4_ABY "4 ABY").[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11) The returned Anakin Skywalker betrayed and killed Sidious,[[35]](#cite_note-RotJ-35) who secretly managed to cheat death via the power of [transference](/wiki/Transference "Transference").[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) Sidious's empire would be formally defeated a year after his death with the [Battle of Jakku](/wiki/Battle_of_Jakku "Battle of Jakku") of [5 ABY](/wiki/5_ABY "5 ABY"). In [35 ABY](/wiki/35_ABY "35 ABY"),[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11) Sidious announced his return to the galaxy and attempted to forge a new regime,[[23]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Sith-23) only to be destroyed in both body and soul[[22]](#cite_note-TSWB-22) during the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol") by his paternal granddaughter, the Jedi [Rey](/wiki/Rey_Skywalker "Rey Skywalker").[[11]](#cite_note-Timelines-11)

## Behind the scenes[]

 [![The New Sith Wars as depicted in Star Wars Legends](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/95/NewSithWars.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20051103161654) [![The New Sith Wars as depicted in Star Wars Legends](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/95/NewSithWars.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20051103161654)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/95/NewSithWars.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20051103161654) 

The New Sith Wars as depicted in *Star Wars* Legends

 

The Jedi-Sith War was first mentioned in the [2016](/wiki/2016 "2016") reference book *[Star Wars Propaganda: A History of Persuasive Art in the Galaxy](/wiki/Star_Wars_Propaganda:_A_History_of_Persuasive_Art_in_the_Galaxy "Star Wars Propaganda: A History of Persuasive Art in the Galaxy")*.[[4]](#cite_note-SWP-4) It was first identified in the [2019](/wiki/2019 "2019") [Fantasy Flight Games](/wiki/Fantasy_Flight_Games "Fantasy Flight Games") reference book *[Rise of the Separatists](/wiki/Rise_of_the_Separatists "Rise of the Separatists")*.[[6]](#cite_note-Rise-6)

In [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"), this conflict was known as the [New Sith Wars](/wiki/New_Sith_Wars "New Sith Wars") where it was fought from [2000](/wiki/2000_BBY/Legends "2000 BBY/Legends") to [1000 BBY](/wiki/1000_BBY/Legends "1000 BBY/Legends").[[36]](#cite_note-nec-36) and made its first appearance in *[Dark Forces: Jedi Knight](/wiki/Dark_Forces:_Jedi_Knight "Dark Forces: Jedi Knight")*.[[37]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knight-37)

[George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas") states that there was never a Jedi-Sith war, and that the Sith destroyed themselves, not the Jedi. "'Oh, well, there was a war between the Jedi and the Sith.' Well, that never happened. That's just made up by fans or somebody. What really happened is, the Sith ruled the universe for a while, 2,000 years ago. Each Sith has an apprentice, but the problem was, each Sith Lord got to be powerful. And the Sith Lords would try to kill each other because they all wanted to be the most powerful. So in the end they killed each other off, and there wasn't anything left."[[38]](#cite_note-Ep._1_Oral_History-38)
