***The Clone Wars*** is a [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") formed of a group of [Wookieepedians](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") seeking to upgrade and maintain all articles related to the [2008](/wiki/2008 "2008")–[2020](/wiki/2020 "2020") *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")* television series, including articles related to the [feature film](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(film) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (film)"), [Legacy](/wiki/The_Clone_Wars_Legacy "The Clone Wars Legacy"), the [webcomics](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(webcomics) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (webcomics)"), [monthly comic series](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(comic_series) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (comic series)"), *[The Clone Wars – Battle Tales](/wiki/Star_Wars_Adventures:_The_Clone_Wars_%E2%80%93_Battle_Tales "Star Wars Adventures: The Clone Wars – Battle Tales")*, the [novel series](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(novels) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (novels)"), and the [graphic novellas](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(graphic_novellas) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (graphic novellas)"). This also includes video and platform games, such as the [mobile game](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(mobile) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (mobile)"), *[Jedi Alliance](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars:_Jedi_Alliance "Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Jedi Alliance")*, *[Lightsaber Duels](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars:_Lightsaber_Duels "Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Lightsaber Duels")*, *[Republic Heroes](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars:_Republic_Heroes "Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Republic Heroes")*, and *[Clone Wars Adventures](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Clone_Wars_Adventures_(video_game) "Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures (video game)")*.

In [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023"), it was [decided](/wiki/Forum:SH:A_bold_new_future_for_WP:TCW "Forum:SH:A bold new future for WP:TCW") to incorporate the [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021") sequel series to *The Clone Wars*, *[Star Wars: The Bad Batch](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Bad_Batch "Star Wars: The Bad Batch")*, and the [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") *[Tales](/wiki/Tales "Tales")* season *[Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Tales_of_the_Jedi_(television_series) "Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi (television series)")* into this project. This decision was made because of the shared animation style and how these shows incorporate many topics also featured in *The Clone Wars*. Therefore, keeping these topics under one banner will prevent Wookieepedians from dividing their focus across multiple projects. Further *Tales* content, such as the [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024") *Tales* season *[Star Wars: Tales of the Empire](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Tales_of_the_Empire "Star Wars: Tales of the Empire")*, also fall under this project banner.

Please note that while the target audience of animated *Star Wars* content can be a somewhat younger audience, this is by no means a hindrance to our goal. We still expect the same amount of quality that would be expected of any other *Star Wars* articles, which includes acceptable spelling and grammar.

If you are considering joining the project, take a look at our mission. If you would like to join, please add your name with three tildes (~~~) at the bottom of the page under the "[Participants](#Participants)" section.

## Instructions[]

### Writing[]

1. Use (at least) acceptable English grammar and spelling.
2. Write from a balanced and fair perspective with a [neutral point-of-view](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style#Perspective "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style").
3. Follow the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style"), [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide"), and all other [policies on Wookieepedia](/wiki/Category:Policies_on_Wookieepedia "Category:Policies on Wookieepedia").
4. Do not add [speculation](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Attribution#No_original_research "Wookieepedia:Attribution") of any kind, whether it is unconfirmed info or trivial information. If it is not stated by a [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm_Ltd. "Lucasfilm Ltd.")-affiliated (or official) source, it is not [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and will not be accepted on Wookieepedia.
5. All information in an article must be [sourced](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing").
6. "Behind the scenes" section should include notes on appearances. If the subject appears in multiple parts of a series (such as many episodes of a season), the series itself can be mentioned, along with a general summary.
7. Don't forget a "Personality and traits" section for characters where applicable, as well as a "Powers and abilities" section, also where applicable.
8. Adhere to the established TCW timeline, most recently updated in *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Episode Guide](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars:_Episode_Guide "Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Episode Guide")* ([June](/wiki/June "June") [2013](/wiki/2013 "2013")). Please see [this page](/wiki/Forum:SH:TCW_Timeline_2012 "Forum:SH:TCW Timeline 2012") for the timeline.

### Editing[]

1. Check for flawless spelling and grammar, as well as cohesive sentence flow and context.
2. Make sure articles follow the Manual of Style, Layout Guide, and all other Wookieepedia policies.
3. Make sure all information and included pictures and quotes are properly sourced.
4. Fix templates and check for excessive/incorrect links.
5. Check for any appearances or sources that may have been missed.

## Articles[]

**All articles originating from *The Clone Wars* series have a goal to eventually obtain Featured, Good, or Comprehensive status.** Articles that cannot reach the required length can be put up for nomination for Comprehensive status. Even the most minor characters and articles have the potential to be upgraded to higher article status.

### Choosing an article[]

Many characters appear in *The Clone Wars* series and related material—some old, some new. Please note that while the project aims to cover many subjects appearing in the series, we cannot cover every single person, place, or thing that appears. Furthermore, it is improper to try to use *this project* to focus on subjects that extensively appear elsewhere. For all articles that appear in *The Clone Wars* series, their related and corresponding sections should be maintained.

In choosing articles to add as projects, the following questions should be asked about the article. **For an article to be added as a planned/future project, the answers to at least one of these questions must be "yes."**

1. Does the subject appear for the **first time** in *The Clone Wars* material? If not,
2. Are the **majority** of the subject's appearances in *The Clone Wars* material? If not,
3. Do(es) the subject's appearance(s) in *The Clone Wars* material have an **important impact**?

If you have questions, do not hesitate to bring the article up on the [project's talk page](/wiki/Wookieepedia_talk:WookieeProject_The_Clone_Wars "Wookieepedia talk:WookieeProject The Clone Wars").

Once the article is added as a future project, please place the [notice](#Talk_page_template) below on the article's talk page so that others may get involved.
