**Reva Sevander** was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") who served as a [member](/wiki/Sister_(Inquisitorius) "Sister (Inquisitorius)") of the [Inquisitorius](/wiki/Inquisitorius "Inquisitorius") under the title of **Third Sister** during the early [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") and briefly held the position of **Grand Inquisitor**. Prior to the [rise](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") of [Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") [Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), Sevander held the rank of [Jedi Initiate](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") during her [time](/wiki/Time "Time") in the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). Ambitious and reckless, the Third Sister quickly rose through the ranks of the Inquisitorius despite being ostracized and distrusted by the other [Inquisitors](/wiki/Inquisitor "Inquisitor"). Though she willingly served the Empire, and [harried](/wiki/Jedi_hunter "Jedi hunter") fugitive [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") and other [Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force")-sensitives, Sevander was driven by an ulterior motive. Haunted by the trauma from her past, the Third Sister sought to exact revenge on the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") for his slaughter of the Jedi during [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), which had taken away the only family she had ever known.

Sevander was only a Jedi [youngling](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") at the time of the [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") at the end of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"). She and her fellow initiates were in the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") at the time it was [attacked](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall") by the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Vader and his [501st Legion](/wiki/501st_Legion "501st Legion"). She watched as her friends were slain, and played dead amidst the bodies. [Years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") later, she resurfaced and joined the Inquisitorius of the [Galactic](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") Empire, both of which had the Dark [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") counted among its leaders. Because she knew that Vader had been the [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") Anakin Skywalker before joining the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith"), Sevander, christened as Third Sister, became obsessed with [locating](/wiki/Hunt_for_Obi-Wan "Hunt for Obi-Wan") his former [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master"), the fugitive [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi"), as a means of exacting revenge by entering the Dark Lord's good graces and [assassinating](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") him whilst he was fixated on Kenobi. To lure her quarry out of hiding, the Third Sister arranged the [kidnapping](/wiki/Ambush_on_Alderaan "Ambush on Alderaan") of [Princess](/wiki/Princess "Princess") [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo"), the [daughter](/wiki/House_of_Organa "House of Organa") of [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Bail Organa](/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa "Bail Prestor Organa"), who she believed would turn to his old ally for help.

Though she came close to capturing Kenobi when he [rescued](/wiki/Search_for_Princess_Leia "Search for Princess Leia") Organa on [Daiyu](/wiki/Daiyu "Daiyu"), the Third Sister was prevented from doing so by the intervention of [the Grand Inquisitor](/wiki/The_Grand_Inquisitor "The Grand Inquisitor"), who had enough of her rashness. The Third Sister unexpectedly turned on the [Grand Inquisitor](/wiki/Grand_Inquisitor "Grand Inquisitor") and left him for dead, but lost the Jedi Master and the Princess when they fled the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet"). Having been notified of his old mentor's resurgence, Vader offered the Third Sister the opportunity to prove herself worthy of assuming the position of Grand Inquisitor at his side if she found the Jedi Master. Kenobi was [tracked](/wiki/Battle_on_Mapuzo "Battle on Mapuzo") to [Mapuzo](/wiki/Mapuzo "Mapuzo"), where the Third Sister discovered that he had help from a Jedi-ferrying network known as the [Hidden Path](/wiki/Hidden_Path "Hidden Path"). There she took Organa [hostage](/wiki/Prisoner "Prisoner") once again to root both Kenobi and his new allies out of hiding. With the Princess in captivity in the [Fortress Inquisitorius](/wiki/Fortress_Inquisitorius "Fortress Inquisitorius") on [Nur](/wiki/Nur "Nur"), the Third Sister laid another trap for the Jedi Master when he and Path member [Tala Durith](/wiki/Tala_Durith "Tala Durith") [infiltrated](/wiki/Rescue_of_Princess_Leia_(9_BBY) "Rescue of Princess Leia (9 BBY)") the base to rescue the young girl. The Third Sister planted a tracking device on Organa's [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid"), [L0-LA59](/wiki/L0-LA59 "L0-LA59"), and allowed Kenobi and Durith to escape with Organa so that she could [uncover](/wiki/Hunt_for_the_Hidden_Path "Hunt for the Hidden Path") the Path.

Impressed with her, Vader awarded the Third Sister the rank of Grand Inquisitor, and had her oversee the [attack](/wiki/Attack_on_Jabiim "Attack on Jabiim") on the Path's headquarters on [Jabiim](/wiki/Jabiim "Jabiim"). There Third Sister confronted Kenobi, who realized what her true intentions were. The Jedi Master convinced her that they could defeat the [Emperor's Fist](/wiki/Emperor%27s_Fist "Emperor's Fist") together, giving her the chance to slay him with a seeming distraction provided by his and the Path's escape. The Third Sister made an attempt on the Dark Lord's life but was outmatched and left for dead by him and the much-alive Grand Inquisitor. While lying in the dirt, Sevander found a [imagecaster](/wiki/Imagecaster "Imagecaster") that belonged to Kenobi, which played a message from Bail Organa detailing a boy under the protection of [Owen Lars](/wiki/Owen_Lars "Owen Lars") who, along with Leia, was in danger of being discovered. Using information from that message, no longer an Inquisitor, a vengeance-hungry Sevander traveled to [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") to kill [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), one of the [offspring](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family") of the unaware Vader who had been under Kenobi's watch. As she prepared to kill him, however, she realized that she would become like the Dark Lord, and decided to return the boy to [his guardians](/wiki/Lars_family "Lars family"). Sevander was again met by Kenobi, and at his advice she looked to let go of her past and move on with her life, leaving her ultimate fate a mystery.

## Biography

### Early life to Order 66

:   "*We thought he was there to help us. I tried to help them, but I couldn't. I was too weak. When he left, I played dead. Hid with the bodies. Felt them go cold. They were the only family I knew… and he slaughtered them.*"
:   ―The Third Sister remembering Darth Vader's attack on the Jedi Temple[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

A [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender")[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) [born](/wiki/Reproduction "Reproduction") around [31 BBY](/wiki/31_BBY "31 BBY"),[[1]](#cite_note-Birth_date-1) Reva Sevander[[8]](#cite_note-BTS-8) was taken into the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") and lived within the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet")[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9) [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), where she trained as a [Jedi Initiate](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") during her [childhood](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling").[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) During her time at the temple, she was trained by [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda")[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Side_Pocket_Expert-3) and came to consider her fellow younglings her family.[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9) In [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY"), she and her [clan](/wiki/Reva_Sevander%27s_Initiate_clan "Reva Sevander's Initiate clan")[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) of [Jedi Initiates](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") were [training](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") under the instruction of [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Minas Velti](/wiki/Minas_Velti "Minas Velti").[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) As the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") issued [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), his [apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice"), the former [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") led the [501st Legion](/wiki/501st_Legion "501st Legion") in an [assault on the Jedi Temple](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall").[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_III-10)

[Clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") entered the room Velti, Sevander, and the other younglings were in and began to fire at Velti, who quickly cut them down and led the younglings away. The group made their way through the [temple](/wiki/Jedi_temple "Jedi temple") halls as the Jedi Master protected them from the [clones](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") until she was [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") by them, leaving her charges to fend for themselves.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) They ran from the fighting until they came across Skywalker himself. Sevander and the other younglings assumed he was there to help, but became horrified when he started slaughtering them, having not realized he had turned to the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") as the newly-christened [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Vader. Sevander was stabbed but survived and hid under the corpses of the other younglings in the group she was with.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) After being impaled by the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord"), she later received cybernetic replacements due to the injury,[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) but she would never forget the pain of betrayal and feeling of abandonment she felt amongst the corpses of her fallen family.[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9)

### Turning to the dark

Reva Sevander began a new life for herself as the mysterious Third Sister of the Inquisitorious.

 

Sevander eventually escaped the Jedi Temple and faced extreme survivor's guilt, [turning to the dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_adept "Dark side adept").[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) Wanting revenge on Vader, currently acting as the [commander-in-chief](/wiki/Commander-in-Chief "Commander-in-Chief") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire")'s [forces](/wiki/Imperial_Military "Imperial Military"), she willingly joined the regime's [Inquisitorius](/wiki/Inquisitorius "Inquisitorius") as an [Inquisitor](/wiki/Inquisitor "Inquisitor"), in order to get close to him. Reva formulated a plan to kill Vader by bringing in his former mentor, [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") to the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith"),[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) seemingly with the goal of winning his approval,[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6) only to kill him while he was distracted with Kenobi.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) This plan led to her becoming fixated on capturing the Jedi Master.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) Becoming known as the Third [Sister](/wiki/Sister_(Inquisitorius) "Sister (Inquisitorius)"),[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) she was tasked with [hunting](/wiki/Jedi_hunter "Jedi hunter") the last of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). [The Grand Inquisitor](/wiki/The_Grand_Inquisitor "The Grand Inquisitor") wrote her off as "least of them," recalling that she had come to them from "the gutter" and that her dark side ability was the only reason she was able to remain within the Inquisitorius' ranks.[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)

During her service to the Inquisitorius, she hunted surviving Jedi and Force-sensitives across the galaxy, including within far-flung locations. Her missions included a [hunt](/wiki/Hunt_on_Pantora "Hunt on Pantora") on[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) the [Outer Rim](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories") [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon")[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9) [Pantora](/wiki/Pantora "Pantora"), a [hunt](/wiki/Hunt_on_Valo "Hunt on Valo") on[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) the Outer Rim planet[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9) [Valo](/wiki/Valo "Valo"), a [hunt](/wiki/Hunt_on_Cerea "Hunt on Cerea") on[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) the [Mid Rim](/wiki/Mid_Rim_Territories "Mid Rim Territories") planet[[11]](#cite_note-Guide_to_Batuu-11) [Cerea](/wiki/Cerea "Cerea"), a [hunt](/wiki/Hunt_on_Boz_Pity "Hunt on Boz Pity") on[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) the Mid Rim[[12]](#cite_note-TFABG-12) planet[[13]](#cite_note-Collapse-13) [Boz Pity](/wiki/Boz_Pity "Boz Pity"), and a [hunt](/wiki/Hunt_on_Alsakan "Hunt on Alsakan") on[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) the planet[[14]](#cite_note-Boba_and_others-14) [Alsakan](/wiki/Alsakan "Alsakan")[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) in the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds").[[12]](#cite_note-TFABG-12) Nonetheless, much like the [Third Brother](/wiki/Third_Brother "Third Brother"), the specific actions the Third Sister carried out in the name of the Galactic Empire remained a mystery.[[15]](#cite_note-Encyclopedia-15)

### A hunt on Tatooine

:   "*Tell me where the Jedi is, or this man and his family die! The Jedi are cowards. They failed you, abandoned you. There is no point in protecting them. They would not do the same for you. But you can save this man. Save his family.*"
:   ―The Third Sister to the people of Anchorhead, while threatening Owen Lars[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)

 [![The Third Sister's attempt to cut down Nari is stopped by the Grand Inquisitor.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/NotYetSister-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220701011048) [![The Third Sister's attempt to cut down Nari is stopped by the Grand Inquisitor.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/NotYetSister-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20220701011048)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/NotYetSister-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220701011048) 

The Third Sister's attempt to cut down Nari is stopped by the Grand Inquisitor.

 

In [9 BBY](/wiki/9_BBY "9 BBY"),[[16]](#cite_note-Kenobi_date-16) the Third Sister was dispatched to the [desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine")[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) in the Outer Rim[[9]](#cite_note-CompEncyclopedia-9) alongside the Grand Inquisitor and the [Fifth Brother](/wiki/Fifth_Brother "Fifth Brother"), [tasked](/wiki/Search_for_Nari "Search for Nari") with locating the Jedi [Nari](/wiki/Nari "Nari"). While her superior attempted to intimidate the locals in a saloon, the impatient Reva interrupted him by throwing a [knife](/wiki/Knife "Knife") at the saloon owner. Before it could kill the saloon owner, Nari, who was in the saloon, caught it with [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"). The Third Sister tried to cut him down with her lightsaber, only to be stopped from doing so by the invisible grip of the Grand Inquisitor, who instead wanted him alive for interrogation, allowing Nari to escape. The Third Sister then attempted to give chase, but was halted by the Grand Inquisitor, who reprimanded her for her recklessness and fixation on Kenobi, while the Fifth [Brother](/wiki/Brother_(Inquisitorius) "Brother (Inquisitorius)") instead chased the fugitive Jedi.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)

The Third Sister and Fifth Brother later went to [Anchorhead](/wiki/Anchorhead "Anchorhead"), and began to ask for information. Much to her companion's irritation, she began to threaten the townspeople, at one point [severing](/wiki/Dismemberment "Dismemberment") the hand of a [local](/wiki/Unidentified_Anchorhead_woman "Unidentified Anchorhead woman") who claimed the Empire had no power over them on Tatooine. Reva focused her attention on [moisture farmer](/wiki/Moisture_farmer "Moisture farmer") [Owen Lars](/wiki/Owen_Lars "Owen Lars"), announcing to the crowd that she would kill the man and his [family](/wiki/Lars_family "Lars family") if no one spoke up about their knowledge about the Jedi, at which point the Fifth Brother intervened and forced her to stand down. Afterwards, he pulled her aside and chastised her for overstepping and continuing her pursuit of Kenobi in spite of the Grand Inquisitor explicitly forbidding it.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)

:   ―The Third Sister to Obi-Wan Kenobi[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)

The Grand Inquisitor confronts the Third Sister for having Leia Organa abducted.

 

Defying the misgivings and demands of her peers, the Third Sister hired a group of [bounty hunters](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") to kidnap the adopted daughter of [Alderaanian](/wiki/Alderaanian "Alderaanian") [Viceroy](/wiki/Viceroy "Viceroy") [Bail Prestor Organa](/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa "Bail Prestor Organa"), [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo"), hoping to attract Kenobi's attention and lure him into the open. She ordered the bounty hunters to bring the [Princess](/wiki/Princess "Princess") to [Daiyu](/wiki/Daiyu "Daiyu"), where she intended to confront the Jedi Master.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)

On Daiyu, the Third Sister was once again confronted by the Grand Inquisitor, accompanied by the Fifth Brother and the [Fourth Sister](/wiki/Fourth_Sister "Fourth Sister"), who ordered her to stand down while he took over her operation of hunting down Kenobi. Additionally, he berated her for kidnapping the daughter of an Imperial [senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") in order to achieve her goal, though she argued that they had done worse. The Grand Inquisitor then told the Third Sister that she would be punished for stepping out of line at a later point in [time](/wiki/Time "Time").[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)

Once again disobeying her superior's orders, the Third Sister placed a bounty on the Jedi Master's head, intending for the [credit](/wiki/Galactic_Credit_Standard "Galactic Credit Standard")-purposed lowlifes and [mercenaries](/wiki/Mercenary "Mercenary") to draw him out to her, while she waited on the rooftops of the city. After seeing an exchange of [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") fire between Kenobi and several bounty hunters, she began to run towards him. When she eventually reached the ground level she was stopped by the [con artist](/wiki/Con_artist "Con artist") [Haja Estree](/wiki/Haja_Estree "Haja Estree"), who pretended to be a Jedi in order to stall her and aid in Kenobi and Organa's escape. The Third Sister instead [probed](/wiki/Mind_probe "Mind probe") the charlatan's mind and discovered that her target would be making their escape from Daiyu via a cargo [shipyard](/wiki/Shipyard "Shipyard").[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)

 [![The Third Sister intercepted Kenobi in a hangar on Daiyu.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9b/ICanFeelYou-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220530202837) [![The Third Sister intercepted Kenobi in a hangar on Daiyu.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9b/ICanFeelYou-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20220530202837)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9b/ICanFeelYou-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220530202837) 

The Third Sister intercepted Kenobi in a hangar on Daiyu.

 

The Third Sister soon confronted her quarry at the shipyard, revealing to him that she intended to keep him alive so she could deliver him to Vader, revealing to Kenobi that he was still alive, and admitting that she was privy to her master's true identity as Anakin Skywalker. The Grand Inquisitor soon arrived and interrupted the Third Sister, bidding her to move out of his way and to watch and learn as he personally retrieved the Jedi Master. Briefly feigning obedience, the Third Sister stepped aside, only to stab the Grand Inquisitor in the abdomen, mockingly remarking that she had no intention whatsoever of letting him take all the glory of her achievement. Kenobi took the distraction to escape on a cargo ship with Organa as the Third Sister watched on and yelled threats at him.[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)

The Third Sister contacted her master to provide an update on the Jedi runaway. The Dark Lord told her that if she captured him, the position of Grand Inquisitor would be hers, but if she failed, she would not live to regret it. Afterwards, she met with the Fifth Brother and the Fourth Sister, where she instructed them to send out all the remaining [Probe droids](/wiki/Probe_droid "Probe droid") they had. The Fifth Brother questioned her authority and claimed that he was the next in line for the Grand Inquisitor's position, to which the Third Sister explained that she had just spoken with their master personally, and that he had asked her to lead the hunt. Despite his reluctance to following her orders, the Fifth Brother obliged.[[17]](#cite_note-Part_III-17)

Probes were sent to [Mapuzo](/wiki/Mapuzo "Mapuzo") in order to locate the Jedi Master and, once discovered, the Third Sister, the Dark Lord and the other Inquisitors journeyed to the planet to confront the runaway Jedi. The Third Sister watched as Vader tried to lure out Kenobi by killing some of the townspeople in a [mining village](/wiki/Mapuzo_mining_village "Mapuzo mining village") and at one point tried to interfere, in which the Fifth Brother stopped her and reminded her of their orders to stand back. As the townspeople were being rounded up, the Third Sister found a safe house belonging to a network known as the [Hidden Path](/wiki/Hidden_Path "Hidden Path"), where [Force-sensitives](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") escaped Imperials and given new identities. In the safehouse, she saw the emblem of the Jedi scratched onto a wall, bringing back painful memories she had; along with discovering a tunnel leading to the spaceport. Realizing that her quarry likely headed down it, the Third Sister made her way to the spaceport and killed the pilot waiting for them there. As her quarry, consisting only of Organa, arrived, The Third Sister kidnapped her once again.[[17]](#cite_note-Part_III-17)

### Invasion of Fortress Inquisitorius

:   "*You were warned what defeat would bring! I will tolerate your weakness no longer.*"

Organa was brought to [Fortress Inquisitorius](/wiki/Fortress_Inquisitorius "Fortress Inquisitorius") on the moon of [Nur](/wiki/Nur "Nur"), where the Third Sister attempted to interrogate the young Princess for the location of the Path. However, despite claiming that Kenobi was killed on Mapuzo and that no one was coming to save her, the girl refused to cooperate and remained defiant to the Inquisitor. Before Organa could be [tortured](/wiki/Torture "Torture") for answers, however, Kenobi and [Tala Durith](/wiki/Tala_Durith "Tala Durith") were able to distract the Third Sister and rescue the princess.[[18]](#cite_note-Part_IV-18)

As the trio attempted to escape, the Third Sister chased after them despite the Fifth Brother demanding the fortress locked down, accusing her of bungling the situation. Flanked by [stormtroopers](/wiki/Stormtrooper "Stormtrooper") and [Purge Troopers](/wiki/Purge_Trooper "Purge Trooper"), she was able to confront the three, cornered a hangar, but before they could be executed, a pair of [T-47 airspeeders](/wiki/T-47_airspeeder "T-47 airspeeder") arrived and rescued the three, though the Third Sister was able to knock one out of the sky. The Third Sister's bungling of the situation caused an infuriated Vader to confront and [Force choke](/wiki/Force_choke "Force choke") her in anger upon his arrival, preparing to kill her for her failure. However, her master was placated when she revealed that she installed a [homing beacon](/wiki/Homing_beacon "Homing beacon") inside Organa's droid [L0-LA59](/wiki/L0-LA59 "L0-LA59") and that it would lead them to Kenobi and whoever is with them.[[18]](#cite_note-Part_IV-18)

The Third Sister was promoted to Grand Inquisitor.

 

Having impressed Vader by tracking the Princess and the Jedi Master to [Jabiim](/wiki/Jabiim "Jabiim"), which housed the Path's main fortress, through Organa's droid, Lola, the Third Sister, aboard the Commander-in-Chief's personal [Star Destroyer](/wiki/Imperial-class_Star_Destroyer "Imperial-class Star Destroyer") [flagship](/wiki/Flagship "Flagship"), the *[Devastator](/wiki/Devastator "Devastator")*, was elevated to the rank of [Grand Inquisitor](/wiki/Grand_Inquisitor "Grand Inquisitor").[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Lola, having been reprogrammed while Leia was held hostage, snuck into the base's defenses and locked everyone inside while The Third Sister and Vader's troops, arriving on the Imperial shuttle *[Scythe](/wiki/Scythe_(Phi-class) "Scythe (Phi-class)")*, started bombarding the blast doors, beginning the [attack on Jabiim](/wiki/Attack_on_Jabiim "Attack on Jabiim").[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Soon, Kenobi tried to parley with Reva, deducing that she was present during the night of Order 66 and witnessing Vader leading the attack against her fellow younglings herself, having initially thought he was there to help fend off the clones. She revealed she survived Order 66 by playing dead and Kenobi soon realized that Reva did not truly serve Vader, but instead sought to bring justice to her fallen younglings by gaining his trust and then betraying him. However, the Third Sister, distrusting of the Jedi Master as he was not at the Temple to help her and her "family" escape, rejected his help, pointing out that the Dark Lord had been his own Padawan and asking why he did not stop him.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Sevander attempted to assassinate Vader.

 

After a brief but fierce exchange of fire between the Imperial troops and the members of the Path, which saw their leader Durith sacrifice herself against the oncoming troops, Kenobi decided to surrender with the intention of bringing Reva to Vader himself, asking her if she will let him slaughter more civilians, including children, and offered her to stop him again. When asked if the Dark Lord would see it coming, the Jedi Master reassured her that he would be too fixated in settling the score with his old mentor.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Soon, Kenobi was able to escape with the Path and Organa, leaving Vader, who had just reached the fortress, behind, open to Sevander's attack. The Inquisitor snuck up behind him with the intention to assassinate her master, but the Dark Lord, sensing her, stopped her before she could do the deed. Sevander, imbued with rage, tried to charge at him, but she quickly underestimated Vader's true powers and was knocked down multiple times. They quickly engaged in a lightsaber duel, though the Third Sister, momentarily haunted by memories of the Dark Lord slaughtering her fellow younglings, kept her guard down and was momentarily stabbed through the torso, weakening her. Sevander, realizing that the Grand Inquisitor survived his injuries, was relieved of her title of Grand Inquisitor due to her treachery and left to die. However, she endured and was able to activate a [holoprojector](/wiki/Holoprojector "Holoprojector") of a message from Bail Organa intended for Kenobi of his plans with Owen Lars and [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), a farm boy living on the desert planet of Tatooine who happened to be the son of the unaware Vader.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

### Mission to Tatooine

 [![Blinded by her rage, Reva Sevander returned to Tatooine with the aim of finding Luke Skywalker.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/Reva_on_Tatooine_Episode_6.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20220802052313) [![Blinded by her rage, Reva Sevander returned to Tatooine with the aim of finding Luke Skywalker.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/Reva_on_Tatooine_Episode_6.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20220802052313)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/Reva_on_Tatooine_Episode_6.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20220802052313) 

Blinded by her rage, Reva Sevander returned to Tatooine with the aim of finding Luke Skywalker.

 

With Kenobi and the Path occupied with *Devastator* opening fire on them, Reva set off for Tatooine on a mission to hunt for Owen Lars and Luke Skywalker, tracking them to the [Lars Homestead](/wiki/Lars_homestead "Lars homestead"). After he was informed by [Dardin Shull](/wiki/Dardin_Shull "Dardin Shull"), Lars, returning to the homestead, told his wife, [Beru Whitesun Lars](/wiki/Beru_Whitesun_Lars "Beru Whitesun Lars"), about the incoming attack, and prepared to fend off against Reva.[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

Sevander soon entered the homestead, where she was ambushed by Owen and Beru opening fire on her. The former Inquisitor was able to enter the interior of the homestead and disarm the farmer after a brief scuffle, but his wife got the jump on her, buying enough time for the boy to make a run for it. With Reva dead set on killing Luke as revenge, she initiated a pursuit against the young farm boy.[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

Sevander followed Skywalker into [Beggar's Canyon](/wiki/Beggar%27s_Canyon "Beggar's Canyon"), where she was finally able to halt the boy in his tracks by using the Force to dislodge the rock he was standing on. With Skywalker now unconscious, Sevander moved in for the kill, but was haunted by memories of Vader during the siege on the Jedi Temple and herself as a youngling in Skywalker's place. Sevander stopped herself before she was able to deal the killing blow, and instead returned, albeit wearily, to the Lars homestead, with an unconscious Skywalker in her arms.[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

Kenobi, who had returned to Tatooine after an intense [duel against Vader](/wiki/Fight_on_a_barren_moon "Fight on a barren moon"), faced Sevander and reassured her that she did not fail, nor fall to the dark side like her cruel former master, but brought peace and honor to her fallen younglings by showing mercy to Skywalker and was now free to choose her own destiny. Tearfully laying down her lightsaber, Sevander chose her destiny to be free.[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

### Legacy

While the Inquisitorius was extinct by the time of the [Battle of Scarif](/wiki/Battle_of_Scarif "Battle of Scarif")[[19]](#cite_note-TSWB-19) in [1 BBY](/wiki/1_BBY "1 BBY"),[[20]](#cite_note-Timelines-20) the Third Sister's fate remained a mystery, much like that of the Third Brother.[[15]](#cite_note-Encyclopedia-15)

## Personality and traits

 [![The Third Sister was extremely violent, making use of threats and even dismemberments for lack of respect.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/ThirdSisterThreat-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220704152153) [![The Third Sister was extremely violent, making use of threats and even dismemberments for lack of respect.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/ThirdSisterThreat-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20220704152153)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/ThirdSisterThreat-KenobiPartI.png/revision/latest?cb=20220704152153) 

The Third Sister was extremely violent, making use of threats and even dismemberments for lack of respect.

 

Reva Sevander had [brown](/wiki/Color "Color") [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye"), dark skin, and black [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"), which she wore in a braided ponytail with cornrows on the sides.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) As the Third Sister, she was highly ambitious. Thus, her mind was set on tracking down Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi, with her fellow Inquisitors assuming she hoped his capture would gain her Darth Vader's favor and therefore improve her station.[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6) In reality, she sought only to get close to the Sith Lord to kill him for revenge on his actions against her fellow younglings.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Her aspiration put her at odds with the [Grand Inquisitor](/wiki/Grand_Inquisitor "Grand Inquisitor")[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6) and Fifth Brother,[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2) who both found her to be rash and unbalanced, in addition to looking down upon her as the "least of them."[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6) Sevander was aware of Vader's former persona of Anakin Skywalker due to his raid on the Temple. Her desire for revenge blinded her to the hypocrisy of her own actions; she wanted revenge on the Dark Lord for killing the Jedi she saw as her childhood family, only for that quest to lead her down a path where she was willing to kill children and families herself if it meant avenging her own. However, Reva considered her revenge against Vader fair. In order to carry out her revenge, Reva cynically pretended while infiltrating the [Inquisitorius](/wiki/Inquisitorius "Inquisitorius") to be a loyal servant of Vader and to have patriotic tendencies towards the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

Failing in her attempt to hunt down Vader and learning of the existence of the boy [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), Sevander was blinded by her rage and devised a plan to assassinate the innocent child in an attempt to have some kind of jusitice for her fallen family. In the end, despite having gone to kill Luke Skywalker, Sevander could not bring herself to do it, as she realized doing so would have made her just like the one who killed her family, and she dreaded becoming him. She chose mercy and tearfully returned Luke to his uncle and aunt, and was assured by Obi-Wan that she had found her way out of the darkness and could at last be free.[[7]](#cite_note-Part_VI-7)

## Powers and abilities

 [![Although very skilled in her own right, the Third Sister could not match a Sith Lord.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/af/RevaStrikesOut-OWKpV.png/revision/latest?cb=20220720033433) [![Although very skilled in her own right, the Third Sister could not match a Sith Lord.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/af/RevaStrikesOut-OWKpV.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20220720033433)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/a/af/RevaStrikesOut-OWKpV.png/revision/latest?cb=20220720033433) 

Although very skilled in her own right, the Third Sister could not match a Sith Lord.

 

A former [Jedi Initiate](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") and member of [Inquisitorius](/wiki/Inquisitorius "Inquisitorius"), the Third Sister was skilled with her lightsaber; under the tutelage of the Grand Inquisitor, she was skilled in both [Jar'Kai](/wiki/Jar%27Kai "Jar'Kai")[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) and [Form II](/wiki/Form_II "Form II").[[21]](#cite_note-Savage_preview-21) Notable, she was also able to effectively block blasts from a [T-47 airspeeder](/wiki/T-47_airspeeder "T-47 airspeeder") with it.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)[[18]](#cite_note-Part_IV-18) While being considered unpredictable and possessing hidden depths, her dueling abilities were inferior to her superiors[[21]](#cite_note-Savage_preview-21) as demonstrated with her duel against Vader, who was able to effortlessly evade each of her strikes.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5)

Sevander also displayed the ability to use [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), being able to [lift and throw](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") an explosive at an [airspeeder](/wiki/Airspeeder "Airspeeder") that was trying to escape [Fortress Inquisitorius](/wiki/Fortress_Inquisitorius "Fortress Inquisitorius").[[18]](#cite_note-Part_IV-18) She was also able to [extract information from the minds](/wiki/Mind_probe_(Force_power) "Mind probe (Force power)") of individuals and use the Force to collapse an antenna tower while chasing Kenobi.[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6) Despite this, the Third Sister was somewhat defenseless against attacks from other Force-users, as she was easily kicked back by the Grand Inquisitor after he blocked her lightsaber strike with the Force, she was also prevented from sitting on a chair by the Fifth Brother, was non-[fatally](/wiki/Death "Death") [Force choked](/wiki/Force_choke "Force choke") by the Dark Lord, and was Force-pushed back by Kenobi, being easily overwhelmed on all four occasions.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)[[17]](#cite_note-Part_III-17)[[18]](#cite_note-Part_IV-18)

Third Sister was a keen and capable hunter[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4) whose abilities were recognized by Vader, whom later elevated her to the rank of Grand Inquisitor.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) She also possessed considerable athleticism, which she demonstrated by jumping across the rooftops of Daiyu and evading some of the strikes of her former master after betraying him[[6]](#cite_note-Part_II-6)[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) and was proficient with many weapons, including throwing knives.[[4]](#cite_note-Dawn_of_Rebellion-4)

The character of Reva Sevander was originally created by screenwriter [Stuart Beattie](/wiki/Stuart_Beattie "Stuart Beattie") for an Obi-Wan Kenobi-centric movie that ultimately got canceled. Beattie described the character as a "very confused, conflicted, blinded character filled with hate and rage and all the stuff that makes people want to be Sith and Sith Acolytes." The movie eventually evolved into the [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") limited series *[Star Wars: Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Star Wars: Obi-Wan Kenobi")*,[[23]](#cite_note-TheDirect-23) where she was portrayed by [Moses Ingram](/wiki/Moses_Ingram "Moses Ingram").[[22]](#cite_note-Photos-22) Her younger self was portrayed by [Ayaamii Sledge](/wiki/Ayaamii_Sledge "Ayaamii Sledge"),[[5]](#cite_note-Part_V-5) who was credited as a Jedi Youngling in the series.[[2]](#cite_note-Part_I-2)

The idea of a "Third Sister" Inquisitor was first mentioned in "[Darth Maul and Other Followers of the Dark Side](/wiki/Darth_Maul_and_Other_Followers_of_the_Dark_Side "Darth Maul and Other Followers of the Dark Side")," a [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021") issue of [De Agostini](/wiki/De_Agostini "De Agostini")'s [*Star Wars Encyclopedia*](/wiki/Star_Wars_Encyclopedia_(series) "Star Wars Encyclopedia (series)") reference booklets.[[15]](#cite_note-Encyclopedia-15) The character was first pictured and fully revealed as Inquisitor Reva by an article published by *[Entertainment Weekly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Entertainment_Weekly "wikipedia:Entertainment Weekly")* magazine on [March 9](/wiki/March_9 "March 9"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"), providing a first look at the *Obi-Wan Kenobi* series.[[24]](#cite_note-Reveal-24) [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") later confirmed that the new coming Inquisitor character was, in fact, the Third Sister.[[25]](#cite_note-Highlights-25) The character's codename was Riley.[[26]](#cite_note-Riley-26)

### Original version

:   "*Yeah, killed by Vader at the end of it. Yeah, I wanted this story, I wanted her story to end. I wanted Reva to play her part in the Kenobi-Vader story, which was, essentially, at the end, she was the one that allowed Vader, basically told Vader to stop hunting Kenobi. You know, she ended the obsession Vader had with Kenobi. She claimed it was over, it's done. So that was, that was her role to play. And she'd done so many terrible things, I felt she had to die [laughs]. You can only redeem so much.*"
:   ―Stuart Beattie[[23]](#cite_note-TheDirect-23)

 [![The original version of Reva was similar to Trilla.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bd/Trilla-JFO.png/revision/latest?cb=20200222170758) [![The original version of Reva was similar to Trilla.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bd/Trilla-JFO.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/210?cb=20200222170758)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bd/Trilla-JFO.png/revision/latest?cb=20200222170758) 

The original version of Reva was similar to Trilla.

 

Stuart Beattie created Reva because Kenobi needed someone to defeat or save, since he was not going to do that with Vader. He also wanted to create a brand new character that wasn't bound by any pre-existing [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon"). Beattie thought of having Vader leave Reva for dead during the Great Purge of the Jedi Temple, sending her on the path to become an Inquisitor. This allowed the writer to create a confused and blinded character filled with hate and rage. The original, movie version of Reva didn't know that Anakin Skywalker became Darth Vader, although the part where Skywalker killed her fellow younglings and tried to kill her and left her for dead was the same. This version of Reva viewed the Jedi as villains, believing the Empire's lies about them plotting a coup to overtake the Republic, but they were stopped by the clone [troopers](/wiki/Trooper "Trooper"), resulting in Reva hunting her fellow Jedi.[[23]](#cite_note-TheDirect-23)

Reva led a [squad](/wiki/Squad "Squad") of ten Stormtrooper [Marshals](/wiki/Marshal "Marshal") that were the equivalent of the [U.S. Marshals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Marshals_Service "wikipedia:United States Marshals Service").[[27]](#cite_note-Cody_Scrapped_Role-27) Reva didn't find out about Vader's real identity until Kenobi told her. Realizing that she had been wrong the whole time, Reva saved Kenobi by sacrificing her own life, telling Vader that she killed Kenobi. This would result in Vader killing her, like she knew he would, and end his obsession with finding Kenobi. The original plans for the series also didn't include any other Inquisitors.[[23]](#cite_note-TheDirect-23) Although this version of the character shares some similarities with the Inquisitor [Trilla Suduri](/wiki/Second_Sister "Second Sister") from the [2019](/wiki/2019 "2019") [video game](/wiki/Star_Wars_video_games "Star Wars video games") *[Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order](/wiki/Star_Wars_Jedi:_Fallen_Order "Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order")*, Beattie maintains that this was a pure coincidence, as Reva's character was created before the video game was even released.[[23]](#cite_note-TheDirect-23)

### New version

:   "*[Reva] plays the offensive, she always wants to be first and ten steps ahead. She's very passionate about what she does and if she's gonna be the best, she has to make certain concessions.*"
:   ―Moses Ingram[[28]](#cite_note-Kenobi_leaves_Tatooine-28)

 [![An updated version of Reva Sevander was created for the Kenobi series.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/eb/RevaThirdSister-AG.png/revision/latest?cb=20221108062110) [![An updated version of Reva Sevander was created for the Kenobi series.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/eb/RevaThirdSister-AG.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20221108062110)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/eb/RevaThirdSister-AG.png/revision/latest?cb=20221108062110) 

An updated version of Reva Sevander was created for the *Kenobi* series.

 

The story for *Obi-Wan Kenobi* was crafted by director [Deborah Chow](/wiki/Deborah_Chow "Deborah Chow") and writer [Joby Harold](/wiki/Joby_Harold "Joby Harold"), who described Reva as a ruthless and ambitious Jedi hunter, and stated that the character will have a very significant role in the upcoming series while contributing to the legacy of *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* villains "in a really interesting way."[[29]](#cite_note-Cover_story-29) Harold found it was important to define how singular of purpose she is through action as well as monologuing. To do this, Harold had Reva be unpredictable such as when she [cut off](/wiki/Dismemberment "Dismemberment") a [woman's](/wiki/Unidentified_Anchorhead_woman "Unidentified Anchorhead woman") hand. Harold also established Reva as being impulsive and committed to her own goals, so he felt like there was enough room for her character to push further than the other Inquisitors.[[30]](#cite_note-Vader_terrifying-30)

Deborah Chow wanted Reva to be played by a young woman that the audience would believe could take on Darth Vader. Chow liked Ingram's strength, intelligence, and charisma, so she was excited when she and the crew casted her in the part of Reva.[[31]](#cite_note-Return-31) After Ingram spent a week auditioning, Deborah Chow told her that she got the part as Reva in a [Zoom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zoom_Video_Communications "wikipedia:Zoom Video Communications") call. The role required more athleticism than anything Ingram had done before and she spent about four [months](/wiki/Standard_month "Standard month") training before she started acting.[[32]](#cite_note-7_Questions-32) The costume worn by Ingram was one of the first costumes that costume designer [Suttirat Anne Larlarb](/wiki/Suttirat_Anne_Larlarb "Suttirat Anne Larlarb") attended to. Every single piece started out as as sketches from Larlarb, which went to costume illustrator [Darell Fuentes](/wiki/Darell_Fuentes "Darell Fuentes"), and then it received more technical drawings. The workshop made the costume pieces feel like a second skin to Ingram. Ingram's costume was mostly black on black, so it was broken up with some textural differences. [Stacia Lang](/wiki/Stacia_Lang "Stacia Lang"), an assistant costume desginer and Larlarb's helper, oversaw the build of the costume.[[33]](#cite_note-Suttirat_interview-33) The costume made Ingram stand in a certain way, and she felt bold and strong. Ingram felt like no one was looking for her to be something specific,[[32]](#cite_note-7_Questions-32) so she didn't look at other *Star Wars* lore[[34]](#cite_note-Rotten_Tomatoes_TV-34) and she had free reign to be who she thought Reva is.[[32]](#cite_note-7_Questions-32) Ingram said that her character could accomplish her mission "at all costs when given the opportunity." Ingram also expressed that she hoped the look for Reva's hair, which was finalized after a lot of conversations with Chow, would allow Black children with [kinky hair](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afro-textured_hair "wikipedia:Afro-textured hair") to wear their natural hair in [Halloween costumes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halloween_costume "wikipedia:Halloween costume").[[29]](#cite_note-Cover_story-29) Ingram said that "it's all heart" that makes her character stand out from The Grand Inquisitor and Vader.[[24]](#cite_note-Reveal-24)

 [![Moses Ingram training with a lightsaber](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/d4/MosesIngramTraining.png/revision/latest?cb=20221007183947) [![Moses Ingram training with a lightsaber](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/d4/MosesIngramTraining.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20221007183947)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/d4/MosesIngramTraining.png/revision/latest?cb=20221007183947) 

Moses Ingram training with a lightsaber

 

Reva's lightsaber was a completely original design and Ingram received it as a gift from [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") president [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy").[[35]](#cite_note-Reva_Lightsaber-35) Lucasfilm vice president and executive creative director [Doug Chiang](/wiki/Doug_Chiang "Doug Chiang") created many of the original concepts for the lightsaber.[[36]](#cite_note-Weapons_of_Inquis-36) The weapon was customized to fit Ingram's hands.[[34]](#cite_note-Rotten_Tomatoes_TV-34) She spent a lot of time [icing](/wiki/Water "Water") her hands before she learned how to wield her lightsaber.[[37]](#cite_note-Kino_Plus-37) Ingram said that lifting the lightsaber required a lot of forearm strength, but she eventually got the hang of it.[[38]](#cite_note-Ingram_etonline-38) The actor received fight training from [Ewan McGregor](/wiki/Ewan_McGregor "Ewan McGregor"), the actor who played Kenobi in the series. He showed her encouragement as she fumbled through rookie mistakes.[[29]](#cite_note-Cover_story-29) During training, Ingram learned what needed to change with the lightsaber, so it went through several processes. Depending on the scene that was being shot, Ingram used three different lightsabers: a full length prop, a medium prop, and a very small prop. Ingram also had a practice lightsaber that she was sent home with.[[34]](#cite_note-Rotten_Tomatoes_TV-34) The first scene that was shot with Ingram was her and the other Inquisitors stepping off of their ship on Tatooine.[[32]](#cite_note-7_Questions-32) In the beginning of filming, Ingram felt like she could do her job without going through "a period of demystifying" a deep love for the franchise, which made playing the character easier for her.[[39]](#cite_note-Ingram_Yahoo-39)
