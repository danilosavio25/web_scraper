## Add images

Some articles would benefit greatly with the addition of one or more images. You can find articles that have been requested to be enhanced with an image [here](/wiki/Category:Articles_needing_illustration "Category:Articles needing illustration"). To add an image to an article you must first [upload](/wiki/Special:Upload "Special:Upload") the image, also including the image's source and licensing. [Here](/wiki/Special:ListFiles "Special:ListFiles") is the list of uploaded files and [here](/wiki/Special:NewFiles "Special:NewFiles") the gallery of the most recent files. [Here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Picture_tutorial "wikipedia:Wikipedia:Picture tutorial") you can find a picture tutorial that tells you how to insert and modify an image inside an article. If the article has an image request tag, don't forget to remove it once you add the image.

## Merge articles that need merging

Sometimes, two users get the same idea for an article, but put them on pages with different names. When we find them, we tag them to be merged, in hopes someone will combine the two into a single, cohesive article. You can find articles in need of merging [here](/wiki/Category:Articles_to_be_merged "Category:Articles to be merged"). Don't forget to remove the merge tags when you're done!

## Source an unsourced article

Many of our articles were created without proper source citation. Since anyone can create an article on Wookieepedia, sources are important to distinguish between [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and [fanon](/wiki/Fanon "Fanon"). You can find articles in need of sources [here](/wiki/Category:Verify "Category:Verify"), and articles that need proper citation [here](/wiki/Category:Pages_needing_citation "Category:Pages needing citation").

## Create redlinks in articles

Many articles reference topics that haven't been addressed in Wookieepedia yet, creating redlinks. Some reference *many* such topics; these articles are less useful to our users, because much of the related information they might want to seek is unavailable. It is helpful to create new articles to fill in the gaps of these articles with an excess of redlinks; you can find such articles [here](/wiki/Category:Articles_with_an_excess_of_redlinks "Category:Articles with an excess of redlinks"). Don't forget to remove the redlink tag from the original article once the majority of the links have been filled in.

## Create a wanted page

Some topics are referenced by a great many pages, but haven't had an article created about them yet. You can find a list of these heavily-referenced topics, sorted by how many references they have, [here](/wiki/Special:WantedPages "Special:WantedPages").

## Correct spelling

**Note: This feature is currently unavailable.**
Not everyone spells correctly. Bad spelling reflects poorly on the professionalism of Wookieepedia. You can find a list of commonly misspelled words [here](/wiki/Wookieepedia:List_of_common_misspellings "Wookieepedia:List of common misspellings").

Another resource to help users interested in correcting common spelling errors can be found [here](/wiki/Wookieepedia:The_Great_Typo_Hunt "Wookieepedia:The Great Typo Hunt"), where users post frequently seen typos to be fixed.

## Fix double redirects

Sometimes, an article redirects to another article, which in turn redirects to yet another article. When this happens, instead of smoothly being redirected, a user must instead manually click through to the third page. In order to prevent this, we try to eliminate double redirects, pointing all redirects at their final destination. You can find a list of double redirects [here](/wiki/Special:DoubleRedirects "Special:DoubleRedirects").

## Categorize a page

Categories assist in easy navigation by grouping similar topics together. You can find a list of articles in need of categorization [here](/wiki/Special:UncategorizedPages "Special:UncategorizedPages").

## Expand a stub

Some articles are merely placeholders, waiting for an intrepid [Wookieepedian](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") like yourself to come along and expand upon them. You can find a list of these "stubs" [here](/wiki/Category:Stubs "Category:Stubs"). Note that short pages that are already complete should not be listed as stubs. Also, see [Category:Articles to be expanded](/wiki/Category:Articles_to_be_expanded "Category:Articles to be expanded").
