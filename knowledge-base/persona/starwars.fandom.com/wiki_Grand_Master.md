**Grand Master** or **Grandmaster**, also known as **Grand Jedi Master**, was a title generally bestowed on the oldest and wisest [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi"). Whereas the [Master of the Order](/wiki/Master_of_the_Order "Master of the Order") served as leader of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council"), the Grand Master was the head of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). The title was shared by [Jedi Masters](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Xo Lahru](/wiki/Xo_Lahru "Xo Lahru"), [Pra-Tre Veter](/wiki/Pra-Tre_Veter "Pra-Tre Veter"), and [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"). After Veter's [capture](/wiki/Mission_to_the_edge_of_the_Stormwall "Mission to the edge of the Stormwall") by the [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [pirates](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate"), [Ry Ki-Sakka](/wiki/Ry_Ki-Sakka "Ry Ki-Sakka") was appointed in his place, before Veter was ultimately [executed](/wiki/Pra-Tre_Veter%27s_execution "Pra-Tre Veter's execution") by the Nihil. Yoda continued to serve as the Grand Master in the [final years](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"), and was one of the few survivors of the [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") that [commenced](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") near the end of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars").

## Roles and powers[]

 [![The training of Jedi younglings was a responsibility that Yoda carried as Grand Master of the Jedi Order.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f4/Yoda_teaching.png/revision/latest?cb=20121231133247) [![The training of Jedi younglings was a responsibility that Yoda carried as Grand Master of the Jedi Order.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f4/Yoda_teaching.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20121231133247)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f4/Yoda_teaching.png/revision/latest?cb=20121231133247) 

The training of Jedi younglings was a responsibility that Yoda carried as Grand Master of the Jedi Order.

 

The Grand Master oversaw the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order").[[3]](#cite_note-VisDic-3) The title of Grand Master was generally reserved for the oldest and wisest living [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master"). It was distinct from the office of [Master of the Order](/wiki/Master_of_the_Order "Master of the Order"), the holder of which led the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council").[[6]](#cite_note-Rise_of_the_Separatists-6) However, it was possible for a single individual to hold both titles at the same [time](/wiki/Time "Time").[[7]](#cite_note-AbEverything-7) The Grand Master would humbly teach the most [junior members](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") of the Order, [younglings](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling"), completing the circle of [Jedi training](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training").[[8]](#cite_note-VE-8) According to [Tera Sinube](/wiki/Tera_Sinube "Tera Sinube"), it was rare for a Grand Master to train his own [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") and was therefore regarded as an honor.[[4]](#cite_note-Dooku:_Jedi_Lost-4) Sometimes, the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") Order had multiple Grand Masters simultaneously.[[9]](#cite_note-THR_1-9)

## History[]

:   "*For 800 years have I trained Jedi. My own counsel will I keep on who is to be trained.*"
:   ―Yoda[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_V-10)

During the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"), at the time of the [Great Hyperspace Disaster](/wiki/Great_Hyperspace_Disaster "Great Hyperspace Disaster"),[[9]](#cite_note-THR_1-9) several Jedi shared the rank of Grand Master, including Masters [Xo Lahru](/wiki/Xo_Lahru "Xo Lahru"),[[11]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-11) [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"),[[9]](#cite_note-THR_1-9) and [Pra-Tre Veter](/wiki/Pra-Tre_Veter "Pra-Tre Veter"),[[12]](#cite_note-BTS_1-12) who attended the [dedication of the Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Dedication_of_the_Starlight_Beacon "Dedication of the Starlight Beacon").[[9]](#cite_note-THR_1-9) Jedi Master [Ry Ki-Sakka](/wiki/Ry_Ki-Sakka "Ry Ki-Sakka") also attained the title after the death of Veter.[[13]](#cite_note-Eye_of_Darkness-13)

By the time of the [Invasion of Naboo](/wiki/Invasion_of_Naboo "Invasion of Naboo"),[[14]](#cite_note-TPM-14) two hundred years after the Great Disaster,[[15]](#cite_note-Date-15) the still-living Yoda was the sole holder of the rank of Grand Master.[[7]](#cite_note-AbEverything-7) Yoda had trained generations of Jedi for eight [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century"),[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_V-10) and continued as Grand Master through the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars").[[7]](#cite_note-AbEverything-7)

 [![During the Clone Wars, Grand Master Yoda commanded the Grand Army of the Republic as a Jedi General.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3d/Yoda_and_his_squad.png/revision/latest?cb=20230211001650) [![During the Clone Wars, Grand Master Yoda commanded the Grand Army of the Republic as a Jedi General.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3d/Yoda_and_his_squad.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20230211001650)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3d/Yoda_and_his_squad.png/revision/latest?cb=20230211001650) 

During the Clone Wars, Grand Master Yoda commanded the Grand Army of the Republic as a Jedi General.

 

During the conflict in which Yoda and other members of the Order led the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") as [Jedi Generals](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General"), [Ki-Adi-Mundi](/wiki/Ki-Adi-Mundi "Ki-Adi-Mundi") became concerned about Yoda's state of mind, given the Grand Master's role in training [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") prior to Dooku's descent to the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force"). However, [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") countered that every Jedi in their time had been apprenticed to the Grand Master, and therefore dismissed the notion that Yoda was responsible for Dooku's actions as a [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord").[[5]](#cite_note-Voices-5) After the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") managed to [kill](/wiki/Death "Death") the majority of Jedi throughout [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"), the Grand Master went into exile on the planet [Dagobah](/wiki/Dagobah "Dagobah").[[16]](#cite_note-ROTS-16)
