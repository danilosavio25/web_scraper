## Re-using Wookieepedia content

Original text content on the wiki is released under the CC BY-SA license—the general public is free to share and adapt the material so long as appropriate credit is given and the re-used material is also released under the same license. You should **attribute** Wookieepedia, or list relevant Wookieepedian authors according to page history logs; **license** your work under the same license or a later version; and **indicate** if the content has been modified. Relevant hyperlinks or URLs should be provided.

Attribution example for content that uses material from Wookieepedia's [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano") article:

:   *This article uses material from the “[Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano")” article on Wookieepedia and is licensed under the [Creative Commons Attribution-Share Alike License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0). The original work has been modified.*

## Copyright policy for Wookieepedia contributors

### General expectations

Wookieepedia contributors are expected to submit their own work. The wiki's software logs, such as the [page history](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Help:Page_history "Help:Page history"), indicates authorship.

1. Never post, upload, or link to material that infringes intellectual property rights, as stipulated by Fandom's Terms of Use. Quotations from copyrighted works may be included in articles in accordance with the fair use principle.
2. Never use [generative artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence "wikipedia:Generative artificial intelligence") tools for your contributions.
3. Never plagiarise content from one page to another.
   * When you merge or split an article, or otherwise move text from one page to another within Wookieepedia, you should indicate the source page in your edit summaries at the destination page with a corresponding link (e.g. "merged from [85th](/wiki/85th "85th")"). It is good practice to do likewise at the source page as well (e.g. "merged to [85th Fighter Wing](/wiki/85th_Fighter_Wing "85th Fighter Wing")")
   * To avoid confusion, it is good practice to do the above even when you are re-using text created by yourself.
4. Abide by [Wookieepedia's file policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Files "Wookieepedia:Files") when uploading a file, at minimum accurately specifying on the file page the relevant source, authors or copyright holders, and licensing information via the {{[Information](/wiki/Template:Information "Template:Information")}} template.
