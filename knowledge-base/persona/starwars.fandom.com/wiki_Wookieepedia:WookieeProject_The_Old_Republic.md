The driving goal behind **WookieeProject: *The Old Republic*** is the improvement and maintainance of [Wookieepedia](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") articles related to the [LucasArts](/wiki/LucasArts "LucasArts") [MMORPG](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massively_multiplayer_online_role-playing_game "wikipedia:Massively multiplayer online role-playing game") *[Star Wars: The Old Republic](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Old_Republic "Star Wars: The Old Republic")*. The announcement of the game inspired a multi-platform, multimedia project that comprises [novels](/wiki/List_of_books "List of books"), [comic books](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books"), and other media. This project includes all of these various forms of media within its scope. Specific works within *The Old Republic* timeframe can be found in the [novel series](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Old_Republic_(novel_series) "Star Wars: The Old Republic (novel series)") and [comic series](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Old_Republic_(comics) "Star Wars: The Old Republic (comics)"). The project also covers topics related to the material released via the official site's [HoloNet feature](http://swtor.com/info/holonet).

Due to the nature of the game itself, there is little certainty as to the canonical conclusion to many stories presented within this timeframe. As such, there is plenty of room for speculation. Please note that such speculation has *no place* on Wookieepedia, and a major goal of this project is to avoid conjecture and assumption and eliminate it where we can.

If you would like to contribute to Wookieepedia as a member of this project, please take a look at our mission and other articles of quality, such as those that have attained [Featured](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_The_Old_Republic/Featured_Articles "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject The Old Republic/Featured Articles"), [Good](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_The_Old_Republic/Good_Articles "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject The Old Republic/Good Articles"), or [Comprehensive](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_The_Old_Republic/Comprehensive_Articles "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject The Old Republic/Comprehensive Articles") status. If you wish to join, please add your name at the bottom of this page under the "Participants" section.

## Editing guidelines[]

The following are simple guidelines to follow while writing or editing articles. Please note that *The Old Republic* is a role-playing game, and therefore has several story options. Player-created characters and stories are not allowed on Wookieepedia.

### General editing[]

1. Adhere to all [site policies](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Policies "Wookieepedia:Policies"), such as the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style") and [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide").
2. [Properly source](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing") all information in an article with TOR-specific citation templates such as [TORcite](/wiki/Template:TORcite "Template:TORcite"), [TOR dates](/wiki/Template:TOR_dates "Template:TOR dates"), [TOR updates](/wiki/Template:TOR_updates "Template:TOR updates"), [TORweb](/wiki/Template:TORweb "Template:TORweb"), and [TORYoutube](/wiki/Template:TORYoutube "Template:TORYoutube").
   * To cite Tweets from the [official SWTOR Twitter account](https://twitter.com/SWTOR), use [Template:Twitter](/wiki/Template:Twitter "Template:Twitter"). Similarly, use {{[Facebook](/wiki/Template:Facebook "Template:Facebook")}} and {{[Instagram](/wiki/Template:Instagram "Template:Instagram")}} for the [SWTOR Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/starwarstheoldrepublic) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/swtorofficial) accounts, respectively.
3. Wookieepedia also assumes 100% game completion (see [Template:Gamemechanics](/wiki/Template:Gamemechanics "Template:Gamemechanics"), [Template:GameLSmechanics](/wiki/Template:GameLSmechanics "Template:GameLSmechanics"), and [Template:GameDSmechanics](/wiki/Template:GameDSmechanics "Template:GameDSmechanics"))
4. Always include a thorough and well-written "Behind the scenes" that notes the subject's origin and relation to the series.
5. Please note that all sources released via the official site's [HoloNet feature](http://swtor.com/info/holonet) are to be listed under the "Sources" section of an article. **This does not include the [Galactic Timeline](/wiki/Galactic_Timeline "Galactic Timeline"), which should be under "Appearances."**

### Important note on player choices[]

#### Base game[]

Per [this consensus](/wiki/Forum:CT:TOR_and_video_game_canon "Forum:CT:TOR and video game canon"), it is Wookieepedia policy that all articles be written under the assumption that Republic player characters ([Jedi Counsular](/wiki/The_Barsen%27thor "The Barsen'thor"), [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Hero_of_Tython "Hero of Tython"), [trooper](/wiki/Meteor_(Havoc_Squad) "Meteor (Havoc Squad)"), [smuggler](/wiki/Voidhound "Voidhound")) selected light-side dialogue options in storyline conversations. Conversely, it has been agreed that Imperial player characters ([Sith Inquisitor](/wiki/Darth_Nox "Darth Nox"), [Sith Warrior](/wiki/Empire%27s_Wrath "Empire's Wrath"), [Imperial agent](/wiki/Cipher_Nine "Cipher Nine"), [bounty hunter](/wiki/Hunter_(individual) "Hunter (individual)")) chose dark-side conversation options. Alternative choices may be detailed in the "Behind the scenes" section.

Wookieepedia does not assert that these are the canonical story resolutions, and as such, editors are required to place the following tags on articles wherein such assumptions are found:

* For Republic player characters: {{[RepublicHeader](/wiki/Template:RepublicHeader "Template:RepublicHeader")}} will remain at the top of the article and {{[GameLS](/wiki/Template:GameLS "Template:GameLS")}} and {{[GameLSmechanics](/wiki/Template:GameLSmechanics "Template:GameLSmechanics")}} will be used on articles related to their actions
* For Empire player characters: {{[EmpireHeader](/wiki/Template:EmpireHeader "Template:EmpireHeader")}} will remain at the top of the article and {{[GameDS](/wiki/Template:GameDS "Template:GameDS")}} and {{[GameDSmechanics](/wiki/Template:GameDSmechanics "Template:GameDSmechanics")}} will be used on articles related to their actions

#### Expansions: the Alliance Commander[]

Similarly, per [community consensus](/wiki/Forum:CT:The_Old_Republic:_Commander%27s_storyline_affiliation "Forum:CT:The Old Republic: Commander's storyline affiliation"), it is policy that all articles assume that the [Alliance Commander](/wiki/Alliance_Commander "Alliance Commander")—the player character from Update 2.7 Forged Alliances onwards—was affiliated with the Republic and made light side decisions. In addition, contents in the Empire storyline that directly contradict the Republic storyline's contents are considered alternative gameplay choices, which may be written in the "Behind the scenes" section. Saboteur options shall also be considered alternative gameplay choices and may be recorded in the Bts section.
