Some of these wikis are hosted on the Fandom platform (Fandom.com) like Wookieepedia, and others are externally hosted. Wookieepedia's choice of interwiki partners is subject to [internal community consensus](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus"), and those accessible via {{Interlang}} are indicated in **bold** below.

Pursuant to the [Files](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Files#Shared_image "Wookieepedia:Files") policy, *Star Wars* wikis that are accessible via Interlang and hosted on Fandom may request Fandom staff to enable the [shared images](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Help:Shared_images "Help:Shared images") tool, so long as their image licensing templates specify the origin of the corresponding image on the English wiki.

## Policy and consensus updates

Wookieepedia has partnered with *Star Wars* wikis in other languages that are hosted on Fandom (formerly Wikia) by default. Changes to interwiki partnership, such as switching to an externally hosted site, are logged here:

* [Forum:CT:New Polish sister site](/wiki/Forum:CT:New_Polish_sister_site "Forum:CT:New Polish sister site") (April 16, 2007) – Vote to change Polish interlang links from the Wikia-hosted wiki to Biblioteka Ossus **passed**

  :   → See also: [Forum:SH Archive/There is yet another: in Polish](/wiki/Forum:SH_Archive/There_is_yet_another:_in_Polish "Forum:SH Archive/There is yet another: in Polish") and [Forum:Ossus.pl - New Polish sister site](/wiki/Forum:Ossus.pl_-_New_Polish_sister_site "Forum:Ossus.pl - New Polish sister site")
* [Forum:CT:French Interwiki Links](/wiki/Forum:CT:French_Interwiki_Links "Forum:CT:French Interwiki Links") (January 16, 2011) – Vote to change French interlang links from the Wikia-hosted wiki to Wookiepédia **passed**

  :   → See also: [Forum:CT:French interwiki links and what to do with them](/wiki/Forum:CT:French_interwiki_links_and_what_to_do_with_them "Forum:CT:French interwiki links and what to do with them")
* [Forum:CT:Finnish interwiki-links](/wiki/Forum:CT:Finnish_interwiki-links "Forum:CT:Finnish interwiki-links") (February 18, 2011) – Vote to change Finnish interlang links from the Wikia-hosted wiki to Jedipedia (Finnish) **failed** with a majority opposed
* [Forum:CT:Foreign language link issues](/wiki/Forum:CT:Foreign_language_link_issues "Forum:CT:Foreign language link issues") (July 15, 2011) – After the externally-hosted [Jedipedia](/wiki/Jedipedia "Jedipedia") (German) migrated to Wikia, the Jedipedia community created a fork at a new site, Jedipedia.net, and Wookieepedia's vote to change German interlang links from the Wikia-hosted wiki to Jedipedia.net **passed**. Another vote to implement independent French and German interlang links via the new {{[Interlang](/wiki/Template:Interlang "Template:Interlang")}} template also **passed**

  :   → See also: [Forum:CT:Links to German version](/wiki/Forum:CT:Links_to_German_version "Forum:CT:Links to German version")
* [Forum:CT:Changing Finnish Jedipedia interwiki links](/wiki/Forum:CT:Changing_Finnish_Jedipedia_interwiki_links "Forum:CT:Changing Finnish Jedipedia interwiki links") (July 20, 2015) – Vote to change Finnish interlang links from the Wikia-hosted wiki to Jedipedia (Finnish) **failed** with a majority opposed
* [Forum:CT:French Interwiki Links, Revisited](/wiki/Forum:CT:French_Interwiki_Links,_Revisited "Forum:CT:French Interwiki Links, Revisited") (August 20, 2016) – Vote to change French interlang links from Wookiepédia back to the Wikia-hosted wiki **passed**
* [Forum:CT:Change Finnish interlanguage links to jedipedia.fi](/wiki/Forum:CT:Change_Finnish_interlanguage_links_to_jedipedia.fi "Forum:CT:Change Finnish interlanguage links to jedipedia.fi") (August 16, 2021) – Vote to change Finnish interlang links from the Fandom-hosted wiki to Jedipedia.fi **passed**
* January 8, 2023 – Wookieepedia's [administration](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators") removed Russian interlang links
* [Forum:CT:File sharing to Turkish wiki Sithpedi](/wiki/Forum:CT:File_sharing_to_Turkish_wiki_Sithpedi "Forum:CT:File sharing to Turkish wiki Sithpedi") (July 25, 2023) – Vote to enable the shared images tool with the Turkish-language wiki **passed**
* [Forum:CT:Restore links to the Russian-language wiki](/wiki/Forum:CT:Restore_links_to_the_Russian-language_wiki "Forum:CT:Restore links to the Russian-language wiki") (May 10, 2024) – Vote to restore Russian interlang links **failed** with a majority opposed
* [Forum:CT:Change Turkish interlanguage links to starwarsturkiye.com/wiki](/wiki/Forum:CT:Change_Turkish_interlanguage_links_to_starwarsturkiye.com/wiki "Forum:CT:Change Turkish interlanguage links to starwarsturkiye.com/wiki") (December 4, 2024) – Vote to change Turkish interlang links from the Fandom-hosted wiki to Sithpedi **passed**
