## Behind the scenes[]

The *Star Hopper* was first mentioned, indirectly, in the [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021") novel *[The High Republic: Light of the Jedi](/wiki/The_High_Republic:_Light_of_the_Jedi "The High Republic: Light of the Jedi")*,[[7]](#cite_note-LotJ-7) and first appeared in the [first issue](/wiki/The_High_Republic_Adventures_(2021)_1 "The High Republic Adventures (2021) 1") of [*Star Wars: The High Republic Adventures*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic_Adventures_(2021) "Star Wars: The High Republic Adventures (2021)").[[1]](#cite_note-HRA_1-1) In the original script of [*The High Republic* (2021) 1](/wiki/The_High_Republic_(2021)_1 "The High Republic (2021) 1"), Yoda referred to the *Star Hopper* as the *Crimson Shred*.[[8]](#cite_note-BTS_1-8)
