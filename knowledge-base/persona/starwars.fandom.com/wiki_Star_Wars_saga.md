The **Star Wars saga**, also referred to as the **Skywalker saga**, is the main line of *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* films that began in [1977](/wiki/1977 "1977") by [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas"), with [*Star Wars*: Episode IV *A New Hope*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_IV_A_New_Hope "Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope") being the first film. While a larger universe of stories and characters has evolved around it, the saga films tell the story of the [Skywalker family](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family") through a set of three trilogies. The [original trilogy](/wiki/Original_trilogy "Original trilogy") ran from 1977 to [1983](/wiki/1983 "1983"), which was followed by the [prequel trilogy](/wiki/Prequel_trilogy "Prequel trilogy") from [1999](/wiki/1999 "1999") to [2005](/wiki/2005 "2005") and the [sequel trilogy](/wiki/Sequel_trilogy "Sequel trilogy") from [2015](/wiki/2015 "2015") to [2019](/wiki/2019 "2019").

## Plot summary[]

Chronologically, the story spans 67 years. It begins with [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"), the [Chosen One](/wiki/Chosen_One "Chosen One") of [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") prophecy, as the [prequel trilogy](/wiki/Prequel_trilogy "Prequel trilogy") charts his rise as a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") through his fall to the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") and becoming Darth Vader. The [original trilogy](/wiki/Original_trilogy "Original trilogy") tells the story of Anakin's children, [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") and [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo"), as they lead the [fight](/wiki/Galactic_Civil_War "Galactic Civil War") against the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), and Luke saves Anakin from the dark side and becomes a Jedi in his own right. The [sequel trilogy](/wiki/Sequel_trilogy "Sequel trilogy") focuses on [Rey](/wiki/Rey_Skywalker "Rey Skywalker"), a [Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") prodigy, who is swept up into a [new galactic conflict](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War") and faces off against Anakin's fallen grandson, [Ben Solo](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), now known as Kylo Ren, while becoming the first of a new generation of Jedi.

## Development[]

The series was created by [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas"), who, as the writer and director of *A New Hope* and the entire prequel trilogy, has written and directed more films in the Skywalker saga than any other person. Other saga film directors include [Irvin Kershner](/wiki/Irvin_Kershner "Irvin Kershner"), [Richard Marquand](/wiki/Richard_Marquand "Richard Marquand"), [J.J. Abrams](/wiki/J.J._Abrams "J.J. Abrams"), and [Rian Johnson](/wiki/Rian_Johnson "Rian Johnson"). [Leigh Brackett](/wiki/Leigh_Brackett "Leigh Brackett"), [Lawrence Kasdan](/wiki/Lawrence_Kasdan "Lawrence Kasdan"), [Michael Arndt](/wiki/Michael_Arndt "Michael Arndt"), Abrams, Johnson, and [Chris Terrio](/wiki/Chris_Terrio "Chris Terrio") have all been writers on the films. [Gary Kurtz](/wiki/Gary_Kurtz "Gary Kurtz") and [Howard G. Kazanjian](/wiki/Howard_G._Kazanjian "Howard G. Kazanjian") produced the original trilogy, [Rick McCallum](/wiki/Rick_McCallum "Rick McCallum") produced the prequel trilogy, and [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy") produced the sequel trilogy.

## Characteristics ‡[]

### Episode titles[]

The nine released films of the [Skywalker](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family") saga begin their designation with "Episode" followed by their chronological order displayed as a Roman numeral. *Star Wars*: Episode IV *A New Hope* was originally released simply as *Star Wars*, as it was not known whether it would be followed by a sequel. Beginning with its [April 10, 1981 re-release](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_IV_A_New_Hope#Re-releases "Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope"), it was renamed to its current title.

### Opening crawls[]

The [opening crawl](/wiki/Opening_crawl "Opening crawl") of each film in the Skywalker saga follows the same format. The introduction and crawl begins with the blue text "A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...," which is then immediately followed by the *Star Wars* logo and the slanted, yellow-text-on-starfield summaries of immediately preceding off-screen action. While the typefaces vary slightly, these all follow the same overall general format. These crawls were inspired by the Flash Gordon serials from George Lucas' childhood.

The prequel trilogy

 

The *Star Wars* saga follows the Skywalker family through all nine films:

**The original trilogy** (out-of-universe: [1977](/wiki/1977 "1977")–[1983](/wiki/1983 "1983"); [in-universe](/wiki/In-universe "In-universe"): [1 BBY](/wiki/1_BBY "1 BBY")–[4 ABY](/wiki/4_ABY "4 ABY")) focuses on protagonist [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), his unbeknownst-to-him sister [Princess Leia](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo"), the [smuggler](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler") [Han Solo](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo"), and the redemption of [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker").

**The prequel trilogy** (out-of-universe: [1999](/wiki/1999 "1999")–[2005](/wiki/2005 "2005"); in-universe:[32 BBY](/wiki/32_BBY "32 BBY")–[19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY")) focuses on protagonist Anakin Skywalker as he matures for a 9-year-old [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") [slave](/wiki/Slavery "Slavery") child to a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight"). The prequel trilogy also focuses on Skywalker's eventual wife, [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala"), and his mentor, [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi"), and sets the groundwork and basis of the plot in the original trilogy.

**The sequel trilogy** (out-of-universe: [2015](/wiki/2015 "2015")–[2019](/wiki/2019 "2019"); in-universe: [34 ABY](/wiki/34_ABY "34 ABY")—[35 ABY](/wiki/35_ABY "35 ABY")) focuses on the [scavenger](/wiki/Scavenger "Scavenger") [Rey](/wiki/Rey_Skywalker "Rey Skywalker") as she discovers and trains with [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), the ex-[stormtrooper](/wiki/First_Order_stormtrooper "First Order stormtrooper") [Finn](/wiki/Finn "Finn"), the [ace](/wiki/Ace "Ace") [pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") [Poe Dameron](/wiki/Poe_Dameron "Poe Dameron"), and the antagonist [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), who is the son of Leia Organa and Han Solo.
