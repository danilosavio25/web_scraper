## Plot summary[]

:   "*You called. The* Jedi *have* answered*.*"
:   ―Mace Windu to Azita Cruuz[[3]](#cite_note-Windu-3)

On the ocean moon of [Devshi](/wiki/Devshi "Devshi"), [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") emerges from the water after infiltrating the prison complex on the ocean floor, and swims over to nearby docks. Spotting a [Lutrillian](/wiki/Lutrillian "Lutrillian") worker, Windu questions if he’s seen a group of [droids](/wiki/Droid "Droid") passing through, and the man tells him that five of them stepped out of the prison elevator and fled on a [skiff](/wiki/Skiff "Skiff"). Windu introduces himself and informs the man that he is in need of a vessel and captain. The Lutrillian explains that he only runs the docking remotes, so Windu uses a [Jedi Mind Trick](/wiki/Mind_trick "Mind trick") to gain his aid. The Lutrillian, named [Regump](/wiki/Regump "Regump"), and Windu take off in pursuit of the droids on a skiff of their own, and the latter uses [The Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") to push the boat faster through the water in order to gain on their fleeing target. Windu is able to get close enough to hurl his [lightsaber](/wiki/Mace_Windu%27s_lightsaber "Mace Windu's lightsaber") at the skiff and take out their engines, leaving them dead in the water. After the two board, Mace’s plan comes to fruition, with the steam short-circuiting all the droids. Investigating the downed droids, Mace realizes that they all worked at the prison, but their programming was overridden, and one of them was carrying a system-wide [Skeleton key](/wiki/Skeleton_key "Skeleton key").

With his mission complete, Windu returns to the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") and meets with [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"). Inside the briefing room, Yoda asks Windu if he has heard of [coaxium](/wiki/Coaxium "Coaxium"), Windu is familiar with the incredible unstable hyper-fuel. Another [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") named [Gara](/wiki/Gara "Gara") presents the planet [Ro Mira](/wiki/Ro_Mira "Ro Mira"), a moon in the [Vergara system](/wiki/Vergara_system "Vergara system"), a deadly ice planet. Beneath the surface, coaxium refinery uses blue-hot [lava](/wiki/Magma "Magma") to create a new coaxium formula. Due to this new coaxium formula boosting [sublight engines](/wiki/Sublight_engine "Sublight engine"), ships now have the ability to run blockades and outrun Republic patrols if it were to fall into the wrong hands. Windu is informed that someone called [Azita Cruuz](/wiki/Azita_Cruuz "Azita Cruuz") has stolen the formula, and is offering a large amount in exchange for a large sum of [credits](/wiki/Galactic_Credit_Standard "Galactic Credit Standard") and safe passage off world. Windu is instructed to track down Cruuz and her cargo before anyone else takes it from her.

Windu arrives at Ro Mira, landing his [Jedi Starfighter](/wiki/Delta-7_Aethersprite-class_light_interceptor "Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptor") outside the city. Windu surmises that he should go to the origin of Cruuz's request and searches the [security cameras](/wiki/Security_camera "Security camera") for where she went next. Passing through an alleyway, Mace is approached by a shady group seeking for a way off of the planet. The group begins circling the Jedi, Windu has a [vision](/wiki/Force_vision "Force vision") of the future, showing him defeating them all in hand-to-hand combat. Informing the attackers, they decide to back off and leave the Jedi alone.

Windu concludes that Cruuz would hide somewhere with tight security, but also a constant stream of people coming in and out. Entering the local hospital, he asks for a patient who doesn't know their name, but previously worked at the local refinery. Claiming to be a representative of the refinery, he begins closing in on Cruuz, however, several refinery authorities are already in pursuit of her. Cruuz escapes the hospital and begins running across rooftops, until she slips and falls to the ground. Refinery authorities are in hot pursuit aboard a [speeder](/wiki/Speeder "Speeder"), eventually pinning her against a wall and holding her at gunpoint. However, Windu arrives to save Cruuz, lightsaber in hand, and offers the authorities to drop their weapons and run. The authorities don't listen and open fire, Windu defends them and defeats the authorities. As saviour of Cruuz, Windu gives her a hand, informing her that the Jedi have answered her call.
