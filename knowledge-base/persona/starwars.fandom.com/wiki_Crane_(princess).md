## Behind the scenes[]

Crane appeared in "[The Song of Four Wings](/wiki/The_Song_of_Four_Wings "The Song of Four Wings")", a [non-canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") short film directed by [Hiroyasu Kobayashi](/wiki/Hiroyasu_Kobayashi "Hiroyasu Kobayashi") that released as part of the [third volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_3 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 3")  of the *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* series. The animated short, produced by [Project Studio Q](/wiki/Project_Studio_Q "Project Studio Q"), released on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") on [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"). The character was first introduced at the *Star Wars: Visions* panel at [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)") on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), 2025.[[2]](#cite_note-Day_3-2) She is voiced by [Manaka Iwami](/wiki/Manaka_Iwami "Manaka Iwami") in the original [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_language "wikipedia:Japanese language") version and by [Stephanie Hsu](/wiki/Stephanie_Hsu "Stephanie Hsu") in the English dub.[[3]](#cite_note-Getty-3)
