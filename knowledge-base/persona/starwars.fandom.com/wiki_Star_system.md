A **star system**, also known as a **solar system**,[[2]](#cite_note-AOTC-2) **planetary system**,[[3]](#cite_note-Disapp_II-3) or simply a **system**, was a group of celestial bodies, usually [planets](/wiki/Planet "Planet"), [orbiting](/wiki/Orbit "Orbit") a [star](/wiki/Star "Star"). Most stars in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") had their own planetary system.[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1) [Navigational routes](/wiki/Hyperspace_route "Hyperspace route") known as runs connected chains of star systems, and these routes through [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace") were vital for trade and transit between systems, with some of them crossing [sector](/wiki/Sector "Sector") boundaries, which conceptually grouped star systems across swathes of [space](/wiki/Realspace "Realspace") into named regions.[[4]](#cite_note-VE-4) A single system could contain dozens of planets, even more moons, and any number of celestial bodies, while a single sector could contain multiple star systems.[[5]](#cite_note-Star_Wars_Book-5)

During the [crisis](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis") that led to the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), several hundred systems that belonged to the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") seceded and formed the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") under the leadership of [Count](/wiki/Count_of_Serenno "Count of Serenno") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") of [Serenno](/wiki/Serenno "Serenno"), a former [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master").[[6]](#cite_note-DB_Seppies-6)
