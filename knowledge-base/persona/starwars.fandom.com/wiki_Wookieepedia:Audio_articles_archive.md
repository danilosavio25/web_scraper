## Requests

Have a [status article](/wiki/Category:Wookieepedia_status_articles "Category:Wookieepedia status articles") you'd like to see appear on The Showcase? Leave a request [here](https://starwars.fandom.com/f/p/4400000000003826705). The ideal candidates:

* Are not actively expected to appear in new media soon, such as an upcoming live-action or animated show, which would necessitate them being re-recorded.
* Have all available audio files for corresponding quotes added to the page.
* Are not over 1500 words, due to current recording limitations.
* Have an infobox image.

* At the moment, we also avoid much non-canon content due to the difficulties with making clear via audio that a subject is non-canon, in addition to the added risk of a subject becoming canon and thus being heavily restructured to account for that.
  + While *Visions* content is exempt from this risk due to low risk of formal canonization, it is still considered unstable until after currently-announced upcoming *Visions* content is done releasing.
* Though not a firm requirement, articles with {{[Gamemechanics](/wiki/Template:Gamemechanics "Template:Gamemechanics")}} tags are not ideal, as it can be unwieldy to read those out in audio-only form.
