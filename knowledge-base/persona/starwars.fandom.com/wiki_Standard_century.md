A **standard century**,[[2]](#cite_note-Catalyst-2) also referred to simply as a **century**, was a unit used to measure [time](/wiki/Time "Time"), equivalent to one hundred [standard years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year").[[3]](#cite_note-DV3-3) It can also be used for a group of plants.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Axe_Forgets-4)
