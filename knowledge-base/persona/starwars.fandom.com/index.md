Wookieepedia Celebrates 20 Years!

 [![Wook-20th-logo](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") [![Wook-20th-logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/95/Wook-20th-logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20250305061023)](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") 



 

**March 4, starwars.fandom.com**—Twenty years ago, *Star Wars* fans were eagerly awaiting the [*REVENGE OF THE SITH*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_III_Revenge_of_the_Sith "Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith"), exploring the depths of [Nar Shaddaa](/wiki/Nar_Shaddaa/Legends "Nar Shaddaa/Legends") in the [*KNIGHTS OF THE OLD REPUBLIC II*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Knights_of_the_Old_Republic_II:_The_Sith_Lords "Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords"), and beginning to build [WOOKIEEPEDIA](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia")—the *Star Wars* wiki. As an ever-growing community of volunteers, the Wookieepedia project now celebrates its 20th year.

Our encyclopedia officially reached **200,000 articles** in February, 2025. This was only possible with the work of hundreds upon hundreds of fans over the past twenty years. Most Wookieepedians started off editing with little to no writing or coding experience, but our efforts have brought us closer to the goal of creating the most accurate, comprehensive, and well-presented resource about all things *Star Wars*.

Since Wookieepedia's [tenth anniversary](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Tenth_Anniversary_Timeline "Wookieepedia:Tenth Anniversary Timeline") in 2015, we have expanded the **breadth, depth, and quality** of our documentation of both the [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") and the new [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") continuities, as well as coverage of *Star Wars* media and production behind the scenes. Wookieepedia has also been building **a more friendly and welcoming community**. Informed by our contributors' experiences, we have launched ongoing efforts in revamping our [policies](/wiki/Wookieepedia:About "Wookieepedia:About") to be clearer and more fair, improving accessibility, and developing new [editing tutorials](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Editing_tutorial:_Padawan "Wookieepedia:Editing tutorial: Padawan").

Since 2015, editor recruitment has advanced on Wookieepedia, with the great majority of the current [administrators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators") and [review board members](/wiki/Wookieepedia:EduCorps "Wookieepedia:EduCorps") having joined those groups in recent years. The past ten years has also seen the progress of the [Discussions forum](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions") and the launch of twenty-two new collaborative [WookieeProjects](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects"). These include the [Project Stardust](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust") initiative focusing on essential topics and [Project Pride's](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride") efforts to improve the wiki's coverage of LGBTQIA+ topics. Most external links on Wookieepedia now have [backup archival links](/wiki/Forum:SH:Pages_with_missing_archive_links "Forum:SH:Pages with missing archive links") to keep a better record of the ever-changing Internet, and [bot](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Bots "Wookieepedia:Bots") systems have automated many monotonous site maintenance tasks such as page archivals for the [status article](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Featured article nominations") peer review system.

Our work continues unabated. Thousands more pages [need to be created](/wiki/Special:WantedPages "Special:WantedPages"), [lack citations](/wiki/Category:Articles_needing_citation "Category:Articles needing citation"), [await expansion](/wiki/Category:Articles_to_be_expanded "Category:Articles to be expanded"), and could use [more images](/wiki/Category:Articles_needing_illustration "Category:Articles needing illustration"). All the while, the franchise continues to grow and [require updates](/wiki/Category:Articles_in_need_of_updating "Category:Articles in need of updating"). As we enter our third decade, we would love for more readers to [**join our ranks!**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Welcome,_newcomers "Wookieepedia:Welcome, newcomers") May the Force be with us in the next twenty years… ✨

[![Nishr Taskforce](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](/wiki/Nishr_Taskforce "Nishr Taskforce")

The **[Nishr Taskforce](/wiki/Nishr_Taskforce "Nishr Taskforce")** was a unit of [Alliance Special Forces](/wiki/Alliance_Special_Forces/Legends "Alliance Special Forces/Legends") assembled in [0 ABY](/wiki/0_ABY/Legends "0 ABY/Legends") for the [Nishr Campaign](/wiki/Nishr_Campaign "Nishr Campaign"), a series of battles fought on the [planet](/wiki/Planet/Legends "Planet/Legends") [Nishr](/wiki/Nishr "Nishr") during the [Galactic Civil War](/wiki/Galactic_Civil_War/Legends "Galactic Civil War/Legends"). Consisting of three [platoons](/wiki/Platoon/Legends "Platoon/Legends") of [Wilderness Fighters](/wiki/Wilderness_Fighters "Wilderness Fighters") and a single platoon of [Pathfinders](/wiki/Pathfinders/Legends "Pathfinders/Legends"), the unit was commanded by [Major](/wiki/Major/Legends "Major/Legends") [Fisk Csino](/wiki/Fisk_Csino "Fisk Csino") and styled as a light-armor company, employing numerous ground vehicles to keep the company on the move. Created in response to the forceful [suppression](/wiki/Nishr_Suppression "Nishr Suppression") of the native [Nish](/wiki/Nish "Nish") by [Imperial](/wiki/Galactic_Empire/Legends "Galactic Empire/Legends") forces on Nishr, the Nishr Taskforce was assigned to harass the local [garrison](/wiki/Garrison/Legends "Garrison/Legends") and encourage the local population to stage a guerrilla-style rebellion. Operating out of several hidden bases, the company—in concert with local forces from the [Nishr Resistance](/wiki/Nishr_Resistance "Nishr Resistance")—eliminated the [Imperial garrison's](/wiki/Nishr_Imperial_garrison "Nishr Imperial garrison") mobile elements. This forced the Imperials to heavily defend their command base and effectively concede control of the countryside and plains to the Rebel force. As the campaign carried on, Major Csino's request for a unit of [Infiltrator commandos](/wiki/Infiltrators/Legends "Infiltrators/Legends") to directly assault the garrison's command center was denied. Instead, the Alliance initiated [Operation Shadowplay](/wiki/Operation_Shadowplay "Operation Shadowplay"), designed to fool the Imperial planetary commander, [Governor](/wiki/Governor/Legends "Governor/Legends")-[General](/wiki/General/Legends "General/Legends") [Vin Kollis](/wiki/Vin_Kollis "Vin Kollis"), into thinking that the unit had been reinforced with Infiltrators, when in reality they had not been. **([Read more…](/wiki/Nishr_Taskforce "Nishr Taskforce"))**

 Community Projects

  

*Below are some of the current community projects happening around Wookieepedia. If you are interested in joining in, check out our [newcomers hub page](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Welcome,_newcomers "Wookieepedia:Welcome, newcomers") to start your editing journey!*

* [**Project Stardust**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust") is improving the [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") article for [Cloud City](/wiki/Cloud_City "Cloud City") and the [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") article for [Echo Base](/wiki/Echo_Base/Legends "Echo Base/Legends"). Visit the [canon](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust:_Cloud_City "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust: Cloud City") and [Legends](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust:_Echo_Base/Legends "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust: Echo Base/Legends") project pages to join the effort!
* The [**Featured article**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Featured article nominations"), [**Good article**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Good article nominations"), and [**Comprehensive article**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive article nominations") nomination processes help bring articles up to the highest possible standards. Join the effort by helping review nominations.
* [**WookieeProject Creators**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Creators "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Creators") is adding the full credits to all media articles. You can assist the team by visiting any article on a piece of media and expanding the credits list.
* [**WookieeProject Pride**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride") is providing citations for pronouns and gender for [in-universe](/wiki/In-universe "In-universe") topics. See the [Sourcing policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing") for rules on how to cite pronouns and gender.
