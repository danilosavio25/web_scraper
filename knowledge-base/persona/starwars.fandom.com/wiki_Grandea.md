**Grandea** was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [Theelin](/wiki/Theelin "Theelin") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender") who served as a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") and survived [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") and lived well into the [New Republic Era](/wiki/New_Republic_Era "New Republic Era"). Within a village on [Lahtee](/wiki/Lahtee "Lahtee"), [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") seemingly came into conflict with Grandea during the [First Order-Resistance War](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War"). Upon defeating her, however, her body vanished and the whole village around Ren transformed into an abandoned husk of itself, much to Ren's confusion and rage.

## Biography[]

### Purged[]

#### Into hiding[]

:   "*The danger did not end with Order 66. My status as a member of the Order was known. The fact that my body was never discovered was also known. I was being* hunted*, and any person I met could be my downfall. I could trust no one. The Empire had* ensured *it.*"
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

Once a [Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), Grandea fought in the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"). During her time in the Order, Grandea was always fascinated by the [mind trick](/wiki/Mind_trick "Mind trick"), though felt that other [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") dismissed the technique as undignified. As the war neared its end, Grandea was stationed alongside the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic")'s [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") and the [Rodian](/wiki/Rodian "Rodian") Jedi [Alivar](/wiki/Alivar "Alivar"), who studied the [Separatists](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems")' front lines, discovering a weakness to exploit. As the fatigued Grandea retrieved [tea](/wiki/Tea "Tea") from a dispenser, the clones executed [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), a directive to eliminate the Jedi en masse. When confronted, Grandea used a mind trick to make two [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") believe that she had disguised herself as one of them, turning them against each other.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

Grandea received a message from the [Jedi temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2) the planet[[3]](#cite_note-Coruscant_DB-3) [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant")[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2) sent by [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi")[[4]](#cite_note-Episode_III-4)that advised the remaining Jedi not to return and to trust in [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"). Without further instruction and possibly the last Jedi, Grandea reached out to the Force, which instructed her to survive. Knowing that the Order's destruction was being met with celebration and the Jedi themselves put to [death](/wiki/Death "Death"), Grandea destroyed her [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber"), buried her [Jedi robes](/wiki/Jedi_apparel "Jedi apparel"), and abandoned the [Jedi way](/wiki/Jedi_Code "Jedi Code"). Grandea attempted to hide but was aware of the [continued danger](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge"), as [Sheev Palpatine's](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") new [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") instituted [bounties](/wiki/Bounty "Bounty") on any remaining Jedi. Refusing to become a [hermit](/wiki/Hermit "Hermit"), Grandea continued to improve her mastery of the mind trick. Practicing on a [Mon Calamari](/wiki/Mon_Calamari "Mon Calamari") [bartender](/wiki/Bartender "Bartender"), a [transport](/wiki/Transport "Transport") [pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot"), and a [merchant](/wiki/Merchant "Merchant"), among others, she sought out [species](/wiki/Species "Species") with a natural resistance to the ability, including [Toydarians](/wiki/Toydarian "Toydarian") and [Hutts](/wiki/Hutt "Hutt"), learning to overcome the strongest of wills.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

#### The Emperor's Inquisition[]

:   "*I'd gone far beyond anything my teachers in the temple had ever contemplated. What I was doing was no* trick*. I don't know how I did it, really—but it let me survive. And it* still does*.*"
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

At the command of [Darth Vader](/wiki/Darth_Vader "Darth Vader"), Grandea was pursued by two Imperial [Inquisitors](/wiki/Inquisitor "Inquisitor"), the [Ninth](/wiki/Ninth_Sister "Ninth Sister") and [Thirteenth Sisters](/wiki/Thirteenth_Sister "Thirteenth Sister"). Grandea fled, losing her right leg to the Thirteenth Sister. As the Ninth Sister stabbed Grandea with her [lightsaber](/wiki/Ninth_Sister%27s_lightsaber "Ninth Sister's lightsaber"), she used a mind trick to convince the Inquisitors that they had indeed killed their prey, the strain of the feat nearly doing so. Grandea crawled away, tearful, and suffered a nosebleed from the intensity of her earlier effort. Her mind trick successful, the Inquisitors reported Grandea's death to Vader, raising only a momentary suspicion.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

### Relic of a bygone era[]

:   "*Who* are *you? Why do you need my death so badly? The Jedi are* ancient history. *What can killing a stronger who was once a member of a lost order possibly give you? Let's just see.*"  
    "Nyyyaargh! *Get…out…of…my…*head*…*"  
    "*Oh, you poor thing. You could have been so much* more."
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

On [Lahtee](/wiki/Lahtee "Lahtee"), Grandea was confronted by Kylo Ren. When Ren finally lashed out and struck down the Force user, she somehow continued speaking to him with glowing purple eyes, revealing to him that Jedi Master [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") was already dead—having sensed his demise—and pondering why Ren had not sensed his death. Seemingly rising into the air, the speaking corpse taunted Ren by asking who he really was, to which Ren again lashed out to strike her down and stop her taunting. However, her body entirely disappeared with the strike, and the entire village around them suddenly transformed from its lively self to a decrepit ruin. The event confused and enraged Ren.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

## Personality and traits[]

:   "*I* did *trust in the Force. I reached out. It told me…to* survive. *The Order's destruction was* celebrated. *The Jedi were being* executed. *If I wanted to live…I could no longer be one.*"
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

A [Theelin](/wiki/Theelin "Theelin"), Grandea had a fascination with the Jedi mind trick and a fondness for tea.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

## Powers and abilities[]

:   "*I left my Jedi identity behind, except for two things. My connection to the Force…and the* mind trick. *I focused on it exclusively, used it constantly. Honed my skills. I tested myself—sought out individuals known to be resistant to the technique.* Hutts. Toydarians. *You may not believe this, but I learned to sway even their strong minds.*"
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

Over years of practice, Grandea achieved mastery of the Jedi mind trick. She could read surface-level thoughts, though she could not rip information from one's mind, something she claimed was impossible. Grandea was able to identify a blade produced by a cracked [kyber crystal](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal") at a glance and sensed the death of the powerful Jedi [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker").[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

## Equipment[]

:   "*I destroyed my lightsaber, burned my Jedi robes. But I knew that might not be enough.*"
:   ―Grandea[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)

As a Jedi, Grandea wore [Jedi robes](/wiki/Jedi_apparel "Jedi apparel") and used a [lightsaber](/wiki/Grandea%27s_lightsaber "Grandea's lightsaber") powered by a [green](/wiki/Color "Color") kyber crystal. Following the Emperor's purge, she destroyed her lightsaber and burned her robes. At that time, Grandea also adopted a cloak.[[2]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_7-2)
