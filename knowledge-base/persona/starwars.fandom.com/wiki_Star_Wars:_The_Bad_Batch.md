***Star Wars: The Bad Batch*** is an animated television series that acts as both a sequel to and a spin-off of *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")*. It focuses on the [squad](/wiki/Squad "Squad") known as [Clone Force 99](/wiki/Clone_Force_99 "Clone Force 99"), a band of genetically enhanced clone troopers. It was announced on [July 13](/wiki/July_13 "July 13"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020") and premiered on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") on [May 4](/wiki/May_4 "May 4"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021").[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) The [first season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_1 "The Bad Batch Season 1") has sixteen episodes,[[17]](#cite_note-Season_length-17) and its musical score was composed by [Kevin Kiner](/wiki/Kevin_Kiner "Kevin Kiner"), who previously worked on *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and *[Star Wars Rebels](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels "Star Wars Rebels")*.[[18]](#cite_note-Full_of_Sith-18)

The series stars [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") as all [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") members of [The Bad Batch](/wiki/Clone_Force_99 "Clone Force 99"), including [Hunter](/wiki/Hunter "Hunter"), [Wrecker](/wiki/Wrecker "Wrecker"), [Tech](/wiki/Tech "Tech"), [Echo](/wiki/Echo "Echo"), and [Crosshair](/wiki/Crosshair "Crosshair"),[[13]](#cite_note-SYFY-13) as well as [Michelle Ang](/wiki/Michelle_Ang "Michelle Ang") as [Omega](/wiki/Omega "Omega").[[14]](#cite_note-CNET-14)

## Official description[]

*The series follows the elite and experimental clones of the Bad Batch (first introduced in* The Clone Wars*) as they find their way into a rapidly changing galaxy in the immediate aftermath of the Clone War. Members of Bad Batch (Clone Force 99) — a unique squad of clones who vary genetically from their brothers in the Clone Army — each possess a singular exceptional skill, which makes them extraordinarily effective soldiers and a formidable crew. In the post-Clone Wars era, they will take on daring mercenary missions as they struggle to stay afloat and find new purpose.*[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)

## Development[]

:   "*During our first year on Disney+, our animated series* Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, saw tremendous success with its epic seventh and final season. Building on the legacy of* The Clone Wars*, our new animated series* The Bad Batch*, follows the specialist commandos of Clone Force 99 as they embark on new missions in a rapidly changing galaxy.*"
:   ―Kathleen Kennedy, on *The Bad Batch* during the Disney Investor Day 2020[[19]](#cite_note-Investor2020-19)

*The Bad Batch* was announced on [July 13](/wiki/July_13 "July 13"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"), along with the producers and writers attached to the show. [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni"), [Athena Yvette Portillo](/wiki/Athena_Yvette_Portillo "Athena Yvette Portillo"), [Brad Rau](/wiki/Brad_Rau "Brad Rau"), and [Jennifer Corbett](/wiki/Jennifer_Corbett "Jennifer Corbett") were named as executive producers. [Carrie Beck](/wiki/Carrie_Beck "Carrie Beck") also served as co-executive producer and [Josh Rimes](/wiki/Josh_Rimes "Josh Rimes") as producer. Rau also served as supervising director while Corbett was named as the series' head writer.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) Corbett intended to ground *The Bad Batch*'s stories to the real world, and the series creators particularly focused on military veterans who "no longer have a war to fight" and find themselves unemployed and in need of support.[[6]](#cite_note-TBB_end_interview-6)

On [August 5](/wiki/August_5 "August 5"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021"), the series was renewed for a [second season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_2 "The Bad Batch Season 2").[[20]](#cite_note-Season_Two_Announcement-20)

On [April 10](/wiki/April_10 "April 10"), [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023"), the series was renewed for a [third and final season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_3 "The Bad Batch Season 3").[[3]](#cite_note-S3_Announcement-3)

## Release and reception[]

The first trailer for the first season of the *Bad Batch* was unveiled at a [Disney](/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company "The Walt Disney Company") investors presentation on [December 10](/wiki/December_10 "December 10"), 2020.[[21]](#cite_note-Trailer-21) A second trailer was released on [March 30](/wiki/March_30 "March 30"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021").[[22]](#cite_note-Bad_Batch_Trailer-22)

On [May 29](/wiki/May_29 "May 29") [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"), Lucasfilm released the official trailer for the series' second season at the [*Star Wars* Celebration Anaheim 2022](/wiki/Celebration_Anaheim_(2022) "Celebration Anaheim (2022)").[[23]](#cite_note-SWCA_2022-23)[[24]](#cite_note-Season_Two_Official_Trailer-24)

On [January 22](/wiki/January_22 "January 22") [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024"), Lucasfilm released the official trailer for the series' third season.[[25]](#cite_note-Season_Three_Official_Trailer-25)

The first episode of *The Bad Batch* premiered on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") on [May 4](/wiki/May_4 "May 4"), 2021 with a 74-minute[[5]](#cite_note-Aftermath-5) [premiere episode](/wiki/Aftermath_(episode) "Aftermath (episode)").[[26]](#cite_note-D23_May-26) The [first season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_1 "The Bad Batch Season 1") ran for 16 episodes until [August 13](/wiki/August_13 "August 13"), 2021.[[17]](#cite_note-Season_length-17)[[27]](#cite_note-D23_August-27)

The [second season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_2 "The Bad Batch Season 2") ran for 16 episodes from [January 4](/wiki/January_4 "January 4") to [March 29](/wiki/March_29 "March 29"), [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023").[[28]](#cite_note-D23-28)[[29]](#cite_note-Season_2_Dates-29)

The [third season](/wiki/The_Bad_Batch_Season_3 "The Bad Batch Season 3") premiered on [February 21](/wiki/February_21 "February 21"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024").[[30]](#cite_note-OfficialTrailer-30)
