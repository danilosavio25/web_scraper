A **standard year**[[1]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-1) (**SY**),[[2]](#cite_note-Star_Wars:_Scum_and_Villainy-2) also known as a **standard-year**[[3]](#cite_note-CL2016-3) or simply a **year** or a **cycle**,[[4]](#cite_note-Rey's_Story-4) was a measurement of [time](/wiki/Time "Time").

On [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), a year was made up of 365 [days](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day") of 24 [hours](/wiki/Standard_hour "Standard hour") each,[[5]](#cite_note-AEYNK-5) with no leap years.[[6]](#cite_note-SWCE_2016_DK_Books_Liveblog-6) The galaxy used a standardized dating system based off of the [galactic](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") Coruscant.[[7]](#cite_note-Future_of_the_Force_Recon-7) By the time of the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic"), the [dating system](/wiki/%27ABY-BBY%27_dating_system "'ABY-BBY' dating system") would use the [Battle of Yavin](/wiki/Battle_of_Yavin "Battle of Yavin") as the epicenter on which to date years.[[8]](#cite_note-GA-8)

On certain planets, including Lothal, a year was divided into four seasons ([spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring"), [summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"), [autumn](/wiki/Autumn "Autumn"), and [winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter")) characterized by specific meteorological or climatic conditions.[[9]](#cite_note-edge-9) The [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Lothal](/wiki/Lothal "Lothal") based its yearly calendar on a system known as [Lothal Years](/wiki/Lothal_Calendar "Lothal Calendar").[[10]](#cite_note-VG-10) A being's age was usually expressed in years.[[11]](#cite_note-WaldDB-11)[[12]](#cite_note-EzradDB-12)[[13]](#cite_note-ArtooDB-13)

The passage of ten years was referred to as a decade,[[14]](#cite_note-Wine_in_Dreams-14) whilst a hundred years was a [century](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century"),[[15]](#cite_note-DV3-15) and a thousand years being a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium").[[16]](#cite_note-TPM-16)
