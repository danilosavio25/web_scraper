**Vaneé** ([![Quote-audio](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](/wiki/File:VaneeName.flac "File:VaneeName.flac") [pronounced](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6a/VaneeName.flac/revision/latest?cb=20250307223954 "VaneeName.flac") /[vəneɪ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IPA_chart_for_English_dialects "wikipedia:IPA chart for English dialects")/) was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [attendant](/wiki/Aide "Aide") who served [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") in the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord")'s [castle](/wiki/Fortress_Vader "Fortress Vader") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar"). According to a story told by Mustafarians, he was once an [Imperial](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") Inspector, was driven mad by [lava fumes](/wiki/Lava_fume "Lava fume"), and became an obsessive servant to the [Dark Lord](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith"). He, along with his master, witnessed many attacks by the Mustafarians, one by [Criakan](/wiki/Criakan "Criakan") and another by [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Sssl](/wiki/Sssl "Sssl"). In [1 BBY](/wiki/1_BBY "1 BBY"), Vaneé approached Vader—who was meditating in a [rejuvenation chamber](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank")—to inform him that [Director](/wiki/Director "Director") [Orson Krennic](/wiki/Orson_Callan_Krennic "Orson Callan Krennic") had arrived to discuss the [Death Star](/wiki/DS-1_Orbital_Battle_Station "DS-1 Orbital Battle Station"). Later, he was present when a group of [rebels](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic") led by [Lina Graf](/wiki/Lina_Graf "Lina Graf") infiltrated the fortress. They escaped, but Vader captured [Thom Hudd](/wiki/Thom_Hudd "Thom Hudd"), whom Vaneé ruthlessly tortured for months. Lina Graf returned and helped Hudd, and together they escaped Fortress Vader, with Vaneé punished by Vader by being attached to the torture web.

Sometime between [3](/wiki/3_ABY "3 ABY") and [4 ABY](/wiki/4_ABY "4 ABY"), Vaneé witnessed the [Knights of Ren](/wiki/Knights_of_Ren "Knights of Ren") attack the castle, and informed Vader about the intruders. At some point, Vaneé greatly angered Vader, causing Vader to lock him in a pit, only to eventually escape after the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor"). Learning his master was dead, Vaneé got worried, only for Vader's voice to seemingly speak to him. Attempting to resurrect his master, Vaneé inflicted nightmares upon [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"). Eventually, all of Vaneé's enemies arrived, and Vaneé tried to sacrifice and use Lina Graf as the new host for Vader's spirit. It all turned out to be a hallucination caused by lava fumes, and Vaneé was trapped by his enemies and had to live with his nightmares for an undetermined amount of time. At some point later in his life, Vaneé escaped his trap and eventually was somehow reduced to a floating head controlling a cyborg body, living in the decaying castle. In [34 ABY](/wiki/34_ABY "34 ABY"), Vaneé was still present in Fortress Vader and met [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order"), the grandson of his late master. Noticing that Kylo Ren was in a torn state, Vaneé proposed to Kylo Ren that he would guide Ren on exploring the past to help him achieve greater power, to which Kylo Ren agreed.

Inspector Vaneé

 

The origins of Vaneé have remained shrouded in mystery. Even his own accounts of his past were of suspect.[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) However, he was believed to be,[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) according to a story[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) told by [Mustafarians](/wiki/Mustafarian "Mustafarian"),[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5) around [12 BBY](/wiki/12_BBY "12 BBY"),[[9]](#cite_note-Timelines-9) shortly after the construction of [Fortress Vader](/wiki/Fortress_Vader "Fortress Vader"), to be an [Imperial](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") [inspector](/wiki/Inspector "Inspector") for Imperial Command that was sent by [Moff](/wiki/Moff "Moff") [Seward](/wiki/Seward "Seward") to [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar") with his [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid"), [See-Seven](/wiki/See-Seven "See-Seven"), to determine why several of the [garrison](/wiki/Garrison "Garrison")'s [stormtroopers](/wiki/Stormtrooper "Stormtrooper") had gone mad and perished. He and See-Seven examined a trooper of the [castle's](/wiki/Castle/Legends "Castle/Legends") [garrison](/wiki/Fortress_Vader_Imperial_garrison "Fortress Vader Imperial garrison") that had been exposed to the [lava fumes](/wiki/Lava_fume "Lava fume") and became a lunatic. During his questioning, the trooper attacked the Inspector and warned him not to go inside of the castle. He saw that the trooper was just weak-minded. The inspector entered the castle, looked around and searched for[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5) the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord")[[10]](#cite_note-ROASWSN-10) [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"). He found Lord Vader in a [bacta tank](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank").[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5)

He told Lord Vader that the castle was not safe, should be evacuated and have environmental filters installed. Although he stated filters could be installed eventually, Vader refused for the time being, and the old man argued against it until the fumes affected him. He saw hallucinations and Vader offered him a position as his [attendant](/wiki/Aide "Aide"). The inspector heard the hallucinations telling him what Vader needed. The inspector tried to convince himself that the hallucinations were not real and tried to laugh at them. He laughed for days until his laughter became real. His mind broken, swirling with dark visions,[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) Vaneé agreed to become Lord Vader's attendant at the castle and told him his name, offering his eternal servitude to Lord Vader.[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5)[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8)

However, with a dark smile, Vaneé informed the Mustafarian that he was not speaking to him as [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Vader appeared behind the Mustafarians with [his](/wiki/Darth_Vader%27s_lightsaber "Darth Vader's lightsaber") [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") drawn and proceeded to [kill](/wiki/Death "Death") the invading Mustafarians.[[4]](#cite_note-Tales_from_Vader_Castle_5-4)

#### Mustafarian plague[]

Vaneé stood on the lower level of an audience chamber within Fortress Vader near [Imperial advisor](/wiki/Emperor%27s_adviser "Emperor's adviser") [Rersey](/wiki/Rersey "Rersey") as his master stood on the upper level looking out the room's window with two [Imperial Royal Guards](/wiki/Emperor%27s_Royal_Guard "Emperor's Royal Guard") standing below. Rersey was demanding an explanation from Vader, which angered Vaneé due to the advisor's lack of respect towards his master. Vaneé reminded Rersey that he was a guest in his master's residence and would act accordingly, which Rersey rebuffed. Vader halted the pair's squabbling as he felt a disturbance in [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") moments before a chilling scream echoed in the chamber, startling all but Vader.[[11]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-11)

 [![Vader pretended to be mind-controlled](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20191030102307) [![Vader pretended to be mind-controlled](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20191030102307)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20191030102307) 

Vader pretended to be mind-controlled

 

Vaneé and Rersey followed Vader and the two guards as they went to investigate the source of the scream. Rersey remarked how the fortress was supposed to be secured, to which Vaneé quietly suggested that Rersey secure his own mouth. A loud heavy booming sound echoed through the corridor. Arriving at the fortress' entrance, Rersey demanded to know what was going on. Vaneé answered that the castle was under attack as a horde of [plague](/wiki/Unidentified_plague_(Mustafar) "Unidentified plague (Mustafar)")-infested Mustafarians and a [lava flea](/wiki/Lava_flea "Lava flea") broke through the doors. Vaneé looked on in shock as his master and the guards engaged the rampaging horde, which included an infected [lava trooper](/wiki/Volcanic_environment_stormtrooper "Volcanic environment stormtrooper"). After Vader was bitten by the infected trooper, Vaneé cried out in horror to Rersey that they had to escape, only to look on in horror as the horde and Vader slowly advanced on them until he was halted by [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Sssl](/wiki/Sssl "Sssl") of [Clan Rrrt](/wiki/Clan_Rrrt "Clan Rrrt").[[11]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-11)

As Rersey confronted Sssl, Vaneé cautiously advised him to be careful, as she controlled the infected. After the advisor snidely asked Sssl what secrets she was protecting, Vaneé pointed out to Rersey that Sssl would not tell them. Vaneé and Rersey recoiled in fear as Sssl commanded Vader to kill the both of them as Vaneé cried out in fear. Rersey shoved Vaneé to the floor between him and Vader as he told the Dark Lord he needed to resist Sssl's power over him. As Vader advanced towards them, Vaneé cowered in fear on the floor until the Dark Lord revealed he was not under Sssl's power by levitating her off the ground.[[11]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-11)

 [![Vaneé and Rersey](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20210807134450) [![Vaneé and Rersey](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20210807134450)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20210807134450) 

Vaneé and Rersey

 

Getting up off the ground, Vaneé revealed to Sssl that it had all been a trap to lure her into attacking the castle. As Sssl struggled in Vader's grip, Vaneé gleefully told her that she was no match for his master's ingenuity and power. Vaneé watched as Vader willed Sssl to order her horde to drag her into the [lava](/wiki/Magma "Magma") and followed his master and Rersey outside as they watched Sssl and her infected horde burn in the lava. Once the ordeal was over, Vaneé congratulated his master, stating how all Mustafarians would tremble at the very mention of his name. Vader informed Vaneé that it was of no importance before telling Advisor Rersey that his [shuttle](/wiki/Theta-class_T-2c_shuttle "Theta-class T-2c shuttle") was waiting for him to take him back to the Emperor. Vaneé looked on Rersey with an expression of exasperation as the traumatized advisor continually recounted the sight of Mother Sssl being dragged into the lava.[[11]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-11)

### Later life and meeting with Kylo Ren[]

#### The lost boy[]

:   "*The past* strangles *me. Its expectations and legacies and vendettas. I will destroy every last vestige of it. I will* free myself*.*"

However, despite his second imprisonment, Vaneé nonetheless escaped the chair he was strapped in and continued to reside within his master's fortress for the next decades as it slowly crumbled and decayed. Following the deaths of Vader and Galactic Emperor Sheev Palpatine, Vaneé dutifully maintained Fortress Vader, awaiting any sign of his master's return.[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) Vaneé also used Vader's codes to access Palpatine's records and learn about Vader's past, considering the struggles his master endured to be beautiful.[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24) Approaching an age considered ancient for most, Vaneé chose to extend his life through unnatural measures, undergoing surgery that reduced his physical body to only his head, which was subsequently supported on an artificial body.[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8)

While scouring the HoloNet for evidence of Vader's survival, Vaneé learned of [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), the grandson of Vader and [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order").[[8]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vaneé-8) In [34 ABY](/wiki/34_ABY "34 ABY"),[[25]](#cite_note-Timelines_LoV-25) after ascending to power, Ren sought to eradicate his past; to achieve this, he traveled to Mustafar. Vaneé observed as Ren fought and killed the [Alazmec of Winsit](/wiki/Alazmec_of_Winsit "Alazmec of Winsit"), [Sith cultists](/wiki/Sith_cultist "Sith cultist") guarding the path to Fortress Vader, musing that Ren was a plunderer; a pretender. As Ren entered Fortress Vader, Vaneé bid him welcome, claiming that it was a day long awaited. Questioning Ren's identity, Vaneé deduced him to be a blood relative of the late Sith Lord, and Ren provided that he was Vader's grandson. Vaneé introduced himself as a servant of Vader, one now in service to Ren if Ren would allow it, but he instead threatened Vaneé with death.[[6]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader-6)

Remarking on Ren's similarity to Vader, Vaneé requested an indulgence for his decades of service, asking why Ren wanted to kill him. Ren replied that Vaneé, Vader, and the fortress were all the past, that he sought to extinguish its constraints, causing Vaneé to berate him as foolish and ignorant. Vaneé explained that Vader derived power from his past, fueled by the horrors of loss, pain and rage, never looking back but never letting go. Offering the same power to Ren, Vaneé espoused that the boy was lost—that he had come because he needed it. Ren ordered Vaneé to show him.[[6]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader-6)

#### Scorched sands[]

:   "*From the very beginning, Vader's life was a* crucible*. Undending* toil*, an existence in chains. It was what he* rose above*. The blade in his soul, slicing away all that held him back so he might rise to greatness.*"
:   ―Vaneé[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24)

Vaneé brought Ren to Tatooine. Ren found the planet insignificant, a world abandoned by both his grandfather and uncle. Acknowledging its remoteness, Vaneé countered that Tatooine had held significance to Anakin Skywalker, recounting his introduction to [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala"), his induction into the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), and the death of [Shmi Skywalker](/wiki/Shmi_Skywalker_Lars "Shmi Skywalker Lars"), the latter loss leading him to experience the true power of the dark side. Vaneé concluded that the past was fuel for Vader's inferno, advising Ren not to dismiss Tatooine's role in forging the Sith Lord. At Ren's request, Vaneé told Ren that he knew of Vader's past through the Emperor, who used Vader's intimacies to shape him into a weapon. Ren felt that Sidious used Vader, though Vaneé was delighted by the prospect, desiring that all could be so lucky. Vaneé offered to share stories of Vader with Ren to guide the dark prince, so that he could walk his grandfather's path.[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24)

Vaneé led Ren to the [Mos Espa Grand Arena](/wiki/Mos_Espa_Grand_Arena "Mos Espa Grand Arena"), the site of one of Vader's earliest triumphs. Enslaved, untrained, and only nine years old, Skywalker became the only human ever to win the [Boonta Eve Classic](/wiki/Boonta_Eve_Classic "Boonta Eve Classic") [podrace](/wiki/Podrace "Podrace"). Ren was taken aback by the revelation that Skywalker and his mother were enslaved, and Vaneé elaborated that the Jedi Order took Anakin in only after they became aware of his great power, leading Ren to anger that the Jedi left Shmi in bondage. Vaneé emphasized the extent of the hardships that Vader had overcome on his own ascent to greatness, suspecting that the powerful Ren could relate to Vader's agonizing upbringing—incorrectly so. A trio of Tatooinians approached and joined in reminiscing about Skywalker's victory, one noting a rumor that [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") had destroyed a fleet of First Order ships. Dropping false pretenses, the muggers attempted to rob Ren, but Ren killed two and told the third to spread word that talking about Luke Skywalker carried the threat of death. Ren then asked Vaneé who had owned his grandfather.[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24)

Ren and Vaneé arrived at the ruined shop of Toydarian junk dealer Watto, Anakin Skywalker's final owner. Ren theorized that Vader had enacted revenge upon Watto, but Vaneé clarified that no such a thing had taken place. Vaneé went on to explain that Watto had acquired Skywalker from the [Hutt](/wiki/Hutt "Hutt") [Gardulla Besadii the Elder](/wiki/Gardulla_Besadii_the_Elder "Gardulla Besadii the Elder"), who still lived in a fortress among Tatooine's wastelands. Ren, dissatisfied with Vader's past, decided that it should be erased. As Ren took a speeder to confront Gardulla, Vaneé contacted an unknown party via a comlink, warning them that Ren would be arriving angry. Gardulla captured[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24) and tortured Ren, but the Supreme Leader escaped and instructed the First Order to bombard the Hutt's palace. In that time, Vaneé traveled to Naboo. Contacted by Ren, the Sith servant claimed to fear that the strike against Gardulla was retaliation for Ren's death. Ren agreed in retort that Vaneé should have known better. Vaneé asked if Ren's deeper understanding of Vader had led to greater power, which Ren affirmed. Pleased, Vaneé asked if their journeys would continue, desiring to show Ren more of Vader. Ren agreed, so Vaneé summoned him to [Varykino](/wiki/Varykino "Varykino").[[26]](#cite_note-LoV_3-26)

#### Of love and loss[]

:   "*It was not the* love *that gave Vader power. It was the* loss*. He was not afraid to grow* attached*, to allow* one thing *to become the center of his universe. There is* strength *in that. Focus. Vader's fear of losing Padmé made it possible for him to do anything—*anything*—to keep her safe. Do you not see how* powerful *that is? It meant he did not* limit *himself as other Jedi did. There were no* rules *for Darth Vader.*"
:   ―Vaneé[[27]](#cite_note-LoV_4-27)

Reunited at the dilapidated [Varykino Villa](/wiki/Varykino_Villa "Varykino Villa"), Ren assessed Vaneé's instruction as an attempt to show him that the galaxy was infested with rot, something he had already known a long time. Vaneé amended that Naboo was once a world of culture and power. Indicating nearby lakeside retreats, one belonging to the San Tekka clan, Vaneé asked if Ren was familiar with the lineage, alluding to the Supreme Leader's previous murder of [Lor San Tekka](/wiki/Lor_San_Tekka "Lor San Tekka"). Continuing on, Vaneé described Naboo's prosperous association with the late Emperor Palpatine and eventual decline. Ren assumed that the building belonged to Palpatine, interrupting Vaneé as he tried to correct him, and dismissed the Emperor as irrelevant, perceiving the villa as unrelated to Vader and a waste of his time. Vaneé denied this, expounding on Naboo as a source of greater pain and power for Vader than even Tatooine, as Naboo was where Vader fell in love with Padmé Amidala.[[27]](#cite_note-LoV_4-27)

Vaneé lauded Amidala as a match worthy of Vader; once wedded on Naboo, Vader never let Amidala go. Outraged, Ren rejected the idea that to understand Darth Vader, he must fall in love, though Vaneé assured him that was not the case. Vaneé explained that it was Vader's fear of loss, born from his inimitable fixation on Amidala that unbound him; Ren need only find a singular attachment to center himself around to know Vader's power. Ren denied having any limitations. Believing that Vader's heir was unfulfilled, Vaneé taunted Ren to strike him down if that was truly the case. Upon leaving, Vaneé and Ren encountered two Naboo boaters, [Sanga](/wiki/Sanga "Sanga") and a companion, who warned them that the villa was cursed since the death of Amidala, who was once Nabbo's queen. Sanga recounted the arrival of a dark spirit, in truth the Sith Lord Vader, who sought to obscure Amidala's memory, desecrating and flooding the place. The specter haunted Naboo, and to acknowledge Amidala's existence brought death, leading locals to avoid the villa, fearing Vader's return.[[27]](#cite_note-LoV_4-27)

Ren rejected the boaters' claims as superstition, but was attacked by raiders led by [Storg Veruna](/wiki/Storg_Veruna "Storg Veruna"), an enforcer of the Verunas, a former Naboo ruling family. Killing the raiders, Ren asked why the Naboo did not fight back. Sanga's companion cited the interconnectivity of the ruling class; on Naboo, lineage mattered. As the boaters left, Ren told Vaneé that he was wrong, as Vader had realized his past was a weakness and returned to destroy it. Ren telekinetically strangled the boaters and, two days later, broadcast a message wherein he declared himself king of Naboo.[[27]](#cite_note-LoV_4-27)

#### Self-loathing and self-destruction[]

:   "*Darth Vader hated himself at the beginning. He had lost everything he cared about and, on some level, considered himself responsible. He was lost…just like you, Kylo Ren.* […] *You are obsessed with killing your past. We are our pasts, whether we like it or not. What do you think you are actually trying to destroy, boy?* Yourself*. Stop wasting your potential on these silly games. Find somewhere* true *to put your hate. Find yourself…an* enemy worthy of you*.*"
:   ―Vaneé, to Kylo Ren[[28]](#cite_note-LoV_5-28)

While the First Order launch an assault against the Naboo, Vaneé accompanied Ren in infiltrating the people and leading the resistance effort out of [Spinnaker](/wiki/Spinnaker "Spinnaker"). With inside information from [General](/wiki/General "General") [Armitage Hux](/wiki/Armitage_Hux "Armitage Hux"), Ren pitted the resistance in an arial battle against a [Mandator IV-class Siege Dreadnought](/wiki/Mandator_IV-class_Siege_Dreadnought "Mandator IV-class Siege Dreadnought"), leading Vaneé to question his motivations. Ren claimed he was a catalyst for Naboo's evolution, but Vaneé accused him of self-loathing. Snapping at his compatriot, Ren detached Vaneé's head in a jar from his mechanical body with the Force. Vaneé elaborated that Vader had also hated himself at first, blaming himself for the loss of everything that mattered to him, leading him astray. Vaneé extrapolated that Ren was doing the same, as attacking the First Order was as close as he could come to attacking himself.[[28]](#cite_note-LoV_5-28)

Ren tore off a leg of Vaneé's mech suit, inquiring if he could offer the power he promised, or only words. Vaneé placated Ren by divulging the existence of a vault within Vader's castle that contained unlimited power, one Ren could not find without Vaneé, let alone open. Ren demanded immediate access to the vault, but Vaneé feared Ren would destroy it as he was attempting to do at present, finding it preferable to die rather than pass on Vader's legacy to someone unworthy. Questioned by Ren, Vaneé told the dark prince that to be worthy, he must redirect his hatred away from himself and towards a worthy adversary. Ren called off Hux's forces and the Naboo celebrated their victory up until Ren slaughtered the lot of them. Ren asked Vaneé for a rewarding opponent, to which Vaneé suggested he had just the thing.[[28]](#cite_note-LoV_5-28)

Among Vader's files, Vaneé discovered an old report of Jedi activity, though the target was unidentified. Confirming it through his own operatives, Vaneé contacted Ren, and the two set out for the [Mid Rim](/wiki/Mid_Rim "Mid Rim") world of [Lahtee](/wiki/Lahtee "Lahtee"). Ren and Vaneé parted ways, and[[29]](#cite_note-LoV_6-29) Ren was ultimately bested by Grandea, though learned of Luke Skywalker's demise.[[30]](#cite_note-LoV_7-30)

#### Simpleton, servant, storyteller, spy[]

:   "*I kept Lord Vader's castle clean. Cared for his needs. I was not privy to his deepest secrets.*"  
    "*It is interesting to me, Vaneé, that sometimes you know all there is to know about Darth Vader. And yet other times, you profess to be an ignorant, innocent, servant. A* simpleton*. I weary of threatening you. Be of use to me or die.*"
:   ―Vaneé and Kylo Ren[[31]](#cite_note-LoV_10-31)

At Fortress Vader, Ren told Vaneé that Skywalker's spirit haunted him. Desiring Skywalker to appear to confront him, Ren asked how Vader would have accomplished such a thing. Vaneé claimed shameful ignorance, as although he tended Vader's castle, he was not aware of the Sith Lord's darkest secrets. Ren observed that Vaneé often knew intimate details about Vader one moment while espousing that he was an innocent servant the next. Instructing him to be of use or die, Ren reminded Vaneé of the vault containing Vader's true power and ordered that it be shown to him.[[31]](#cite_note-LoV_10-31)

Vaneé led Ren to the vault's entrance, unable to enter it himself, though proposed that Ren might, as he shared the dark lord's bloodline and purpose. Ren tried navigating the tunnel before him, but it led him back to where he started, causing Ren to lash out at Vaneé and slam him into a wall, dislodging his head from its mechanical apparatus. Protesting that the vault alone decided who to let enter, Vaneé cried out that he knew another way for Ren to obtain his goal. Crumpled on the floor, his head jar cracked and leaking fluid, Vaneé swore himself to Ren's service, begging to serve him.[[31]](#cite_note-LoV_10-31)

Repaired, Vaneé stood before Darth Vader's meditation chamber, explaining that the Sith Lord had meditated there to achieve clarity, sometimes for days. As Ren sealed himself inside of it, Vaneé proposed that he may also find answers. When Ren emerged, viciously injured and unconscious, Vaneé rushed to his side. Considering Ren to have fought "a mighty battle," Vaneé laid him down and stripped him of his clothing, remarking that he made his grandfather proud. Hoping Ren found the answers he sought, Vaneé encouraged him to focus on healing, placing him in a bacta tank.[[31]](#cite_note-LoV_10-31)

#### Vader anew[]

:   "*Before I die, know this, Kylo Ren… Everything I did was in service of you realizing your potential.*"  
    "*My* potential? *I've been hearing about my potential since I was* three years old*. Listen to me.* I am already everything I need to be*.*"  
    "*Yes! Yes! That is the truth you needed to see! I knew my faith in you was not misplaced. You* will *honor your legacy! Truly… you are Vader rebor—*"
:   ―Vaneé and Kylo Ren[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

As Ren rested in the bacta tank, Vaneé reflected on his journey. At the time of their introduction, Vaneé felt that Ren was little more than a child despite the titles he held. As Vader's servant, Vaneé recognized the greatness within him, though claimed that to manifest it, Kylo Ren must die. Vaneé then administered an injection. Telling Ren not to worry, Vaneé informed him that Anakin Skywalker had died too and claimed that, for the Skywalkers, death was part of the journey.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

Vaneé encased Ren in a Darth Vader helmet, bound him to a slab, and suspended him above a pit of lava. Ren awoke, and Vaneé told him to breathe the fumes around him to calm his mind,[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32) the very same fumes that had once transformed Vaneé into Vader's servant.[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5) Ren began to drag Vaneé to the chasm's edge, but Vaneé reasoned that if Ren killed him, there would be no one to raise Ren out of the pit, causing him to relent. Thankful, Vaneé explained that Ren would be immersed in the lava like his grandfather before him, an important part of Vader's ascendance. Enraged, Ren broke free of his restraints, worrying Vaneé and causing him to scuttle away. Casting off Vader's helmet, Ren called out after him.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

As Ren pursued Vaneé, Vaneé spoke about his extended life, including the fall of the Jedi, the birth and destruction of the Galactic Empire, and the decades after. Considering himself to have become wise, Vaneé reached the conclusion that the galaxy needed a Darth Vader. Vaneé explained the power and breadth of Vader's presence, his embodiment of authority—and consequences. Under Vader, defiance equaled death without exception, a core Imperial principle that "inspired" his subordinates not to fail. Lethal consequences were extended down the chain of command, resulting in order and peace. By contrast, the current galactic state was one of chaos, something Ren knew firsthand, describing the dark side warrior as resentful, frustrated, and confused. By helping Ren, Vaneé sought for him to understand Vader's path so that Ren could one day adopt his grandfather's mantle, a gift to the galaxy and Vaneé's own legacy.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

Upon seeing Ren, Vaneé comprehended his potential, feeling hope for the first time in decades. Intending to serve him forever, Vaneé accused Ren of ruining that. Saddened, Vaneé professed that he had enjoyed their time together without reservation and desired many more decades to come, feeling he was victim to tragedy. Mourning what could have been, Vaneé, who had thus far fled and activated [DT-series sentry droids](/wiki/DT-series_sentry_droid "DT-series sentry droid") to fight Ren, accepted the his impending death as inevitable, beaconing Ren to him. Vaneé welcomed Ren, who demanded to know if the power within Vader's vault was real or a lie to deceive him into fulfilling Vaneé's fantasy. Vaneé admitted to not knowing; Vader had never let him see inside. Ren accused Vaneé of wasting his time and, ignoring his pleas, suspended him above the surrounding lava.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

Believing his end to be a rightful honor, Vaneé expressed satisfaction at dying by one of Vader's line. As his mech suit dropped into the lava below, Vaneé informed Ren that everything he did had been to help him realize his potential. Aggravated, Ren, having heard about his potential from a young age, declared himself as everything he needed to be. Fulfilled, Vaneé revealed that this was the truth Ren had needed to see. Realizing that Ren would honor the legacy he carried, Vaneé declared him Vader reborn as the dark prince crushed his head, ending the Sith servant's life.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

## Personality and traits[]

### Torturous storyteller[]

:   "*Who am I? I am the servant of the Dark Lord, the one true Acolyte of the Beyond. I am* Vaneé*, keeper of the obsidian flame. I am* death *to all those who profane the name of the Sith.*"
:   ―Vaneé[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5)

 [![Driven mad by , Vaneé submitted himself as the servant of Darth Vader.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2a/Vanee-ShadowOfVadersCastle.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20201107113415) [![Driven mad by , Vaneé submitted himself as the servant of Darth Vader.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2a/Vanee-ShadowOfVadersCastle.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20201107113415)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2a/Vanee-ShadowOfVadersCastle.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20201107113415) 

Driven mad by [lava fumes](/wiki/Lava_fume "Lava fume"), Vaneé submitted himself as the servant of Darth Vader.

 

Vaneé was a male [human](/wiki/Human "Human") with [white](/wiki/Color "Color") [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"), light skin,[[2]](#cite_note-Rogue_One-2) and a corpse-like complexion.[[16]](#cite_note-Star_Wars_Adventures:_Return_to_Vader's_Castle_3-16) Vaneé's [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye") were originally black, but later turned red[[4]](#cite_note-Tales_from_Vader_Castle_5-4) and then green.[[2]](#cite_note-Rogue_One-2) Afterwards, Vaneé's eyes returned to red. He was insane due to breathing in the lava fumes. He was rather sadistic, taking delight in the pain of others. He claimed he was quite content with being Vader's servant.[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5)

As Vaneé became old, he chose to preserve himself through dark science, reducing his original physical form down to nothing but his head.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

### Reverence of Vader[]

:   "The galaxy needs a Vader. *During his life, his power…his calming presence…spread through the galaxy like dark oil across a turbulent sea. Darth Vader could not be everywhere at once, of course. But wherever he* did *go, he was the very embodiment of authority. More importantly…of* consequences*. The living representation of a core principle of the Empire:* defiance equaled death*. There was no appeal, no other outcome. Vader's direct underlings knew that, and knew that failing him was, in its way, defiance. So…they simple* refused to fail*. Those good soldiers offered the same consequences to their own subordinates, so they would be similarly inspired. In this way, Vader's example, his influence, his* will*…spread throughout the galaxy. And what was the result? Order. Peace.*"
:   ―Vaneé[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

Vaneé found the struggles Vader endured in his youth to be beautiful,[[24]](#cite_note-LoV_2-24) and he came to believe that the existence of a Vader figure was a necessity, a lethal embodiment of authority and consequences to bring peace and order to the galaxy. Vaneé thoroughly enjoyed his travels with Vader's grandson, Kylo Ren, whom he referred to as his prince. Desiring help Ren understand Vader's path and to serve Ren for all eternity, Vaneé considered death at his hands to be an honor.[[32]](#cite_note-LoV_11-32)

### Creation and background[]

:   ―[Pablo Hidalgo](/wiki/Pablo_Hidalgo "Pablo Hidalgo")[[33]](#cite_note-Pablo_tweet-33)

 [![Vaneé was going to appear in Star Wars Rebels, but was replaced by Veris Hydan.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Hydan-discourse.png/revision/latest?cb=20220224152642) [![Vaneé was going to appear in Star Wars Rebels, but was replaced by Veris Hydan.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Hydan-discourse.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20220224152642)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Hydan-discourse.png/revision/latest?cb=20220224152642)

Vaneé was portrayed by British actor [Martin Gordon](/wiki/Martin_Gordon "Martin Gordon") in the [2016](/wiki/2016 "2016") [*Star Wars* Anthology](/wiki/Star_Wars_Anthology_Series "Star Wars Anthology Series") film *[Rogue One: A Star Wars Story](/wiki/Rogue_One:_A_Star_Wars_Story "Rogue One: A Star Wars Story")*.[[2]](#cite_note-Rogue_One-2) He was scripted as appearing in *[Star Wars Rebels](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels "Star Wars Rebels")* [Season Four](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels_Season_Four "Star Wars Rebels Season Four"), but as the story concept evolved he was replaced with [Veris Hydan](/wiki/Veris_Hydan "Veris Hydan").[[34]](#cite_note-Trivia_Gallery-34) [Pablo Hidalgo](/wiki/Pablo_Hidalgo "Pablo Hidalgo") recalled that Vaneé's name was created for *Rebels*.[[33]](#cite_note-Pablo_tweet-33)

A [character](/wiki/Unidentified_Sith_assistant "Unidentified Sith assistant") similar in appearance to Vaneé appears in [*Darth Vader* (2017) 8](/wiki/Darth_Vader_(2017)_8 "Darth Vader (2017) 8"), informing Vader on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") that the [Imperial Security Bureau](/wiki/Imperial_Security_Bureau "Imperial Security Bureau") had discovered evidence of a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jocasta_Nu "Jocasta Nu") in [Galactic City](/wiki/Imperial_City "Imperial City").[[35]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_8-35) According to a story told by Lellis in *[Star Wars Adventures: Shadow of Vader's Castle](/wiki/Star_Wars_Adventures:_Shadow_of_Vader%27s_Castle "Star Wars Adventures: Shadow of Vader's Castle")*, however, Vaneé did not begin serving Vader until after the [construction](/wiki/Battle_of_Fortress_Vader "Battle of Fortress Vader") of Fortress Vader, meaning that the servant in *Dark Lord of the Sith* 8 cannot be him.[[5]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Vader-5) When Cavan Scott was asked of this on Twitter, he simply said that the unidentified Sith assistant is part of the same semi-religious order as Vaneé and that the robes the servant was wearing is the robes they wear.[[36]](#cite_note-36)

However, the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") accused her of helping the Resistance and destroyed it. People got scared and wouldn't hire her anymore, leaving him as the breadwinner. Dean fears that if he loses this job, his family will have nothing.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Poe comforts Dean, telling the boy that he had a tough childhood. He adds that it is a big galaxy and that you can't let fear get to you. They are surprised by Graballa, who is obsessed with turning the chamber into luxury suites. Vaneé leads his guests into the lightsaber repository, telling them that they are looking for something very special. He finds the Saber of Scardont, which he describes as a Sith lightsaber of unusual power. To Vaneé's horror, Graballa toys with it and breaks the hilt. Graballa demands that Dean fix it, but Poe reminds Dean that it is a Sith lightsaber, and Dean responds that he must keep his job. While Dean works on the damaged Saber, Vaneé asks if they are interested in hearing the Tale of the Saber of Scardont. Poe is not surprised about what Vaneé has to offer. Vaneé claims that it is a tale of two monstrosities (which were actually [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") and [General Grievous](/wiki/Grievous "Grievous")), leading to the crackling of lightning inside the chamber, which startled Baash, Raam, and Poe. After finishing the tale, Dean finished fixing the lightsaber, but is interested in the dark-side artifact. Vaneé explains that Maul learned that power comes not from serving others but from serving yourself. Dean appears to get the message, and Vaneé tells him to follow where the saber leads him. Dean uses the Saber of Scardont to unlock a second chamber. Vaneé suggests to NI-L8 that he placed his hopes in the wrong pilot, which the droid agrees with.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

The possessed Dean leads the others to the first Temple of Mustafar. Poe says he has a bad feeling about this but BB-8 rolls in. Vaneé explains that the Temple of Mustafar was built generations ago as as a repository of ancient lore. The temple is littered with treasure. Graballa tells Baash and Raam to pack it up. Graballa grabs the Saber of Scardont, claiming it as the property of "Graballa the Hutt Enterprises, LLC." Poe tells Graballa that they need to talk but the Hutt thinks that he doesn't need a spokesman anymore with all the Sith treasure they have found.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Poe tries to warn about the danger of the Sith treasure but Graballa is only concerned about making money. Dean hears Maul's voice calling out to him from a [Sith holocron](/wiki/Sith_holocron "Sith holocron") inside a pillar, telling him to seize the power to free his mother. Vaneé is pleased that Dean has found the holocron. He tells Dean that this holocron has the power to make any dream come true. Graballa is dismissive about the holocron having the power to grant wishes. Dean says that a [Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee")'s paw can do that.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Vaneé surmises that the child is aware of the Wookiee's paw. Dean explains that the pilots who came to his mother's shop would tell stories. Poe says that he heard those stories but thinks they are made up. Addressing Dean, Vaneé tells them the tale of a boy searching for a better life, much like him. After explaining the story, Dean asks if that is really what happened. Poe says this is not true, prompting Graballa to asks if he means that [Luke](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") did not blow up the [Death Star](/wiki/Death_Star "Death Star"). Poe says that Luke destroyed the Death Star but not in the way that Vaneé told the story. Vaneé says that just as the Wookiee's paw gave Skywalker all that he wanted, he claims that the holocron will give him what he desires. Poe warns Dean that the story is a warning about being careful what he wishes for.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Vaneé tells Dean that he understands that the boy wants his family to be safe. He tells the boy to open it and he won't have to worry about them again. Poe tries to plead with Dean but he is accosted by NI-L8. Vaneé tells Dean to open the holocron with his heart. The boy unlocks the holocron but nothing happens. When Dean confronts Vaneé, the Sith servant reveals that it makes his dreams come true before snatching it out of the boy's hand. Vaneé reveals that his plan was to manipulate Dean into unlocking the holocron and tells the boy that he is strong with the Force.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Vaneé uses the holocron to summon the various objects in the Sith chamber including Ren's mask and the Saber of Scardont to form a giant armored suit. Poe, Dean and BB-8 realize that the three stories that Vaneé told were for himself. Vaneé vows to make the galaxy fear him. When Poe asks him and what army, NI-L8 arms herself. Poe is dismissive of the droid, but NI-N8 activates an army of [B1-series battle droids](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid"). Vaneé orders NI-L8 and her droids to annihilate his enemies. Poe decides to fight back and tells Dean that they have to remove Vaneé's holocron from his armor. Poe shoots the holocron off Vaneé's armor and flees with Dean and BB-8. Vaneé orders NI-L8 to recover the holocron since his power is dependent on it. He orders his droid army to get them. Vaneé eventually caught up to Dean and Poe down the hallway, and beheads the Darth Vader puppet, which says that he "finds his lack of souvenirs disturbing." As Dean and Poe were running down the Iron Tree forest, Poe was determined to escape offworld with the Sith holocron. Dean blames himself for fixing the lightsaber and opening the holocron because he was scared. Poe reassures him that everyone gets scared. Dean says that Poe doesn't, but the fighter pilot disagrees, admitting he is scared. He tells Dean that they can't control fear but they can choose what to do with it. He says that without fear, they can't have courage. BB-8 warns that the battle droids are approaching their X-wing.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

BB-8 looses his grappling cable to swing himself around the rampaging battle droids, knocking several out. Poe and Dean climb into the starfighter only to discover the X-wing hasn't been repaired. While Poe grumbles, Dean volunteers to repair the starship. Poe decides to buy Dean time to fix the starfighter. He returns to Castle Vader, where Vaneé is growing enraged by the loss of the holocron. Poe attacks Vaneé's battle droids with a parked AT-ST walker. Graballa, Baash, and Raam cower while Poe's AT-ST engages with NI-L8's [AT-AT walker](/wiki/All_Terrain_Armored_Transport "All Terrain Armored Transport"), pushing it to the edge of the walkway.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Vaneé attacks Poe's walker from behind, slicing off one of its joints. Both walkers fall over the edge into the lava below, where they are destroyed. Poe survives and climbs to the top only to be confronted by battle droids. Dean arrives in Poe's X-wing and strafes the battle droids. He lands the starfighter nearby. Vowing to rule the galaxy, Vaneé grabs Poe with his metallic body. He threatens to throw Poe into the lava below unless Dean gives him the holocron. Poe tells Dean to fly away since Vaneé is getting weaker in his armored body. As Vaneé threatened to throw Poe over the edge. Dean considers the ultimatum but recalls the moral of the story of the Wookiee's paw, which is to be careful what you wish for. He throws the holocron over the edge of the walkway into the lava below. Vaneé jumps after the holocron and is consumed by the lava, seemingly killing him. However, this was not the end for Vaneé: as NI-L8 was dragging herself out of the lava, she expressed regret that she failed him, to which Vaneé responds by gripping on to her hand out of the lava, laughing.[[37]](#cite_note-Terrifying_Tales-37)

Tony Hale inhabited his role with abandon. During recordings, Hale physicalized what Vaneé was doing, and this informed much of the way Vaneé was approached in animation. The producers purposely had Vaneé tell stories spanning all eras of *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* so that there would be something for everyone to enjoy.[[38]](#cite_note-Ghoulactic_Event-38) Vaneé introduces the story "The Wookiee's Paw" by saying "Submitted for your approval," referencing an iconic line from [*The Twilight Zone*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Twilight_Zone "wikipedia:The Twilight Zone"). Vaneé's leap to recover the Sith holocron and subsequent dive in the lava is a reference to when [Gollum](https://lotr.fandom.com/wiki/Gollum "lotr:Gollum") tried to recover the [One Ring](https://lotr.fandom.com/wiki/One_Ring "lotr:One Ring") in the [2003](/wiki/2003 "2003") film *[The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King](https://lotr.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_the_Rings:_The_Return_of_the_King "lotr:The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King")*.[[39]](#cite_note-20_Easter_Eggs-39)
