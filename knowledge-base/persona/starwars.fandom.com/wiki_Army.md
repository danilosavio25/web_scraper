Stormtroopers served as units of the Imperial Army.

 

An **army** was a faction of [troops](/wiki/Troop "Troop") and [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") who were dedicated to a central cause. Major armies throughout [galactic](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") [history](/wiki/Timeline_of_galactic_history "Timeline of galactic history") included the [Gungan Grand Army](/wiki/Gungan_Grand_Army "Gungan Grand Army"), the [Trade Federation Droid Army](/wiki/Trade_Federation_Droid_Army "Trade Federation Droid Army"),[[2]](#cite_note-SWE1-2) the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic"), the [Separatist Droid Army](/wiki/Separatist_Droid_Army "Separatist Droid Army"),[[3]](#cite_note-Episode_II-3) the [Imperial Army](/wiki/Imperial_Army "Imperial Army"), the [Alliance Army](/wiki/Alliance_Army "Alliance Army"), the [New Republic army](/wiki/New_Republic_army "New Republic army"),[[4]](#cite_note-BF-4) the [First Order Army](/wiki/First_Order_Army "First Order Army"), the [Resistance Army](/wiki/Resistance_Army "Resistance Army"),[[5]](#cite_note-Episode_VIII-5) and the [Sith Eternal army](/wiki/Sith_Eternal_army "Sith Eternal army").[[6]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-6)

In order to invade a [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet"), a group could organize an **invasion army** and use [landing craft](/wiki/Landing_craft "Landing craft") to move them to the surface.[[2]](#cite_note-SWE1-2) The [ecumenopolis](/wiki/Ecumenopolis "Ecumenopolis") of [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") effectively had a small army of [repair droids](/wiki/Maintenance_droid "Maintenance droid"), [INS-444s](/wiki/INS-444 "INS-444") and [CLE-004 window cleaning droids](/wiki/CLE-004_window_cleaning_droid "CLE-004 window cleaning droid") among them, to maintain the [skyscrapers](/wiki/Skyscraper "Skyscraper").[[7]](#cite_note-BuildR2_38-7)
