## Events[]

* The [Rod of Seasons](/wiki/Rod_of_Seasons "Rod of Seasons") is [stolen](/wiki/Theft_of_the_Rod_of_Seasons "Theft of the Rod of Seasons") from [Hynestia Prime](/wiki/Hynestia_Prime "Hynestia Prime").[[7]](#cite_note-POD-7)
* [Zallah Macri](/wiki/Zallah_Macri "Zallah Macri") and [Kevmo Zink](/wiki/Kevmo_Zink "Kevmo Zink") encounter the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion") while [investigating](/wiki/Mission_to_Dalna_(Macri_and_Zink) "Mission to Dalna (Macri and Zink)") the theft of the rod. A Path member named [Marda Ro](/wiki/Marda_Ro "Marda Ro") falls for Zink, but both he and Macri are killed by the Path's newly-hatched [Leveler](/wiki/Great_Leveler "Great Leveler"), a [Nameless](/wiki/Shrikarai "Shrikarai") creature that feeds on [Force-sensitives](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive").[[7]](#cite_note-POD-7)
* In the aftermath of the [death](/wiki/Death "Death") of her lover, Marda Ro spurs the Path into expanding its reach by moving to the holy [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Jedha](/wiki/Jedha "Jedha").[[7]](#cite_note-POD-7)
* [Outer Rim Expedition Two-Five-Two](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Expedition_Two-Five-Two "Outer Rim Expedition Two-Five-Two") is largely [wiped out](/wiki/Expedition_to_Gloam "Expedition to Gloam") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Gloam](/wiki/Gloam "Gloam") after a betrayal, with only [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Rok Buran](/wiki/Rok_Buran "Rok Buran") surviving to send out a [distress signal](/wiki/Distress_signal "Distress signal"). Master [Silandra Sho](/wiki/Silandra_Sho "Silandra Sho") and her [Pathfinder team](/wiki/Master_Sho%27s_Pathfinder_team "Master Sho's Pathfinder team") [respond](/wiki/Mission_to_Gloam "Mission to Gloam"), discovering the true history of Gloam and rescuing Buran, as well as stranded father-and-son [prospectors](/wiki/Prospector "Prospector") [Spence](/wiki/Spence_Leffbruk "Spence Leffbruk") and [Dass Leffbruk](/wiki/Dass_Leffbruk "Dass Leffbruk").[[8]](#cite_note-QFTHC-8)
* The [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") and the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") hold a [summit](/wiki/Summit_aboard_the_Paxion "Summit aboard the Paxion") between the [warring](/wiki/Eiram_and_E%27ronoh_War "Eiram and E'ronoh War") planets [Eiram](/wiki/Eiram "Eiram") and [E'ronoh](/wiki/E%27ronoh "E'ronoh") that is resolved with the heirs of both planets, [Princess](/wiki/Princess "Princess") [Xiri A'lbaran](/wiki/Xiri_A%27lbaran "Xiri A'lbaran") of E'ronoh and Prince [Phan-tu Zenn](/wiki/Phan-tu_Zenn "Phan-tu Zenn") of Eiram, agreeing to marry in the hopes of uniting the planets and ending the war.[[1]](#cite_note-Convergence-1)
* The Path of the Open Hand and [terrorist](/wiki/Terrorist "Terrorist") group the [Children of E'ronoh](/wiki/Children_of_E%27ronoh "Children of E'ronoh") attempt to assassinate Princess A'lbaran and Prince Zenn in the lead up to and during their [wedding](/wiki/Wedding_of_Phan-tu_Zenn_and_Xiri_A%27lbaran "Wedding of Phan-tu Zenn and Xiri A'lbaran").[[1]](#cite_note-Convergence-1)
* Jedi Masters [Porter Engle](/wiki/Porter_Engle "Porter Engle") and [Barash Silvain](/wiki/Barash_Silvain "Barash Silvain") perform a [mission](/wiki/Mission_on_Gansevor "Mission on Gansevor") to the planet [Gansevor](/wiki/Gansevor "Gansevor") to help warring factions there.[[9]](#cite_note-The_Blade_2-9) They successfully stop the [Attack on Firevale](/wiki/Siege_of_Firevale "Siege of Firevale") and Engle defeats [Abediah Viess](/wiki/Abediah_Viess "Abediah Viess") and her [mercenary army](/wiki/Viess%27s_army "Viess's army"). However, Silvain, believing she made a grave mistake during the mission decides to leave from active participation in the Jedi Order and starts the [Barash Vow](/wiki/Barash_Vow "Barash Vow").[[10]](#cite_note-The_Blade_4-10)
* [Maz Kanata](/wiki/Maz_Kanata "Maz Kanata") and her [pirate crew](/wiki/Maz_Kanata%27s_pirate_crew "Maz Kanata's pirate crew") engage in a [turf war](/wiki/Turf_war_with_the_Dank_Graks "Turf war with the Dank Graks") with the [Dank Graks](/wiki/Dank_Graks "Dank Graks").[[11]](#cite_note-THRA_2-11)
* A [summit](/wiki/Summit_on_Jedha "Summit on Jedha") is held in the [holy city](/wiki/Jedha_City "Jedha City") of [Jedha](/wiki/Jedha "Jedha") to officially end the Eiram and E'ronoh war, but a series of bombings and riots spurred by the Path of the Open Hand leads to the war reigniting on the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") in the form of the [Battle of Jedha](/wiki/Battle_of_Jedha "Battle of Jedha").[[12]](#cite_note-Battle_of_Jedha-12)
* After returning to Dalna, the Path of the Open Hand conducts a [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Planet_X_(Path_of_the_Open_Hand) "Mission to Planet X (Path of the Open Hand)") to the mysterious [Planet X](/wiki/Sophros "Sophros") to retrieve more Nameless.[[13]](#cite_note-PoV-13)
* The [Hyperspace Chase](/wiki/Hyperspace_Chase "Hyperspace Chase") is held, which [Sky Graf](/wiki/Sky_Graf "Sky Graf"), Dass Leffbruk, and Master Sho's Padawan [Rooper Nitani](/wiki/Rooper_Nitani "Rooper Nitani") utilize to try and find Planet X, though they ultimately abandon the mission.[[14]](#cite_note-QfPX-14)
* After receiving an ultimatum from her son Axel Greylark and the Path's [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)"), [Elecia Zeveron](/wiki/Elecia_Zeveron "Elecia Zeveron") who wish to exploit her power, Kyong Greylark steps down as [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor")[[15]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-15)
* Marda Ro reorganizes the militant members of the Path present on Dalna into the [Path of the Closed Fist](/wiki/Path_of_the_Closed_Fist "Path of the Closed Fist").[[13]](#cite_note-PoV-13)
* The Path of the Open Hand attacks several groups of Jedi that arrived on Dalna to investigate them, erupting into a battle that becomes known as the [Night of Sorrow](/wiki/Night_of_Sorrow "Night of Sorrow") in which many Jedi are killed by the Path's Nameless[[13]](#cite_note-PoV-13)[[15]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-15)
* In the aftermath of the Night of Sorrow, the Path of the Open Hand is disbanded and Elecia Zeveron is killed by Marda Ro.[[13]](#cite_note-PoV-13)
* Marda Ro takes the Path's flagship the *[Gaze Electric](/wiki/Gaze_Electric "Gaze Electric")* and the Leveler, leaving Dalna and founding what will become the [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil").[[13]](#cite_note-PoV-13)
* [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and Master [Creighton Sun](/wiki/Creighton_Sun "Creighton Sun") decide to keep the Nameless secret and out of the [Jedi Archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives"), believing it will help protect the Jedi.[[15]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-15)
* After being driven to madness by encountering the Nameless three times during the Night of Sorrow, Jedi Knight [Azlin Rell](/wiki/Azlin_Rell "Azlin Rell") writes a [journal](/wiki/Azlin%27s_journal "Azlin's journal") about the creatures before abandoning the Jedi Order, falling to the dark side of the Force, and dedicating his life to studying the Nameless.[[16]](#cite_note-Precedent-16)
