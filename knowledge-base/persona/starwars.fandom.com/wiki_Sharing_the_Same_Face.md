"**Sharing the Same Face**" is a short story that was published in the anthology *[The Clone Wars: Stories of Light and Dark](/wiki/The_Clone_Wars:_Stories_of_Light_and_Dark "The Clone Wars: Stories of Light and Dark")*, adapting the *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")* premiere episode "[Ambush](/wiki/Ambush "Ambush")." It was written by [Jason Fry](/wiki/Jason_Fry "Jason Fry"), and published on [August 25](/wiki/August_25 "August 25"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020") by [Disney–Lucasfilm Press](/wiki/Disney%E2%80%93Lucasfilm_Press "Disney–Lucasfilm Press").
