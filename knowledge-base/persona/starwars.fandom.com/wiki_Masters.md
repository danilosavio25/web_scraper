"**Masters**" is a short story published in the *[Stories of Jedi and Sith](/wiki/Stories_of_Jedi_and_Sith "Stories of Jedi and Sith")* short-story collection. The story was written by [Tessa Gratton](/wiki/Tessa_Gratton "Tessa Gratton"), illustrated by [Jake Bartok](/wiki/Jake_Bartok "Jake Bartok"), and published on [June 7](/wiki/June_7 "June 7"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"). The story features [Emperor](/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") [Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious").
