**N'Kata Del Gormo** was a [Hysalrian](/wiki/Hysalrian "Hysalrian") who, according to rumor, was one of the early [instructors](/wiki/Teacher "Teacher") of [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), future [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). Yoda, who spoke little of his early [days](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"),[[1]](#cite_note-The_Glass_Abyss-1) himself, began training [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") as a [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") in [796 BBY](/wiki/796_BBY "796 BBY").[[2]](#cite_note-Timelines-2) By [32 BBY](/wiki/32_BBY "32 BBY"),[[3]](#cite_note-Date-3) Yoda kept a sand sculpture that depicted the [Hysalria system](/wiki/Hysalria_system "Hysalria system") within his [quarters](/wiki/Yoda%27s_quarters "Yoda's quarters") in the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant").[[1]](#cite_note-The_Glass_Abyss-1) In that [year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year"),[[3]](#cite_note-Date-3) upon entering Yoda's quarters, [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") recognized the [star system](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") in the sculpture and mentally noted the rumor about N'Kata Del Gormo's tuition of Yoda, though the Grand Master, dismissing the sculpture, used [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") to melt the sand into the form of an Alderaanian rose.[[1]](#cite_note-The_Glass_Abyss-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

N'Kata Del Gormo, as depicted in the *Star Wars* Legends continuity

 

In the current *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon"), N'Kata Del Gormo was first mentioned in the [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024") novel *[Mace Windu: The Glass Abyss](/wiki/Mace_Windu:_The_Glass_Abyss "Mace Windu: The Glass Abyss")*, written by [Steven Barnes](/wiki/Steven_Barnes "Steven Barnes").[[1]](#cite_note-The_Glass_Abyss-1) [Yoda's old Master](/wiki/Yoda%27s_old_Master "Yoda's old Master") was mentioned in *[The Empire Strikes Back: So You Want to Be a Jedi?](/wiki/The_Empire_Strikes_Back:_So_You_Want_to_Be_a_Jedi%3F "The Empire Strikes Back: So You Want to Be a Jedi?")*, a [2015](/wiki/2015 "2015") young-readers novel written by [Adam Gidwitz](/wiki/Adam_Gidwitz "Adam Gidwitz") that retells the events of[[4]](#cite_note-SYWtBaJ-4) the [1980](/wiki/1980 "1980") film[[5]](#cite_note-Year_By_Year-5) [*Star Wars*: Episode V *The Empire Strikes Back*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_V_The_Empire_Strikes_Back "Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back").[[4]](#cite_note-SYWtBaJ-4) *The Glass Abyss* does not explicitly connect the two, additionally establishing Del Gormo's tutelage of Yoda to be an [in-universe](/wiki/In-universe "In-universe") rumor.[[1]](#cite_note-The_Glass_Abyss-1)

In the [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") continuity, [N'Kata Del Gormo](/wiki/N%27Kata_Del_Gormo/Legends "N'Kata Del Gormo/Legends") was a [Hysalrian](/wiki/Hysalrian/Legends "Hysalrian/Legends") [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master/Legends "Jedi Master/Legends") who instructed [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda/Legends "Yoda/Legends")[[6]](#cite_note-DAA-6) according to an in-universe legend.[[7]](#cite_note-Leland_Twitter-7) Del Gormo was created by [Chuck Hamilton](/wiki/Chuck_Hamilton "Chuck Hamilton") as part of the backstory in the Hysalrian entry for the "[Design An Alien](/wiki/Design_An_Alien "Design An Alien")" contest in the *[Star Wars Galaxy Magazine](/wiki/Star_Wars_Galaxy_Magazine "Star Wars Galaxy Magazine")*, receiving an honorable mention in the [ninth issue](/wiki/Star_Wars_Galaxy_Magazine_9 "Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 9") of the magazine[[6]](#cite_note-DAA-6) on [October 1](/wiki/October_1 "October 1"), [1996](/wiki/1996 "1996").[[8]](#cite_note-Cargo_Bay-8)
