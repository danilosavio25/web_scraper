The following events took place in or around the year **230 BBY**, aka **3047 LY** according to the [Lothal Calendar](/wiki/Lothal_Calendar "Lothal Calendar"), **211 BFE** according to the [Imperial Calendar](/wiki/Imperial_calendar "Imperial calendar"), and **Year 7747** according to the [C.R.C.](/wiki/Coruscant_reckoning_calendar "Coruscant reckoning calendar") calendar.

## Events[]

* [Farzala Tarabal](/wiki/Farzala_Tarabal "Farzala Tarabal") and [Qort](/wiki/Qort "Qort") are promoted to Jedi Knights on the *[Star Hopper](/wiki/Star_Hopper "Star Hopper")*.[[7]](#cite_note-THRA_13-7)
* [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon") is [destroyed](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon") by the [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil").[[1]](#cite_note-Fallen_Star-1)
* [Reath Silas](/wiki/Reath_Silas "Reath Silas") is promoted to [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") on [Corellia](/wiki/Corellia "Corellia"), and [his](/wiki/Gender "Gender") mentor, [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Cohmac Vitus](/wiki/Cohmac_Vitus "Cohmac Vitus"), leaves the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order").[[6]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-6)
* [Nan Yujin](/wiki/Nan_Yujin "Nan Yujin"), the younger brother of [Nan Holi](/wiki/Nan_Holi "Nan Holi"), becomes friends with [Nan Qiwen](/wiki/Nan_Qiwen "Nan Qiwen").[[8]](#cite_note-NanQiwen-8)
* [Nan Zaizhou](/wiki/Nan_Zaizhou "Nan Zaizhou") begins to work under Nan Holi as the latter's secretary.[[9]](#cite_note-NanZaizhou-9)
* The [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") implements the [Guardian Protocols](/wiki/Guardian_Protocols "Guardian Protocols").[[10]](#cite_note-SOS_1-10)
