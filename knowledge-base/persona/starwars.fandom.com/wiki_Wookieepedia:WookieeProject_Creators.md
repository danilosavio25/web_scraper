**WookieeProject Creators** is a [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") dedicated to improving articles featuring actors, authors, composers, directors, editors, illustrators, and anyone else who has contributed to *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")*. If you wish to join this Project, please sign your name [below](#Participants) in the "Participants" section.

## Objectives[]

The main goal of this WookieeProject is to elevate the overall quality of creators articles on Wookieepedia, and help editors to bring those to status ([Featured articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_articles "Wookieepedia:Featured articles"), [Good articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_articles "Wookieepedia:Good articles"), [Comprehensive articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_articles "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive articles")).

A lot of articles still requires to conform to policies, such as the [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide/Out-of-universe "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide/Out-of-universe"), the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style") and the [Sourcing policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing"), including:

* Limiting the information regarding creators careers outsides *Star Wars* to the [strict minimum](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide/Out-of-universe#Real-world_person_articles "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide/Out-of-universe"), and removing any listing unrelated to *Star Wars*. If you are unsure about what to do exactly, you can always leave the {{[StayOnTarget](/wiki/Template:StayOnTarget "Template:StayOnTarget")}} template on a page for a more experienced editor to deal with it.
* Ensuring that the list of works of a creator are [correctly organized](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide/Out-of-universe#Real-world_person_articles "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide/Out-of-universe"), under a single "Works" section, itself subsectionned between Bibliography, Discography, Filmography, Gameography and/or Other works. Note that the order of the subsections must follow the chronological order of releases, thus if a Gameography subsection first release is from 1990 and a Filmography subsection first release is from 2000, the Gamegraphy subsection will come first.
* Each works list inside subsections must be made into a [table](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide#Tables "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide"), using, in order, Year and Title columns, with the possible addition of others, including but not limited to Series, Contributions, Platforms, Format, and Notes. **The inclusion of properly formatted tables is currently the task that need the most work on creators articles, and help on this matter would be greatly appreciated**.
* Another great concern is the lack of [references](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing") in a great deal of articles. Remember that you should always includes a reference to the addition of new information to an article. Note that tables too needs to be referenced.
* As those articles often support several [external links](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide#External_links "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide"), be sure that those are listed in the proper order.
