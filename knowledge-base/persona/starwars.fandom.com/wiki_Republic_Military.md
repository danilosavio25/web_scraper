The **Republic Military**, also known as the **Republic Armed Forces** or **clone military**, was the military of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), consisting of the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") and the [Republic Navy](/wiki/Republic_Navy "Republic Navy"). The [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") was the [Commander-in-Chief](/wiki/Commander-in-Chief "Commander-in-Chief") of the Republic Military; the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") and the [Republic High Command](/wiki/Republic_High_Command "Republic High Command") were principle organs by which military policy was carried out. [Republic Intelligence](/wiki/Republic_Intelligence "Republic Intelligence") served as the Republic Military's [intelligence-gathering](/wiki/Spy "Spy") and covert operations branch.

The Republic Military existed during the [Dark Age](/wiki/Fall_of_the_Old_Republic "Fall of the Old Republic"). After the culmination of that era, it was disbanded in around [1032 BBY](/wiki/1032_BBY "1032 BBY"), following the [Jedi-Sith War](/wiki/Jedi-Sith_War "Jedi-Sith War") and the [supposed defeat of the Sith](/wiki/Defeat_of_the_Sith "Defeat of the Sith"). At the [time](/wiki/Time "Time") of the modern Republic Military's inception, the [Galactic](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") Republic presided over an [era of peace](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") that lasted for a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium").

During the Republic's [final years](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era"), however, [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") was on the verge of civil war as a result of the [Separatist Crisis](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis"). After discovering that the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems")—a confederation of [star systems](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") in rebellion—formed a [droid-based military](/wiki/Confederacy_military "Confederacy military"), the Republic acquired an [army](/wiki/Army "Army") of [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") to serve as the backbone of its own military. [Bred](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") from the [genetic](/wiki/DNA "DNA") [template](/wiki/Clone_template "Clone template") of the [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett"), clone [troopers](/wiki/Trooper "Trooper") fulfilled various functions in every branch of the Republic Military, from [infantry](/wiki/Infantry "Infantry") [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier"), [navigation officers](/wiki/Clone_navigation_officer "Clone navigation officer"), and [starfighter](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") [pilots](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot"), to the elite [ARC troopers](/wiki/Advanced_Recon_Commando "Advanced Recon Commando") and [clone commandos](/wiki/Clone_commando "Clone commando"). Next to the chancellor, the highest-[ranking](/wiki/Military_rank "Military rank") [officers](/wiki/Officer "Officer") in the Republic Military were the [Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), who were commissioned as [Jedi Generals](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General") and served by a cadre of [Clone Commanders](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") and non-clone [Republic officers](/wiki/Republic_officer "Republic officer").

For three [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") Republic and CIS forces fought across the galaxy, from the [First Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis") in [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY") to the [Outer Rim Sieges](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Sieges "Outer Rim Sieges") in [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY"). During the final [days](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day") of the Clone Wars, the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") were betrayed by their [troops](/wiki/Troop "Troop") due to a [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") [conspiracy](/wiki/Behavioral_modification_biochip "Behavioral modification biochip") to [liquidate](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") the Order and take over the Republic. Supreme Chancellor [Sheev Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), who was in fact the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Sidious, commanded the clones to initiate [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66")—a secret protocol that compelled the clones to [summarily execute](/wiki/Death_penalty "Death penalty") their Jedi [generals](/wiki/General "General") as [enemies of the state](/wiki/Treason "Treason").

With the Jedi removed as an obstacle, Palpatine supplanted the old [democracy](/wiki/Democracy "Democracy") of the Republic with the [fascist](/wiki/Fascism "Fascism") [New Order](/wiki/New_Order "New Order"), [proclaiming](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") himself [Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") of the [First Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"). The Republic Military was consequently transformed into the [Imperial Military](/wiki/Imperial_Military "Imperial Military"). Upon the [formation](/wiki/Declaration_of_a_New_Republic "Declaration of a New Republic") of the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic"), the Republic Military was restored as the [New Republic Defense Force](/wiki/New_Republic_Defense_Force "New Republic Defense Force") as part of the reorganisation from the [forces](/wiki/Alliance_Military "Alliance Military") of the [Alliance to Restore the Republic](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic").

Military policy was decided by the Supreme Chancellor and the Jedi High Council.

 

The [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") was the [Commander-in-Chief](/wiki/Commander-in-Chief "Commander-in-Chief") of the Republic Military.[[15]](#cite_note-Databank_Cody-15) The ten [Systems Armies](/wiki/Systems_Army "Systems Army") that composed the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") were under the command of the Supreme Chancellor.[[16]](#cite_note-CL-16) Following the passage of the [Reflex Amendment](/wiki/Reflex_Amendment "Reflex Amendment"), the [office](/wiki/Office_of_the_Chancellor "Office of the Chancellor") of Supreme Chancellor gained almost complete control of the Republic Military.[[17]](#cite_note-CotR-17) The rules of military conduct outlined what was acceptable for Republic personnel.[[18]](#cite_note-MaskFear-18)

The [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") was integrated into the Republic Military's command structure through the [Jedi Military Integration Act](/wiki/Jedi_Military_Integration_Act "Jedi Military Integration Act"), establishing the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") as [commanding officers](/wiki/Commanding_officer "Commanding officer") of the Grand [Army](/wiki/Army "Army"). The Integration Act created the [military rank](/wiki/Military_rank "Military rank") of [Jedi General](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General") for [Jedi Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") and [Jedi Masters](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master"), and the rank of [Jedi Commander](/wiki/Jedi_Commander "Jedi Commander") for [Padawans](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan").[[19]](#cite_note-Brotherhood-19) Command of the Grand Army's [Sector Armies](/wiki/Sector_Army "Sector Army") were reserved for members of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council"),[[16]](#cite_note-CL-16) and each [Systems](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") Army was commanded by a [High Jedi General](/wiki/High_Jedi_General "High Jedi General").[[20]](#cite_note-BMF_18-20)

Republic officers were non-clone commissioned officers who served at the side of Jedi Generals.

 

[Republic officers](/wiki/Republic_officer "Republic officer")[[21]](#cite_note-RAS-SWE-21) were non-[clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") veteran [officers](/wiki/Officer "Officer") in the [Galactic Republic Navy](/wiki/Republic_Navy "Republic Navy"). A wartime bargain between Supreme Chancellor [Sheev Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") and the Jedi Order resulted in the latter having Republic officers assigned to Jedi [Generals](/wiki/General "General"), such as [Admiral](/wiki/Admiral "Admiral") [Wullf Yularen](/wiki/Wullf_Yularen "Wullf Yularen") who served with Jedi General [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars").[[22]](#cite_note-YularenDB-22) Though rigid military discipline and Jedi leadership worked in tandem to execute the more audacious objectives of the Republic Military,[[23]](#cite_note-Admiral_Coburn_DB-23) some [fleet](/wiki/Fleet "Fleet") officers struggled to adapt to the Jedi's command style.[[22]](#cite_note-YularenDB-22)[[24]](#cite_note-TarkinDB-24) Yularen respected Skywalker for his skills and valor, but disapproved of the young Jedi's reckless behavior.[[22]](#cite_note-YularenDB-22)

Admiral[[24]](#cite_note-TarkinDB-24) [Wilhuff Tarkin](/wiki/Wilhuff_Tarkin "Wilhuff Tarkin") believed that the Jedi should be removed from the military command structure altogether.[[25]](#cite_note-Citadel_Rescue-25) Having served as a [captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") under Jedi General [Even Piell](/wiki/Even_Piell "Even Piell"), Tarkin was convinced that the [Jedi Code](/wiki/Jedi_Code "Jedi Code") prevented the Republic Military from achieving total victory while trying to adhere to Jedi ethics in wartime.[[24]](#cite_note-TarkinDB-24) The Jedi Order enforced restrictions on what warfare was acceptable that kept individuals like Tarkin from going as far as they would in the later [Imperial Military](/wiki/Imperial_Military "Imperial Military").[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26)

[Clone trooper officers](/wiki/Clone_trooper_officer "Clone trooper officer") formed the lower ranks of the Republic Military's command structure, having the authority to command [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") while being subordinate to Jedi and non-clone officers.[[2]](#cite_note-TCWfilm-2) Clone ranks ranged from the low positions of [Clone Sergeant](/wiki/Clone_Sergeant "Clone Sergeant")[[27]](#cite_note-The_Hidden_Enemy-27) and [Clone Lieutenant](/wiki/Clone_Lieutenant "Clone Lieutenant")[[28]](#cite_note-Ambush-28) to the higher commissions of [Clone Captain](/wiki/Clone_Captain "Clone Captain") and [Clone Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander"). The Republic Military utilized [clone navigation officers](/wiki/Clone_navigation_officer "Clone navigation officer") to crew the [Star Destroyers](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer") and other [starships](/wiki/Starship "Starship") of the Republic [Navy](/wiki/Navy "Navy").[[2]](#cite_note-TCWfilm-2)

The [Republic Center for Military Operations](/wiki/Imperial_Inspectorate_HQ "Imperial Inspectorate HQ") was located on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), the [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of the [Galactic](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") Republic. The [Republic army](/wiki/Republic_army "Republic army") was the armed forces of the early Galactic Republic. Following the Republic's annexation of the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Thisspias](/wiki/Thisspias "Thisspias"), thousands of [Thisspiasian](/wiki/Thisspiasian "Thisspiasian") [warrior-monks](/wiki/Thisspiasian_warrior-monk "Thisspiasian warrior-monk") volunteered to serve in the ranks of the Republic army.[[5]](#cite_note-Unlimited_Power-5)

The Kamino-born clone troopers formed the backbone of the Grand Army of the Republic.

 

In [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY"),[[7]](#cite_note-Galactic_Atlas-7) the Grand Army of the Republic was officially formed[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) as a result of the [Separatist Crisis](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis") that created the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") in opposition to the modern Galactic Republic.[[31]](#cite_note-Propaganda-31) Though its initial strength was 200,000 clone [troopers](/wiki/Trooper "Trooper") with a further million underway,[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) the Grand Army expanded to 3,200,000 troopers, divided into ten Systems Armies which were further broken down into [Sector](/wiki/Sector "Sector") Armies, [corps](/wiki/Corps "Corps"), [legions](/wiki/Legion "Legion"), [regiments](/wiki/Regiment "Regiment"), [battalions](/wiki/Battalion "Battalion"), [companies](/wiki/Company "Company"), [platoons](/wiki/Platoon "Platoon"), [squads](/wiki/Squad "Squad"), and [brigades](/wiki/Brigade "Brigade").[[16]](#cite_note-CL-16)

The clones of the Grand Army wore [Phase I clone trooper armor](/wiki/Phase_I_clone_trooper_armor "Phase I clone trooper armor"), a set of [white](/wiki/Color "Color") [plastoid](/wiki/Plastoid "Plastoid") [armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") plates worn over a black [body glove](/wiki/Body_glove "Body glove"). Phase I was replaced by an improved version, the [Phase II clone trooper armor](/wiki/Phase_II_clone_trooper_armor "Phase II clone trooper armor"), by the late stages of the Clone Wars. Aside from [infantry](/wiki/Infantry "Infantry") units, variant units such as [clone trooper pilots](/wiki/Clone_trooper_pilot "Clone trooper pilot") and [clone ordnance specialists](/wiki/Clone_ordnance_specialist "Clone ordnance specialist") wore specialized versions of [clone trooper armor](/wiki/Clone_trooper_armor "Clone trooper armor") to suit their function.[[32]](#cite_note-Clone_Trooper_Armor-32)

Clone troopers were the backbone of the Republic's forces.[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) However, because of their accelerated aging the Republic Military occasionally used squads of conscripted troopers to reinforce the clones.[[34]](#cite_note-Conscript_Squad-34) During the [Battle of Abrion Bridge](/wiki/Battle_of_Abrion_Bridge "Battle of Abrion Bridge"), the [212th Attack Battalion](/wiki/212th_Attack_Battalion "212th Attack Battalion") was supplemented by a [company](/wiki/Mekedrix%27s_company "Mekedrix's company") of [Roonian](/wiki/Roonian "Roonian") [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier").[[35]](#cite_note-Obi-Wan_3-35)

The Galactic Republic Navy was the naval warfare branch of the Republic Military. It therefore possessed an array of military [warships](/wiki/Warship "Warship")—in particular, the [*Venator*-class Star Destroyers](/wiki/Venator-class_Star_Destroyer "Venator-class Star Destroyer") and [*Acclamator*-class transgalactic military assault ships](/wiki/Acclamator-class_transgalactic_military_assault_ship "Acclamator-class transgalactic military assault ship").[[2]](#cite_note-TCWfilm-2) Making its debut at the [First Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis"), the *Acclamator*-class assault ship was used as a [troop](/wiki/Troop "Troop") [transport](/wiki/Transport "Transport") ferrying thousands of clone troopers across the galaxy.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) The *Venator*-class Star [Destroyer](/wiki/Destroyer "Destroyer") was not only used as a [transport](/wiki/Transport "Transport") vessel for the Republic army; its primary function was ship-to-ship combat. In addition, the *Venator* was developed to act as a [carrier](/wiki/Carrier "Carrier") for various [starfighters](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") and [Low Altitude Assault Transport](/wiki/Low_Altitude_Assault_Transport "Low Altitude Assault Transport") [gunships](/wiki/Gunship "Gunship").[[37]](#cite_note-Venator-37) The *Venator*-class also came to be closely associated with the Republic Military's Jedi leaders and thereby gained the alternative name of "Jedi [cruiser](/wiki/Cruiser "Cruiser")."[[38]](#cite_note-ImpStar-Deuce-38)

The [Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry](/wiki/Low_Altitude_Assault_Transport/infantry "Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry") gunship served an important role in the military, as it worked as a [troop carrier](/wiki/Troop_carrier "Troop carrier") and an escort for other forces. The gunship could also serve as an elevated gun platform and could successfully destroy enemy vehicles with its [missiles](/wiki/Missile "Missile").[[39]](#cite_note-USW-39)

## History[]

### Origins[]

:   "*There hasn't been a full-scale war since the formation of the Republic.*"
:   ―[Sio Bibble](/wiki/Sio_Bibble "Sio Bibble")[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13)

 [![The modern Galactic Republic existed as a demilitarized state for most of its history.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest?cb=20180202010839) [![The modern Galactic Republic existed as a demilitarized state for most of its history.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20180202010839)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/de/Millennial_Celebration_Invitation.png/revision/latest?cb=20180202010839) 

The modern Galactic Republic existed as a demilitarized state for most of its history.

 

The origins of the Republic Military dated back to the age of the Old Republic.[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26) These forces were headquartered on Coruscant,[[31]](#cite_note-Propaganda-31) and ultimately disbanded in around [1032 BBY](/wiki/1032_BBY "1032 BBY") following the [Jedi-Sith War](/wiki/Jedi-Sith_War "Jedi-Sith War").[[9]](#cite_note-Disband_date-9) The Old Republic evolved into the[[40]](#cite_note-TSWB-40) modern Galactic Republic,[[31]](#cite_note-Propaganda-31) a government that maintained a military around the [time](/wiki/Time "Time") of its formation.[[5]](#cite_note-Unlimited_Power-5) For much of its history, however, the Republic did not require a standing military. Local [star](/wiki/Star "Star") systems and sectors became responsible for their own security; the [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") granted the Jedi Order a mandate to uphold the peace of the Republic across the greater galaxy;[[40]](#cite_note-TSWB-40) and the [Republic Era](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") lasted for a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium"), resulting in the rise of peace and [democracy](/wiki/Democracy "Democracy") under the Republic's rule.[[41]](#cite_note-RepublicDB-41)

During the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"), the Republic utilized the [Republic Defense Coalition](/wiki/Republic_Defense_Coalition "Republic Defense Coalition") to protect its interests as it expanded into the [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories").[[42]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-42) The Republic also used [Republic peacekeeping troops](/wiki/Republic_peacekeeping_troop "Republic peacekeeping troop") to defend its interests during the High Republic Era.[[43]](#cite_note-BoJ-43) The [Judicial Forces](/wiki/Judicial_Forces "Judicial Forces") served at the Senate's discretion, however these forces were effectively supplanted by the Jedi Order as the government's preferred [peacekeepers](/wiki/Law_enforcement_agency "Law enforcement agency").[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26) Known as the guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic,[[44]](#cite_note-JediDB-44) the Jedi favored non-violent methods such as diplomacy when resolving disputes, keeping the more aggressive options as a last resort.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13)

### Restoration[]

:   "*This is a crisis. The senate must vote the chancellor emergency powers. He can then approve the creation of an army.*"
:   ―Mas Amedda[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13)

Despite the service of the Jedi and Judicial Forces,[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26) a faction known of the [Militarists](/wiki/Militarists "Militarists") began to advocate for the creation of a formal Republic Military force.[[45]](#cite_note-Rise_Seps-45) The [Invasion of Naboo](/wiki/Invasion_of_Naboo "Invasion of Naboo") instigated by the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") showcased major shortcomings within the Republic to the galaxy at large, including the lack of a standing military.[[3]](#cite_note-On_the_Front_Lines-3) The [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century") of peace that marked the Republic's reign ended as a result of the Separatist Crisis. Disillusioned by the [politics](/wiki/Politics "Politics") of the Senate, thousands of star systems sought to form a new government, the Confederacy of Independent Systems, by seceding from the Republic.[[31]](#cite_note-Propaganda-31)

[Loyalist](/wiki/Loyalist "Loyalist") [senators](/wiki/Senator "Senator") deliberated on the [Military Creation Act](/wiki/Military_Creation_Act "Military Creation Act"), debating whether to establish a standing military or to continue negotiations with the Separatists.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) While Palpatine, in public, resisted the efforts of the Senate to go to war[[1]](#cite_note-Catalyst-1) so as to instead focus on negotiation,[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) a number of Senators still pushed for the act and lobbied for the support of [Wilhuff Tarkin](/wiki/Wilhuff_Tarkin "Wilhuff Tarkin"), a personal friend of Palpatine's. Indeed, the Coruscant officials looked to Tarkin's homeworld of [Eriadu](/wiki/Eriadu "Eriadu") for help in building the Republic Military, seeing as the [Eriaduans](/wiki/Eriaduan "Eriaduan") had already created a military of sorts of their own via the [Outland Regions Security Force](/wiki/Outland_Regions_Security_Force "Outland Regions Security Force").[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26)

 [![Using the clone troopers from Kamino, the Republic Military was reactivated at the start of the Clone Wars.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/48/Clonesmarch.png/revision/latest?cb=20210703213459) [![Using the clone troopers from Kamino, the Republic Military was reactivated at the start of the Clone Wars.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/48/Clonesmarch.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20210703213459)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/48/Clonesmarch.png/revision/latest?cb=20210703213459) 

Using the clone troopers from Kamino, the Republic Military was reactivated at the start of the Clone Wars.

 

Ultimately, the Republic Military was established upon discovering the existence of the [Separatist Droid Army](/wiki/Separatist_Droid_Army "Separatist Droid Army"): [battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid") were secretly being built on [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis") to coerce the Republic into recognizing Separatist independence.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) Seemingly lacking any choice but to strike back[[46]](#cite_note-Palpatine_biography_gallery_Image_9-46) and shocked by the idea that the Droid Army would attack the Republic,[[47]](#cite_note-Palpatine_biography_gallery_Image_10-47) the Senate rallied behind Supreme Chancellor Sheev Palpatine, who activated the Republic Military with the emergency powers bestowed to him by the beleaguered legislature. The Republic made use of an army of genetically grown soldiers—the clone troopers—from the [extragalactic](/wiki/Extragalactic "Extragalactic") planet [Kamino](/wiki/Kamino "Kamino") as its military force, forming a Grand Army of the Republic and Republic Navy.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13)

### Clone Wars[]

:   "*The war left its scars on all of us.*"
:   ―[Rex](/wiki/Rex "Rex"), reflecting on the Clone Wars' effect on the Republic's soldiers[[48]](#cite_note-The_Lost_Commanders-48)

 [![During the Clone Wars, the Republic Military rallied behind the Jedi who fought to restore peace to the galaxy.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5b/Battle_of_Geonosis-OTFL.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180617064712) [![During the Clone Wars, the Republic Military rallied behind the Jedi who fought to restore peace to the galaxy.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5b/Battle_of_Geonosis-OTFL.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180617064712)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5b/Battle_of_Geonosis-OTFL.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180617064712) 

During the Clone Wars, the Republic Military rallied behind the Jedi who fought to restore peace to the galaxy.

 

The Clone Wars marked the last three [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") of the Republic's existence, in which the Republic Military fought against its counterpart, the [Confederacy military](/wiki/Confederacy_military "Confederacy military"), across the known galaxy.[[40]](#cite_note-TSWB-40) At the time of its introduction, the Republic Military was composed of the Galactic Republic Navy and the Grand Army of the Republic. Using the [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett") as a [genetic](/wiki/DNA "DNA") [template](/wiki/Clone_template "Clone template"),[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) [Kaminoan](/wiki/Kaminoan "Kaminoan") [scientists](/wiki/Scientist "Scientist") created the clone troopers who formed the backbone of the Republic Military.[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) These purpose-built soldiers existed for combat, and would fight against the Confederacy's battle [droids](/wiki/Droid "Droid") from the first battle on Geonosis to the last engagements of the war.[[40]](#cite_note-TSWB-40) The Senate oversaw the military through its [Military Oversight Committee](/wiki/Military_Oversight_Committee "Military Oversight Committee").[[49]](#cite_note-Evil_Plans-49)

The Clone Wars was named after the clone troopers who became symbolic[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) of the first large-scale conflict since the formation of the Republic.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) At the time of their deployment from the planet [Kamino](/wiki/Kamino "Kamino"), the clones displayed a level of military might unseen in the [history of the galaxy](/wiki/Timeline_of_galactic_history "Timeline of galactic history"). Throughout the war, clones and Jedi fought alongside each other[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) and, in some cases, developed strong bonds while fighting against the galaxy-wide Separatist rebellion. Though the Jedi disdained aggression, they accepted their newfound role in the Republic Military as part of their commitment to serving the Republic.[[40]](#cite_note-TSWB-40) Meanwhile, the outbreak of the first galaxy-wide war in a thousand years re-ignited the galaxy's military-industrial complex and saw the Republic Senate's restrictions on military spending lifted in the name of securing their borders against the Confederacy, whose members were considered a "menace" that increased militarization could combat.[[50]](#cite_note-DawnVG-50)

 [![The Clone Wars transformed the Galactic Republic into a militarized state.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/Anaxes_Republic_Navy.png/revision/latest?cb=20200515144531) [![The Clone Wars transformed the Galactic Republic into a militarized state.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/Anaxes_Republic_Navy.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20200515144531)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/98/Anaxes_Republic_Navy.png/revision/latest?cb=20200515144531) 

The Clone Wars transformed the Galactic Republic into a militarized state.

 

Throughout the Clone Wars, the Republic and Separatist forces engaged in numerous battles across the galaxy. Many clone troopers sacrificed their lives out of loyalty to the Republic, as well as their Jedi Generals and the Supreme Chancellor.[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) After three years of constant fighting, the Confederacy was on the verge of defeat as the Jedi-led Republic forces escalated the [Outer Rim Sieges](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Sieges "Outer Rim Sieges").[[8]](#cite_note-Episode_III-8) Unknown to both the Jedi and the clones, every clone trooper had been engineered with an [organic chip](/wiki/Behavioral_modification_biochip "Behavioral modification biochip") that rendered them incapable of disobeying [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), a secret protocol that called for the immediate execution of all Jedi. In the waning days of the war, Chancellor Palpatine—now known to the Jedi as [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Sidious, the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith")—issued Order 66 to the entire clone army, compelling the clones to slaughter their longtime allies without hesitation. In the end, the Republic Military was instrumental in the downfall of both the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic,[[33]](#cite_note-ClonesDB-33) consequently resulting in the restoration of Sith dominance through the [rise](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[8]](#cite_note-Episode_III-8)

### Legacy[]

:   "*With the Jedi decimated and the clone army under his command, Palpatine will have control over the galaxy.*"
:   ―Saw Gerrera[[51]](#cite_note-Aftermath-51)

 [![After the Clone Wars, clone troopers served in the Imperial Military during the early years of the Galactic Empire.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6b/Clone_Stormtroopers_Raxus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20210703125142) [![After the Clone Wars, clone troopers served in the Imperial Military during the early years of the Galactic Empire.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6b/Clone_Stormtroopers_Raxus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20210703125142)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6b/Clone_Stormtroopers_Raxus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20210703125142) 

After the Clone Wars, clone troopers served in the Imperial Military during the early years of the Galactic Empire.

 

At the start of Sidious' reign as [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor"), the Galactic Empire inherited the armed forces of the Old Republic and reorganized it into the [Imperial Military](/wiki/Imperial_Military "Imperial Military"). The clone troopers, who had once served as the defenders of the Republic, came to represent the first generation of the [Imperial stormtrooper](/wiki/Stormtrooper "Stormtrooper")—elite enforcers of the Emperor's regime.[[52]](#cite_note-StormtroopersDB-52) Conversely, former allies of the Republic such as [Saw Gerrera](/wiki/Saw_Gerrera "Saw Gerrera") and his [rebels](/wiki/Onderon_rebels "Onderon rebels") were convinced that Palpatine had purged the Jedi Order in order to take control of the galaxy with the clone army's support.[[51]](#cite_note-Aftermath-51)

Wilhuff Tarkin,[[24]](#cite_note-TarkinDB-24) [Nils Tenant](/wiki/Nils_Tenant "Nils Tenant"),[[53]](#cite_note-TenantDB-53) and other ambitious officers who made their careers in war gained even more opportunities for advancement in the Empire. Further, without the Jedi to maintain restrictions on what warfare was acceptable, the Imperial officers were suddenly much more free to commit whatever atrocities they so wished to complete their aims.[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26) In time, Imperial officers became free to commit atrocities for personal reasons, instead of relying on any greater military justification.[[54]](#cite_note-SW46-54)

 [![In time, however, the Imperial Military replaced clone units with natural-born human conscripts.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/46/Imperial-formation.png/revision/latest?cb=20230324195348) [![In time, however, the Imperial Military replaced clone units with natural-born human conscripts.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/46/Imperial-formation.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20230324195348)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/46/Imperial-formation.png/revision/latest?cb=20230324195348) 

In time, however, the Imperial Military replaced clone units with natural-born human conscripts.

 

While assessing the strengths of the [Imperial Army](/wiki/Imperial_Army "Imperial Army"),[[51]](#cite_note-Aftermath-51) Tarkin concluded that the clone trooper program was too expensive to maintain. He therefore supported [Vice Admiral](/wiki/Vice_Admiral "Vice Admiral") [Edmon Rampart](/wiki/Edmon_Rampart "Edmon Rampart")'s efforts to replace clones with [enlisted soldiers](/wiki/Elite_Squad_Trooper "Elite Squad Trooper") through [Project War-Mantle](/wiki/Project_War-Mantle "Project War-Mantle").[[55]](#cite_note-Replacements-55) Although the Clone Wars officially concluded with the Confederacy's defeat,[[8]](#cite_note-Episode_III-8) numerous [Separatist holdouts](/wiki/Separatist_holdouts "Separatist holdouts") persevered in their resistance to the [New Order](/wiki/New_Order "New Order"), particularly in the [Western Reaches](/wiki/Western_Reaches "Western Reaches") of the galaxy. Their continued existence was used to justify the Empire's massive militarization effort, enabling the Imperial Military to grow larger and more advanced than its Republic precursor.[[26]](#cite_note-Tarkin-26)

## Behind the scenes[]

The Republic Military first appeared in the [2002](/wiki/2002 "2002") theatrical film, [*Star Wars*: Episode II *Attack of the Clones*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_II_Attack_of_the_Clones "Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones").[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_II-13) It was seen again in the [2005](/wiki/2005 "2005") film, [*Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_III_Revenge_of_the_Sith "Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith"),[[8]](#cite_note-Episode_III-8) and subsequently appeared throughout the [2008](/wiki/2008 "2008") animated series *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")*.[[56]](#cite_note-Clone_Wars_TV-56)
