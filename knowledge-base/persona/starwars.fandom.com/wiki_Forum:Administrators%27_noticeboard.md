The **administrators' noticeboard** is a discussion area for addressing topics requiring intervention from [Wookieepedia administrators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators"). Any user is welcome to leave messages here for administrators regarding anything related to the administration and their responsibilities, such as asking for help in [settling a dispute](/wiki/Wookieepedia:User_conduct_policy#Dispute_resolution "Wookieepedia:User conduct policy"), reporting [administrative abuse](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators#Administrative_abuse "Wookieepedia:Administrators"), editing a [fully protected page](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Protection_policy "Wookieepedia:Protection policy"), or requesting a [bot flag](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Bots "Wookieepedia:Bots").

Please report vandalism at [Wookieepedia:Vandalism in progress](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Vandalism_in_progress "Wookieepedia:Vandalism in progress") and ask for page protections at [Wookieepedia:Requests for protection](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_protection "Wookieepedia:Requests for protection"). Bugs can be reported in the [Forum:SH:General bug thread](/wiki/Forum:SH:General_bug_thread "Forum:SH:General bug thread").

You may also request help from administrators via their respective talk pages or in Wookieepedia's [Discord server](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord"). For further information on contacting administrators, see [Wookieepedia:Contact](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Contact "Wookieepedia:Contact").

New threads are automatically broadcasted in the #announcements channel in Wookieepedia's [Discord server](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord"). Threads will be archived by an [administrator](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators") or a [bot](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Bots "Wookieepedia:Bots") after thirty days of inactivity. In limited circumstances, threads may be archived early if they violate another Wookieepedia policy, such as the [user conduct policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:User_conduct_policy "Wookieepedia:User conduct policy").
