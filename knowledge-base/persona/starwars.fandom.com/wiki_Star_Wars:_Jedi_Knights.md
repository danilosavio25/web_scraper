:   "*For over a thousand generations, the Jedi Knights were the guardians of peace and justice in the Old Republic. Before the dark times... before the Empire.*"
:   ―[Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") to [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker")[[5]](#cite_note-Episode_IV-5)

***Star Wars: Jedi Knights*** is a [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025") ongoing [comic book](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") series written by [Marc Guggenheim](/wiki/Marc_Guggenheim "Marc Guggenheim") and illustrated by [Madibek Musabekov](/wiki/Madibek_Musabekov "Madibek Musabekov") and [Luis Guerrero](/wiki/Luis_Guerrero "Luis Guerrero"). The series takes place before the events of [*Star Wars*: Episode I *The Phantom Menace*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_I_The_Phantom_Menace "Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace"), with each issue following two different [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") on a different mission with an overarching threat that binds the stories together. The [first issue](/wiki/Jedi_Knights_1 "Jedi Knights 1") was published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [March 5](/wiki/March_5 "March 5"), 2025.[[1]](#cite_note-Marvel-1) The series was first pitched by Guggenheim in [May](/wiki/May "May") [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024")[[6]](#cite_note-Marc-6) and officially announced on [October 18](/wiki/October_18 "October 18"), 2024.[[1]](#cite_note-Marvel-1)

While its events can be narrowed down to between the [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") [40 BBY](/wiki/40_BBY "40 BBY") and [34 BBY](/wiki/34_BBY "34 BBY"),[[4]](#cite_note-Date-4) Guggenheim was purposely vague with what specific years the series took place in, as per the wishes of the [Lucasfilm Story Group](/wiki/Lucasfilm_Story_Group "Lucasfilm Story Group").[[7]](#cite_note-DateRange-7)
