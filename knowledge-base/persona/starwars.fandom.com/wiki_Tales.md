***Tales***,[[14]](#cite_note-Disneypress-14)[[4]](#cite_note-Underworld_Announced-4) later known as ***Star Wars Tales***,[[15]](#cite_note-Disney_Plus_collection-15) is a series of animated anthology shorts that each covers characters from different factions in the [Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars") [Universe](/wiki/Universe "Universe"). [*Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Tales_of_the_Jedi_(television_series) "Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi (television series)"), the first season, was released on [October 26](/wiki/October_26 "October 26"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"). *[Star Wars: Tales of the Empire](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Tales_of_the_Empire "Star Wars: Tales of the Empire")*, the second season, premiered on [May 4](/wiki/May_4 "May 4"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024"). *[Star Wars: Tales of the Underworld](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Tales_of_the_Underworld "Star Wars: Tales of the Underworld")*, the third season, premiered on May 4, [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"). Shortly after *Underworld*'s release, Disney+ created a *Star Wars Tales* collection featuring the three series.[[15]](#cite_note-Disney_Plus_collection-15)
