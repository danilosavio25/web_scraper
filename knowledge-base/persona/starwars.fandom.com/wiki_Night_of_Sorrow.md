The **Night of Sorrow**, also known as the **Battle of Dalna**,[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1) or as the **massacre on Dalna**,[[7]](#cite_note-Out_of_the_Shadows-7) was a devastating battle in which the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") and [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") fought the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion") and its newly organized [Path of the Closed Fist](/wiki/Path_of_the_Closed_Fist "Path of the Closed Fist") subgroup, on the[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1) [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories")[[8]](#cite_note-A_Test_of_Courage-8) [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Dalna](/wiki/Dalna "Dalna") in [382 BBY](/wiki/382_BBY "382 BBY")[[3]](#cite_note-Timelines-3) when the planet was newly settled.[[5]](#cite_note-Mission_to_Disaster-5) The battle began when several different groups of Jedi arrived at the [Path compound](/wiki/Path_compound "Path compound") to investigate the Path's role in the [Battle of Jedha](/wiki/Battle_of_Jedha "Battle of Jedha") and the [Eiram and E'ronoh War](/wiki/Eiram_and_E%27ronoh_War "Eiram and E'ronoh War"), which the Path used as an excuse to attack the Jedi. The battle erupted quickly and its scale was compounded as Jedi reinforcements, Republic reinforcements, forces from Eiram and E'ronoh, and [Dalnan](/wiki/Dalnan "Dalnan") citizens arrived for backup,[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1) resulting in many [Dalnan](/wiki/Dalnan "Dalnan") [deaths](/wiki/Death "Death").[[5]](#cite_note-Mission_to_Disaster-5) During the battle, the Path, led by their [Guide](/wiki/Guide_of_the_Closed_Fist "Guide of the Closed Fist") [Marda Ro](/wiki/Marda_Ro "Marda Ro"), deployed many creatures they had collected called the [Nameless](/wiki/Shrikarai "Shrikarai"), who weakened and fed on [Force-sensitives](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive"), causing the deaths of many Jedi via disintegration.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) In the aftermath of the battle, the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") was dissolved[[4]](#cite_note-Planet_X-4) and the [Dalna temple](/wiki/Dalna_temple "Dalna temple") was built, but Dalnans blamed the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") for the deaths that occurred.[[5]](#cite_note-Mission_to_Disaster-5)

## Prelude[]

### The Battle of Jedha[]

:   ![Blue Glass Arrow](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D) *See also: [Battle of Jedha](/wiki/Battle_of_Jedha "Battle of Jedha")*

:   "*The Battle of Jedha is over. […] But the Herald acted without my bidding. It's not how I wish to lead the Path. It is far easier to act in the moment, as he did, than to manifest the larger vision.*"  
    "*Your vision, Mother?*"  
    "*That I* am *the Path. And the Path's hand will soon reach to the ends of the galaxy, far more than the Republic or the Jedi could ever imagine.*"
:   ―Elecia Zeveron, the Mother of the Path of the Open Hand, to Binnot Ullo[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

In [382 BBY](/wiki/382_BBY "382 BBY"),[[3]](#cite_note-Timelines-3) the planets [Eiram](/wiki/Eiram "Eiram") and [E'ronoh](/wiki/E%27ronoh "E'ronoh") held a [summit](/wiki/Summit_on_Jedha "Summit on Jedha") on the holy [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Jedha](/wiki/Jedha "Jedha") to sign a [peace treaty](/wiki/Eiram%E2%80%93E%27ronoh_peace_treaty "Eiram–E'ronoh peace treaty") and end the years long [war](/wiki/Eiram_and_E%27ronoh_War "Eiram and E'ronoh War") that had been raging between their planets. At the same time, the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion"), who [hated Jedi](/wiki/Anti-Jedi_sentiment "Anti-Jedi sentiment") and [Force-sensitives](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") began machinations to exacerbate the conflict and tensions that had been rising on the moon once known as a monolith of peace. Their leader, [The Mother](/wiki/Elecia_Zeveron "Elecia Zeveron"), through [Tilson Graf](/wiki/Tilson_Graf "Tilson Graf") of the [Graf family](/wiki/Graf_family "Graf family"), had the [Brotherhood of the Ninth Door](/wiki/Brotherhood_of_the_Ninth_Door "Brotherhood of the Ninth Door") conduct a series of bombings and attacks. Beyond Graf giving the forces of Eiram [enforcer droids](/wiki/Security_droid "Security droid"), Graf also supplied the Mother with a shipment of enforcer droids.[[9]](#cite_note-Battle_of_Jedha-9) Meanwhile, the Path's other leader, [the Herald](/wiki/Werth_Plouth "Werth Plouth") petitioned the [Convocation of the Force](/wiki/Convocation_of_the_Force "Convocation of the Force"), a council of Force-affiliated groups, for the council's disbandment and for all use of the Force to be banned.[[10]](#cite_note-THR_4-10)

After their petition was rejected, the Herald incited a riot in the [Square of the Supplicants](/wiki/Square_of_the_Supplicants "Square of the Supplicants") outside the [Convocation Chambers](/wiki/Convocation_Chambers "Convocation Chambers") against the Convocation, the Jedi, and Force-sensitives in general. This was exacerbated by him having Path member [Yana Ro](/wiki/Yana_Ro "Yana Ro") send the Path's living weapon named the [Leveler](/wiki/Great_Leveler "Great Leveler") into the crowd using the [Force artifact](/wiki/Force_artifact "Force artifact") known as the [Rod of Seasons](/wiki/Rod_of_Seasons "Rod of Seasons").[[10]](#cite_note-THR_4-10) The Leveler affected the Force-sensitives in the crowd, causing them to hallucinate and lash out. Jedi Master [Silandra Sho](/wiki/Silandra_Sho "Silandra Sho") was affected by the creature in the riot, and noticed that she had felt the same feeling when she had visited the Path's [healing house](/wiki/Healing_house "Healing house") on Jedha earlier. The riot eventually made its way to the [Second Spire](/wiki/Second_Spire "Second Spire"), where the summit was being held, which along with a well-timed attack by the Brotherhood caused the forces of Eiram and E'ronoh to begin fighting each other. This quickly erupted into a large scale battle in the streets of Jedha's [Holy City](/wiki/Jedha_City "Jedha City").[[9]](#cite_note-Battle_of_Jedha-9)

In the aftermath, the Herald was arrested and the Mother claimed that he had acted alone and did not represent the Path. She and the rest of the cult then left Jedha in their flagship, the *[Gaze Electric](/wiki/Gaze_Electric "Gaze Electric")*, and returned to their [compound](/wiki/Path_compound "Path compound") on their [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of origin, [Dalna](/wiki/Dalna "Dalna"). However, the battle had also in part been a ruse by the Path and the Herald to steal the [Rod of Daybreak](/wiki/Rod_of_Daybreak "Rod of Daybreak"), which could combine with Rod of Seasons and allow them to control the Leveler to greater degree. They were successful, but when the Herald was arrested the Rod of Daybreak was also taken. Utilizing an operative the Path had within the Temple of the Kyber, Archvist [Feric Oranalli](/wiki/Feric_Oranalli "Feric Oranalli"), Yana Ro stole back the Rod of Daybreak for the Mother and freed the Herald from prison.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

After the Battle of Jedha, many different contingents of Jedi traveled to Dalna to investigate the Path at their compound. Jedi Master [Creighton Sun](/wiki/Creighton_Sun "Creighton Sun") and [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Aida Forte](/wiki/Aida_Forte "Aida Forte"), who had been on the ground on Jedha during the battle, traveled to [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") and received permission from [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") [Kyong Greylark](/wiki/Kyong_Greylark "Kyong Greylark") to investigate the Path of the Open Hand due to their suspicions and concerns about them initiating the Battle of Jedha. They were also particularly curious about the weapon that Master Sho had encountered that the Path seemed to be in possession of. When they arrived in the system, they were joined by Jedi Master [Yaddle](/wiki/Yaddle "Yaddle"), who had misunderstood directions from Yoda due to poor connection and thus joined Sun and Forte for their mission. Yaddle was accompanied by [Cippa Tarko](/wiki/Cippa_Tarko "Cippa Tarko"), a [Jedi Initiate](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") who she was tutoring. Chancellor Greylark also sent two of her guards with them, [Lu Sweet](/wiki/Lu_Sweet "Lu Sweet") and [Priv Ittik](/wiki/Priv_Ittik "Priv Ittik").[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

After hearing of the Battle of Jedha, Jedi [Wayseeker](/wiki/Wayseeker "Wayseeker") [Gella Nattai](/wiki/Gella_Nattai "Gella Nattai") and Jedi Master [Orin Darhga](/wiki/Orin_Darhga "Orin Darhga") traveled to a [Republic Correctional Facility](/wiki/Republic_Correctional_Facility "Republic Correctional Facility") on the planet [Pipyyr](/wiki/Pipyyr "Pipyyr") to speak with prisoner, criminal, and son of Chancellor Greylark [Axel Greylark](/wiki/Axel_Greylark "Axel Greylark"). Nattai had long suspected that Greylark, who had been arrested for sabotaging peace negotiations between Eiram and E'ronoh by means of espionage, murder, and terrorism, had been working for the Path of the Open Hand and the Mother. When they interrogated him, however, Greylark did not seem to have any knowledge of what had happened on Jedha, but seemingly confirmed he had been working for the Path. After Greylark seemed to fall ill, Nattai and Dargha stayed on Pipyyr for days until the [warden](/wiki/Warden "Warden"), [Zoleh Prin](/wiki/Zoleh_Prin "Zoleh Prin"), received a message seemingly from Chancellors Greylark and Mollo ordering Axel Greylark's transfer to a different facility.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

Nattai, who cared for Greylark and was concerned that the atmosphere of Pipyyr was making him ill, agreed to perform the transfer themselves. She and Dargha left with Greylark in her ship, the *[Eventide](/wiki/Eventide "Eventide")* which had once been Greylark's. However, the message from the Chancellors had been fabricated by the Path, and a group of pirates working with the Path and lead by Greylark's friend [Binnot Ullo](/wiki/Binnot_Ullo "Binnot Ullo") attacked the ship. They successfully liberated Greylark, who went with them willingly. They also stole one of [Nattai's lightsabers](/wiki/Gella_Nattai%27s_lightsabers "Gella Nattai's lightsabers"). After utilizing the help of a Republic Pathfinder team to fake their deaths, Nattai and Dargha, following Nattai's [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber")'s crystal in the Force, tracked Ullo and Greylark to Jedha. There, they stowed away on the Path members' ship among crates that appeared to be filled with grain.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

The ship traveled to Dalna, where Nattai and Dargha attempted to locate, get through to, and stop Greylark in a building called The Crystal. However, they were discovered by Ullo, who killed Dargha with a [poison glove](/wiki/Binnot_Ullo%27s_poison_glove "Binnot Ullo's poison glove") and locked Nattai up underground.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

After a ship crashed and caused an explosion on [Eirie](/wiki/Eirie "Eirie"), the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") between Eiram and E'ronoh, [Captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Plana Van](/wiki/Plana_Van "Plana Van") of Eiram investigated and discovered that it was carrying a bioweapon called klytobacter, which could destroy and devastate [ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") planets like Eiram. Van and her commanding officer [Ailee](/wiki/Ailee "Ailee") reported this to the Queen of Eiram, [Adrialla](/wiki/Adrialla "Adrialla"), who blames E'ronoh. When news arrived that the peace talks on Jedha failed, it compounded with the klytobacter discovery caused both planets to return to a state of war. A clash between starfighters from both worlds destroyed both planets' ability to communicate with the rest of the galaxy. Meanwhile, Jedi Master [Char-Ryl-Roy](/wiki/Char-Ryl-Roy "Char-Ryl-Roy") and his Padawan [Enya Keen](/wiki/Enya_Keen "Enya Keen") traveled to the [Eiram system](/wiki/Eiram_system "Eiram system") per Yoda's instructions. They heard of the situation from Chancellor [Orlen Mollo](/wiki/Orlen_Mollo "Orlen Mollo") on Eiram, deciding to investigate it themselves. Alongside married couple [Princess](/wiki/Princess "Princess") [Xiri A'lbaran](/wiki/Xiri_A%27lbaran "Xiri A'lbaran") of E'ronoh and Prince [Phan-tu Zenn](/wiki/Phan-tu_Zenn "Phan-tu Zenn") of Eiram, both of whom did not want the war to continue, the two Jedi went to Eirie to speak with the two people from the crashed ship ship brought into custody by Van: [Goi Ganok](/wiki/Goi_Ganok "Goi Ganok") and Binnot Ullo.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

Not believing Binnot and Ganok's story, the four toured them around the [waystation](/wiki/Unidentified_waystation "Unidentified waystation") on the moon, during which it became clear that the two were weary of the Jedi, before Ganok let it slip that they were from Dalna. Suspecting they were part of the Path of the Open Hand, the Jedi grilled them more until they admitted it, before realizing that the klytobacter was the Path's backup plan for restarting the war. As they returned to their cell, Ullo and Ganok faked a fight and then threw poison they secretly retrieved from their ship on A'lbaran and Zenn when they approached, though Zenn then threw down and seemingly knocked out Ullo. Ganok accidentally exploded a vial of poison all over himself, screaming "Long live the Mother," and "The Force will be free," the Path's creed, as he died. Ullo, who faked being unconscious, tried to stab A'lbaran, but Zenn jumped in front of the blade for her and was stabbed instead. After Ullo claimed that he had planted gaseous canisters of poison all throughout [The Rook](/wiki/The_Rook "The Rook"), E'ronoh's capital city, and said that they would explode if he did not return, they all decided to let him flee.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

When they arrived above the planet, A'lbaran sent a message to the Path announcing their arrival, receiving a response from Path [Elder](/wiki/Elder_(Path_of_the_Open_Hand) "Elder (Path of the Open Hand)") [Yulon Onning](/wiki/Yulon_Onning "Yulon Onning"), who refused to open a dialogue unless they presented them with a gift. Eventually, Keen offered up her lightsaber's [kyber crystal](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal"), which Onning accepted, allowing them to land. After they did, Enya gave up her crystal to Onning, and the two Jedi sensed that there might have been other Jedi on Dalna. The Path refusing take them inside the compound to speak, an insulted A'lbaran explained that two individuals associated with the Path had traveled through the Eiram system with a powerful bioweapon. In the aftermath of the Battle of Jedha, [Marda Ro](/wiki/Marda_Ro "Marda Ro"), Yana Ro's cousin and a member of the Path who served as their [Guide](/wiki/Guide_of_the_Closed_Fist "Guide of the Closed Fist"), was blamed for the Path's losses and failures on Jedha because she had initially led them there. She found comfort and support in [Bokana Koss](/wiki/Bokana_Koss "Bokana Koss"), a member of the Path whose life she had saved during the battle using [Kevmo Zink's lightsaber](/wiki/Kevmo%27s_lightsaber "Kevmo's lightsaber"), which she had kept after the Padawan was killed by the Leveler. She developed romantic feelings for Koss, but lamented seemingly falling out of favor with the Mother. Wanting to prove herself, Ro joined a [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Planet_X_(Path_of_the_Open_Hand) "Mission to Planet X (Path of the Open Hand)") ordered by Zeveron and conducted by [Prospector](/wiki/Prospector "Prospector") [Radicaz Dobbs](/wiki/Radicaz_Dobbs "Radicaz Dobbs"), though she was not sanctioned to do so. The mission was to travel to the mysterious [Planet X](/wiki/Sophros "Sophros"), where Dobbs had originally found the Leveler's [egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg"), and retrieve more of the Nameless. Koss joined her on the journey aboard Dobbs's ship the *[Scupper](/wiki/Scupper "Scupper")*, and the two became a romantic couple on the journey.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

After the *[Scupper](/wiki/Scupper "Scupper")* navigated through the [The Veil](/wiki/The_Veil_(Planet_X) "The Veil (Planet X)"), the planet's living defensive atmospheric layer that destroyed one of the other two ships accompanying them, it crash landed on the surface of the planet. Ro went with Koss and the others to the planet's cave. They all quickly realized that the planet was a paradise and that seemingly every part of it was alive in some way. The Force also seemed to be incredibly strong there, with Path member [Shea Ganandra](/wiki/Shea_Ganandra "Shea Ganandra") even developing Force powers and using them to repair the *[Silverstreak](/wiki/Silverstreak "Silverstreak")*, a ship left behind by Dobbs the last time he visited. As they journeyed deep into the caves, Koss also seemed to develop Force-sensitivity and used it to lead them to where the eggs were. This unnerved Ro greatly because of her belief in the Path's teaching that using the Force was wrong. Ro and the others eventually made it to the eggs and began loading them up on [Repulsorcarts](/wiki/Repulsorcart "Repulsorcart") to take back to the ship. However, Koss then sensed another creature in the Force that he called the [Under-Dweller](/wiki/Under-Dweller "Under-Dweller"), which emerged from a deeper part of the caves and began to pursue them.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

Because the creature was slow to traverse through the caves, vines in the cavern seemed to be actively and consciously holding it back, and it did not like the sunlight when it reached the surface, Ro and the others were able to bring back many eggs to the *Silverstreak* before it could reach them. The Under-Dweller was then attacked and stopped by another being Koss called the [protector](/wiki/Protector_(Planet_X) "Protector (Planet X)"), but as they started to leave in the *Silverstreak* the beast seemingly became enraged by them taking the eggs and grabbed onto and attacked the ship. Koss fought back against the creature, eventually sacrificing his life to knock it off the ship and back to the surface. Ro was left heartbroken and blamed his death on his use of the Force, becoming even more convinced that using it was evil and that the Jedi needed to be taken down and stopped.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

 [![The Rod of Power](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c4/TheMotherHoldingTheRodOfSeasons.png/revision/latest?cb=20221020232205) [![The Rod of Power](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c4/TheMotherHoldingTheRodOfSeasons.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20221020232205)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c4/TheMotherHoldingTheRodOfSeasons.png/revision/latest?cb=20221020232205) 

The Rod of Power

 

Back on Dalna, the Mother, with the Leveler at her side and the complete Rod of Power in her possession, stood with Ro in front of the entire Path gathered in a cavern beneath the Path compound. The Herald then suddenly reappeared, declaring that the Jedi were there and coming after them and making a play for control of the Path. As the infighting between the Mother and the Herald quickly reached a boiling point, Ro screamed "silence" and rose Zink's saber in the air. She said that infighting was what the Jedi wanted and declared that if the Jedi were there on Dalna that it was their chance to act as true liberators of the Force and defeat them. Declaring that the Jedi would pay the price for not listening to their warnings or doing what they said, Ro declared that the Path would become the [Path of the Closed Fist](/wiki/Path_of_the_Closed_Fist "Path of the Closed Fist") and would take the Jedi down. The entire cavern then chanted in allegiance and support to Ro, and both the Herald and the Mother stood behind and supported her. Her cousin Yana attempted to stop her, warning her that there would be a bloodbath and that the Mother and the Herald were using her, but Marda just cut off her hand with Zink's lightsaber and abandoned Yana, rejoining the others in front of the crowd where the Nameless eggs were displayed. Claiming the Rod of Power from the Mother, she activated it and raised it above her head, causing the eggs to hatch.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

## The battle[]

### Failed fleeing[]

:   "*This is Jedi Creighton Sun. Several Jedi and personnel from the chancellor's guard are on the Path of the Open Hand compound on Dalna, taking on heavy fire. We have representatives from Eiram and E'ronoh with us. Anyone in the area, we are asking for your urgent help.*"  
    "*Master Sun. This is Master Ela Sutan. We are under heavy fire in the cave system beneath the Path compound.*"
:   ―Creighton Sun's call for help and Ela Sutan's response, as the battle begins[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

 [![Enya Keen (pictured) and her master Char-Ryl-Roy were attacked by the Path in the Night of Sorrow.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/b3/EnyaKeen_HRCE.png/revision/latest?cb=20250104185406) [![Enya Keen (pictured) and her master Char-Ryl-Roy were attacked by the Path in the Night of Sorrow.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/b3/EnyaKeen_HRCE.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20250104185406)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/b3/EnyaKeen_HRCE.png/revision/latest?cb=20250104185406) 

Enya Keen (pictured) and her master Char-Ryl-Roy were attacked by the Path in the Night of Sorrow.

 

As the Path attacked the diplomatic group from Eiram and E'ronoh, Master Roy ordered Padawan Keen to protect Princess A'lbaran and get to their ship, the *Andesine* as they both furiously defended against the Path guards, who wielded pikes and knives. As more Path members emerged from beneath the surface of the world, Roy, Keen, and A'lbaran met up with Master Sun and Jedi Forte, both groups explaining why they were there. After Sun and Forte revealed they discovered klytobacter and a powerful monster in the compound, the group began to suspect the Path was planning to escalate the fight into a war against the Jedi and decided to flee.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

Meanwhile, Master Ela Sutan and her group wandered through the surface of the Path compound, discovering it to be empty and sensing no one with the Force. Sutan yelled to announce who they were and what they wanted, but Path members, who were watching them via cameras, were ordered by the Mother to activate the trap waiting for the Jedi in the compound: enforcer droids. Though the droids emerged from the ground all around them and surrounded them, the Jedi pressed on, making their way forward to enter the caves. Seeing this, Marda Ro decided to send the Leveler and its brethren out into the caves to hunt the Jedi. Knowing her cousin was fleeing and now believing her to be in league with the Jedi, Marda also decided to pursue her.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

As Roy and his group pressed on, Sun expressed his wish to warn Master Yaddle and Cippa Tarko about what was happening, but knew they would have to reach them in person because comms were down. Before the group could get near the ship, enforcer droids rose out of the fields and marched toward them. More Path members arrived as well, blocking their way to the *Andesine* and forcing the Jedi and A'lbaran to physically take them down while deflecting the droids' blaster bolts. A Path member nearly killed A'lbaran, but she was saved by Prince Zenn, who revealed he stowed away in the *Andesine*. As the Jedi, wielding their lightsabers, finally took down enough Path members to reach the *Andesine*, the Path fired ground [missiles](/wiki/Missile "Missile") into it, destroying it. Sun decided that they should flee to locate Yaddle and use his and Forte's ship the *[Lazuli](/wiki/Lazuli "Lazuli")* to escape, all knowing that staying trapped on Dalna could doom them.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

 [![Jedi Master Yaddle led and organized the Jedi on the surface of Dalna during the Night of Sorrow.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/4c/Yaddle-ConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250105190529) [![Jedi Master Yaddle led and organized the Jedi on the surface of Dalna during the Night of Sorrow.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/4c/Yaddle-ConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20250105190529)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/4c/Yaddle-ConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250105190529) 

Jedi Master Yaddle led and organized the Jedi on the surface of Dalna during the Night of Sorrow.

 

After defeating the Path members already at the *Lazuli*, Yaddle and the Chancellor's guards readied to defend against Path members. Eventually spotting the group of Jedi running toward them, Yaddle instructed the guards to use the ship's guns to take down people attacking them. When the group arrived and they tried to flee, the *Lazuli* was attacked by a Path ship, though still managed to fly. Sun tried to send a message out requesting help, only receiving a response from Master Sutan, who had made it into the caves of the compound with her group and was fighting the Path there. After Sutan said that she could not help them, Sun sent the message out again. Knowing the *Lazuli* was damaged and failing, Yaddle tried to spot her ship, but soon saw it get destroyed. Realizing that they were going to crash with no way out in the heart of the Path compound, Yaddle ordered them to immediately start fighting and to split up when they crashed so that they could not be pinned in and overwhelmed. The ship then crashed and they split into groups of two consisting of Yaddle and Tarko, Sun and Forte, Roy and Keen, and A'lbaran and Zenn.[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

The Night of Sorrow was created for the *[Star Wars: The High Republic](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic "Star Wars: The High Republic")* multimedia project. It was first directly mentioned in the [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021") young-adult novel *[The High Republic: Out of the Shadows](/wiki/The_High_Republic:_Out_of_the_Shadows "The High Republic: Out of the Shadows")*, written by [Justina Ireland](/wiki/Justina_Ireland "Justina Ireland"), where it was identified as the massacre on Dalna.[[7]](#cite_note-Out_of_the_Shadows-7) It was later identified as the Night of Sorrow in the [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") junior novel *[The High Republic: Mission to Disaster](/wiki/The_High_Republic:_Mission_to_Disaster "The High Republic: Mission to Disaster")*, also written by Ireland.[[5]](#cite_note-Mission_to_Disaster-5) It first appeared in the [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023") novel *[The High Republic: Cataclysm](/wiki/The_High_Republic:_Cataclysm "The High Republic: Cataclysm")* by [Lydia Kang](/wiki/Lydia_Kang "Lydia Kang").[[1]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-1)
