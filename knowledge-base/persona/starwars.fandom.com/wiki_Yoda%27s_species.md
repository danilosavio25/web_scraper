The **species** to which the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") belonged was ancient and shrouded in mystery. Members of this species were rarely seen anywhere in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"). The few members of this species seen in the [galaxy](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") were all [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive"), and the species' [homeworld](/wiki/Yoda%27s_homeworld "Yoda's homeworld") and name were unknown.

## Biology and appearance

:   "*Wait. They said fifty years old.*"  
    "*Species age differently. Perhaps it could live many centuries.*"
:   ―Din Djarin and IG-11 upon discovering an infant of the species[[5]](#cite_note-Chapter_1-5)

 [![Din Grogu, an infant member of the species.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Grogu-SWI216.png/revision/latest?cb=20221028144305) [![Din Grogu, an infant member of the species.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Grogu-SWI216.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20221028144305)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f9/Grogu-SWI216.png/revision/latest?cb=20221028144305) 

Din Grogu, an infant member of the species.

 

The [species](/wiki/Species "Species") included male[[11]](#cite_note-Databank_YOD-11) and female [genders](/wiki/Gender "Gender").[[2]](#cite_note-Databank_YAD-2) Members of the species were small in size, roughly comparable to that of a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") child, with [green](/wiki/Color "Color") skin, large [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye"), and long pointed [ears](/wiki/Ear "Ear"). Their three-fingered hands ended in claws.[[3]](#cite_note-Revenge_of_the_Sith-3) One member of the species, [Grogu](/wiki/Din_Grogu "Din Grogu"), caught and [devoured](/wiki/Diet "Diet") live [amphibians](/wiki/Amphibian "Amphibian") whole,[[8]](#cite_note-The_Reckoning-8) while another member, [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), made [rootleaf stews](/wiki/Rootleaf_stew "Rootleaf stew").[[9]](#cite_note-CL2016-9) Their ears were expressive, curling, and unfurling in accordance with their emotions.[[4]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-4)

Members of the species were capable of growing thin [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"), with shades varying from auburn to white being documented, females having longer and thicker hair than males. The species had a very long life expectancy, spanning at least several [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century"); [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") lived nearly a thousand years before dying of old age,[[6]](#cite_note-AEYNTK-6) and they aged very slowly, remaining in infancy for at least fifty [standard years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year").[[5]](#cite_note-Chapter_1-5) Even at this stage of life, certain [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") individuals of this species could use [Force powers](/wiki/Force_power "Force power") such as [telekinesis](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") and [Force healing](/wiki/Force_healing "Force healing");[[8]](#cite_note-The_Reckoning-8)[[12]](#cite_note-Chapter_2-12) they were also capable of complex thought processes, and could understand speech and communicate via the Force at this age.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Jedi-13) Despite their slow aging for the first five [decades](/wiki/Standard_decade "Standard decade") of life,[[5]](#cite_note-Chapter_1-5) members of the species reached maturity by their hundredth year, as exemplified by the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), who began [training](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") other Jedi when he was about one hundred years old.[[6]](#cite_note-AEYNTK-6) According to [Yaddle](/wiki/Yaddle "Yaddle"), Yoda was considered to be middle-aged[[14]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-14) at 514.[[15]](#cite_note-Middle-aged-15)

## Society and culture

:   "*I want to tell you about someone you remind me of a great deal. His name was Yoda. He was small like you, but his heart was huge and the Force was strong in him. He once said to me 'Size matters not.' That's how he talked. He would speak in riddles. Have you heard anyone talk like that back home?*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker to Grogu[[16]](#cite_note-BOBF_6-16)

Due to the rarity of the species in the wider [galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"),[[7]](#cite_note-Complete_Visual_New-7) little was known about them except that the species was ancient and shrouded in mystery.[[17]](#cite_note-Card_Trader-17) Members of the species were capable of speaking [Galactic Basic Standard](/wiki/Galactic_Basic_Standard "Galactic Basic Standard"), although Yoda often used a distinctive syntax.[[1]](#cite_note-The_Empire_Strikes_Back-1)

## History

### Yoda, Grand Master of the Jedi

:   "*Do, or do not. There is no try.*"
:   ―Yoda to Luke Skywalker — ![20?cb=20250116042720](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/ee/Quote-audio.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/20?cb=20250116042720) [Link](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/fc/ThereIsNoTry-ESB.flac/revision/latest?cb=20250405134841)▶️ ([file info](/wiki/File:ThereIsNoTry-ESB.flac "File:ThereIsNoTry-ESB.flac"))[[1]](#cite_note-The_Empire_Strikes_Back-1)

 [![Yoda served as Grand Master of the Jedi Order when he was almost nine centuries old.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/7e/Yoda_Episode_I_Canon.png/revision/latest?cb=20130921110620) [![Yoda served as Grand Master of the Jedi Order when he was almost nine centuries old.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/7e/Yoda_Episode_I_Canon.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20130921110620)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/7e/Yoda_Episode_I_Canon.png/revision/latest?cb=20130921110620) 

Yoda served as Grand Master of the Jedi Order when he was almost nine centuries old.

 

Yoda, the last [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") prior to its [fall](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") at the end of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"),[[3]](#cite_note-Revenge_of_the_Sith-3) was a member of the species born around the [standard year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") [896 BBY](/wiki/896_BBY "896 BBY").[[18]](#cite_note-Yoda_Age-18) He began training other Jedi when he was around a century old.[[6]](#cite_note-AEYNTK-6) Over the centuries, Yoda, who never revealed what [his homeworld](/wiki/Yoda%27s_homeworld "Yoda's homeworld") was,[[7]](#cite_note-Complete_Visual_New-7) trained many Jedi before eventually becoming the Grand Master of the entire order.[[3]](#cite_note-Revenge_of_the_Sith-3) One of his final [Padawans](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") was [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") male who eventually left the Order and fell to the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force"), becoming the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Tyranus, who served as [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious")' right-hand man in the orchestration of the Clone Wars.[[19]](#cite_note-DJL-19) When Sidious, publicly [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") Palpatine, revealed himself and issued [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), which killed most Jedi across the galaxy, Yoda was one of the few survivors, returning to [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") and [dueling](/wiki/Duel_in_the_Galactic_Senate "Duel in the Galactic Senate") the self-declared [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor"); however, Yoda was forced to retreat.[[3]](#cite_note-Revenge_of_the_Sith-3) Yoda subsequently went into hiding on the remote planet [Dagobah](/wiki/Dagobah "Dagobah"), eventually training one final student, [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), during the [Galactic Civil War](/wiki/Galactic_Civil_War "Galactic Civil War") to overthrow Sidious' [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[1]](#cite_note-The_Empire_Strikes_Back-1) Yoda died on Dagobah shortly before the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor"), afterwards becoming a [Force spirit](/wiki/Force_spirit "Force spirit").[[20]](#cite_note-ROTJ-20)

### Yaddle of the Jedi Council

:   "*Yaddle is known for being very kind and quiet, but on the other hand possesses some of the deadliest Force powers.*"
:   ―An artist, in [his journal](/wiki/Unidentified_artist%27s_journal "Unidentified artist's journal")[[21]](#cite_note-Alien_Archive-21)

 [![Yaddle served on the Jedi High Council at the same time as Yoda.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/YaddleJediCouncil.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160615200615) [![Yaddle served on the Jedi High Council at the same time as Yoda.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/YaddleJediCouncil.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160615200615)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/YaddleJediCouncil.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160615200615) 

Yaddle served on the Jedi High Council at the same time as Yoda.

 

During the same period of time, one other member of the species, Yaddle, was also a member of the Jedi Order.[[2]](#cite_note-Databank_YAD-2) Born in [509 BBY](/wiki/509_BBY "509 BBY"),[[22]](#cite_note-Yaddle_Age-22) she was a [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") and member of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") during the [Invasion of Naboo](/wiki/Invasion_of_Naboo "Invasion of Naboo").[[23]](#cite_note-The_Phantom_Menace-23) She trained at least one Padawan, [Oppo Rancisis](/wiki/Oppo_Rancisis "Oppo Rancisis").[[24]](#cite_note-Character_Encyclopedia-24) After the [Battle of Naboo](/wiki/Battle_of_Naboo "Battle of Naboo"), Yaddle decided to take a less active role in Jedi affairs[[25]](#cite_note-USW-25) and stepped down from the Council.[[2]](#cite_note-Databank_YAD-2) Yaddle then pursued [Count of Serenno](/wiki/Count_of_Serenno "Count of Serenno") and former Jedi Master [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") after observing suspicious behavior on his part. She ended up [encountering](/wiki/Duel_in_the_Works "Duel in the Works") the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), who ordered the [count](/wiki/Count "Count") to kill the Jedi. Yaddle was subsequently defeated and murdered by Dooku, another step on his path to the dark side.[[26]](#cite_note-The_Sith_Lord-26)

### Din Grogu, the Mandalorian foundling

:   "*He was raised at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. Many Masters trained him over the years. At the end of the Clone Wars when the Empire rose to power, he was hidden. Someone took him from the Temple. Then his memory becomes dark. He seemed lost. Alone.*"
:   ―Ahsoka Tano feeling Grogu's thoughts[[13]](#cite_note-The_Jedi-13)

Around [41 BBY](/wiki/41_BBY "41 BBY"),[[27]](#cite_note-Child_Age-27) Grogu was born. At some point before [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY"), he was raised at the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on Coruscant, where many masters trained him over the years until the end of the Clone Wars when the Galactic Empire rose to power.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Jedi-13) By [9 ABY](/wiki/9_ABY "9 ABY"), Grogu was kept in a [compound](/wiki/Nikto_hideout "Nikto hideout") on the planet [Arvala-7](/wiki/Arvala-7 "Arvala-7"), guarded by a [faction](/wiki/Unidentified_Nikto_mercenary_gang "Unidentified Nikto mercenary gang") of [Nikto](/wiki/Nikto "Nikto") [mercenaries](/wiki/Mercenary "Mercenary") against various [bounty hunters](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") sent after him. Eventually, [Din Djarin](/wiki/Din_Djarin "Din Djarin") and [IG-11](/wiki/IG-11 "IG-11") [defeated](/wiki/Mission_to_Arvala-7 "Mission to Arvala-7") the mercenaries, with Djarin leaving with Grogu after destroying the [assassin droid](/wiki/Assassin_droid "Assassin droid"), whose instructions had been to kill the infant while Djarin's had been to take him alive if possible.[[5]](#cite_note-Chapter_1-5) Although he initially handed Grogu over to his client, Djarin had a change of heart and chose to return and rescue him,[[28]](#cite_note-The_Sin-28) placing both him and Grogu in the crosshairs of an [Imperial remnant](/wiki/Gideon%27s_Imperial_remnant "Gideon's Imperial remnant") headed by the war criminal [Moff](/wiki/Moff "Moff") [Gideon](/wiki/Gideon "Gideon"), who wanted Grogu for his own purposes.[[8]](#cite_note-The_Reckoning-8) After prevailing in an initial [battle](/wiki/Clash_on_Nevarro "Clash on Nevarro") against Gideon's forces, Djarin, now acting as Grogu's father by [Mandalorian Creed](/wiki/Mandalorian_Creed "Mandalorian Creed"), was charged with protecting Grogu until he could get him to either his own people or a Jedi.[[29]](#cite_note-Redemption-29)

 [![Grogu was a foundling discovered by the Mandalorian bounty hunter Din Djarin.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/1e/Child-Mythosaur-TMS1C8.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200305071645) [![Grogu was a foundling discovered by the Mandalorian bounty hunter Din Djarin.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/1e/Child-Mythosaur-TMS1C8.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20200305071645)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/1e/Child-Mythosaur-TMS1C8.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200305071645) 

Grogu was a foundling discovered by the Mandalorian bounty hunter Din Djarin.

 

After many adventures, Din Djarin finally found [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano"), a former Jedi [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"). However, Ahsoka refused to train Grogu because of his feelings of anger, and fear, and his attachment to Djarin. Ahsoka then told Djarin to go to the ruins of an [ancient temple](/wiki/Force-henge "Force-henge") on [Tython](/wiki/Tython "Tython").[[13]](#cite_note-The_Jedi-13) On Tython, Grogu reached out with the Force while members of Gideon's forces attempted to capture him. He was eventually [captured](/wiki/Battle_of_Tython_(9_ABY) "Battle of Tython (9 ABY)") by Gideon's [Dark Troopers](/wiki/Phase-III_Dark_Trooper "Phase-III Dark Trooper"), and brought aboard his [light cruiser](/wiki/Gideon%27s_light_cruiser "Gideon's light cruiser").[[30]](#cite_note-The_Tragedy-30) He was later [rescued](/wiki/Second_rescue_of_Grogu "Second rescue of Grogu") by Djarin and the timely intervention of Luke Skywalker.[[31]](#cite_note-The_Rescue-31)

## Yoda's species in the galaxy

:   "*Is he your brother?*"  
    "*Hush, child. He is not. He is Master Yoda, a great teacher.*"
:   ―[Cippa Tarko](/wiki/Cippa_Tarko "Cippa Tarko"), questioning Yaddle's relationship to Yoda.[[14]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-14)

The species, its origins shrouded in mystery,[[17]](#cite_note-Card_Trader-17) was rarely seen, with only a few members known to the wider galaxy.[[7]](#cite_note-Complete_Visual_New-7) All known members of the species active in the galaxy were [Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") users and affiliated with the Jedi.[[2]](#cite_note-Databank_YAD-2)[[11]](#cite_note-Databank_YOD-11)[[12]](#cite_note-Chapter_2-12)

## Behind the scenes

:   "*So he's a mystery character. He's a magical character. He has no background. He comes and he goes. He's the subversive secret mysterious stranger that enters the film and then exits at the end.*"
:   ―George Lucas, about Yoda[[32]](#cite_note-Unscripted-32)

*[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* creator [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas") chose to keep the name and background of Yoda's species a mystery.[[32]](#cite_note-Unscripted-32) Both Yoda and [Yaddle](/wiki/Yaddle "Yaddle")'s [Databank](/wiki/Databank_(2014-) "Databank (2014-)") entries on [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") list their species as "Unknown."[[2]](#cite_note-Databank_YAD-2)[[11]](#cite_note-Databank_YOD-11) Interviewed for [*The Clone Wars*: Season Three](/wiki/The_Clone_Wars:_Season_Three "The Clone Wars: Season Three"), Supervising Director [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni") confirmed the show had no plans to reveal [Yoda's homeworld](/wiki/Yoda%27s_homeworld "Yoda's homeworld"), further stating that he believed the world would never actually be seen in any story. To Filoni, giving such an answer would ruin a major part of the fun of Yoda's character: the mystery of his origins.[[33]](#cite_note-Third_Wave-33)

In the documentary "From Puppets to Pixels," available on the DVD bonus disc of [*Star Wars*: Episode II *Attack of the Clones*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_II_Attack_of_the_Clones "Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones"), Lucas joked that Yoda was "the illegitimate child of [Kermit the Frog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kermit_the_Frog "wikipedia:Kermit the Frog") and [Miss Piggy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miss_Piggy "wikipedia:Miss Piggy")."[[34]](#cite_note-FPTP-34)

In *[The Empire Strikes Back: So You Want to Be a Jedi?](/wiki/The_Empire_Strikes_Back:_So_You_Want_to_Be_a_Jedi%3F "The Empire Strikes Back: So You Want to Be a Jedi?")*, a [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") retelling of [*Star Wars*: Episode V *The Empire Strikes Back*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_V_The_Empire_Strikes_Back "Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back") written by [Adam Gidwitz](/wiki/Adam_Gidwitz "Adam Gidwitz"), Luke Skywalker calls Yoda a "little swamp frog."[[35]](#cite_note-wannabe-35) In the [2019](/wiki/2019 "2019") canon audio drama *[Dooku: Jedi Lost](/wiki/Dooku:_Jedi_Lost "Dooku: Jedi Lost")*, the young [noble](/wiki/Noble "Noble") [Ramil](/wiki/Ramil "Ramil") claims that Yoda looked like a [slime-gnome](/wiki/Slime-gnome "Slime-gnome").[[19]](#cite_note-DJL-19)

[Maz Kanata](/wiki/Maz_Kanata "Maz Kanata"), one of the main characters of the *Star Wars* [sequel trilogy](/wiki/Sequel_trilogy "Sequel trilogy"), was originally sketched by [Industrial Light & Magic](/wiki/Industrial_Light_%26_Magic "Industrial Light & Magic") concept artist [Christian Alzmann](/wiki/Christian_Alzmann "Christian Alzmann") as a member of Yoda's species around [March](/wiki/March "March") [2013](/wiki/2013 "2013"). [Three months later](/wiki/May "May"), Alzmann redesigned the character as hunched, green alien with a headdress and, [one month later](/wiki/June "June"), redesigned her face to be bug-eyed and snub-nosed, distancing her from Yoda's design.[[36]](#cite_note-Art-36)
