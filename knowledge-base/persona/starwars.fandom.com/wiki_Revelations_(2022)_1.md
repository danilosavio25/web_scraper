## Plot summary[]

### Meeting With The Eye[]

:   "*Can you see my **pain?**"  
    "I see your pain. **Caring** about it is another matter.*"
:   ―The Eye of Webbish Bog and Darth Vader[[2]](#cite_note-Revelations-2)

On the planet [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar"), [the Eye of Webbish Bog](/wiki/Eye_of_Webbish_Bog "Eye of Webbish Bog") calls to [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") through [The Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"). Meeting with The Eye, he asks Vader what he sees. The Eye shows him the [Handmaidens](/wiki/Naboo_Royal_Handmaidens "Naboo Royal Handmaidens"), standing against the dark lord, but also [Sabé](/wiki/Sab%C3%A9 "Sabé") becoming a servant of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") aboard the [Second Death Star](/wiki/DS-2_Death_Star_II_Mobile_Battle_Station "DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station"). The Eye explains that these visions are of the future, presented to him by [the Dark Side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force"), but they are all potential futures. Vader sees back to a mission on [Golatha](/wiki/Golatha "Golatha"), during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), where himself (as Anakin Skywalker) fought alongside [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and the [326th Clone Battalion](/wiki/Company_326 "Company 326"), before being ambushed by several [Battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid"). Unamused by the riddles of the Eye of Webbish Bog, Vader begins to leave the [Sirroning](/wiki/Sirroning "Sirroning"), but is lured back in by another potential future.

### Visions of the Future[]

:   "*The Fermata Cage was a **trap** created by an Ancient Sith Lord. You know his name, in fact. He designed your fortress. The cage was used to set people, even places, outside time. They could then be recalled to the now as if no time had passed. There would be no greater threat to us. To what we have **built together**.*"
:   ―Darth Sidious to Darth Vader[[2]](#cite_note-Revelations-2)

On [Kligson's Moon](/wiki/Kligson%27s_Moon "Kligson's Moon"), the [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") [Ajax Sigma](/wiki/Ajax_Sigma "Ajax Sigma") was constructed and dared to lead a [droid uprising](/wiki/Kligson%27s_Moon_Uprising "Kligson's Moon Uprising") for freedom. However, [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") of the [High Republic](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era") were sent and struck down the droid. However, his [Neural core](/wiki/Neural_core "Neural core") was preserved and passed down hand to hand, until landing in the hands of [Han Solo](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo") and [Chewbacca](/wiki/Chewbacca "Chewbacca"). Han and Chewbacca bury the Neural Core, but in time, a droid would find and dig up the core to return to his followers on [Mechis III](/wiki/Mechis_III "Mechis III"). Ajax Sigma's followers construct a new body for the droid to spread his freedom once more. Meanwhile, the [Spark Eternal](/wiki/Spark_Eternal "Spark Eternal") continues to possess the body of [Doctor Chelli Aphra](/wiki/Chelli_Lona_Aphra "Chelli Lona Aphra"), but in response, she has devised a plan to control that which controls her. Fed up with the visions, Vader grows inpatient and throws his [lightsaber](/wiki/Darth_Vader%27s_lightsaber "Darth Vader's lightsaber") at The Eye, but the powerful creature stops the weapon and returns it to Vader. The Eye tells Vader that the Spark Eternal has an intertwined fate with the lost [Fermata Cage](/wiki/Fermata_Cage "Fermata Cage").

In the future vision, Vader meets with [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), noticeably having damaged his helmet, informing his apprentice that [Qi'ra](/wiki/Qi%27ra "Qi'ra") has reported to have found the Fermata Cage. Fearful of what this could bring, Vader is tasked with retrieving the device and let nothing stand in his way. Later in time, Vader once again communes with Darth Sidious about the Fermata Cage on his [Star Destroyer](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer"), the [Executor](/wiki/Executor "Executor"). Sidious explains that the device was created by the same [Ancient Sith Lord](/wiki/Darth_Momin "Darth Momin") who designed [Vader's fortress](/wiki/Fortress_Vader "Fortress Vader"), and has the abilities to set people in time and bring them to the present. In a different vision, Vader meets with [Lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") [Jyala Haydenn](/wiki/Jyala_Haydenn "Jyala Haydenn") to discuss the permanent termination of [cyborg](/wiki/Cyborg "Cyborg") [Beilert Valance](/wiki/Beilert_Valance "Beilert Valance"), since he has become a significant liability to the Dark Lord. He requests the best trackers available to the Empire. Haydenn puts in a request for the infamous [Inferno Squad](/wiki/Inferno_Squad "Inferno Squad") to complete Lord Vader's request. Meanwhile, on the [Edgehawk](/wiki/Edgehawk_(starship) "Edgehawk (starship)"), Valance is fighting with [4-LOM](/wiki/4-LOM "4-LOM") and [Zuckuss](/wiki/Zuckuss "Zuckuss"), who appears to be blasted by the cyborg. [Bossk](/wiki/Bossk%27wassak%27Cradossk "Bossk'wassak'Cradossk") attempts to subdue the cyborg, but is swiftly thwarted away. [T'onga](/wiki/T%27onga "T'onga") approaches Valance unarmed, attempting to communicate with the supposedly mind controlled cyborg.

Elsewhere, [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") feels a [disturbance in the Force](/wiki/Disturbance_in_the_Force "Disturbance in the Force"), explaining to [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo") that the feeling was similar to when [Alderaan](/wiki/Alderaan "Alderaan") was [destroyed](/wiki/Destruction_of_Alderaan "Destruction of Alderaan"). Aboard the [Millennium Falcon](/wiki/Millennium_Falcon "Millennium Falcon"), Luke directs [Lando Calrissian](/wiki/Landonis_Balthazar_Calrissian "Landonis Balthazar Calrissian") and Chewbacca to the location of an undocumented planet. Discovering a battlefield littered with the corpses of droids and the remains of a broken lightsaber. Suspecting that the droids were the results of a battle during the Clone Wars, [R2-D2](/wiki/R2-D2 "R2-D2") and [C-3PO](/wiki/C-3PO "C-3PO") disregard this theory since they hold no record of it. The battle, which took place one hundred years ago, instead was between droids and people, who have since decayed, but this tragedy was not what Luke sensed, leading them to an old [Homesteader ship](/wiki/Homesteader_ship "Homesteader ship"). Inside the ship, Luke and Lando discover several bodies, appearing to have been slaughtered. R2-D2 connects into the Homesteader and discovers that this is Kligson's Moon that had been abandoned for many years. The group decide it best to split up and search in pairs to cover more ground. Luke is confronted by Ajax Sigma who returned to the planet to retrieve something for himself, Luke does not believe that the people aboard the Homesteader would attack a droid, he ignites his [lightsaber](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker%27s_yellow_lightsaber "Luke Skywalker's yellow lightsaber") to force Ajax to answer for the massacre he created. Fighting with Skywalker, Sigma is damaged and refuses to surrender to the Jedi. He escapes the Homesteader in an [escape pod](/wiki/Escape_pod "Escape pod").

### Always in Motion[]

:   "*This **Scourge** comes for the entire Galaxy. Even Darth Vader, Dark Lord of the Sith, is part of the Galaxy.*"
:   ―The Eye of Webbish Bog to Darth Vader[[2]](#cite_note-Revelations-2)

The Eye explains these revelations to be warnings of the end of the Force and everything, but the Sirroning's mind is clouded by pain. Vader sees a canister poisoning the eye, and is suddenly attacked by [Mustafarians](/wiki/Southern_Mustafarian "Southern Mustafarian"). Vader easily defeats the majority of them, before having his helmet struck by another. The Dark Lord uses the Force to lower the attacker into the lava surrounding them. The Eye concludes this meeting, telling Vader that the scourge will come for the entire galaxy and bring about the end of everything he knows. Later, Vader returns to his castle and meets with his Master, Darth Sidious, experiencing events similar to a vision The Eye of Webbish Bog showed him. Sidious informs him that Lady Qi'ra of the [Crimson Dawn](/wiki/Crimson_Dawn "Crimson Dawn") has discovered the lost Fermata Cage, he wants his Apprentice to bring it to him and let nothing stand in his way.
