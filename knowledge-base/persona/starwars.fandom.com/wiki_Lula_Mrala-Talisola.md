**Lula Mrala-Talisola**, known as **Lula Talisola** before her [marriage](/wiki/Mrala-Talisola_Wedding "Mrala-Talisola Wedding"), was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") who lived on [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") before joining the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") and being [trained](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") under [Jedi Masters](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), [Torban Buck](/wiki/Torban_Buck "Torban Buck"), and [Kantam Sy](/wiki/Kantam_Sy "Kantam Sy"). Talisola trained aboard the [Padawan Academic Cruiser](/wiki/Padawan_Academic_Cruiser "Padawan Academic Cruiser") *[Star Hopper](/wiki/Star_Hopper "Star Hopper")* during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era") and [Nihil conflict](/wiki/Nihil_conflict "Nihil conflict"), and she was the best friend of fellow Padawans [Farzala Tarabal](/wiki/Farzala_Tarabal "Farzala Tarabal") and [Qort](/wiki/Qort "Qort"), often looking out for them.

When the Padawans of the *Star Hopper* were sent to respond to an [Emergence](/wiki/Trymant_IV_disaster "Trymant IV disaster") in the [Trymant system](/wiki/Trymant_system "Trymant system") after the [Great Hyperspace Disaster](/wiki/Great_Hyperspace_Disaster "Great Hyperspace Disaster"), Talisola met the [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") resident [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola") during a [confrontation](/wiki/Attack_on_Trymant_IV "Attack on Trymant IV") with a group of [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [pirates](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate"). The Nihil threatened the people of [Bralanak City](/wiki/Bralanak_City "Bralanak City"), and the Jedi were able to fight off the Nihil with the help of Mrala, whom they welcomed back to [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon"), where the two grew closer, and even meditated together.

Soon after, the Nihil were spotted on the junk moon of [Quantxi](/wiki/Quantxi "Quantxi"). During a bit of deliberation, Mrala decided to depart on her own to find her friend [Krix Kamerat](/wiki/Krix_Kamerat "Krix Kamerat"), who had joined the Nihil, and Talisola and her friends followed. Planetside, the Padawans once again found themselves facing scores of Nihil raiders while defending a [Junk Maven](/wiki/Junk_Mavens "Junk Mavens") campsite on the moon. Talisola split off from the other two to search for Mrala, and tracked Kamerat's location to find her and her former friend facing off, Mrala choking Kamerat with [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"). Talisola quickly intervened, but Kamerat was rescued by [Marchion Ro](/wiki/Marchion_Ro "Marchion Ro"). The Jedi attempted to prevent the Nihil's [Spider Cruiser](/wiki/Spider_Cruiser "Spider Cruiser") from escaping, but were unsuccessful. Afterwards, Talisola reunited with her Padawan friends, happy they were okay.

During a trip back to Trymant IV, Talisola felt envious of the progress [Vernestra Rwoh](/wiki/Vernestra_Rwoh "Vernestra Rwoh") had made as a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") at a similar age to her. The Jedi touched down on the now-damaged world, and met with [Elder](/wiki/Elders "Elders") [Barbatash](/wiki/Barbatash "Barbatash"), who stopped a fight from breaking out with some locals. A conversation with Barbatash lead the Jedi to [Vrant Tarnum](/wiki/Vrant_Tarnum "Vrant Tarnum"), where Talisola and Rwoh descended to search the rocky planet. They found many Nihil ships moving in formation, and eventually departing the hidden base with their [Path engines](/wiki/Path_engine "Path engine"). Talisola received news that the Nihil were planning to attack [Valo](/wiki/Valo "Valo"), where the [Republic Fair](/wiki/Republic_Fair "Republic Fair") was being held, but [Crashpoint Tower](/wiki/Crashpoint_Tower "Crashpoint Tower") had been disabled, so they couldn't be warned. The Jedi soon departed for Valo.

On the way to Valo, Talisola confided her insecurities in Rwoh, and was reassured of her timing and the pace of her journey. Exiting hyperspace, they found [Lonisa City](/wiki/Lonisa_City "Lonisa City") under Nihil attack, and joined the fight while trying to contact Starlight. Without a response, they descended to the ground and continued the fight there. Working with [Ty Yorrick](/wiki/Ty_Yorrick "Ty Yorrick") and meeting [Ram Jomaram](/wiki/Ram_Jomaram "Ram Jomaram") and some [Bonbraks](/wiki/Bonbrak "Bonbrak"), they rush to Crashpoint Tower. Fighting the Nihil, a [Hragscythe](/wiki/Hragscythe "Hragscythe"), and [Drengir](/wiki/Drengir "Drengir") along the way, on [speeder bike](/wiki/Speeder_bike "Speeder bike"), on [Sanval](/wiki/Sanval "Sanval"), and on foot, the two made it to the Tower. There, Talisola would fight several Nihil ships in the air and on the ground, but was eventually saved by [V-18](/wiki/V-18 "V-18"), though Talisola was wounded in the arm during the fight. After Jomaram repaired the Tower's communications, he asks to join Mrala and Talisola on their future journies, and they accept. Soon after, the group continued to fight the Nihil in the streets of Lonisa City.

After the [attack](/wiki/Valo_Atrocity "Valo Atrocity") on the Republic Fair, the Padawans reunited on Starlight, where Talisola met [Ishnar Ti-Kharatal](/wiki/Ishnar_Ti-Kharatal "Ishnar Ti-Kharatal"), and introduced Qort and Tarabal to Jomaram. After a discussion with their and the Padawans departed for [Takodana](/wiki/Takodana "Takodana") to help defend the [temple](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_(Takodana) "Jedi Temple (Takodana)") there from incoming Nihil attacks. During preparations, two Nihil double agents planted [thermal detonators](/wiki/Thermal_detonator "Thermal detonator") and blew up the temple, as Nihil starfighters approached. Talisola and the others were saved from the attack after Qort's [vonduun crab](/wiki/Vonduun_crab "Vonduun crab") mask was broken in the destruction and he unleashed his unrestricted natural abilities, driving Kamerat and [his Nihil](/wiki/Krix_Kamerat%27s_cell "Krix Kamerat's cell") away.

Back on Starlight, Talisola and the other Padawans participated in the [Great Jedi Rumble Race](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Rumble_Race "Great Jedi Rumble Race") held by masters Buck and Sy, and later was part of a task force to find and capture Kamerat for good. [Bibs](/wiki/Bibs "Bibs") found Kamerat's Nihil encampment on [Dol'har Hyde](/wiki/Dol%27har_Hyde "Dol'har Hyde"), and the Padawans descended upon the world to finally [capture](/wiki/Battle_at_Dol%27har_Hyde "Battle at Dol'har Hyde") Kamerat in the depths of his base. With Kamerat imprisoned on the *Starhopper*, Talisola was presented with the opportunity to be [knighted](/wiki/Knighting_Ceremony "Knighting Ceremony"). After reflecting on, accepting, and finally expressing her love for Mrala, Talisola declined the opportunity to be knighted, but was happy for her two best friends as they became Jedi Knights. By a [year](/wiki/229_BBY "229 BBY") after the [destruction of Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon"), an amnesiac Talisola began operating as a Nihil [warlord](/wiki/Warlord "Warlord") known as **Tartak Vil** on the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Bracront](/wiki/Bracront "Bracront"). After regaining her memories and choosing to leave the Jedi, Talisola would marry Mrala.

## Biography[]

### Early life[]

:   "*I left home so long ago I don't remember it. But I found a new home with my friends here.*"
:   ―Lula Talisola[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12)

Lula Talisola, a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender"),[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12) [born](/wiki/Reproduction "Reproduction")[[13]](#cite_note-Lightsaber_Collection-13) [on](/wiki/Naboo_(people) "Naboo (people)")[[1]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_7-1) the [Mid Rim](/wiki/Mid_Rim_Territories "Mid Rim Territories") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet")[[14]](#cite_note-Timelines-14) [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo").[[1]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_7-1) During her youth, Talisola came to live in an [orphanage](/wiki/Unidentified_orphanage_(Naboo) "Unidentified orphanage (Naboo)") on Naboo. Eventually, she was taken to the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order")'s [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_(Endovar) "Jedi Temple (Endovar)") on [Endovar](/wiki/Endovar "Endovar") by former [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Kantam Sy](/wiki/Kantam_Sy "Kantam Sy"), who returned to the Order in order to [train](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") Talisola.[[15]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-15) Due to her young age, Talisola did not remember her original home.[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12) She became close friends with two other [Padawans](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"),[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16) [Farzala Tarabal](/wiki/Farzala_Tarabal "Farzala Tarabal")[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12) and [Qort](/wiki/Qort "Qort"),[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16) frequently looking out for them when they got up to mischief.[[17]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Padawans-17) Eventually, Talisola became a member of the class of Padawans aboard the [cruiser](/wiki/Cruiser "Cruiser") *[Star Hopper](/wiki/Star_Hopper "Star Hopper")*, who were supervised by [Jedi Masters](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Torban Buck](/wiki/Torban_Buck "Torban Buck") and [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda").[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

### Battle on Trymant[]

In [232 BBY](/wiki/232_BBY "232 BBY"),[[14]](#cite_note-Timelines-14) the [freighter](/wiki/Freighter "Freighter") *[Legacy Run](/wiki/Legacy_Run "Legacy Run")* was destroyed in [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace") outside of the [Hetzal system](/wiki/Hetzal_system "Hetzal system"). This caused the [Great Hyperspace Disaster](/wiki/Great_Hyperspace_Disaster "Great Hyperspace Disaster"), when the [star system](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") was imperiled by large amounts of hyperspace debris. In the aftermath, further [Emergences](/wiki/Emergences "Emergences") of the debris began occurring throughout the [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories").[[18]](#cite_note-LotJ-18) When [one Emergence](/wiki/Trymant_IV_disaster "Trymant IV disaster") threatened the [Trymant system](/wiki/Trymant_system "Trymant system"), the *Star Hopper* was the only Jedi or [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship") within range, and it diverted to assist.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

As Yoda ordered the Padawans to prepare themselves for whatever awaited them at Trymant, Talisola grew nervous, feeling that her skills were not up to going out in the field yet, even though she had aced all of her exams, as she thought she should have been happy about the situation but was unable to focus. In response to a question from Tarabal, Yoda reminded the apprentices that they could not turn back as they were Trymant's only hope, and Talisola thought that things might go terribly wrong.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

When the *Star Hopper* was about to arrive at Trymant, Yoda and Buck gathered the Padawans and had them report on what they had learned about the situation. Tarabal and Qort reported that it was fallout from the Great Disaster, and Talisola, alongside the [Bravaisian](/wiki/Bravaisian "Bravaisian") [Bibs](/wiki/Bibs "Bibs"), reported that several large chunks of debris were on course to hit [Bralanak City](/wiki/Bralanak_City "Bralanak City"), the most densely populated area of [Trymant IV](/wiki/Trymant_IV "Trymant IV"), the most densely populated planet in the system. Yoda reminded the apprentices to trust in [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), and Buck stated that he and Yoda would be handling the largest pieces of debris, before the Padawans were sent to board their [rescue speeders](/wiki/Rescue_speeder "Rescue speeder"). Taking the helm of a [speeder](/wiki/Speeder "Speeder") with Tarabal as her co-[pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot"), Talisola headed into action. Steering around falling debris, she alarmed her [Zygerrian](/wiki/Zygerrian "Zygerrian") friend after narrowly dodging a large piece in response to a warning from him, grinning.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

Once on the ground, Talisola stepped into a leadership role while working with Tarabal, Qort and Bibs to assist civilians, and began to think that things might actually go well. During their efforts, Tarabal spotted and alerted Talisola to a piece of debris heading for a building with people on its roof, so she told Qort and Bibs to follow with their [hover platform](/wiki/Hover_platform "Hover platform") while she and Tarabal hopped into their rescue speeder. As Talisola flew the speeder, Tarabal managed to shoot down the debris just in the nick of time. Talisola complimented him, before wondering how they were going to evacuate the city, as they needed a large craft the people would be able to fit into. Spotting a [large ship](/wiki/Spider_Cruiser "Spider Cruiser") in the area, Tarabal suggested it to his friend.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

The cruiser belonged to the [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [pirates](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate"), who were there to evacuate [Tromak](/wiki/Tromak "Tromak"), a member of the [Elders of the Path](/wiki/Elders_of_the_Path "Elders of the Path"). The Padawans, led by Talisola, arrived at the [building](/wiki/Meeting_house "Meeting house") where the [spider](/wiki/Spider "Spider")-shaped ship had landed just after Tromak had asked the pirates not to [kill](/wiki/Death "Death") [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola")[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16) and [Krix Kamerat](/wiki/Krix_Kamerat "Krix Kamerat"),[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12) two young members of the group who had been attempting to persuade the pirates to allow the use of their ship as an evacuation vessel. With her [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") already ignited, Talisola politely insisted that the pirates' vessel was required for evacuation immediately. Bibs was concerned that they hadn't asked nicely, and [one pirate](/wiki/Unidentified_orange-skinned_Nihil "Unidentified orange-skinned Nihil") snapped that they were not going to hand over their ship to childen, especially "snivelling" Jedi. As Mrala and Kamerat dove out of the way, a firefight occurred as the Nihil opened fire on the Padawans, who deflected the shots.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

Lula and her friends surrounded by the Nihil

 

The sight of Talisola and her friends fighting awed Mrala, who as a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") in a sect that spurned its use had been trying to hide her powers all her life. Mrala realized that she could be like the Jedi and use her powers for good. After deflecting the first barrage, Talisola retorted to the pirates that she had been "asking nicely" earlier, and Tarabal declared that they would take the ship. As the Padawans leapt forward again, Talisola and Mrala both sensed a large piece of debris heading straight for their position. The two girls, strangers to each other, moved as one, Mrala leaping out to [hold off](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") the debris while Talisola [Force pushed](/wiki/Force_push "Force push") away attacking pirates. The Padawans were shocked at Mrala's actions, but Talisola stepped in to help her when the untrained girl began to lose her hold on the debris, reassuring her that they had her back. A shocked Kamerat was dragged away by Tromak as [another figure](/wiki/Marchion_Ro "Marchion Ro") emerged from the Nihil ship, declaring that the Jedi were right where he wanted them. As the pirates surrounded the Padawans and Mrala, the figure ordered their execution.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

Tarabal asked her what they were going to do, and Talisola declared that they had no choice but to fight. Masters Yoda and Buck shortly arrived to assist the apprentices, and Talisola and her friends were taken aback by the [Chagrian](/wiki/Chagrian "Chagrian")'s dramatic "Buckets of [Blood](/wiki/Blood "Blood")" nickname before leaping into action. As the [battle](/wiki/Attack_on_Trymant_IV "Attack on Trymant IV") raged, Mrala asked the Masters for help, as Kamerat had apparently been captured by the Nihil. Talisola immediately backed her up, telling the Masters how Mrala had helped them by using [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), and offered to sneak around and rescue Kamerat herself. Yoda overruled her and said he would do it himself, telling Buck that Talisola was right about their duty to Mrala when the [Jedi healer](/wiki/Jedi_healer "Jedi healer") expressed concern about his ability to lead the Padawans to safety by himself. In the thick of battle, Tarabal shouted a warning to Talisola and Yoda about a projectile flung in their direction by a [Gigoran](/wiki/Gigoran "Gigoran") pirate, which Yoda swiftly destroyed. Talisola was visibly impressed, and after Yoda headed off with Mrala's pet, [Cham Cham](/wiki/Cham_Cham "Cham Cham"), she reassured Master Buck that the Jedi could handle the situation.[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12)

Talisola led the charge as she, her fellow Padawans, Mrala and Buck waded into battle. In response to Tarabal asking how they were looking, Talisola complimented Mrala's use of telekinesis during the battle, saying she was a natural. Tarabal then spotted Buck, who was in danger of being overwhelmed, and Talisola rallied the Padawans to rescue him, driving the pirates back. Just then, a [Republic rescue fleet](/wiki/Republic_rescue_fleet "Republic rescue fleet") arrived to assist, leading Buck to proclaim that they were saved. In the aftermath, as Republic ships began landing, Talisola and Tarabal asked Buck why he called himself Buckets of Blood, but neither apprentice found his explanation, about keeping blood inside the body, to be very convincing. Just after the Spider Cruiser took off, Talisola and her fellow apprentices were surprised by the arrival of Yoda, who stated it was imperative that the ship be stopped. Returning Cham Cham to Mrala with the news that he may have eaten a pirate's [arm](/wiki/Limb "Limb"), Yoda told her that she had to come to the [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon") with them, and he had not been able to rescue Kamerat because he had not wanted to be rescued. Talisola put a hand on Mrala's shoulder to comfort her. The Nihil ship escaped the system by firing on a [medical frigate](/wiki/Pelta-class_frigate "Pelta-class frigate") as a diversion.[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12)

### A new friend[]

While travelling to the Beacon on the *Star Hopper*, Talisola comforted a crying Mrala, who admitted to her that she had left the only place she had lived that felt like home. Mrala added that she might not have had a true home because those who raised her would not have accepted her for who she really was, and Talisola responded that she had left her home so young that she did not remember it, but had found a new one with her friends. Talisola said that she and her friends had Mrala's back, and Tarabal and Qort backed up her statement. She further assured Mrala, as the cruiser prepared to dock with Starlight, that they were going to the best place in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy").[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12)

For the following [week](/wiki/Standard_week "Standard week"), Talisola and her friends easily incorporated Mrala into their group as they had fun together. At the end of that week, Talisola went to Mrala's room to inform her that the Jedi Masters wanted to speak to her, walking in on her crying as she had just watched a message Kamerat had sent her through paired [holoprojectors](/wiki/Holoprojector "Holoprojector") given to them by Yoda. Talisola asked her what was wrong, and Mrala said she was simply processing the changes in her life before asking the Padawan what she wanted. As Talisola was explaining, however, Masters Yoda, Buck, Sy, [Estala Maru](/wiki/Estala_Maru "Estala Maru") and [Avar Kriss](/wiki/Avar_Kriss "Avar Kriss") arrived, Sy asking if they were interrupting. Kriss said that they wanted to talk to Mrala about her future plans.[[19]](#cite_note-HRA_3-19)

During the Masters' discussion, Maru pointed out that Mrala was too old to begin Jedi training, but Kriss noted that they did not know what conditions were like on Trymant IV and Sy argued that Mrala got along well with the apprentices, including Talisola. The topic led Talisola to interrupt, suggesting that they should ask Mrala what she wanted to do as her opinion was the most important, and Tarabal backed her up, adding that they also ask the various Padawans and [younglings](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") Mrala had befriended, whose opinions were second most important.[[19]](#cite_note-HRA_3-19)

### Attack on Valo[]

Talisola returned to Trymant IV with Zeen Mrala and Kantam Sy, where they spoke with Elder [Barbatash](/wiki/Barbatash "Barbatash") about the whereabouts of Elder Tromak. This led the Jedi to [Vrant Tarnum](/wiki/Vrant_Tarnum "Vrant Tarnum"), where they encountered Nihil ships. They received a transmission from [Ram Jomaram](/wiki/Ram_Jomaram "Ram Jomaram") warning about Nihil sabotage on Valo. Talisola and Vernestra Rwoh traveled to Valo, where they found the Nihil attacking the [Republic Fair](/wiki/Republic_Fair "Republic Fair"). Lula met Jomaram and journeyed to [Crashpoint Tower](/wiki/Crashpoint_Tower "Crashpoint Tower") with him in order to restore the planet's communications.[[20]](#cite_note-The_High_Republic:_Race_to_Crashpoint_Tower-20)

### Fall of Starlight[]

In [230 BBY](/wiki/230_BBY "230 BBY"),[[14]](#cite_note-Timelines-14) Talisola participated in a [battle at Dol'har Hyde](/wiki/Battle_at_Dol%27har_Hyde "Battle at Dol'har Hyde"), which resulted in the capture of Kamerat. Talisola accompanied Mrala aboard the *Star Hopper* to take Kamerat back to Starlight Beacon to be imprisoned, though on the way back to the station, Mrala was called to [Corellia](/wiki/Corellia "Corellia") to assist other Jedi who were [investigating](/wiki/Battle_of_the_Corellian_Shipyards "Battle of the Corellian Shipyards") a recent attack. Talisola ensured her friend that would she make sure the Nihil was delivered to Starlight. A short time later, the station was [destroyed](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon") by the Nihil. In the first official reports from the scene of the destruction, Talisola, along with Buck, Tarabal, and many others, were said to be missing and presumed dead. Mrala, however, felt that she knew Talisola was alive, as she would have would have felt it if she had died. With this, Mrala made an oath that she would do whatever it took to find her.[[15]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-15)

### Survival[]

Talisola ultimately survived the fall. In the [year](/wiki/229_BBY "229 BBY") after Starlight's destruction, an amnesiac Talisola had begun operating as[[7]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_1-7) a rogue[[21]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_3-21) Nihil [warlord](/wiki/Warlord "Warlord") known as "Tartak Vil" until she was reunited with Mrala on the moon [Bracront](/wiki/Bracront "Bracront").[[7]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_1-7)

## Personality and traits[]

:   "*Masters, we have to help her rescue her friend! She saved our lives!* She used the Force!"
:   ―Lula Talisola advocates helping Zeen Mrala rescue her friend[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12)

 [![Lula Talisola](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/fe/Lula_Talisola.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20201224000403) [![Lula Talisola](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/fe/Lula_Talisola.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20201224000403)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/fe/Lula_Talisola.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20201224000403) 

Lula Talisola

 

Lula Talisola was a human female with [brown](/wiki/Color "Color") skin, brown [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye") and dark brown [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair") styled in many [braids](/wiki/Braid "Braid"). She stood 1.65 [meters](/wiki/Meter "Meter") (5 [ft](/wiki/Foot "Foot"), 5 in) tall.[[5]](#cite_note-Character_Encyclopedia-5) She was intelligent and skilled, but was unsure of herself due to her skills never having been tried, and was nervous about her first mission on Trymant IV, although she did not show her nervousness externally.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16) She frequently looked out for her best friends, Farzala and Qort, when they got up to mischief.[[17]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Padawans-17) When she did go into action on Trymant IV, she naturally fitted into a leadership role with her fellow apprentices. When approaching the Nihil to request the use of their ship, she considered insisting on it first was "asking nicely" before resorting to violence after being fired upon. She also reassured Zeen Mrala that she had her back when helping the girl, who was a complete stranger, hold back falling hyperspace debris.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

Talisola was also quick to advocate that the Jedi had a duty to help Mrala rescue her friend Krix Kamerat, since she had helped them. She also comforted the [Mikkian](/wiki/Mikkian "Mikkian") after she had been separated from Kamerat in the battle's aftermath, reassuring her that she and her friends would support Mrala if she needed it.[[12]](#cite_note-HRA_2-12) She developed romantic feelings for Mrala, which were difficult for her to process because of the Jedi Order's rules on attachment. As such, despite it having been her life's dream up to that point, she requested to not be made a Jedi Knight when the chance arose, hoping to take more time to understand herself.[[11]](#cite_note-HRA_13-11)

## Powers and abilities[]

Lula Talisola had strong [Force powers](/wiki/Force_power "Force power"), being the top of her class in all subjects. She was skilled in [lightsaber combat](/wiki/Lightsaber_combat "Lightsaber combat"), being able to expertly deflect blaster bolts fired at her. She was also capable of telekinesis, being able to assist Zeen Mrala in holding back a large piece of falling debris and Force-push multiple individuals simultaneously. Talisola could also expertly pilot a rescue speeder.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16) During the year she spent without her memories she became experienced in the use of the [Vibrosword](/wiki/Vibrosword "Vibrosword"), the [Double-bladed vibrosword](/wiki/Double-bladed_vibrosword "Double-bladed vibrosword") and the blaster.[[21]](#cite_note-HRA_2023_3-21)

## Equipment[]

Lula Talisola wore brown-and-ivory [Jedi robes](/wiki/Jedi_apparel "Jedi apparel") with golden accents and a blue sash. She wielded a blue-bladed [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber"). She piloted a rescue speeder while assisting in the Trymant IV rescue effort.[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)

## Behind the scenes[]

Lula Talisola was first revealed in the announcement of the *[Star Wars: The High Republic](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic "Star Wars: The High Republic")* multimedia project.[[22]](#cite_note-Announcement-22) Her last name was first revealed in a [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") article on Padawan characters within the project.[[17]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Padawans-17) She made her first appearance in the comic book [*The High Republic Adventures* (2021) 1](/wiki/The_High_Republic_Adventures_(2021)_1 "The High Republic Adventures (2021) 1").[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_1-16)
