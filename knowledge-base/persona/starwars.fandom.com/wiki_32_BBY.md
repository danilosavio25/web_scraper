## Events[]

* [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala") is [elected](/wiki/Coronation_of_Queen_Amidala "Coronation of Queen Amidala") [Queen](/wiki/Monarch_of_Naboo "Monarch of Naboo") of [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo").[[8]](#cite_note-SWP-8)
* Amidala hosts the [Chommell sector summit](/wiki/Chommell_sector_summit "Chommell sector summit") for the leaders of other [planets](/wiki/Planet "Planet") in the [Chommell sector](/wiki/Chommell_sector "Chommell sector"), which, unbeknownst to her, leads [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") to accelerate his plans regarding Naboo.[[9]](#cite_note-Queen's_Peril-9)
* The [Trade Federation](/wiki/Trade_Federation "Trade Federation") [invades](/wiki/Invasion_of_Naboo "Invasion of Naboo") Naboo.[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") wins the [Boonta Eve Classic](/wiki/Boonta_Eve_Classic "Boonta Eve Classic"), thus securing his freedom.[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* After 1,000 [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") operating in secret, the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") make their presence known to the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order").[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") Sheev Palpatine is elected [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"), replacing [Finis Valorum](/wiki/Finis_Valorum "Finis Valorum"),[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1) thus beginning the [downfall](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic").[[8]](#cite_note-SWP-8)

Obi-Wan Kenobi and Darth Maul face each other on Naboo in 32 BBY.

 

* The [Battle of Naboo](/wiki/Battle_of_Naboo "Battle of Naboo") occurs:[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
  + The Trade Federation's occupation of Naboo is overthrown.[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
  + [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") kills [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn"), but Maul himself is then bested by [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi").[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1) Maul survives but is presumed dead.[[10]](#cite_note-Brothers-10)
* Kenobi is promoted to the rank of [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight"). Anakin Skywalker is assigned as Kenobi's [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") learner.[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* [Doctor](/wiki/Doctor "Doctor") [Nuvo Vindi](/wiki/Nuvo_Vindi "Nuvo Vindi") disappears and does not resurface for another [ten years](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY").[[11]](#cite_note-Blue_Shadow_Virus-11)
* [Supreme Chancellor Election](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor_election,_32_BBY "Supreme Chancellor election, 32 BBY")[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* [Funeral of Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Funeral_of_Qui-Gon_Jinn "Funeral of Qui-Gon Jinn")[[1]](#cite_note-TPM-1)
* [Yaddle](/wiki/Yaddle "Yaddle") and [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") have a [duel](/wiki/Duel_in_the_Works "Duel in the Works") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), with Dooku being victorious and completely falling to the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force").[[12]](#cite_note-The_Sith_Lord-12) Dooku replaces Maul as Sidious's [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice").[[13]](#cite_note-The_Lawless-13) Dooku is given the name of Darth Tyranus.[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14)
* Dooku hires the [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett") to serve as the [clone template](/wiki/Clone_template "Clone template"), the [Kaminoans](/wiki/Kaminoan "Kaminoan") began creating [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") on [Kamino](/wiki/Kamino "Kamino").[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14)[[15]](#cite_note-AORJF-15)
* The first generation of [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") are created on [Kamino](/wiki/Kamino "Kamino").[[16]](#cite_note-Rex_birth-16)
