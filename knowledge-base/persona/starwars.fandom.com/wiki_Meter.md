The **meter** was an established unit of measurement of length. [Starfighter](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") [pilots](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") and their [targeting computers](/wiki/Targeting_computer "Targeting computer") used meters.[[1]](#cite_note-ANH-1) Species like the [Ewoks](/wiki/Ewok "Ewok"),[[2]](#cite_note-EncyclopediaE-2) the [Lurmen](/wiki/Lurmen "Lurmen"),[[3]](#cite_note-EncyclopediaL-3) and the [Jawas](/wiki/Jawa "Jawa") all had an average height of one meter,[[4]](#cite_note-EncyclopediaJ-4) while the [armored](/wiki/Armor "Armor") [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") was slightly taller than two meters.[[5]](#cite_note-EncyclopediaDV-5) [Grav-ball](/wiki/Grav-ball "Grav-ball") was played on a 64-meter grid that was divided into eight octets.[[6]](#cite_note-gridiron-6) Meters were also divided into [centimeters](/wiki/Centimeter "Centimeter") and [millimeters](/wiki/Millimeter "Millimeter").[[7]](#cite_note-EncyclopediaVE-7) For very large scale measurements, 1,000 meters were put together to make one [kilometer](/wiki/Kilometer "Kilometer").[[8]](#cite_note-SWE-Super_Star_Destroyer-8)[[9]](#cite_note-SWE-Admiral_Ozzel-9)

According to [Lugubrious Mote](/wiki/Lugubrious_Mote "Lugubrious Mote"), most [sentient](/wiki/Sentience "Sentience") species in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") were about the same size, ranging in height between about half a meter to three meters.[[10]](#cite_note-Legends-10)
