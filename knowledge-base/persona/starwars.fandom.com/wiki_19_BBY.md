## Events[]

* [Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul")'s criminal alliance [Shadow Collective](/wiki/Shadow_Collective "Shadow Collective") is founded,[[9]](#cite_note-Eminence-9) initially dissolved,[[10]](#cite_note-Darth_Maul_4-10) and later reformed.[[11]](#cite_note-Together_Again-11)
* Despite being found innocent at her [trial](/wiki/Trial_of_Ahsoka_Tano "Trial of Ahsoka Tano") for the [bombing of the Jedi Temple hangar](/wiki/Bombing_of_the_Jedi_Temple_hangar "Bombing of the Jedi Temple hangar") (the real culprit was [Barriss Offee](/wiki/Barriss_Offee "Barriss Offee")), [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano") leaves the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order").[[12]](#cite_note-TWJ-12)
* [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") prematurely activates for [clone trooper](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") [Tup](/wiki/Tup "Tup") during the [Battle of Ringo Vinda](/wiki/Battle_of_Ringo_Vinda "Battle of Ringo Vinda"), leading [ARC trooper](/wiki/Advanced_Recon_Commando "Advanced Recon Commando") [Fives](/wiki/Fives "Fives") to discover the [control chips](/wiki/Behavioral_modification_biochip "Behavioral modification biochip") and learn how they can force clone troopers to [eliminate](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") without question. However, the ARC trooper is [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") by members of the [Coruscant Guard](/wiki/Coruscant_Guard "Coruscant Guard") before he can properly warn his fellow [clones](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") and the Jedi.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Unknown-13)[[14]](#cite_note-Fugitive-14)[[15]](#cite_note-Orders-15)
* After a [genocidal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/genocide "wikipedia:genocide") [attack](/wiki/Atrocity_on_Mahranee "Atrocity on Mahranee") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Mahranee](/wiki/Mahranee "Mahranee"), the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") reluctantly approves a plan to assassinate [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), sending [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Quinlan Vos](/wiki/Quinlan_Vos "Quinlan Vos") on an undercover operation to team up with Dooku's former assassin [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress") for the job.[[16]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-16)
* [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Talzin](/wiki/Talzin "Talzin") is killed by [General](/wiki/General "General") [Grievous](/wiki/Grievous "Grievous") during the [Second Battle of Dathomir](/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Dathomir "Second Battle of Dathomir").[[10]](#cite_note-Darth_Maul_4-10)
* Tano befriends [Level 1313](/wiki/Level_1313 "Level 1313") resident [Trace Martez](/wiki/Trace_Martez "Trace Martez") and her sister [Rafa](/wiki/Rafa_Martez "Rafa Martez"), and the three become [embroiled](/wiki/Escape_from_Oba_Diah "Escape from Oba Diah") in trouble with the [Pyke Syndicate](/wiki/Pyke_Syndicate "Pyke Syndicate") after Rafa is forced to recruit her sister for a [spice](/wiki/Spice "Spice")-running job.[[17]](#cite_note-Gone_with_a_Trace-17)[[18]](#cite_note-Deal_No_Deal-18)
* ARC trooper [Echo](/wiki/Echo "Echo") is discovered to be alive and a captive of the [Techno Union](/wiki/Techno_Union "Techno Union"). He is rescued by [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"), [Captain](/wiki/Clone_Captain "Clone Captain") [Rex](/wiki/Rex "Rex"), and [Clone Force 99](/wiki/Clone_Force_99 "Clone Force 99").[[19]](#cite_note-ADE-19)
* The [Siege of Mandalore](/wiki/Siege_of_Mandalore "Siege of Mandalore") begins. Ahsoka Tano, working with [Lady](/wiki/Lady "Lady") [Bo-Katan Kryze](/wiki/Bo-Katan_Kryze "Bo-Katan Kryze") and the [Mandalore resistance](/wiki/Mandalore_resistance "Mandalore resistance"), asks Skywalker and [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") for help. When the two [Jedi Generals](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General") are abruptly called away to the [Battle of Coruscant](/wiki/Battle_of_Coruscant "Battle of Coruscant"), Skywalker splits the [501st Legion](/wiki/501st_Legion "501st Legion") and gives Tano a [new division](/wiki/332nd_Division "332nd Division"), including the [332nd Company](/wiki/332nd_Company "332nd Company"). Since Tano is no longer a member of the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic"), Skywalker promotes Rex to [Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") to lead the unit, with Tano officially serving as an [advisor](/wiki/Advisor "Advisor").[[20]](#cite_note-Old_Friends-20)
* Following a duel, Count Dooku is decapitated by Anakin Skywalker aboard the *[Invisible Hand](/wiki/Invisible_Hand "Invisible Hand")*.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Skywalker and Kenobi manage to rescue [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") [Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") from the *Invisible Hand*.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Chancellor Palpatine reveals to Skywalker that he is a [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* General Grievous is [slain](/wiki/Battle_of_Utapau "Battle of Utapau") by Kenobi on [Utapau](/wiki/Utapau "Utapau").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Anakin Skywalker informs [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") of his findings about Chancellor Palpatine.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Mace Windu, [Kit Fisto](/wiki/Kit_Fisto "Kit Fisto"), [Agen Kolar](/wiki/Agen_Kolar "Agen Kolar"), and [Saesee Tiin](/wiki/Saesee_Tiin "Saesee Tiin") [attempt](/wiki/Duel_in_Palpatine%27s_office "Duel in Palpatine's office") to arrest Palpatine after learning that the Chancellor is a [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord"), but fail and are killed by Sidious with assistance from the manipulated Anakin Skywalker. Skywalker subsequently becomes Darth Vader, Sidious's new [apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Clone troopers led by Vader [besiege](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall") the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* The [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") begins with [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* The [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") is [founded](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") by the self-anointed [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") Palpatine, replacing the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") and putting [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") directly under the control of the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* The [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") becomes the [Imperial Senate](/wiki/Imperial_Senate "Imperial Senate").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Darth Vader [slaughters](/wiki/Mission_to_Mustafar "Mission to Mustafar") the [Separatist Council](/wiki/Executive_Separatist_Council "Executive Separatist Council") on [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar") and remotely deactivates the [Separatist Droid Army](/wiki/Separatist_Droid_Army "Separatist Droid Army"), dissolving the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") and ending the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Yoda and Sidious [duel](/wiki/Duel_in_the_Galactic_Senate "Duel in the Galactic Senate") in the [Senate Building](/wiki/Imperial_Senate_Building "Imperial Senate Building").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Kenobi and Vader [duel](/wiki/Duel_on_Mustafar "Duel on Mustafar") on Mustafar. After losing his remaining organic limbs in the duel, Vader is sealed into a [cybernetic](/wiki/Cybernetics "Cybernetics") [suit of armor](/wiki/Darth_Vader%27s_armor "Darth Vader's armor").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala"), the secret wife of Anakin Skywalker, dies on [Polis Massa](/wiki/Polis_Massa "Polis Massa") after giving birth to their children, [Luke](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") and [Leia](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo"). Her [funeral](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala%27s_funeral "Padmé Amidala's funeral") takes place on [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Fearing that Anakin's children will be hunted down because they could pose a threat to the Emperor, the Jedi Masters [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi"), as well as [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Bail Organa](/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa "Bail Prestor Organa"), agree to hide the whereabouts of the [Skywalker](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family") [twins](/wiki/Twin "Twin").[[21]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-21)[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1) As a result, the [House of Organa](/wiki/House_of_Organa "House of Organa"), the royal family of [Alderaan](/wiki/Alderaan "Alderaan"), adopts Leia, while the [Lars family](/wiki/Lars_family "Lars family") of Tatooine takes in Luke Skywalker.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* Yoda goes into a self-imposed exile on [Dagobah](/wiki/Dagobah "Dagobah"), while Kenobi goes to live as a [hermit](/wiki/Hermit "Hermit") on [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") watching over Luke Skywalker.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)
* After the crash[[22]](#cite_note-Victory_and_Death-22) of the *[Tribunal](/wiki/Tribunal "Tribunal")*[[23]](#cite_note-TCW_Character_Encyclopedia-23) as a result of [onboard fighting](/wiki/Skirmish_aboard_the_Tribunal "Skirmish aboard the Tribunal") caused by the issuance of Order 66, as well as the escape of Maul,[[22]](#cite_note-Victory_and_Death-22) Ahsoka Tano and Rex go into hiding in the Outer Rim to escape the Empire, with Tano faking her death[[24]](#cite_note-Ahsoka-24) by abandoning her [lightsabers](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano%27s_lightsabers "Ahsoka Tano's lightsabers") at the crash site.[[25]](#cite_note-DB:Ahsoka-25)
* [Saw Gerrera](/wiki/Saw_Gerrera "Saw Gerrera") of [Onderon](/wiki/Onderon "Onderon") forms his rebel cell, the [Partisans](/wiki/Partisans "Partisans").[[26]](#cite_note-Rogue_One_Visual_Guide-26)
* With the [fall of the Republic](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era"), the galaxy enters a period known as the "[dark times](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era")."[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_III-1)[[27]](#cite_note-Episode_IV-27)
* [Tipoca City](/wiki/Tipoca_City "Tipoca City") is [destroyed](/wiki/Attack_on_Kamino "Attack on Kamino") by the Empire[[28]](#cite_note-Return_to_Kamino-28)[[29]](#cite_note-TBB_Date_note-29)
* Tano, under the false identity of Ashla, settles with the [Fardi clan](/wiki/Fardi_clan "Fardi clan") on the Outer Rim world of [Thabeska](/wiki/Thabeska "Thabeska").[[24]](#cite_note-Ahsoka-24)
* The [Hidden Path](/wiki/Hidden_Path "Hidden Path") is formed.[[8]](#cite_note-Timelines-8)
