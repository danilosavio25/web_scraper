"**The Padawan Path**" is the fourth episode of the first season of the animated micro-series *[Star Wars Forces of Destiny](/wiki/Star_Wars_Forces_of_Destiny "Star Wars Forces of Destiny")*. It was released on [July 6](/wiki/July_6 "July 6"), [2017](/wiki/2017 "2017"). The episode takes place between [*The Clone Wars*: Season Three](/wiki/The_Clone_Wars:_Season_Three "The Clone Wars: Season Three") episodes "[Hunt for Ziro](/wiki/Hunt_for_Ziro "Hunt for Ziro")" and "[Heroes on Both Sides](/wiki/Heroes_on_Both_Sides "Heroes on Both Sides")" and focuses on [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano").[[1]](#cite_note-The_Padawan_Path-1)

## Plot summary[]

 [![Ahsoka Tano runs into some complications on her way to the temple.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Ahsoka-ThePadawanPath.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20240331120158) [![Ahsoka Tano runs into some complications on her way to the temple.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Ahsoka-ThePadawanPath.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20240331120158)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/02/Ahsoka-ThePadawanPath.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20240331120158) 

Ahsoka Tano runs into some complications on her way to the temple.

 

Having finished patrolling the streets of [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), [Ahsoka](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano") is heading to the [Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace"), where [Anakin](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") and [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") are waiting for [her](/wiki/Gender "Gender") to start a [ceremony](/wiki/Unidentified_Jedi_ceremony "Unidentified Jedi ceremony").

While hurrying to get to the temple, she hears someone in the crowd scream for help. Following the scream she sees a [female](/wiki/Unidentified_Aleena_mother "Unidentified Aleena mother") [Aleena](/wiki/Aleena "Aleena") and [her child](/wiki/Unidentified_Aleena_child_(Coruscant) "Unidentified Aleena child (Coruscant)") being attacked by a [malfunctioning](/wiki/Unidentified_industrial_cleaning_droid "Unidentified industrial cleaning droid") [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid"). She [Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force")-pushes some crates to take down the droid, but this does not work, so she ignites [her lightsabers](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano%27s_lightsabers "Ahsoka Tano's lightsabers"). As the droid nears her, she spots water pipes running above the alley they are in and comes up with a plan. She sprints at the droid, slicing one of its legs, and then leaps and slices the water pipes above them. Water gushes out of the broken pipes, causing the droid to deactivate. After checking that the mother and child are all right, she remembers that she is late for the ceremony and rushes to the temple.

She arrives at the [training grounds](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_training_ground "Jedi Temple training ground") near the [Great Tree](/wiki/Great_Tree_(Coruscant) "Great Tree (Coruscant)"), where Anakin and Yoda are waiting for her. While Anakin scolds her for being late, [Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") Yoda states that Ahsoka has been humble and brave, which he sees as a sign of maturity. As the ceremony begins, he tells her that she is on the right path to becoming a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight"), and gives her an extension to her [Padawan braid](/wiki/Padawan_braid "Padawan braid").
