**Sith Lord**, also known as a **Lord of the Sith**, was a title conferred upon members of the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") Order. Sith [Lords](/wiki/Lord "Lord") drew upon the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force"), using it to gain power.

The title "[Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth")" was given to newly christened Sith Lords in place of their old identities. It roughly translated to "[Dark Lord](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith")." Following the [defeat of the Sith](/wiki/Defeat_of_the_Sith "Defeat of the Sith"), [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane") limited the Order's ranks to two Sith Lords—a [Sith Master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") and a [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice")—at any one [time](/wiki/Time "Time") under the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two"). The [establishment](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") later saw [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") controlled by the Sith Lords [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") and [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker").

Darth Bane founded a lineage of Sith Lords that culminated in the fall of the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic.

 

Founded by a [rogue Jedi](/wiki/Unidentified_rogue_Jedi "Unidentified rogue Jedi")[[7]](#cite_note-Sith_SWE-7) during the [Hundred-Year Darkness](/wiki/Hundred-Year_Darkness "Hundred-Year Darkness"),[[8]](#cite_note-Showdown_on_the_Smuggler's_Moon,_Part_II-8) the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") were an ancient order who used the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") and fought the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi").[[7]](#cite_note-Sith_SWE-7) Every Sith [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") used the title of [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") within their name, with "Darth" roughly translating into "Dark Lord."[[9]](#cite_note-SWEasy-9) The title of [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") was itself a title used within the Sith Order by its lords.[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_II-10) During the [days](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day") of the [Old Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"), many individuals held the mantle of Sith Lord and fought countless wars against the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). However, their numbers began to dwindle due to infighting, which the Jedi took advantage of, ultimately resulting in the Sith being [defeated](/wiki/Defeat_of_the_Sith "Defeat of the Sith") and nearly destroyed by the Jedi. After [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane") established the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two"),[[11]](#cite_note-Sacrifice-11) Sith tradition dictated that there could only be two Sith Lords—a [master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") and an [apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice")—at any given [time](/wiki/Time "Time").[[3]](#cite_note-Episode_I-3)

The Sith were still in hiding by the time of Darth Plagueis, a Sith Lord who lived during the High Republic Era.

 

During the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"),[[13]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte-13) the Sith Lord [Darth Plagueis](/wiki/Darth_Plagueis "Darth Plagueis"), who had been trained by [Darth Tenebrous](/wiki/Darth_Tenebrous "Darth Tenebrous"),[[14]](#cite_note-BMF_44-14) took on a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") former Jedi[[13]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte-13) as his apprentice.[[15]](#cite_note-Leslye_EW-15) The human became a Sith Lord[[16]](#cite_note-229_Companion-16) under Plagueis's tutelage[[15]](#cite_note-Leslye_EW-15) and acted as a [Sith warrior](/wiki/Sith_warrior "Sith warrior").[[17]](#cite_note-VFX-17) The man would come to claim he had no name,[[12]](#cite_note-Night-12) instead simply being identified as "[the Stranger](/wiki/The_Stranger "The Stranger")."[[18]](#cite_note-Day-18) By [148 BBY](/wiki/148_BBY "148 BBY"),[[19]](#cite_note-Acolyte_timeline-19) the Stranger, introducing himself as the Master, came in contact with and recruited [Mae-ho Aniseya](/wiki/Mae-ho_Aniseya "Mae-ho Aniseya"), the sole surviving [witch](/wiki/Witch "Witch") of [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Aniseya](/wiki/Aniseya "Aniseya")'s [coven](/wiki/Brendok_coven "Brendok coven").[[20]](#cite_note-Destiny-20)

The so-called "Master" trained Mae extensively in the ways of [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") as his own [Sith acolyte](/wiki/Sith_acolyte "Sith acolyte").[[18]](#cite_note-Day-18) In [132 BBY](/wiki/132_BBY "132 BBY"),[[21]](#cite_note-DateOsha-21) he tasked her with a [mission](/wiki/Mae%27s_quest "Mae's quest") to [assassinate](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") four Jedi that had been stationed on her [homeworld](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") [Brendok](/wiki/Brendok "Brendok")[[22]](#cite_note-Revenge_/_Justice-22) at the time of her coven's destruction.[[20]](#cite_note-Destiny-20) Mae succeeded in killing her first two targets, first by ambushing [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Indara](/wiki/Indara "Indara") on [Ueda](/wiki/Ueda "Ueda")[[23]](#cite_note-Lost/Found-23) and then by coercing Master [Torbin](/wiki/Torbin "Torbin") to [die](/wiki/Death "Death") by suicide in the [Olega Jedi temple](/wiki/Olega_Jedi_temple "Olega Jedi temple"),[[22]](#cite_note-Revenge_/_Justice-22) attracting the attention of and causing significant concern to the Jedi Order. After identifying the connection between Indara and Torbin, a Jedi Order panel led by Masters [Vernestra Rwoh](/wiki/Vernestra_Rwoh "Vernestra Rwoh"), [Ki-Adi-Mundi](/wiki/Ki-Adi-Mundi "Ki-Adi-Mundi"), and [Holden](/wiki/Veo_Holden "Veo Holden") decided to keep the matter a secret from the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council"), as they would be required to notify the [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") of the matter. Instead they assembled a task force, led by Master [Sol](/wiki/Sol "Sol") and consisting of his [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Jecki Lon](/wiki/Jecki_Lon "Jecki Lon"), [Jedi Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Yord Fandar](/wiki/Yord_Fandar "Yord Fandar") and [Ithia Paan](/wiki/Ithia_Paan "Ithia Paan") as well as [Wellig Yuns](/wiki/Wellig_Yuns "Wellig Yuns") and Mae's sister [Verosha Aniseya](/wiki/Verosha_Aniseya "Verosha Aniseya") who Sol believed could approach Mae without risk.[[18]](#cite_note-Day-18)

 [![The Stranger was behind a series of Jedi killings during the High Republic Era.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/Celebration-PeaceisaLie.png/revision/latest?cb=20250223160520) [![The Stranger was behind a series of Jedi killings during the High Republic Era.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/Celebration-PeaceisaLie.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20250223160520)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/Celebration-PeaceisaLie.png/revision/latest?cb=20250223160520) 

The Stranger was behind a series of Jedi killings during the High Republic Era.

 

The team traveled to [Khofar](/wiki/Khofar "Khofar"), where the third Jedi from Brendok, Master [Kelnacca](/wiki/Kelnacca "Kelnacca") lived. Unbeknownst to them, Mae was already on the planet with the Stranger, who had assumed the persona of Qimir, a friend of Mae and supposed associate of the Sith Lord. The pair made their way to [Kelnacca's shelter](/wiki/Kelnacca%27s_shelter "Kelnacca's shelter") before the Jedi did, but before they reached it Mae tricked Qimir and drew him to one of Kelnacca's traps having decided to betray the Master and surrender to the Jedi. The Stranger freed himself from the trap, donned his [cortosis](/wiki/Cortosis "Cortosis") [armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") and reached Kelnacca's shelter before Mae, killing the [Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee") and waiting for Mae to confront her. Mae, however, arrived at the same time as the group of the Jedi, who witnessed the Stranger.[[18]](#cite_note-Day-18) To keep his existence a secret, the Sith drew [his lightsaber](/wiki/The_Stranger%27s_lightsaber "The Stranger's lightsaber") and attacked them, killing the entire team save for Sol, Osha, and their [tracker](/wiki/Tracker "Tracker") [Bazil](/wiki/Bazil "Bazil").[[12]](#cite_note-Night-12)

With Mae switching places with her sister,[[12]](#cite_note-Night-12) the Stranger took Osha back to his [island hideaway](/wiki/The_Stranger%27s_cave "The Stranger's cave")[[24]](#cite_note-Teach_/_Corrupt-24) and tried to tempt her to the dark side as his new acolyte. Both eventually made their way to Mae and Osha's homeworld of Brendok, with their departure secretly witnessed by Plagueis. On Brendok, which Sol had also arrived upon with Mae, the Stranger engaged Sol in a [duel](/wiki/Duel_on_Brendok "Duel on Brendok") while Osha and Mae came to blows. However, upon learning that Sol himself had killed their mother years prior, Osha herself killed the Jedi Master and fell under the sway of the dark side. With Mae's blessing, the Stranger wiped Mae's memory of her sister while Osha departed back to the island with the Stranger, who sought to train her as a new Sith acolyte. Meanwhile, Jedi Master Vernestra Rwoh covered up the recent Jedi murders as Sol betraying the Order and began a [hunt for the Stranger](/wiki/Search_for_the_Stranger "Search for the Stranger"), whom she had sensed on Brendok before his departure, with the amnesic Mae.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte-13)

### Twilight of the Republic[]

:   "*He was trained in the Jedi arts. My only conclusion can be that it was a Sith lord.*"
:   ―[Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn"), referring to Darth Maul[[3]](#cite_note-Episode_I-3)

 [![Darth Sidious and Darth Maul were Sith Lords during the late era of the Galactic Republic.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e1/BenHarveyTextless-MaulBWR1.png/revision/latest?cb=20251103010628) [![Darth Sidious and Darth Maul were Sith Lords during the late era of the Galactic Republic.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e1/BenHarveyTextless-MaulBWR1.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20251103010628)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e1/BenHarveyTextless-MaulBWR1.png/revision/latest?cb=20251103010628) 

Darth Sidious and Darth Maul were Sith Lords during the late era of the Galactic Republic.

 

In time, Darth Plagueis took on an apprentice named [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), who went onto kill Plagueis and take on his own apprentice, [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul").[[25]](#cite_note-Palpatine_encyclopedia-25) Sidious' alter ego Sheev Palpatine became the [senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") of [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") in order to inflitrate, deceive and eventually destroy the Jedi and Galactic Republic. Sidious planted the seeds for what would become the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") by forming an alliance with the [Trade Federation](/wiki/Trade_Federation "Trade Federation"), a corporation led by [Viceroy](/wiki/Viceroy_of_the_Trade_Federation "Viceroy of the Trade Federation") [Nute Gunray](/wiki/Nute_Gunray "Nute Gunray"). Sidious convinced the Trade Federation to use their [army](/wiki/Trade_Federation_Droid_Army "Trade Federation Droid Army") of [battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid") to [blockade](/wiki/Blockade "Blockade") and invade the peaceful [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of Naboo, in order to [unseat](/wiki/Vote_of_No_Confidence "Vote of No Confidence") [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") [Finis Valorum](/wiki/Finis_Valorum "Finis Valorum") and have Palpatine become the Chancellor. The invasion [failed](/wiki/Battle_of_Naboo "Battle of Naboo") and Maul was seemingly killed by a [Jedi Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"), [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi"), but Palpatine was [elected as Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor_election,_32_BBY "Supreme Chancellor election, 32 BBY") of the Galactic Republic.[[3]](#cite_note-Episode_I-3)

The Sith then resurfaced [ten years](/wiki/Standard_decade "Standard decade") later. [Darth Tyranus](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Sidious' apprentice, met with Gunray and other influential magnates on [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis"). They [supported](/wiki/Geonosian_accord "Geonosian accord") the Confederacy of Independent Systems, leading to the [start](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis") of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") against the Republic.[[10]](#cite_note-Episode_II-10) A number of other Sith were also active during the war, such as Sidious' former apprentice Maul, Tyranus' first apprentice [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress"), and Tyranus' second apprentice and Maul's brother [Savage Opress](/wiki/Savage_Opress "Savage Opress").[[26]](#cite_note-Monster-26) Due to Sidious being both Chancellor of the Republic and the master of Tyranus, the Separatists' [Head of State](/wiki/Head_of_State "Head of State"),[[27]](#cite_note-Rise-27) he was able to manipulate both sides of the war to suit his own schemes. A number of other Sith agents were also active during the war: [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress") served as a [Sith Acolyte](/wiki/Dark_Acolyte "Dark Acolyte"),[[28]](#cite_note-BMF-28) one Tyranus' disciples, as did [Savage Opress](/wiki/Savage_Opress "Savage Opress").[[26]](#cite_note-Monster-26) Both were ultimately cast aside, with Opress becoming the pupil of the returned Maul.[[4]](#cite_note-Eminence-4) The two [Sith warriors](/wiki/Sith_warrior "Sith warrior") founded the nefarious organization of the [Shadow Collective](/wiki/Shadow_Collective "Shadow Collective"), but were soon defeated by Sidious, who viewed them as a threat. The machinations of the reigning Sith Lords led to [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") and the [establishment](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"),[[5]](#cite_note-Episode_III-5) at the cost of Tyranus' life.[[5]](#cite_note-Episode_III-5)

The Galactic Empire was ruled by the Sith Lords Darth Sidious and Darth Vader.

 

Not long after Tyranus was sacrificed and betrayed by Sidious, Jedi Knight [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") turned to the dark side and became Sidious' new apprentice, receiving the name "Vader" which was preceded by the moniker Darth. With the [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") in effect, Sidious consolidated his power over [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") as the self-proclaimed [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor"),[[5]](#cite_note-Episode_III-5) while Vader would act as his [forces](/wiki/Imperial_Military "Imperial Military")' [Commander-in-Chief](/wiki/Commander-in-Chief "Commander-in-Chief").[[30]](#cite_note-Bust_Collection-30)

Years into the [Imperial Era](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era"), [Governor](/wiki/Governor "Governor") [Wilhuff Tarkin](/wiki/Wilhuff_Tarkin "Wilhuff Tarkin") and Lord Vader oversaw the deployment of the [Death Star](/wiki/DS-1_Orbital_Battle_Station "DS-1 Orbital Battle Station") [battle station](/wiki/Battle_station "Battle station"). However, the [Rebel Alliance](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic") destroyed it along with [Grand Moff](/wiki/Grand_Moff "Grand Moff") Tarkin during the [Battle of Yavin](/wiki/Battle_of_Yavin "Battle of Yavin"). The Rebels then continued to defy Imperial rule[[31]](#cite_note-Episode_IV-31) for a further five years,[[32]](#cite_note-Galactic_Atlas-32) leading to the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor") with the destruction of the [DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station](/wiki/DS-2_Death_Star_II_Mobile_Battle_Station "DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station") and both Sith Lords. As the battle turned in the Alliance's favor,[[33]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-33) Vader sacrificed his life by killing his master in order to protect his son, [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"), thereby renouncing the dark side, fulfilling his role as the [Chosen One](/wiki/Chosen_One "Chosen One"), and dying as Anakin Skywalker.[[34]](#cite_note-Anakin_DB-34)

The [thousand-year](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") lineage that started with Darth Bane came to an end with the demise of the last Sith Lord, Darth Vader.[[35]](#cite_note-TLJ_VD-35) Unknown to the galaxy, Darth Sidious was resurrected when his consciousness [transferred](/wiki/Transference "Transference") to a [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") body on the desolate planet [Exegol](/wiki/Exegol "Exegol"), where his [followers](/wiki/Sith_cultist "Sith cultist"), the [Sith Eternal](/wiki/Sith_Eternal "Sith Eternal") [cult](/wiki/Sith_cult "Sith cult"),[[6]](#cite_note-SOTS-6) preserved the teachings of the Sith [religion](/wiki/Religion "Religion"). In addition to a Sith [fleet](/wiki/Fleet "Fleet") known as the [Final Order](/wiki/Final_Order "Final Order"), the Sith Eternal trained an [army](/wiki/Sith_Eternal_army "Sith Eternal army") of [Sith troopers](/wiki/Sith_trooper "Sith trooper"), divided into [legions](/wiki/Legion "Legion") that were named after ancient Sith Lords, such as [Revan](/wiki/Revan "Revan"), [Andeddu](/wiki/Andeddu "Andeddu"), [Darth Tanis](/wiki/Darth_Tanis "Darth Tanis"), [Darth Tenebrous](/wiki/Darth_Tenebrous "Darth Tenebrous"), [Phobos](/wiki/Phobos "Phobos"), and [Desolous](/wiki/Desolous "Desolous").[[36]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-36)

The Dark Lord sought to restore the Sith Order through Vader's grandson, [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") and his granddaughter, [Rey](/wiki/Rey_Skywalker "Rey Skywalker").[[37]](#cite_note-Episode_IX-37) When Ren journeyed to Exegol, he heard the whispers of Sith Lords long dead—[Locphet](/wiki/Locphet "Locphet"), [Mindran](/wiki/Mindran "Mindran"), [Felkor](/wiki/Felkor "Felkor"), [Naga Sadow](/wiki/Naga_Sadow "Naga Sadow"), and [Sissiri](/wiki/Sissiri "Sissiri")—and met Sidious, who had been grooming him for use in his plans for decades.[[38]](#cite_note-TROS_JN-38) Ren was not a Sith Lord,[[6]](#cite_note-SOTS-6) but he embodied the Sith Order's teachings as well as those of the Jedi Order.[[39]](#cite_note-Kylo_Ren_DB-39) Sidious doubted that Ren could overcome his connection to the [light side of the Force](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force"), but still tasked him with killing Rey as a final chance to prove himself.[[6]](#cite_note-SOTS-6) Despite Ren's intention of becoming the new Dark Lord of the Sith,[[38]](#cite_note-TROS_JN-38) for which he had been groomed to inherit through the machinations of the Sith Eternal,[[36]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-36) he ultimately [rejected](/wiki/Mission_to_Kef_Bir "Mission to Kef Bir") the dark side like his grandfather before him. During the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol"), Rey prevented the return of the tyranny of the Sith by destroying Sidious.[[37]](#cite_note-Episode_IX-37)
