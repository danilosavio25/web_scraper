**Ki-Adi-Mundi** was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [Cerean](/wiki/Cerean "Cerean") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") and a [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") who was active during the [High Republic](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"). He was a member of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") during the last [decades](/wiki/Standard_decade "Standard decade") of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"). Following the outbreak of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") after the [First Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis"), Mundi became a [Jedi General](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General") of the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic"). Like his Jedi colleagues, he led the Republic [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") against the [Separatist Alliance](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") [forces](/wiki/Confederacy_military "Confederacy military") in several battles across [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"), including the [second campaign](/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Geonosis "Second Battle of Geonosis") on [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis").

In [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY"), the third and final year of the war, the Republic was engaged in the [Outer Rim Sieges](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Sieges "Outer Rim Sieges"), during which Mundi oversaw the [Republic invasion](/wiki/Mygeeto_Campaign "Mygeeto Campaign") of [Mygeeto](/wiki/Mygeeto "Mygeeto") with the [21st Nova Corps](/wiki/Galactic_Marines "Galactic Marines") under his command. During the invasion, [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") [Sheev Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious")—secretly the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") Darth Sidious—instructed the Grand [Army](/wiki/Army "Army") [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") to execute their Jedi leaders in accordance with [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), an act that resulted in the [death](/wiki/Death "Death") of Mundi along with the majority of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order").

Despite Mundi's death, his [lightsaber](/wiki/Ki-Adi-Mundi%27s_lightsaber "Ki-Adi-Mundi's lightsaber") endured many long years and eventually came into the possession of the [collector](/wiki/Collector "Collector") [Dok-Ondar](/wiki/Dok-Ondar "Dok-Ondar"), who recounted some elements of its past to a [sergeant](/wiki/Sergeant "Sergeant") of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order").

### Murder mystery

Ki-Adi-Mundi was a [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Cerean](/wiki/Cerean "Cerean") who [hailed](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") from[[3]](#cite_note-Mundi_Databank-3) the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet")[[12]](#cite_note-Cerean-12) [Cerea](/wiki/Cerea "Cerea").[[1]](#cite_note-Mundi_Encyclopedia-1) In [132 BBY](/wiki/132_BBY "132 BBY"),[[13]](#cite_note-Timeline-13) Mundi was a newly elevated[[14]](#cite_note-AcolyteVD-14) [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order")[[5]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte_4-5) whose expertise had garnered the attention of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council"). He was one of ten [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") who participated in a Jedi tactical council[[14]](#cite_note-AcolyteVD-14) meeting called by Master [Vernestra Rwoh](/wiki/Vernestra_Rwoh "Vernestra Rwoh") to discuss the emergence of the [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [assassin](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") [Mae-ho Aniseya](/wiki/Mae-ho_Aniseya "Mae-ho Aniseya"). After the group reviewed [holocam](/wiki/Holocamera "Holocamera") footage provided by Master [Sol](/wiki/Sol "Sol") of Mae in combat with him, Mundi pointed out that Mae had demonstrated some skill, indicating that she had received training. This led [Holden](/wiki/Veo_Holden "Veo Holden") to ask who had done so, with Sol stating that Mae did not know her Master's identity, an idea ridiculed by Mundi.[[5]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte_4-5)

Mundi declared that Mae could pose a great threat to them. When he said that they must inform the High Council, Rwoh insisted that they keep the matter to themselves, arguing that the Council would be obliged to inform the [Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate"), which could result in a scandal capable of spreading fear and mistrust.[[5]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte_4-5)

Decades later, following the [death](/wiki/Death "Death") of [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") Master [Katri](/wiki/Katri "Katri") on [Raxus Secundus](/wiki/Raxus_Secundus "Raxus Secundus") at the hands of a group of [security guards](/wiki/Security_guard "Security guard"), Mundi gathered at the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on Coruscant alongside other Jedi and attended her [funeral](/wiki/Funeral_of_Katri "Funeral of Katri"). As her body was lowered into the grounds, Mundi gave a eulogy regarding Katri's patience with [younglings](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") and other Jedi, as well as how [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") flowed with certainty through her. After the service, Mundi exited the Jedi Temple alongside [Tera Sinube](/wiki/Tera_Sinube "Tera Sinube") and [Jocasta Nu](/wiki/Jocasta_Nu "Jocasta Nu").[[15]](#cite_note-TOTJ-15)

Ki-Adi-Mundi had a respectful albeit somewhat tenuous relationship with Master Yarael Poof.

 

At some point, Mundi earned a seat on the High Council.[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_I-6) In [33 BBY](/wiki/33_BBY "33 BBY"),[[17]](#cite_note-TLFDate-17) Mundi collaborated with fellow [Councilor](/wiki/Councillor "Councillor") [Yarael Poof](/wiki/Yarael_Poof "Yarael Poof") in teaching a class of younglings about dueling positions. The irascible Poof joked around, exercising his abilities in mental control by attempting a mind trick on Mundi, then using the technique to get several students to bar Mundi's path to him, saying that this was how he would defend himself against someone shorter than him, with that being almost everyone. Mundi pointed out that not only was his method reliable, but it was also immoral to force people to face danger against their will. Yarael rebutted by stating that the blame lay with their students not having enough mental discipline. They responded that he hadn't *actually* controlled them. Poof reacted theatrically, getting laughs from the children and commented that it paid to be popular. As ever, Mundi tolerated Poof's antics despite often finding them annoying.[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16)

The class was then interrupted by the arrival of [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi"), who had recently returned to Coruscant from a mission with his master, [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn"). Kenobi explained that they had brought back a number of refugees, and that the local relief agencies had not been able to supply their needs; he asked for the two Masters' help. Mundi proposed giving the younglings a teaching moment by letting them choose an item to donate, but Poof disagreed, saying that the Jedi could afford to give the refugees new clothes and so on. Mundi retorted that Poof would say such a thing, since his uncommon build did not make second-hand an option for him. Poof then quipped that Mundi was not much better when it came to helmets due to the size of his head. After the group chuckled, Kenobi asked if Qui-Gon had spoken to the two Masters about addressing a meeting of the High Council. Mundi replied that such a gathering would have to take place after they finished their class, giving Poof a meaningful look as he noted that they were running behind.[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16)

#### Qui-Gon's proposal

:   "*We spend a great deal of time seeking balance—and we do this because we inherently know when something is out of balance. Well, something* is *out of balance, for each of us. Fortunately, it requires no grand plan, no great work. And this wise Council already knows what is needed...*Help one person. *A Jedi needs no permission for that.*"
:   ―Qui-Gon Jinn to Mundi and the Jedi High Council[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16)

 [![Master Qui-Gon Jinn proposed greater contact with the galactic public to Mundi and the Jedi High Council.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c0/Quigonheadshot.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100309144123) [![Master Qui-Gon Jinn proposed greater contact with the galactic public to Mundi and the Jedi High Council.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c0/Quigonheadshot.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100309144123)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c0/Quigonheadshot.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100309144123) 

Master Qui-Gon Jinn proposed greater contact with the galactic public to Mundi and the Jedi High Council.

 

Sometime after, Ki-Adi-Mundi and Poof joined the rest of their colleagues in the meeting of the High Council requested by Qui-Gon Jinn. In response to [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") and [Even Piell](/wiki/Even_Piell "Even Piell")'s comments on the latter's involvement in a relatively unimportant intercession at the request of Supreme Chancellor [Valorum](/wiki/Finis_Valorum "Finis Valorum"), Mundi quipped that the Council would soon be settling parking disputes. Jinn then spoke before the Council about the Order's recent [outpost](/wiki/Jedi_outpost "Jedi outpost") closures in [the Slice](/wiki/The_Slice "The Slice") and how he and his Padawan had witnessed the destabilization of the region as the Jedi and subsequently many corporations began to pull out. Jinn insisted that the Order needed to take a more direct role in the area in order to positively impact the beings living there, to get more hands on and personal with the general populace than they had of late. Jinn also expressed his concern that without such action, the Order's reputation among the people was in jeopardy, arguing that the Jedi becoming more visible was the key. Mundi—who believed councilors already provided help wherever they went, making him annoyed with Jinn's consistent criticism—was one of several Councilors to express the belief that Jinn's pronouncement was extreme, while others agreed that the Jedi should be considered no better than any other being, that the Order had become too withdrawn from the galaxy and the Council too tethered to Coruscant.[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16)

During the outreach mission, focused on the planet [Kwenn](/wiki/Kwenn "Kwenn"), Mundi approached a [Tasty Comet](/wiki/Tasty_Comet "Tasty Comet") [ice mound](/wiki/Ice_mound "Ice mound") vendor of the [Tasty Comet Frozen Confection Company](/wiki/Tasty_Comet_Frozen_Confection_Company "Tasty Comet Frozen Confection Company") in [Langdam Key](/wiki/Langdam_Key "Langdam Key") and requested a cone without any flavoring, confusing the vendor, who charged customers depending on what flavor they chose. Nonetheless receiving his plain ice cone, Mundi—who was himself confused by the vendor's confusion, thinking he had been clear—paid with a credit and suggested it be used towards a new sign that would explain how much a plain cone cost. Mundi then departed from the vendor in good spirits, feeling as though he had helped the individual.[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16)

### Resurgence of the Sith

:   "*Your thoughts dwell on your mother.*"
:   ―Ki-Adi-Mundi to Anakin Skywalker[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_I-6)

In [32 BBY](/wiki/32_BBY "32 BBY"), Master [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn") and his Padawan, Obi-Wan Kenobi, approached the Jedi Council and informed them of the [lightsaber duel](/wiki/Duel_on_Tatooine "Duel on Tatooine") they had had on [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") with a [Zabrak](/wiki/Zabrak "Zabrak"). Jinn believed the [warrior](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") to be a [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith"), a claim that the Council, met with skepticism, with Mundi in stating in particular that "the Sith have been extinct for a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium")." Jinn also brought [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") in front of the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") Council, the boy had a high [Midi-chlorian](/wiki/Midi-chlorian "Midi-chlorian") count and had a good grasp on [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), but the Council denied his and Jinn's wishes to [train](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") him as a Jedi, believing he was too old, and had much fear in him. Following the [Battle of Naboo](/wiki/Battle_of_Naboo "Battle of Naboo") and the [death](/wiki/Death "Death") of Jinn at the hands of the warrior [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul"), the Council reversed their decision to allow Skywalker to be trained. Mundi traveled to [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") for Jinn's [Funeral](/wiki/Funeral_of_Qui-Gon_Jinn "Funeral of Qui-Gon Jinn").[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_I-6)

[Several years later](/wiki/29_BBY "29 BBY"), Mundi traveled with a Republic task force to [Carnelion IV](/wiki/Carnelion_IV "Carnelion IV") where they [rescued](/wiki/Mission_to_Carnelion_IV "Mission to Carnelion IV") Kenobi and Skywalker and also ended the [war](/wiki/Open-Closed_War "Open-Closed War") between the [Open](/wiki/Open "Open") and [Closed](/wiki/Closed "Closed") human inhabitants of the world.[[18]](#cite_note-OW&A_5-18)

Just before the dawn of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), Mundi attended a meeting with Supreme Chancellor Sheev Palpatine in his [office](/wiki/Emperor%27s_office "Emperor's office"), along with fellow Jedi Mace Windu, [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), [Plo Koon](/wiki/Plo_Koon "Plo Koon"), [Kit Fisto](/wiki/Kit_Fisto "Kit Fisto"), [Luminara Unduli](/wiki/Luminara_Unduli "Luminara Unduli"), and [Barriss Offee](/wiki/Barriss_Offee "Barriss Offee"). Palpatine explained an alarming number of [star systems](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") were [breaking away](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis") from the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") to join the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems"), a Separatist movement led by the former Jedi Master [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"). The [Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") was to consider a vote on the establishment through the [Military Creation Act](/wiki/Military_Creation_Act "Military Creation Act"), a move Palpatine claimed he wished to avoid if possible, as some feared it could lead to war with the Separatists. As the meeting concluded, [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala") of Naboo, who had just survived an [assassination attempt](/wiki/Plot_to_assassinate_Senator_Amidala "Plot to assassinate Senator Amidala"), arrived and explained she believed Dooku was the one behind the attack. Windu reminded Amidala that Dooku had been a Jedi once, and Mundi reasoned that he was simply a [political](/wiki/Politics "Politics") idealist.[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7)

Later, when [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") reported that the [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett") was behind the [assassination](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") attempts on Amidala, Mundi and the Council ordered Kenobi to track the [bounty](/wiki/Bounty "Bounty") hunter down, and commanded Skywalker to bring Amidala safely back to Naboo. Later, Mundi listened along with the rest of the Jedi High Council to a message from Kenobi, who had tracked Fett to the [droid foundries](/wiki/Geonosis_droid_factories "Geonosis droid factories") of [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis"), where it appeared the [Trade Federation](/wiki/Trade_Federation "Trade Federation") was about to take delivery of a [droid army](/wiki/Separatist_Droid_Army "Separatist Droid Army"). Kenobi explained Trade Federation [Viceroy](/wiki/Viceroy_of_the_Trade_Federation "Viceroy of the Trade Federation") [Nute Gunray](/wiki/Nute_Gunray "Nute Gunray") was behind the attempts on Amidala's life. Kenobi's transmission was interrupted as he was attacked by a [droidekas](/wiki/Droideka "Droideka"), prompting a meeting between Mundi, Yoda, Windu, Palpatine, and Senators [Bail Organa](/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa "Bail Prestor Organa"), [Ask Aak](/wiki/Ask_Aak "Ask Aak"), [Jar Jar Binks](/wiki/Jar_Jar_Binks "Jar Jar Binks"), and [Mas Amedda](/wiki/Mas_Amedda "Mas Amedda"). When the Jedi explained only about 200 fellow Jedi were available to go to Geonosis to stop the [Separatist forces](/wiki/Confederacy_military "Confederacy military") from assembling, the current senator of Naboo, Jar Jar Binks made plans to vote Palpatine emergency powers so he could call the recently-discovered [clone army](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") into battle.[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7)

Windu assembled a [strike team](/wiki/Jedi_strike_team "Jedi strike team") of Jedi to rescue Kenobi on Geonosis. Mundi was among the Jedi who traveled to the planet's surface and infiltrated the [arena](/wiki/Petranaki_Arena "Petranaki Arena"), where Kenobi, Skywalker and Amidala were about to be executed. Mundi appeared at a ledge overlooking the arena along with [Aayla Secura](/wiki/Aayla_Secura "Aayla Secura") and Koon, all of whom ignited their [lightsabers](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") as Windu personally confronted Dooku. Although they were desperately outnumbered by the Separatist [Droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") [Army](/wiki/Army "Army"), Mundi and his fellow Jedi engaged the droids in a bloody [battle](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis").[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7)

 [![Ki-Adi-Mundi, Mace Windu, and Yoda aboard a gunship on Geonosis.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/10/Yoda_mace_windu_ki-adi-mundi_battle_of_geonosis.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20111116045153) [![Ki-Adi-Mundi, Mace Windu, and Yoda aboard a gunship on Geonosis.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/10/Yoda_mace_windu_ki-adi-mundi_battle_of_geonosis.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/330?cb=20111116045153)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/10/Yoda_mace_windu_ki-adi-mundi_battle_of_geonosis.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20111116045153)

After several tense [minutes](/wiki/Standard_minute "Standard minute") of fighting, the majority of the Jedi were slain and Dooku's forces gained the upper hand, rounding Mundi and the other surviving Jedi into the center of the arena. Before he could kill them, however, the Jedi were rescued by the Republic's newly-acquired [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") army, led into the arena by Yoda aboard [Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantrys](/wiki/Low_Altitude_Assault_Transport/infantry "Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry"). As the [gunships](/wiki/Gunship "Gunship") opened [fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire") on the droids, the Jedi resumed their lightsaber battle against the droids, with Mundi deflecting [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") shots and fighting back-to-back with Amidala.[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7)

The [Cerean](/wiki/Cerean "Cerean") helped Fisto get aboard before taking off and escaping the arena. As they flew away, Yoda insisted to Windu and Mundi that they must attempt to prevent Dooku's escape or he would rally more systems to his cause. Windu ordered the [pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") to land in an assembly area in the [desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert"). Mundi continued to fight until the Separatists ordered a full retreat. Mundi was one of the few Jedi to survive the battle at Geonosis, which formally marked the beginning of the Clone Wars.[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7)

#### Calming Mace Windu

:   "*Thank you for taking the time to speak with me, my old friend.*"  
    "*To put your mind at ease is to place the galaxy in steady hands.*"
:   ―Mace Windu and Ki-Adi-Mundi[[19]](#cite_note-Mace_Windu_I-19)

Shortly after the battle of Geonosis, Mundi met with Windu via [hologram](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") to discuss the battle. The two of them discussed how so many Jedi were killed in the battle, and that those Jedi would be missed, but that it was good that they were one with the Force. The two old friends also discussed how the Clone Wars were upon the [galaxy](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy"), with Windu stating he felt uneasy about leading the Clone Army, but stated that sometimes they must be offensive. Mundi then remarking the title of General must be added to the Jedi among their other titles. The two raised their cups to peace and the fallen. Mundi was also at the Council meeting a short time later, [assigning](/wiki/Operation_on_Hissrich "Operation on Hissrich") Windu to go to [Hissrich](/wiki/Hissrich "Hissrich").[[19]](#cite_note-Mace_Windu_I-19)

Later, Mundi was present via hologram for the Council meeting where Windu reported his mission to be a success. However, during the mission, one of [Windu's team](/wiki/Mace_Windu%27s_squad "Mace Windu's squad"), [Prosset Dibs](/wiki/Tenth_Brother "Tenth Brother"), had turned against them and attempted to murder Windu. He was therefore brought before the Council to stand trial for this. Mundi chastised Dibs for his actions, which were deemed both treasonous and heretical, but nevertheless gave him the chance to defend himself. Dibs refused to find fault in his actions, insisting that the Council were the ones in the wrong and they were twisting the Jedi Code for their own selfish gain. Ultimately, Dibs was found guilty of treason and, at the request of Windu, sentenced to confinement in the [Jedi Archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives").[[20]](#cite_note-Mace_Windu_5-20)

Several weeks into the war, Mundi had endured an encounter with a recently emerged Separatist leader, [General](/wiki/General "General") [Grievous](/wiki/Grievous "Grievous"),[[21]](#cite_note-Brotherhood-21) who wounded Mundi,[[22]](#cite_note-The_Comprehensive_Guide_to_the_Star_Wars_Galaxy-22) leaving him with several scars on his cheek. By the time of the [Cadesura disaster](/wiki/Cadesura_disaster "Cadesura disaster") on the planet [Cato Neimoidia](/wiki/Cato_Neimoidia "Cato Neimoidia"), the scars were still healing. Following the disaster, Mundi was present for a Jedi Council meeting concerning Kenobi's upcoming [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Cato_Neimoidia_(Kenobi_and_Skywalker) "Mission to Cato Neimoidia (Kenobi and Skywalker)") to investigate the crisis and listened intently to Kenobi's plan.[[21]](#cite_note-Brotherhood-21)

Although Geonosis had been captured by the Republic at the onset of the Clone Wars, resistance from the [Geonosian](/wiki/Geonosian "Geonosian") natives and their loyalty to Count Dooku proved stronger than the Jedi Order anticipated, and the Jedi soon discovered Separatist leader [Poggle the Lesser](/wiki/Poggle_the_Lesser "Poggle the Lesser") had established several [battle droid](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid") [foundries](/wiki/Factory "Factory") on the planet capable of creating thousands of weapons. The Jedi Council dispatched Mundi, Kenobi, and Unduli to lead a massive [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") army [fleet](/wiki/Fleet "Fleet") to [retake Geonosis](/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Geonosis "Second Battle of Geonosis") and destroy the droid foundries, the destruction of which was viewed as essential to the Republic war effort. Aboard the [bridge](/wiki/Bridge_(starship) "Bridge (starship)") of one of the [Star Destroyers](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer") during the voyage, Mundi and Kenobi lamented at the Republic's failure to recognize how loyal the native Geonosians remained to the Confederacy.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

The Jedi plan their assault of Geonosis.

 

Later, a hologram meeting session was held between Mundi, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, Luminara Unduli, [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano"), Sheev Palpatine, Yoda, and Mace Windu to discuss the battle plan. Mundi, Kenobi and Skywalker were to lead three separate fronts of a three-pronged attack on the [ray shield](/wiki/Ray_shield "Ray shield") generator that protected the [primary droid foundry](/wiki/Geonosis_primary_droid_foundry "Geonosis primary droid foundry") where Poggle had established his command post. Mundi and [Jet](/wiki/Jet "Jet") were to lead a northern advance through the Geonosian defense lines to a [staging area](/wiki/Staging_area "Staging area") called [Point Rain](/wiki/Point_Rain "Point Rain") a short distance from the [shield generator](/wiki/Deflector_shield_generator "Deflector shield generator"). The [troops](/wiki/Troop "Troop") would establish a defensive perimeter around the staging area, where Mundi's [forces](/wiki/Jet%27s_battalion "Jet's battalion") would meet up with those of Kenobi and Skywalker, who would be coming in after leading their forces through central and southern advances, respectively. Once they rendezvoused, the three Jedi were to combine their forces on an attack against the shield generator. Mundi suggested if either of the three forces were met with heavy resistance that prevented them from reaching the staging area on time, the others should wait until they arrive before advancing on the shield generator.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

With their planning complete, the Jedi split up to mobilize forces for the upcoming battle. Shortly after Kenobi and Skywalker's men entered the battle, Mundi boarded an LAAT/i gunship and launched along with Jet and a [company](/wiki/Company "Company") of [clones](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") aboard the *[Resolute](/wiki/Resolute "Resolute")*, the [flagship](/wiki/Flagship "Flagship") of [Admiral](/wiki/Admiral "Admiral") [Wullf Yularen](/wiki/Wullf_Yularen "Wullf Yularen"). Mundi led several other gunships into the battle and were immediately met with extremely heavy anti-artillery batteries. Several of the [ships](/wiki/Starship "Starship") among Mundi's forces were destroyed, and Mundi quickly lost contact with Skywalker after his own gunship was shot down. Many of the ships were carrying [All Terrain Tactical Enforcers](/wiki/All_Terrain_Tactical_Enforcer "All Terrain Tactical Enforcer") and Mundi ordered them to be dropped to the ground right away, before they were all destroyed all with the gunships. Immediately after issuing this order, the gunship was struck by enemy fire and crashed onto the planet surface below. Many of the clones aboard were killed but Jet and Mundi survived, although the Cerean was injured. Jet reported that only Kenobi's forces made it to the staging area, and that Skywalker and his men had crashed were caught in a firefight with the Geonosians.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

Mundi ordered the injured among his men to be loaded onto the AT-TE [walker](/wiki/Walker "Walker"), and then for the forces to move forward toward a nearby bridge, where Mundi hoped to meet up with Skywalker's forces on their way to Kenobi's position. As the forces traveled through a large [caltrop field](/wiki/Caltrop_field "Caltrop field"), Mundi went up ahead and used his lightsaber to cut down the giant caltrops so his walkers could pass them unharmed. Jet requested air support from Yularen, but the admiral denied the request, claiming all [starfighters](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") and other resources were engaged elsewhere in the planetary invasion. Upon arriving at a trail leading up to a clifftop [cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave"), Mundi split up his forces, ordering the AT-TE walkers to take the longer route to Kenobi's position while the Jedi took a squad of 13 clones to investigate what Mundi believed was a shortcut through the cave. Mundi admitted to Jet he did not know for sure the cave would be a faster route, but could only trust his instincts. After entering the cave, which disrupted their communications, Mundi sensed an incoming presence and ordered his men to take defensive positions just as a swarm of winged Geonosian warriors attacked them.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

Several of the clones were swept up and carried deeper into the cave to be killed, but Mundi ordered the men not to stop and continue pushing forward. Despite heavy casualties, Mundi's men cut their way through the cave, and upon reaching the other side, the Cerean ordered his [clone flametroopers](/wiki/Clone_flametrooper "Clone flametrooper") to fire their [flamethrowers](/wiki/Flamethrower "Flamethrower"). Most of the remaining Geonosians were killed, allowing Mundi and his surviving troops to exit the cave. Afterward, Mundi made contact with Skywalker, who informed him the path to the staging area was now clear but that Kenobi's forces were being overwhelmed by the enemy there. Upon a request for air support from Skywalker, Yularen dispatched a [BTL-B Y-wing starfighter/bomber](/wiki/BTL-B_Y-wing_starfighter/bomber "BTL-B Y-wing starfighter/bomber") [squadron](/wiki/Squadron "Squadron"), which bombed the advancing Separatist tanks just as Mundi, Skywalker and Tano arrived at Kenobi's position. The Jedi regrouped and determined that, despite their losses, their combined forces were still sufficient to destroy the shield generator. Skywalker took a small force through the shield barrier and jammed the Geonosians' scanners, which freed the AT-TE walkers to fire upon the shield generator without risk of being targeted. Once the generator was destroyed, Mundi led the rest of the clone troopers into the battle via gunships.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

After they landed, the remaining Geonosians at the ray shield generator were outnumbered by the clones and forced to surrender, marking the end of the initial phase of the battle. The injured Mundi and Kenobi were unable to continue with the rest of the attack, and were to be taken back to the [fleet](/wiki/Republic_Navy "Republic Navy") along with the other injured soldiers for [bacta tank](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank") treatment. Skywalker was left in charge of the remaining [Republic forces](/wiki/Republic_Military "Republic Military"), which were to set out for Poggle's primary droid foundry. Before departing the planet, Mundi overheard Skywalker and Tano boasting about their number of kills during the battle, and the Cerean noted that his own kill total of 65, which was greater than that of either of the two other Jedi and thus earned Skywalker's respect. Mundi and the rest of the injured left the planet,[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11) while Skywalker and Unduli later led a successful effort to destroy the primary droid foundry.[[23]](#cite_note-Weapons_Factory-23)

By the time Mundi recovered from his injuries in the bacta tank, he was aboard a Jedi [transport](/wiki/Transport "Transport") docked at a Geonosian landing zone. Anxious to return to the mission at hand, Mundi reviewed strategic charts and maps to help continue the search for Poggle, who had escaped the primary droid foundry. Mundi and Kenobi were contacted via [holocomm](/wiki/Holocomm "Holocomm") by Unduli, who had been tracking Poggle and believed he was hiding at the nearby the [Progate Temple](/wiki/Progate_Temple "Progate Temple"). Mundi felt that location was unlikely because little would have survived the first wave of Republic attacks there, which would leave Poggle to exposure at the temple. Unduli nevertheless decided to investigate, despite Mundi's warnings against it due to an incoming [sandstorm](/wiki/Sandstorm "Sandstorm"). The storm left the Jedi unable to leave the transport and provide Unduli assistance and, when Skywalker grew concerned that she failed to report in several hours later, Mundi urged him to have patience.[[24]](#cite_note-LegacyTerror-24)

When Unduli finally reported back, she explained Poggle had been tracked to the temple, and her holocomm transmission cut out just as she was attacked by unseen assailants. Skywalker wished to rescue her immediately, but Mundi and Kenobi said they had no choice but to wait for the sandstorm to die down. Later, Mundi stayed behind while Kenobi and Skywalker led forces into the temple to rescue her. After getting attacked by Geonosians infected with [brain worms](/wiki/Brain_worm "Brain worm"), the Jedi tried to send clone [troopers](/wiki/Trooper "Trooper") to Mundi for reinforcements, but they were killed and the message never reached him. Kenobi and Skywalker ultimately succeeded in rescuing Unduli and taking Poggle [prisoner](/wiki/Prisoner "Prisoner").[[24]](#cite_note-LegacyTerror-24)

#### Attack on the Jedi Temple

 [![Mundi attends the funeral for the six deceased Jedi.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/TempleBombingFuneral.png/revision/latest?cb=20131027060205) [![Mundi attends the funeral for the six deceased Jedi.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/TempleBombingFuneral.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/330?cb=20131027060205)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/TempleBombingFuneral.png/revision/latest?cb=20131027060205) 

Mundi attends the funeral for the six deceased Jedi.

 

Later that year, the Jedi Temple was [attacked](/wiki/Bombing_of_the_Jedi_Temple_hangar "Bombing of the Jedi Temple hangar") by an unknown assailant. The attack killed six Jedi, maintenance crew and clone soldiers within the Jedi Temple [hangar](/wiki/Hangar "Hangar"). As a result of this attack, [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") Yoda contacted Skywalker and Tano, recalling them from the [battle](/wiki/Defense_of_Cato_Neimoidia "Defense of Cato Neimoidia") on [Cato Neimoidia](/wiki/Cato_Neimoidia "Cato Neimoidia") to question witnesses and lead the investigation. In the [Council chamber](/wiki/Jedi_Council_Chamber "Jedi Council Chamber"), the Council briefed Skywalker and Tano as to what they needed to do. Windu told them to be on alert and to not make any assumptions. The Council also aired out their concern that someone within the Jedi would have turned to the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") and committed the act. Soon after, it was discovered that [anti-Jedi](/wiki/Anti-Jedi_sentiment "Anti-Jedi sentiment") activist [Letta Turmond](/wiki/Letta_Turmond "Letta Turmond") fed her [husband](/wiki/Marriage "Marriage"), Jedi Temple worker [Jackar Bowmani](/wiki/Jackar_Bowmani "Jackar Bowmani"), [nano-droids](/wiki/NM-K_reconstitutor "NM-K reconstitutor"), which unwittingly caused him to explode, thus causing the damage to the hangar bay.[[33]](#cite_note-Sabotage-33) Shortly after Turmond's arrest, Mundi and the rest of the Council attended a joint [funeral](/wiki/Funeral_of_the_Jedi_Temple_bombing_victims "Funeral of the Jedi Temple bombing victims") for the six Jedi—including Jedi Knight [Tutso Mara](/wiki/Tutso_Mara "Tutso Mara")—that perished.[[34]](#cite_note-The_Jedi_Who_Knew_Too_Much-34)

#### Fugitive Padawan

:   "*Your Padawan status will be stripped from you, and you shall forfeit all rank and privileges within the Grand Army of the Republic. You will be turned over to the Republic court to await your trial, and whatever punishment they will set for you. Henceforth, you are barred from the Jedi Order.*"
:   ―Ki-Adi-Mundi informing Ahsoka Tano of her expulsion from the Jedi Order and transfer to Republic custody[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35)

Soon after, the Council learned that Tano had been arrested by [Clone Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") [Fox](/wiki/Fox "Fox") after she allegedly murdered the [imprisoned](/wiki/Prison "Prison") Turmond via the Force. Soon after that, Tano broke free and [escaped](/wiki/Escape_from_the_Republic_military_base "Escape from the Republic military base") a [Republic military base](/wiki/Imperial_Inspectorate_HQ "Imperial Inspectorate HQ"), eventually reaching the [portal](/wiki/Underworld_portal "Underworld portal") to the [Coruscant Underworld](/wiki/Coruscant_Underworld "Coruscant Underworld"). Skywalker, Fox and Rex failed to re-apprehend Tano as she made her getaway.[[34]](#cite_note-The_Jedi_Who_Knew_Too_Much-34)

After that, the Council held a meeting to discuss the course of action to take. Koon and Skywalker were sent with [Commander](/wiki/Commander "Commander") [Wolffe](/wiki/Wolffe "Wolffe") and Rex to find Tano and bring her back into Jedi custody. Their search led them to the Coruscant Underworld level of [Level 1315](/wiki/Level_1315 "Level 1315"), where Tano was found in a [warehouse](/wiki/Abandoned_munitions_warehouse "Abandoned munitions warehouse") with crates full of nano-droids.[[36]](#cite_note-TCAJ-36)

A little while later, the Council received a transmission from Admiral [Wilhuff Tarkin](/wiki/Wilhuff_Tarkin "Wilhuff Tarkin"), who requested, on behalf of the Senate, that Tano be expelled from the Jedi Order so that she could be dealt with by the Republic in a more easier fashion. Once the transmission ended, Kenobi strongly suggested that the Council side with Tano,[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35) but Mundi, [Saesee Tiin](/wiki/Saesee_Tiin "Saesee Tiin"), and Windu refused,[[37]](#cite_note-Ultimate_Star_Wars-37) noting that in addition to the evidence against her, failing to comply could be regarded as an act of opposition to the Senate. Yoda then stated that Tano should come before them in the [Chamber of Judgment](/wiki/Chamber_of_Judgment "Chamber of Judgment") alongside her master, Skywalker.[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35)

Mundi was a part of that meeting, seated alongside Kenobi, Windu, Koon, Yoda, and [Shaak Ti](/wiki/Shaak_Ti "Shaak Ti"). Although Tano insisted she was innocent, Mundi remained unconvinced and pointed out the fact that Tano was alone with Letta Turmond when she died. Ultimately, Windu closed the matter by formally expelling Tano from the Jedi Order. As Tano's platform lowered, Mundi informed her that she was banished from the Jedi Order, and therefore stripped of her Padawan status. In addition, her battlefield privileges were revoked and she was subsequently remanded to the custody of the Republic.[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35)

Afterward, a military [trial](/wiki/Trial_of_Ahsoka_Tano "Trial of Ahsoka Tano") was held for Tano. But before the verdict could be given out for Tano, Skywalker burst into the room and revealed that the true culprit that framed Tano was Offee, who openly confessed to her crimes and claimed that the Jedi had fallen to the dark side by fighting in the Clone Wars. As a result, Offee was imprisoned while Tano was cleared of all charges.[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35)

Following Tano's acquittal, the Council personally invited her to rejoin the Order, with Mundi and Tiin praising her efforts to prove her innocence, and Windu observing her ordeal as the [great trial](/wiki/Jedi_Trials "Jedi Trials") in her path to Jedi Knighthood.[[8]](#cite_note-Bio_gallery-8) But, to Mundi and the others' surprise, Tano refused to come back, having become disillusioned with both herself and the Jedi as a result of the Council's lack of faith in her.[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35)

#### Tyranus revealed

Later that year, while on a [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Oba_Diah_(Clone_Wars) "Mission to Oba Diah (Clone Wars)") to uncover the secrets regarding the [death](/wiki/Mission_to_Oba_Diah_(Sifo-Dyas) "Mission to Oba Diah (Sifo-Dyas)") of Master [Sifo-Dyas](/wiki/Sifo-Dyas "Sifo-Dyas"), Skywalker and Kenobi learned that the mysterious "Tyranus," who helped to create the clone army was in fact Dooku. The Council realized the implications of this, that Dooku had helped to create the army he was fighting against, but decided to keep these revelations a secret as it would damage the war effort, and the Jedi trusted the clones they'd fought alongside.[[38]](#cite_note-tLO-38)

#### Rescuing Quinlan Vos

:   "*Many lives will be lost because of what Quinlan Vos has done. Yet again, the Order has been shaken to its core at a time when we can ill afford anything but solidarity and faith in one another.*"
:   ―Ki-Adi-Mundi[[40]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-40)

Later that year, Mundi attended a Council meeting to discuss rescuing Master [Quinlan Vos](/wiki/Quinlan_Vos "Quinlan Vos"), who had been captured by Dooku while on an undercover [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Raxus_Secundus "Mission to Raxus Secundus") to assassinate the Separatist [leader](/wiki/Head_of_State "Head of State"). Mundi was also aware about the damage that Dooku's new right-hand man "Admiral Enigma" (who was actually Vos) had inflicted on the Republic. He advocated capturing him in order to prevent another Sith Lord from being let loose in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"). After learning from Admiral Yularen that Enigma's [*Providence*-class Dreadnought](/wiki/Providence-class_Dreadnought "Providence-class Dreadnought") was planning to attack [Taris](/wiki/Taris "Taris"), the Council decided to authorize a [mission](/wiki/Rescue_of_Quinlan_Vos "Rescue of Quinlan Vos") to rescue Vos. In addition, the Council agreed to pardon [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress"), Dooku's former [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice") in return for helping to rescue Vos.[[40]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-40)

Following the rescue of Vos, Mundi was present at a Council meeting where Vos was assigned to lead a Jedi and Republic mission to [raid](/wiki/Destruction_of_the_Separatist_supply_storage_base "Destruction of the Separatist supply storage base") a [Separatist supply storage base](/wiki/Separatist_supply_storage_base "Separatist supply storage base") for supplies. Mundi reported that the base contained medications, weapons, ship repair materials, and [foodstuffs](/wiki/Foodstuff "Foodstuff") that would be of valuable assistance to desperate worlds. Unknown to the Council, Vos was secretly colluding with Dooku in order to discover the identity of Darth Sidious so that he could destroy the Sith Order. Vos leaked intelligence of the Republic raid to Dooku and planted explosives which destroyed much of the supplies in the [asteroid](/wiki/Asteroid "Asteroid").[[40]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-40)

After the failed asteroid heist and another [failed raid](/wiki/Destruction_of_the_Vanqor_listening_station "Destruction of the Vanqor listening station") on a [Separatist](/wiki/Vanqor_listening_station "Vanqor listening station") [listening post](/wiki/Listening_post "Listening post") on Vanqor, Mundi took part in a Council emergency meeting to discuss Vos' [treason](/wiki/Treason "Treason") against the Jedi. During the meeting, he cited Offee's actions as a sign that the Jedi had a problem with traitors within their own ranks. He supported Windu's suggestion that they should execute Vos as a deterrent. When Kenobi recommended banishing him, Mundi argued that Vos posed a serious threat to the galaxy due to status as a highly-trained Jedi Master who had turned to the dark side. In the end, the Council decided to test Vos' loyalty by sending him on a mission to kill Dooku.[[40]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-40)

Later, Mundi was present when the Council deliberated including Ventress in this mission. Mundi supported Vos' arguments that Ventress should come along on the grounds that the Council had given her a full pardon. Ventress informed the Council that Dooku was last sighted in a Separatist dreadnought above [Christophsis](/wiki/Christophsis "Christophsis"). The Council's suspicions were proven when Vos failed to kill Dooku and instead attempted to force the Sith Lord to lead him to Darth Sidious. Kenobi and the Skywalker apprehended Vos but the turncoat Jedi and Dooku escaped to Christophsis, leading to the events of the [Second battle of Christophsis](/wiki/Second_battle_of_Christophsis "Second battle of Christophsis"); which ended in Vos' redemption.[[40]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-40)

Before his departure from Coruscant,[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_III-9) Mundi sent Jedi Knights [Tualon Yaluna](/wiki/Tualon_Yaluna "Tualon Yaluna"), [Iskat Akaris](/wiki/Thirteenth_Sister "Thirteenth Sister") and [Sunghi Silpari](/wiki/Sunghi_Silpari "Sunghi Silpari") on a [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Frong "Mission to Frong") to [Frong](/wiki/Frong "Frong"), noting the possibility that Grievous was there and ordering them to capture the General if he was.[[46]](#cite_note-Inquisitor:_Rise_of_the_Red_Blade-46) As the sieges went on, however, Grievous led a counteroffensive with a Confederate fleet into Coruscant, kidnapped Chancellor Palpatine, and took him hostage aboard his ship, the *[Invisible Hand](/wiki/Invisible_Hand "Invisible Hand")*. In the ensuing [battle](/wiki/Battle_of_Coruscant "Battle of Coruscant"), Generals Skywalker and Kenobi managed to retrieve the captive Chancellor, killing Dooku in the process.[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_III-9)

In the wake of the battle, Palpatine appointed Skywalker as his personal representative on the Jedi Council, hoping to increase his influence over the body and further ingratiate himself with Anakin and lure him to the dark side. The Council begrudgingly accepted Palpatine's interference, but they refused to grant Skywalker the rank of Master, infuriating him. Furthermore, the Council assigned Skywalker the unofficial task of spying on the Chancellor, an assignment that Skywalker was less than enthusiastic about because he considered Palpatine to be a friend and mentor. Mundi then reported that, despite surveying all Republic systems, they had found no sign of Grievous. When Yoda recommended searching the outlying systems, Mundi reminded him of the Separatist attack on the [Wookiees](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee") of [Kashyyyk](/wiki/Kashyyyk "Kashyyyk"). To this, Yoda said he would go to Kashyyyk himself, as he had good relations with the Wookiees.[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_III-9)

Just days after Grievous's death at the hands of General Kenobi, the Council confirmed its suspicions that Chancellor Palpatine was in fact, Darth Sidious. ### Legacy

:   "*Ki-Adi-Mundi? Was that the one with the pointy head? I never paid much attention to history--I was more concerned with the future.*"
:   ―A [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") [stormtrooper sergeant](/wiki/Unidentified_First_Order_stormtrooper_sergeant "Unidentified First Order stormtrooper sergeant")[[50]](#cite_note-GE2-50)

On Mygeeto, Mundi's lightsaber would find its way into the hands of Banking Clan [Vice Chairman](/wiki/Vice_Chairman "Vice Chairman") [Anolo](/wiki/Anolo "Anolo"), only to be stolen by the bounty hunter [Greedo](/wiki/Greedo "Greedo") during the [trade wars](/wiki/Trade_wars "Trade wars"). Upon Greedo's return to [Jabba's Palace](/wiki/Boba_Fett%27s_Palace "Boba Fett's Palace"), he offered it to [Jabba Desilijic Tiure](/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure "Jabba Desilijic Tiure"), who regarded it as useless until the [collector](/wiki/Collector "Collector") [Dok-Ondar](/wiki/Dok-Ondar "Dok-Ondar") offered to buy it.[[50]](#cite_note-GE2-50) Three years before the [Battle of Yavin](/wiki/Battle_of_Yavin "Battle of Yavin"), Mundi's lightsaber was seen in former Jedi [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Kanan Jarrus](/wiki/Kanan_Jarrus "Kanan Jarrus")' vision of the Jedi Temple's [dojo](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_dojo "Jedi Temple dojo") during his second visit to [Lothal](/wiki/Lothal "Lothal")'s [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Lothal_Jedi_Temple "Lothal Jedi Temple").[[51]](#cite_note-SoD-51) The real lightsaber remained in Ondar's possession and was shown to members of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") during the [First Order-Resistance War](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War").[[50]](#cite_note-GE2-50)

## Personality and traits

:   "*If he does not give up his emergency powers after the destruction of Grievous, then he should be removed from office.*"
:   ―Ki-Adi-Mundi, on Supreme Chancellor Palpatine[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_III-9)

Ki-Adi-Mundi was a Cerean male who had [white](/wiki/Color "Color") [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"), yellow [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye"), light skin,[[3]](#cite_note-Mundi_Databank-3) and a height of 1.98 [meters](/wiki/Meter "Meter") (6 [feet](/wiki/Foot "Foot"), 6 inches)[[4]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-4). In his younger years, he had brown hair.[[5]](#cite_note-The_Acolyte_4-5) He was a logical and methodical Jedi Master who was well-known for his wisdom,[[52]](#cite_note-Character_Encyclopedia-52) his noble thinking,[[53]](#cite_note-SWTVE-53) and his courage. Like other Cereans, he was highly intelligent due to his binary brain, which allowed him to observe both sides of a situation simultaneously.[[52]](#cite_note-Character_Encyclopedia-52) His wise way of thinking made him an excellent strategist. When Jedi Masters needed advice, they often sought Mundi's opinion.[[54]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-54)

 [![Logical and wise, Ki-Adi-Mundi became a Jedi General during the Clone Wars.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5d/KiAdiMundiMygeeto-GalaxysEdge2.png/revision/latest?cb=20210531070604) [![Logical and wise, Ki-Adi-Mundi became a Jedi General during the Clone Wars.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5d/KiAdiMundiMygeeto-GalaxysEdge2.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/280?cb=20210531070604)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/5d/KiAdiMundiMygeeto-GalaxysEdge2.png/revision/latest?cb=20210531070604) 

Logical and wise, Ki-Adi-Mundi became a Jedi General during the Clone Wars.

 

While Mundi established a good working relationship with Jedi Master Yarael Poof, he often found his colleague's sense of humor irritating.[[16]](#cite_note-The_Living_Force-16) In Mundi's opinion, the adventurous natures of Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker and his Padawan Ahsoka Tano were unusual.[[52]](#cite_note-Character_Encyclopedia-52) During the second battle of Geonosis, Mundi stated to Wullf Yularen "there is no such thing as luck," showing he strongly believed that success was a matter of effort and preparation. However, he didn't reject the concept entirely, as he later told his troops they needed "some luck" to reach the landing zone on Geonosis.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11) Like his fellow Jedi Master Luminara Unduli, he was disturbed by Skywalker's brutal interrogation methods on Poggle the Lesser shortly after Poggle's capture during the Second Battle of Geonosis.[[3]](#cite_note-Mundi_Databank-3) Mundi had a friendly rapport with fellow Jedi Master [Yarael Poof](/wiki/Yarael_Poof "Yarael Poof"), who contrasted Mundi's stoic nature with a more humorous disposition. Despite this rapport, Mundi found Poof's demeanor unbefitting of a member of the Jedi High Council.[[55]](#cite_note-LivingForce-55) However, Mundi himself was open to lightheartedness even in dire situations, willing to participate in the game with Anakin Skywalker to see who was able to destroy more battle droids during the second battle on Geonosis.[[11]](#cite_note-LaPR-11)

Mundi was one of the more conservative members of the Jedi Council.[[4]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-4) Due to his strict adherence to logic,[[54]](#cite_note-Secrets_of_the_Jedi-54) Mundi had a tendency to refute unlikely events,[[49]](#cite_note-SWTCVDNE-49) and though he was a contemplative master, he had a weakness for jumping to favored conclusions.[[14]](#cite_note-AcolyteVD-14) He refused to believe the galaxy could change as quickly as it did during the Republic's chaotic final years;[[4]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-4) for example, he doubted Qui-Gon Jinn's revelation that the Sith had returned,[[3]](#cite_note-Mundi_Databank-3) arguing that such a notion was "impossible" because they had been widely believed to be extinct for a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium"). He was also dubious about Count Dooku's possible involvement in an assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala, believing he was incapable of violence or murder because of his Jedi past.[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_I-6) When Yoda talked to Qui-Gon Jinn's spirit, Mundi was skeptical, pointing out the fact that a person, once becoming one with the [Cosmic Force](/wiki/Cosmic_Force "Cosmic Force") upon death, lost their individuality.[[39]](#cite_note-Voices-39) When Ahsoka Tano was framed for treason, the evidence against her, as well as the wish to keep the relationship between the Jedi Order and the Galactic Senate undamaged, led Mundi and the Council to expel Tano from the Order and turn her over to the Republic military. However, after her innocence was proven and the real traitor apprehended, Mundi joined his colleagues in offering to reinstate Tano into the Order, praising her efforts to prove her innocence and stating that she had shown the "true sign of a Jedi Knight."[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35) He was aghast when she instead chose to walk away from the Order.[[8]](#cite_note-Bio_gallery-8) Mundi was the only Jedi who called attention to the powerful bond between Master Yoda and Count Dooku, theorizing that the Sith would try and take advantage of that, a suspicion which turned out to be correct.[[39]](#cite_note-Voices-39)

## Behind the scenes

### Concept and design

:   "*When I first saw the design, I just thought it was amazing, because it incorporates so many different things. To me, he looks part Native American and part European, and he's got a wonderful, wise face. Once I'd seen that design, I had a pretty good idea of what he should be like.*"
:   ―Silas Carson[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)

 [![Concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi for The Phantom Menace.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/MundiConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100219055358) [![Concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi for The Phantom Menace.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/MundiConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20100219055358)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/MundiConceptArt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100219055358) 

Concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi for *The Phantom Menace*.

 

Ki-Adi-Mundi was designed by artistic director and concept designer [Iain McCaig](/wiki/Iain_McCaig "Iain McCaig") for the [1999](/wiki/1999 "1999") film [*Star Wars*: Episode I *The Phantom Menace*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_I_The_Phantom_Menace "Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace"), the first installment of the *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [prequel trilogy](/wiki/Prequel_trilogy "Prequel trilogy"). The design for what eventually became Mundi began when *Star Wars* creator and executive producer [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas") asked McCaig to draw sketches for alien characters to be featured on the Jedi High Council. In doing so, McCaig experimented by drawing some unusual parodies of the elderly Obi-Wan Kenobi, including one with whale-like features, and an elongated head with ridges along the back of the neck.[[59]](#cite_note-Art_of_Episode_I-59)

While drawing early conceptual sketches of the Jedi characters for the [2005](/wiki/2005 "2005") film [*Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_III_Revenge_of_the_Sith "Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith"), the final installment in the trilogy, concept artist [Derek Thompson](/wiki/Derek_Thompson "Derek Thompson") proposed a version of Mundi that had lost his left eye during the battles of the Clone Wars and now wore an [eye patch](/wiki/Eye_patch/Legends "Eye patch/Legends") along with a black and gold cape. The concept was ultimately not used in the final film.[[60]](#cite_note-Art_of_Episode_III-60)

### Film portrayal

:   "*I enjoy playing this very wise man who is part of the great council. You get the feeling that he is kind of like your uncle, because he's this nice, kind, wise, protective master. I always think that way of him.*"
:   ―[Silas Carson](/wiki/Silas_Carson "Silas Carson")[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)

 [![Ultimately unused concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi from Revenge of the Sith](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9f/Ki_adi_mundi_eyepatch_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110926221043) [![Ultimately unused concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi from Revenge of the Sith](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9f/Ki_adi_mundi_eyepatch_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/120?cb=20110926221043)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/9f/Ki_adi_mundi_eyepatch_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110926221043) 

Ultimately unused concept art of Ki-Adi-Mundi from *Revenge of the Sith*

 

Ki-Adi-Mundi was portrayed by [Silas Carson](/wiki/Silas_Carson "Silas Carson") in all three prequel films. Carson originally auditioned for the supporting role of a fighter pilot role, but the character was eliminated from the script a few days after the audition.[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)[[61]](#cite_note-EHerald-61) However, George Lucas screened a video of Silas' audition and was so impressed he encouraged the actor to accept the role of Mundi,[[61]](#cite_note-EHerald-61) which led him to play the other parts as well. In addition to appearing on-screen, Carson also performed Mundi's voice, mimicking that of an elderly man who speaks slowly and deliberately. During the filming of *The Phantom Menace*, executive producer George Lucas had not yet decided whether he would keep Carson's voice or dub in that of another actor, as he had done for several other characters, but Lucas found he liked Carson's vocal performance and decided to keep it.[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)[[62]](#cite_note-HendryS-62) To create Mundi's movements, Carson spent time with elderly people and watched the ways they moved and spoke. In crafting Mundi's manner of speech, Carson said he spoke slowly not only because of the Jedi's age, but because he believed Mundi was wise and would not waste his words.[[63]](#cite_note-SWI44-63)

 [![Ki-Adi-Mundi was portrayed in the prequel trilogy by Silas Carson, who also played Nute Gunray,  and .](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/64/Lieutenant_Williams.png/revision/latest?cb=20130117233532) [![Ki-Adi-Mundi was portrayed in the prequel trilogy by Silas Carson, who also played Nute Gunray,  and .](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/64/Lieutenant_Williams.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20130117233532)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/64/Lieutenant_Williams.png/revision/latest?cb=20130117233532) 

Ki-Adi-Mundi was portrayed in the prequel trilogy by Silas Carson, who also played Nute Gunray, [Lott Dod](/wiki/Lott_Dod "Lott Dod") and [Antidar Williams](/wiki/Antidar_Williams "Antidar Williams").

 

Lucas did not give Carson any backstory from which to build his performance as Mundi, but simply left it up to the actor to create. Once the shooting of each film was completed, Carson did not know right away whether he would be returning for the next film. According to *[Star Wars Insider](/wiki/Star_Wars_Insider "Star Wars Insider")* writer [Scott Chernoff](/wiki/Scott_Chernoff "Scott Chernoff"), Lucas, producer [Rick McCallum](/wiki/Rick_McCallum "Rick McCallum") and casting director [Robin Gurland](/wiki/Robin_Gurland "Robin Gurland") were so impressed with Carson's four performances in *The Phantom Menace* that they decided to expand upon Mundi's role in the second film.[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)

The Mundi costume and prosthetics were designed by the [Industrial Light & Magic](/wiki/Industrial_Light_%26_Magic "Industrial Light & Magic") visual effects creature shop. They included a prosthetic head, fake hair and two cheek pieces that were built and glued onto Carson's face so they would actually move along with his facial reactions.[[63]](#cite_note-SWI44-63)[[64]](#cite_note-SWI88-64) During the first film, it took about four hours to apply Mundi's make-up and prosthesis,[[63]](#cite_note-SWI44-63) but in the later films the process was narrowed down to three hours. However, while the process took longer to prepare for Mundi than it did for Nute Gunray or Lott Dod, Carson said the Mundi make-up was less hot and more comfortable to wear than those other character and that he would often walk around off-set and forget he had the make-up on at all.[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)[[63]](#cite_note-SWI44-63) During filming for the [2002](/wiki/2002 "2002") film [*Star Wars*: Episode II *Attack of the Clones*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_II_Attack_of_the_Clones "Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones"), the make-up artists used a different brand of make-up glues and removers, causing Silas' skin to break out due to allergies. Silas was trained by stunt coordinator [Nick Gillard](/wiki/Nick_Gillard "Nick Gillard") for his lightsaber scenes in *Attack of the Clones*. Upon learning Mundi would be fighting in that film, Carson worked out a great deal before filming began to get physically fit, and claimed he was even outrunning some of the [stunt performers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stunt_performer "wikipedia:Stunt performer"). For these scenes, Carson decided that although Mundi appeared slow and frail at normal times, he should be fast and strong while fighting, although the actor struggled to maintain the balance between those two different aspects of the character.[[58]](#cite_note-SWI55-58)

 [![Ki-Adi-Mundi is shown with a green-bladed lightsaber, while he has a blue lightsaber in all other scenes.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/30/Ki-adi-mundi_green_lightsaber.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110926223511) [![Ki-Adi-Mundi is shown with a green-bladed lightsaber, while he has a blue lightsaber in all other scenes.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/30/Ki-adi-mundi_green_lightsaber.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20110926223511)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/30/Ki-adi-mundi_green_lightsaber.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110926223511) 

Ki-Adi-Mundi is shown with a green-bladed lightsaber, while he has a blue lightsaber in all other scenes.

 

Mundi is the only sitting Jedi High Council member other than Yoda and Windu to have a speaking role in the final cut of the first three films.[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_III-9)[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_I-6) In *Attack of the Clones*, Mundi can be seen fighting with a green-bladed lightsaber during a shot when he stands back-to-back with Padmé Amidala. The character uses a blue-bladed lightsaber in every other shot in the film, including those that come immediately before and after that particular shot.[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_II-7) [Pablo Hidalgo](/wiki/Pablo_Hidalgo "Pablo Hidalgo") decided to tackle the contradiction by writing it into lore that Mundi's lightsaber had two kyber crystals, one blue and the other green, after his dual brain bonded with two crystals. However, he did admit it did raise the question of why the Geonosians returned him his lightsaber after capturing him.[[65]](#cite_note-Pablo_Mundi-65)

A scene was partially filmed for *Attack of the Clones* in which Mundi and Plo Koon led a group of Jedi on a raid of a Trade Federation ship during the battle of Geonosis.
