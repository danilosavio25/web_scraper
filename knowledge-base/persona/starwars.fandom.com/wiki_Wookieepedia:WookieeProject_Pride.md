**WookieeProject: Pride** is a [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") dedicated to improving our [in-universe](/wiki/In-universe "In-universe") articles about [Canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") [**characters who are LGBTQIA+**](/wiki/Category:LGBTQIA%2B_individuals "Category:LGBTQIA+ individuals"), and out-of-universe articles about **LGBTQIA+** ***[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")*** **contributors**, such as artists and actors. LGBTQIA+ is an initialism for lesbian, gay, bisexual, trans, queer, intersex, asexual/aromantic, and other related communities and identities.

[Wookieepedians](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") who wish to participate do not have to identify as LGBTQIA+; outing yourself is not required to participate. However, we do request that you already support and have knowledge regarding LGBTQIA+ rights and representation. This is for the safety and comfort of those involved, and because it is the basis of this project. We encourage those who are comfortable doing so to state your pronouns, both to prevent misgendering and to help foster an environment where pronoun-sharing is normalized so others feel safe to share.

Thank you to **[Robin Pronovost](/wiki/Robin_Pronovost "Robin Pronovost")** who graciously gave permission to use their artwork for this project!

## Project goals

Our initial goals are expanding [stubs](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Stub "Wookieepedia:Stub") and addressing [maintenance](/wiki/Category:Wookieepedia_maintenance "Category:Wookieepedia maintenance") issues in articles listed on our [current projects tab](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride_Projects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride Projects"). We also seek to promote articles to [featured](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_articles "Wookieepedia:Featured articles"), [good](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_articles "Wookieepedia:Good articles"), or [comprehensive](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_articles "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive articles") status—peer-reviewed articles that are highlighted to readers as examples of the best work on Wookieepedia. Elevating these pages to "status articles" will better represent the contributions of LGBTQIA+ authors and artists to the franchise and reflect the importance of LGBTQIA+ representation within these stories. Check out the [current status articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride/Status_Articles "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride/Status Articles") and nominations!

This WookieeProject is focused not on fighting what we hate, but [saving what we love](/wiki/Rose_Tico "Rose Tico"). However, some articles do not represent the community well; it may be due to how they're written or because the source material is problematic. We intend to address these articles as they're identified rather than focusing our attention on them.

Though WookieeProjects are dedicated to articles, we will also discuss and propose site policies that better represent and protect this community. No one should have to feel alone when they're advocating for change. If a consensus is reached here regarding a policy proposal, it will then be taken to the Senate Hall for Wook-wide discussion.

**[Toqgers](/wiki/User:Toqgers "User:Toqgers")** (any pronouns), a long-time advocate for change on Wookieepedia, has maintained a thorough Google spreadsheet on [Canon LGBT Star Wars Characters](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1aNJidiAx8kX8kBssdy9KKehnBZYqYLIf9I18rEEVOS4/edit) for *years* now, which has helped guide this project's canon coverage.

***Our initial focus is:***

* **[Chelli Lona Aphra](/wiki/Chelli_Lona_Aphra "Chelli Lona Aphra")**. For Pride Month 2021, we collaborated with [Project Stardust](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust") and [WookieeProject Galaxy's Edge](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Galaxy%27s_Edge "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Galaxy's Edge") to un-doom the article for the official *Star Wars* disaster lesbian, in: [***Project Stardust: Chelli Lona Aphra***](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust:_Chelli_Lona_Aphra "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust: Chelli Lona Aphra")! Someday, she won't be doomed... someday.
* **[Ferus Olin](/wiki/Ferus_Olin "Ferus Olin")**, former featured article to [restore to status](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride_Projects/Ferus_Olin "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride Projects/Ferus Olin")
* [Expanding stub articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride_Projects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride Projects"), especially those about real folks
