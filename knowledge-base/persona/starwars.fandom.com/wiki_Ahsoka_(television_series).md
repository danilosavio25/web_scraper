## Chronological information

### Timeline

[9 ABY](/wiki/9_ABY "9 ABY")[[15]](#cite_note-date-15)

[[Source]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Template:TelevisionSeries)

:   "*Today, I'm excited to share with you that Jon and Dave are concurrently developing two new spinoff series exclusively for Disney+: one, The Rangers of the New Republic, and the other featuring fan-favorite Ahsoka Tano. Set within the timeline of The Mandalorian, these interconnected shows, along with future stories, will excite new audiences, embrace our most passionate fans, and will culminate in a climactic story event.*"
:   ―President Kathleen Kennedy announces *Ahsoka* during Disney Investor Day 2020[[16]](#cite_note-Disney_Investor-16)

***Star Wars: Ahsoka*** is a live-action television series that was first announced on [December 10](/wiki/December_10 "December 10"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"). Its [first season](/wiki/Ahsoka_Season_1 "Ahsoka Season 1") streamed exclusively on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") in [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023"). It is written by [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni") and produced by him and [Jon Favreau](/wiki/Jon_Favreau "Jon Favreau") and stars [Rosario Dawson](/wiki/Rosario_Dawson "Rosario Dawson") as [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano"). It is set in [9 ABY](/wiki/9_ABY "9 ABY") after Ahsoka Tano's appearance in *[The Mandalorian](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Mandalorian "Star Wars: The Mandalorian")* [season two](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_Season_Two "The Mandalorian Season Two") and follows her search for [Imperial](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") [Grand Admiral](/wiki/Grand_Admiral "Grand Admiral") [Thrawn](/wiki/Mitth%27raw%27nuruodo "Mitth'raw'nuruodo") started in the epilogue of the animated series *[Star Wars Rebels](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels "Star Wars Rebels")*. A [second season](/wiki/Ahsoka_Season_2 "Ahsoka Season 2") was announced on [January 9](/wiki/January_9 "January 9"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024") and the series' story will be concluded by an [upcoming untitled film](/wiki/Untitled_Star_Wars_film_(Dave_Filoni) "Untitled Star Wars film (Dave Filoni)") directed by Filoni.

## Official description[]

*Set after the fall of the Empire,* Ahsoka *follows the former Jedi knight Ahsoka Tano as she investigates an emerging threat to a vulnerable galaxy.*[[17]](#cite_note-D23_showcase-17)

## Development[]

:   "Ahsoka *is a continuous story. It is definitely driving toward a goal, in my mind, as opposed to being little singular adventures. That's what I want the character to be doing, and I think that's what fans want now. They have such a relationship with her. I've only recently started to understand that all those kids that watched Clone Wars are now a lot older—they're very excited about all the things they grew up with, as they should be.*"
:   ―Dave Filoni[[18]](#cite_note-Vanity_Fair-18)

 [![Jon Favreau, Dave Filoni, and Kathleen Kennedy presenting a production still from Ahsoka at D23 Expo 2022](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/63/Ahsoka_D23.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20221016211312) [![Jon Favreau, Dave Filoni, and Kathleen Kennedy presenting a production still from Ahsoka at D23 Expo 2022](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/63/Ahsoka_D23.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20221016211312)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/63/Ahsoka_D23.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20221016211312) 

Jon Favreau, Dave Filoni, and Kathleen Kennedy presenting a production still from *Ahsoka* at D23 Expo 2022

 

Rumors of a live-action [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano")–led series first circulated on the internet back in [March](/wiki/March "March") of [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"),[[19]](#cite_note-Manabyte-19) just weeks after [Disney](/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company "The Walt Disney Company") CEO Bob Iger hinted at an earnings call that spinoffs of characters from *[Star Wars: The Mandalorian](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Mandalorian "Star Wars: The Mandalorian")* were in development.[[20]](#cite_note-The_HR-20) When Tano made her live-action debut in "[Chapter 13: The Jedi](/wiki/Chapter_13:_The_Jedi "Chapter 13: The Jedi")" in [*The Mandalorian* Season Two](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_Season_Two "The Mandalorian Season Two"), she became a character that could be the focus of a *Mandalorian* spin-off,[[21]](#cite_note-Chapter_13-21) and the series was officially announced by [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") President [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy") on an investors day presentation on [December 10](/wiki/December_10 "December 10"), 2020. A [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") post later that day confirmed that [Jon Favreau](/wiki/Jon_Favreau "Jon Favreau") and [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni") would executive produce the series, and that it would be written by Filoni and star [Rosario Dawson](/wiki/Rosario_Dawson "Rosario Dawson").[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)

On [October 22](/wiki/October_22 "October 22"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021"), Rosario Dawson announced on [Instagram](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Instagram "wikipedia:Instagram") that [Hayden Christensen](/wiki/Hayden_Christensen "Hayden Christensen") would reprise his role as [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") in *Ahsoka*. However, the post was later taken down.[[22]](#cite_note-Instagram-22)

On [May 9](/wiki/May_9 "May 9"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"), the show started production[[23]](#cite_note-SW_Production_tweet-23) under the name "Stormcrow."[[24]](#cite_note-Stormcrow-24) The creator of [Grand Admiral](/wiki/Grand_Admiral "Grand Admiral") [Mitth'raw'nuruodo](/wiki/Mitth%27raw%27nuruodo "Mitth'raw'nuruodo") "Thrawn," [Timothy Zahn](/wiki/Timothy_Zahn "Timothy Zahn"), was consulted, and will continue to be consulted with, for matters concerning the Grand Admiral's character[[25]](#cite_note-Ahsoka_Zahn_tweet_1-25) to make sure Thrawn was written just right.[[26]](#cite_note-Ahsoka_Zahn_tweet_2-26) Zahn himself said he had "spoken a couple of times" about Thrawn and would "discuss things more thoroughly in the future."[[27]](#cite_note-Zahn_interview_clip-27)

On [January 9](/wiki/January_9 "January 9"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024"), a StarWars.com announcement of the film *[The Mandalorian and Grogu](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_and_Grogu "The Mandalorian and Grogu")* revealed that a [second season](/wiki/Ahsoka_Season_2 "Ahsoka Season 2") of *Ahsoka* was being developed by Dave Filoni.[[28]](#cite_note-The_Mandalorian_&_Grogu-28) The [next day](/wiki/January_10 "January 10"), a formal announcement of *Ahsoka* season 2 was published to the website, including a sketch from Filoni depicting Ahsoka Tano and [Sabine Wren](/wiki/Sabine_Wren "Sabine Wren") standing on a [statue](/wiki/Monument_to_the_Ones "Monument to the Ones") featured in the finale of Season 1 with the caption "The story continues…."[[29]](#cite_note-Ahsoka_Season_2_article-29)

## Release and reception[]

At the [76th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/76th_Primetime_Creative_Arts_Emmy_Awards "wikipedia:76th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards"), held on [September 7](/wiki/September_7 "September 7") and [8](/wiki/September_8 "September 8"), 2024, *Ahsoka* won the award for [Outstanding Fantasy/Sci-Fi Costumes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Fantasy/Sci-Fi_Costumes "wikipedia:Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Fantasy/Sci-Fi Costumes") for "[Part Eight: The Jedi, the Witch, and the Warlord](/wiki/Part_Eight:_The_Jedi,_the_Witch,_and_the_Warlord "Part Eight: The Jedi, the Witch, and the Warlord")." The show was nominated for, but did not win, [Outstanding Special Visual Effects in a Season or a Movie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Special_Visual_Effects "wikipedia:Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Special Visual Effects"), [Outstanding Period or Fantasy/Sci-Fi Hairstyling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Hairstyling "wikipedia:Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Hairstyling") (for "[Part One: Master and Apprentice](/wiki/Part_One:_Master_and_Apprentice "Part One: Master and Apprentice")"), [Outstanding Prosthetic Makeup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Prosthetic_Makeup "wikipedia:Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Prosthetic Makeup") (for "Part Eight: The Jedi, the Witch, and the Warlord"), and [Outstanding Sound Editing for a Comedy or Drama Series (Half-Hour) and Animation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Sound_Editing_for_a_Comedy_or_Drama_Series_(Half-Hour)_and_Animation "wikipedia:Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Sound Editing for a Comedy or Drama Series (Half-Hour) and Animation") (for "[Part Four: Fallen Jedi](/wiki/Part_Four:_Fallen_Jedi "Part Four: Fallen Jedi")," for which [Matthew Wood](/wiki/Matthew_Wood "Matthew Wood") and [David Acord](/wiki/David_Acord "David Acord") were included in the nomination).[[30]](#cite_note-EW-30)
