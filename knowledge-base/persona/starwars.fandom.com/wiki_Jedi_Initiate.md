**Jedi Initiates**, also known as **Jedi younglings**, were [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [children](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") recruited by the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") from various [species](/wiki/Species "Species") across [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"). At an early age they began their initial lessons in the [Jedi arts](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") at the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), although their training also extended to the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Ilum](/wiki/Ilum "Ilum") where younglings were required to undergo a sacred rite of passage known as [the Gathering](/wiki/Gathering "Gathering") where the younglings would claim their [kyber crystal](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal") to use in their [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber"). Before the [time](/wiki/Time "Time") came for a youngling to advance to a higher degree of education, [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") served as an instructor in the ways of [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") to all younglings in the course of his long career. In order to qualify for the rank of [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"), younglings had to complete a series of tests—the [Initiate Trials](/wiki/Initiate_Trials "Initiate Trials")—in order to become apprenticed to more experienced [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") such as [Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") and [Masters](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master").

Over the course of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), the Jedi Order's future was threatened by machinations of [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") and nemesis of the Jedi. Seeking to train a force of [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") acolytes who would serve as his enforcers in the [New Order](/wiki/New_Order "New Order"), Sidious orchestrated the abduction of several Force-sensitive infants who were chosen to become Jedi younglings. The Jedi succeeded in foiling his plot, however. Yet despite their efforts to safeguard the next generation of Jedi, the younglings perished along with the majority of the Jedi Order as a result of [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") and the subsequent [rise](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").

### Future of the Jedi[]

 [![Force-sensitive children represented the future of the Jedi Order.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/ee/PloDiscoversAhsoka.png/revision/latest?cb=20131019051150) [![Force-sensitive children represented the future of the Jedi Order.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/ee/PloDiscoversAhsoka.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20131019051150)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/ee/PloDiscoversAhsoka.png/revision/latest?cb=20131019051150) 

Force-sensitive children represented the future of the Jedi Order.

 

The [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), a monastic organization dedicated to the preservation of peace and justice in the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"),[[10]](#cite_note-Jedi_Order-10) recruited its members very young, but not typically straight after birth, as [Wee Dunn](/wiki/Wee_Dunn "Wee Dunn") was considered too young to be taken in by the Order as an infant.[[11]](#cite_note-Children_of_the_Force-11) Utilizing [blood](/wiki/DNA "DNA") tests to estimate the [midi-chlorian](/wiki/Midi-chlorian "Midi-chlorian") count of prospective students,[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_I-7) the Order was able to identify [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [younglings](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") of various [species](/wiki/Species "Species") throughout [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy").[[11]](#cite_note-Children_of_the_Force-11) Before their training, younglings were frequently only skilled in a single aspect of the Force, while they would begin to advance in others once their training began.[[12]](#cite_note-Ahsoka-12) After their sensitivity to [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") was confirmed, the infants[[1]](#cite_note-DB_Younglings-1) or more usually young children[[13]](#cite_note-The_Gathering-13) were brought to the [Jedi crèche](/wiki/Jedi_cr%C3%A8che "Jedi crèche") of the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), where they were to live until they were chosen to become a [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan").[[1]](#cite_note-DB_Younglings-1)[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14)

Some of these younglings were rescued from dangerous situations such as [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), whose father left him to die after learning of his infant son's Force-sensitivity and [Depa Billaba](/wiki/Depa_Billaba "Depa Billaba") and her sister [Sar Labooda](/wiki/Sar_Labooda "Sar Labooda") whose parents were killed by pirates.[[15]](#cite_note-Dooku:_Jedi_Lost-15) [[16]](#cite_note-SWCEUE-16) Initiates were usually brought to the Temple very young. However, old arrivals included the nine-year old [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"),[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_I-7) the five-year-old [Rael Averross](/wiki/Rael_Averross "Rael Averross"),[[17]](#cite_note-Master_&_Apprentice-17) and the eight-year-old [Verosha Aniseya](/wiki/Verosha_Aniseya "Verosha Aniseya"), who went on to leave the Order.[[18]](#cite_note-Lost_Found-18) Upon entering the Order, they quickly learned that their true family was the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") Order.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Gathering-13) [Marda Ro](/wiki/Marda_Ro "Marda Ro") of the [Path of the Closed Fist](/wiki/Path_of_the_Closed_Fist "Path of the Closed Fist") accused the Jedi Order of cruelty by taking children away from their families,[[19]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-19) but the Jedi actively sought the permission of families when finding younglings.[[11]](#cite_note-Children_of_the_Force-11)[[7]](#cite_note-Episode_I-7)[[20]](#cite_note-Destiny-20)

The Jedi maintained a list pertaining to confirmed Force-sensitive infants, particularly their identities and locations.[[21]](#cite_note-COD-21) Due to the children's importance to the future of the Jedi,[[9]](#cite_note-Holocron_Heist-9) the [High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") went to great lengths to keep the list's contents internal to the Order.[[21]](#cite_note-COD-21)[[9]](#cite_note-Holocron_Heist-9) The list itself was contained within the [kyber memory crystal](/wiki/Kyber_memory_crystal "Kyber memory crystal") which could only be accessed through a [holocron](/wiki/Holocron "Holocron"),[[21]](#cite_note-COD-21) an informational device stored within the [vaults](/wiki/Holocron_Vault "Holocron Vault") of the Temple.[[9]](#cite_note-Holocron_Heist-9) Only a Force-sensitive, trained in the use of [telekinesis](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis"), had the ability to combine the [crystal](/wiki/Crystal "Crystal") with a holocron.[[21]](#cite_note-COD-21) The crystal itself was entrusted to a member of the Order who served as its keeper.[[22]](#cite_note-Bolla_Ropal-22)

### Youngling clans[]

:   "*Younglings…* Younglings*. A visitor, we have.*"
:   ―Yoda, introducing [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") to the Bear Clan[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14)

Jedi younglings were divided into ten clans consisting of approximately twenty students.[[1]](#cite_note-DB_Younglings-1) Among these groups was [Bear Clan](/wiki/Bear_Clan "Bear Clan"), a class of younglings between the ages of four and eight.[[23]](#cite_note-Bear_Clan-23) The [youngling clans](/wiki/Clan_(Jedi) "Clan (Jedi)") would remain as one unit, living and training together, until the [time](/wiki/Time "Time") came to go their separate ways as Padawan learners.[[24]](#cite_note-Kanan_7-24) The younglings who were not chosen to become Padawans were supervised by the [Council of Reassignment](/wiki/Council_of_Reassignment "Council of Reassignment").[[25]](#cite_note-Graphics-25)

The new generation of Jedi Knights began their training as younglings under the guidance of Grand Master Yoda.

 

Upon joining a clan, younglings began their basic training in the Jedi arts.[[1]](#cite_note-DB_Younglings-1) Their lessons included various tests and rituals that had to be completed in order to advance to the higher levels of the Jedi arts. Between the ages of four and eight,[[23]](#cite_note-Bear_Clan-23) younglings acquired the basic skills of [lightsaber combat](/wiki/Lightsaber_combat "Lightsaber combat") using low-powered[[26]](#cite_note-SWE_Jedi_Order-26) [training sabers](/wiki/Training_lightsaber "Training lightsaber") to practice the deflection of [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") shots fired by training [droids](/wiki/Droid "Droid").[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14) Both the training droids and lightsabers were non-lethal.[[27]](#cite_note-Convergence-27)

The next step in their progress as aspiring Jedi was the construction of a genuine [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber")—the signature weapon of the Jedi which first required a [kyber crystal](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal"). This lesson ultimately led to the creation of [the Gathering](/wiki/Gathering "Gathering");[[13]](#cite_note-The_Gathering-13) an ancient and significant tradition that began [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century") before the events of the Clone Wars,[[28]](#cite_note-Ilum-28) the Gathering tested the younglings' ability to locate their particular crystal through the Force. It required younglings to travel offworld from the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to the [Crystal Cave](/wiki/Crystal_Cave "Crystal Cave") of [Ilum](/wiki/Ilum "Ilum"),[[13]](#cite_note-The_Gathering-13) a frigid world[[29]](#cite_note-SWE-29) located in the [Unknown Regions](/wiki/Unknown_Regions "Unknown Regions")[[30]](#cite_note-Ultimate-30) and most sacred to the Jedi Order.[[28]](#cite_note-Ilum-28) After acquiring their crystal, the younglings had to use the Force to telekinetically combine all of the necessary components into a lightsaber.[[31]](#cite_note-ANB-31)

For almost a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") of the Jedi Order's history,[[32]](#cite_note-Episode_V-32) all younglings were trained by [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"),[[33]](#cite_note-Voices-33) the ancient [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master")[[34]](#cite_note-Yoda-34) who also served as an instructor in the Jedi arts for eight centuries by the time of the Clone Wars.[[32]](#cite_note-Episode_V-32) Yoda provided the younglings with a beginner's education in lightsaber combat, a practice which incorporated lessons on using the Force to guide and enhance their reflexes.[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_II-14) In addition to teaching the basic skills of lightsaber combat, Yoda also served as a guide for younglings who participated in the Gathering.[[13]](#cite_note-The_Gathering-13)

In addition to lightsaber training, Yoda served as a guide for younglings who undertook the Gathering.

 

[Tera Sinube](/wiki/Tera_Sinube "Tera Sinube"), another ancient Jedi Master, also took on the responsibility of overseeing the younglings' development as [lightsaber duelists](/wiki/Lightsaber_duel "Lightsaber duel").[[35]](#cite_note-TWJ-35) In addition to guiding the development of their students, it was the Master's prerogative to introduce a Padawan as a guest lecturer to a youngling clan.[[36]](#cite_note-Lightsaber_Lost-36) The task of educating younglings in the proper construction of a lightsaber was the duty of [Huyang](/wiki/Huyang "Huyang"); an [architect droid](/wiki/Architect_droid "Architect droid") [professor](/wiki/Professor "Professor") based out on the Jedi [vessel](/wiki/Starship "Starship"), the *[Crucible](/wiki/Crucible "Crucible")*, he personally oversaw the creation of lightsabers by younglings of a thousand generations—a record which included the one-time youngling Yoda.[[37]](#cite_note-A_Test_of_Strength-37)

Grand Master Yoda trained all Jedi[[33]](#cite_note-Voices-33) as younglings. During the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"), youngling [Gella Nattai](/wiki/Gella_Nattai "Gella Nattai") was a quiet girl but a curious mind, trying to find passages or tombs throughout the Jedi Temple and wandering its halls. She assumed she would be a natural at lightsaber combat and was the first one to arrive at lightsaber training, where she was to wield a training lightsaber against [remotes](/wiki/Remote "Remote"). Unaware that the training spheres were non-lethal, she became scared of its many [photoreceptors](/wiki/Photoreceptor "Photoreceptor") and ran from the room when she saw one, but she was found by Yoda in a temple garden. Together, they watched a frog repeatedly attempt to leap over a stone wall to reach a waterfall. Taught by Yoda to overcome fear and always try again, like that frog, she arrived at training the next day and was hit on her nose by a training blast. However, she overcame her fear as she continued to practice.[[27]](#cite_note-Convergence-27)

One day during the High Republic Era, a [Jedi seeker](/wiki/Unidentified_Jedi_seeker "Unidentified Jedi seeker") approached the [Zeveron family](/wiki/Zeveron_family "Zeveron family"), who had two Force-sensitive daughters;[[19]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-19) [Elecia Zeveron](/wiki/Elecia_Zeveron "Elecia Zeveron") had mild Force-sensitivity[[38]](#cite_note-High_Republic_Character_Encyclopedia-38) that gave her a natural skill in [mind tricks](/wiki/Mind_trick "Mind trick"), while the more Force-sensitive [Oliviah Zeveron](/wiki/Oliviah_Zeveron "Oliviah Zeveron") held skill in [telekinesis](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis"). The seeker ultimately took Oliviah to be trained as a Jedi, while Elecia's abilities were seen as too weak. Unfortunately for the Order, that decision would spell a lifetime of rage for Elecia. Believing she should have been chosen instead, her anger at the Jedi for being overlooked led her down a path[[19]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-19) that saw her try to take over the galaxy as "the [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)")" of the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand"). During the [Battle of Jedha](/wiki/Battle_of_Jedha "Battle of Jedha") instigated by the Mother[[39]](#cite_note-Cataclysm-39) in [382 BBY](/wiki/382_BBY "382 BBY"),[[38]](#cite_note-High_Republic_Character_Encyclopedia-38) Oliviah spotted Elecia and unearthed memories of her past, which she confronted Elecia with during the [Night of Sorrow](/wiki/Night_of_Sorrow "Night of Sorrow").[[19]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-19)

### Late Republic Era[]

During the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") arranged for the [theft](/wiki/Raid_on_the_Jedi_Temple "Raid on the Jedi Temple") of both a holocron[[9]](#cite_note-Holocron_Heist-9) and the memory crystal[[21]](#cite_note-COD-21) in order to abduct members of the Jedi's next generation.[[11]](#cite_note-Children_of_the_Force-11) [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") publicly known as [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") Sheev Palpatine,[[40]](#cite_note-Palpatine/Sidious-40) intended to use the younglings in an experimental procedure to create a force of [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") adepts that would serve him in the [New Order](/wiki/New_Order "New Order"). However, the plan was thwarted by the efforts of the Jedi, particularly the [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") Anakin Skywalker and his Padawan, [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano"), both of whom rescued the children from captivity and returned them to their parents.[[11]](#cite_note-Children_of_the_Force-11)

Consumed by the persona of Darth Vader, the former Anakin Skywalker murdered a group of Jedi younglings.

 

Later, Skywalker became instrumental in the fall of the Order following his seduction to the dark side as the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Vader. With the initiation of [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"), Vader led a [military](/wiki/Republic_Military "Republic Military") [assault](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall") on the Temple at the behest of his new [Master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master"), who commanded him to spare none of his former comrades. This command extended to the younglings, a group of which were confronted in the [High Council Chamber](/wiki/Jedi_Council_Chamber "Jedi Council Chamber") by Vader. [One boy](/wiki/Unidentified_Jedi_youngling "Unidentified Jedi youngling"), recognizing Skywalker and thinking he was still a Jedi Knight, asked him what they should do against the overwhelming number of clones, but Vader did not respond and instead slaughtered the children. In the aftermath of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire")'s [rise to power](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order"), the majority of Jedi were slain along with the youngest of their brethren, resulting in the [destruction](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") of the next generation of the Jedi Order.[[41]](#cite_note-Episode_III-41)

### Legacy and further efforts[]

:   "*In the old days, Force-sensitive children from across the galaxy were selected to begin the first steps on the path to becoming a Jedi. These young initiates lived in clans at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant and were trained in the Jedi arts by legendary masters.*"
:   ―Luke Skywalker reflects on Jedi history[[42]](#cite_note-Star_Wars:_The_Secrets_of_the_Jedi-42)

Despite the loss of the Order,[[41]](#cite_note-Episode_III-41) the remnants of the Jedi endured throughout the [Dark Times](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era"), surviving both the Sith and their Empire.[[43]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-43) However, the survivors elected to remain secret, so many Force-sensitive children were left in the galaxy without the chance of becoming Jedi.[[12]](#cite_note-Ahsoka-12) When the Empire located any Force-sensitive children, they were taken by Vader[[44]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_19-44) or members of the [Inquisitorius](/wiki/Inquisitorius "Inquisitorius") to be made part of [Project Harvester](/wiki/Project_Harvester "Project Harvester"), fulfilling Sidious's plan from the Clone Wars.[[45]](#cite_note-The_Future_of_the_Force-45) Two surviving Jedi, Master [Vanzell Mar-Klar](/wiki/Vanzell_Mar-Klar "Vanzell Mar-Klar") and Padawan [Nedriss Narr](/wiki/Nedriss_Narr "Nedriss Narr"), attempted to train younglings away from Imperial eyes.[[46]](#cite_note-Trials-46)

After the fall of the Empire, the Jedi Order was reborn as a new, albeit short-lived, generation under [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker").[[47]](#cite_note-Episode_VII-47) The Jedi practice of recruiting younglings also served as inspiration to [Commandant](/wiki/Commandant "Commandant") [Brendol Hux](/wiki/Brendol_Hux "Brendol Hux"), an [Imperial Academy](/wiki/Imperial_Academy "Imperial Academy") instructor who greatly admired the training methods of both the Jedi Order and [Grand Army](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper").[[48]](#cite_note-TSA-48) His illegitimate son, [General](/wiki/General "General") [Armitage Hux](/wiki/Armitage_Hux "Armitage Hux"), incorporated his [father's](/wiki/Parent "Parent") views into the [military](/wiki/First_Order_military "First Order military") training program of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order"),[[49]](#cite_note-TFA_Visual_Dictionary-49) causing an entire generation to become [stormtroopers](/wiki/First_Order_stormtrooper "First Order stormtrooper")—super [soldiers](/wiki/Soldier "Soldier")[[50]](#cite_note-FO-Stormtroopers-50) programmed from birth and exceptionally trained in Armitage's view.[[47]](#cite_note-Episode_VII-47)
