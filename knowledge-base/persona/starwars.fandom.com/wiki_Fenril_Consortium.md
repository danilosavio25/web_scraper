The Fenril Consortium—albeit unidentified—debut in the [first issue](/wiki/Star_Wars_(2025)_1 "Star Wars (2025) 1") of the [2025 iteration](/wiki/Star_Wars_(2025) "Star Wars (2025)") of the *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [comic book](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") flagship series, which was written by [Alex Segura](/wiki/Alex_Segura "Alex Segura"), illustrated by [Phil Noto](/wiki/Phil_Noto "Phil Noto"), and published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [May 7](/wiki/May_7 "May 7"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025").[[5]](#cite_note-SW1_Marvel-5) The governmental body was later identified as the Fenril Consortium in the [fourth issue](/wiki/Star_Wars_(2025)_4 "Star Wars (2025) 4"),[[3]](#cite_note-SW4-3) though a proper capitalization was then given in the [seventh issue](/wiki/Star_Wars_(2025)_7 "Star Wars (2025) 7") of the series.[[2]](#cite_note-SW7-2)
