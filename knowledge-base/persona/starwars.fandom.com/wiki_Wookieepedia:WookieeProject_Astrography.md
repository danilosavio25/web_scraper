Welcome to **WookieeProject Astrography**! We are a [community project](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") focused on improving Wookieepedia's articles on *Star Wars* [locations in space](/wiki/Category:Locations "Category:Locations"), [celestial bodies](/wiki/Category:Astronomical_objects "Category:Astronomical objects") and other [astronomical](/wiki/Astronomer "Astronomer") subjects, [space maps](/wiki/Star_map "Star map"), and objects depicted on those.

The ultimate aim of the project is to write up as many of such articles as possible and to bring them to Wookieepedia's article quality status, that is, make them [Featured](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_articles "Wookieepedia:Featured articles"), [Good](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_articles "Wookieepedia:Good articles"), or [Comprehensive articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_articles "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive articles"), depending on how much official information there is on the subjects they're about.

To that end, we strive toward making it so that any and all astrographical articles follow the rules of these core Wookieepedia article **policies**: the [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide/In-universe "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide/In-universe") (in particular note [this section](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide/In-universe#Planet_and_moon_articles "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide/In-universe")), the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style"), and the [Attribution](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Attribution "Wookieepedia:Attribution") and [Sourcing](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing") policies, as well as comply with the [Naming](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Naming_policy "Wookieepedia:Naming policy") and [Notability policies](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Notability_policy "Wookieepedia:Notability policy"). There are also a few [**practices**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Astrography/Precedent "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Astrography/Precedent") relevant to astrographical articles that are not at this time part of Wookieepedia's policies but should nevertheless be paid attention to.

## How can you help?[]

* Jump right in and **start expanding** any of the incomplete articles in the project's scope, such as [star systems](/wiki/Category:System_stubs "Category:System stubs"), [planets](/wiki/Category:Planet_stubs "Category:Planet stubs"), [stars](/wiki/Category:Star_stubs "Category:Star stubs"), [sectors](/wiki/Category:Sector_stubs "Category:Sector stubs"), [moons](/wiki/Category:Moon_stubs "Category:Moon stubs"), [space stations](/wiki/Category:Space_station_stubs "Category:Space station stubs"), [asteroids](/wiki/Category:Asteroid_stubs "Category:Asteroid stubs"), [hyperspace routes](/wiki/Category:Route_stubs "Category:Route stubs"), [nebulae](/wiki/Category:Nebula_stubs "Category:Nebula stubs"), and [comets](/wiki/Category:Comet_stubs "Category:Comet stubs")!
  + New to editing? Check out Wookieepedia's [**Welcome, newcomers**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Welcome,_newcomers "Wookieepedia:Welcome, newcomers") tutorial page, which includes links to helpful editing tutorials.
  + Have any questions? Head to Wookieepedia's [**Discord server**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord"), which, among other things, has a dedicated `#astrography` channel; or, alternatively, reach out to any of the project's members, listed below.
* **Mark articles** as falling into the project's scope by heading to their talk pages and placing `{{WPASTtalk}}` there! (Be sure to read [this](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Talk_page_policy "Wookieepedia:Talk page policy") before you do, though.)
* **Review** the current Astrography status article nominations listed below and help them reach [Featured](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Featured article nominations"), [Good](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Good article nominations"), and [Comprehensive article](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_article_nominations "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive article nominations") status!
* Peruse the project's **coordinate challenge** to write up at least one status article for each of [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy")'s map grid squares, for both the current *Star Wars* [**canon**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Astrography/Coordinate_challenge "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Astrography/Coordinate challenge") and [**Legends**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Astrography/Coordinate_challenge "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Astrography/Coordinate challenge") continuity! Browse [this category](/wiki/Category:Locations_by_grid_square "Category:Locations by grid square"), and perhaps you'll find an article that you can write up in order to contribute to the challenge!
* Check out the cool [**Interactive Maps**](/wiki/Special:AllMaps "Special:AllMaps") feature for ideas on mapping new astrography topics!
