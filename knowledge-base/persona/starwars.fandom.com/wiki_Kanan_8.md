## Plot summary[]

### The present[]

As [Kanan Jarrus](/wiki/Kanan_Jarrus "Kanan Jarrus") recuperates inside a [bacta tank](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank"), the [Spectres](/wiki/Spectres "Spectres") wait inside the [medcenter](/wiki/Medcenter "Medcenter") room. When [Sabine Wren](/wiki/Sabine_Wren "Sabine Wren") asks how long their friend needs to remain inside the tank, the [2-1B surgical droid](/wiki/2-1B_surgical_droid "2-1B surgical droid") responds that it will take a few weeks for Kanan to regain consciousness but that removing him risks his survival. Just then, the Spectres hear an announcement ordering them to come out with their limbs raised up or face certain death.

### Kanan's flashbacks[]

#### The Separatist infiltrator[]

At the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace"), [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Depa Billaba](/wiki/Depa_Billaba "Depa Billaba") saves [Caleb Dume](/wiki/Kanan_Jarrus "Kanan Jarrus") from an explosive wave unleashed by the mini-drone explosives. As the two come to terms with the [attack](/wiki/Bombing_at_the_Jedi_Temple "Bombing at the Jedi Temple") on the Jedi Temple, the masked Separatist infiltrator [Rackham Sear](/wiki/Rackham_Sear "Rackham Sear") watches through the air ducts. He drops more mini-drone explosives, causing more explosions through the Jedi Temple.

In a flashback scene, Rackham Sear justifies his planned attack on the Jedi Temple on the grounds that the Jedi have sided against liberty and justice. [General](/wiki/General "General") [Kleeve](/wiki/Kleeve "Kleeve") observes that Rackham is plotting a strike against the heart of the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") while studying a [holographic](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") map of the Jedi Temple. Rackham explains that he hired the [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Cad Bane](/wiki/Cad_Bane "Cad Bane") to make the specs and that Bane reported that the Jedi have barely upgrade their security.

General Kleeve is concerned that this is a suicide mission but Captain Rackham responds that he is willing to die. Kleeve explains that his plan is to topple the [Temple Spire](/wiki/Palace_spire "Palace spire") in order to show the Republic and the Separatists that the Jedi are not invincible. Meanwhile, [Sammo Quid](/wiki/Sammo_Quid "Sammo Quid") and [Tai Uzuma](/wiki/Tai_Uzuma "Tai Uzuma") watch over [younglings](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") while an older Jedi and [Jedi Temple Guards](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_Guard "Jedi Temple Guard") slice through the mini-drone explosives with their lightsabers.

The Jedi tells one of the Temple Guards to watch over the younglings and Padawns for more mini-drone explosives. The Jedi tells Masters [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") and [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") that the younglings are save. When Kenobi asks about the source of the attacks, he replies that they don't know. Sammo Quid asks where is Dume but Tai Uzuma replies that she does not know but thinks he is hiding.

#### Pursuit and confrontation[]

Meanwhile, Caleb asks Master Billaba if she wants them to hide here. Billaba responds that she wants them to stay here until they have more information. Billaba believes that she could be a liability out there since she is not fully healed. Caleb quickly works out that the attacker is based on the attack floor but is hurling down explosives in order to keep most of the [Jedi Knights](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") and Masters preoccupied. Despite his Master's warnings, Caleb rushes up the air ducts to pursue the intruder on the roof.

Caleb encounters Rackham Sear. He tells the "kid" that he is on the wrong side because the Jedi are the "bad guys" fighting against freedom and self determination. Rackham is unwilling to kill initiates and gives Caleb the chance to run. However, Caleb refuses to run and charges at Rackham, who hurls several mini-drone explosives at him. Caleb dodges the explosives but Rackham tells him that all he did was delay the explosives by a few seconds.

However, Master Billaba retorts that Caleb bought her enough time and tells him to back away from the boy. Rackham attempts to use Caleb as a hostage but Caleb manages to tell Billaba about Rackham's plan topple the Jedi Spire and that he has not set the timer. Rackham attempts to set the timer with a drone explosive but Billaba uses [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") to prevent the liquids inside the explosives from exploding.

Rackham attempts to rush for the bomb but Caleb uses the Force to pull it away from the tower. The bomb explodes harmlessly in the air. Master Billaba offers Rackham mercy but he identifies himself as Rackham Sear of [Quarzite](/wiki/Quarzite "Quarzite"), a [Captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") of the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems"). Rejecting the mercy of "tyrants", he hurls blades at Master Billaba and Caleb but Billaba deflects them with the Force.

Rackham then jumps to his [death](/wiki/Death "Death") over the roof. Before falling, he unleashes several mini-drone explosives, causing an explosion below. Billaba and Caleb survive the explosion. Billaba confides that she is in desperate need of a Padawan to continue her journey. Caleb volunteers but Billaba thinks that he is too young. Still, she relents since the Republic needs him. Just then, Masters Kenobi and Windu arrive to provide aid.

#### Billaba's apprentice[]

Following the defeat of Rackham, Tai and Sammo are shocked and jealous that Billaba has picked Caleb as her new Padawan. A jealous Sammo tells Caleb that he is not safe with her while Tai taunts him that he is not ready to be a Padawan. Reflecting on his friends' jealousy, Master Billaba says that this is a lesson that none of them are perfect.

Over the next three weeks, Caleb travels with Master Billaba to get a [kyber crystal](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal") for a new lightsaber. They travel aboard a [*Venator*-class Star Destroyer](/wiki/Venator-class_Star_Destroyer "Venator-class Star Destroyer") on a mission to the planet [Kardoa](/wiki/Kardoa "Kardoa") to rendezvous with [Depa Billaba's second battalion](/wiki/Depa_Billaba%27s_second_battalion "Depa Billaba's second battalion"), which is led by [Clone Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") [Grey](/wiki/Grey_(clone_trooper) "Grey (clone trooper)") and [Clone Captain](/wiki/Clone_Captain "Clone Captain") [Styles](/wiki/Styles "Styles"), whom Caleb thinks will make "great friends."
