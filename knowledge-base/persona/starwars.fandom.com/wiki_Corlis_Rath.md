**Corlis Rath**, also known as **Davros Hex**, was a [Sinsaran](/wiki/Sinsaran "Sinsaran") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") who lived during the late [Republic Era](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") and tried to [assassinate](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn").

## Biography[]

### Meeting Qui-Gon Jinn[]

Davros Hex was a [Sinsaran](/wiki/Sinsaran "Sinsaran") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") individual from the world [Sinsara](/wiki/Sinsara "Sinsara"), who lived during the late [Republic Era](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era"). He was associated with a bandit group on [Sinsara](/wiki/Sinsara "Sinsara") known as the [Behru](/wiki/The_Banished "The Banished"), or later as "The Banished". After the Behru had raided the local villages for a considerable time, the Sinsarans called for the help of the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") and [Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") and [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn") were sent to deal with the issue. After arriving on Sinsara, the Jedi met the Behru at their base, but were unsuccessful at negotiating and were forced to retreat. Being of the opinion that their situation had confronted the limitations of the [Jedi Code](/wiki/Jedi_Code "Jedi Code"), Dooku urged his apprentice to change to a more violent strategy. Upon their return to the bandits, Jinn, who had studied on the Sinsaran culture's sense of honor, proposed a duel between himself and the [Behru's leader](/wiki/Unidentified_Behru_leader "Unidentified Behru leader"). The conditions being, if Jinn lost, the Jedi would leave Sinsara, but if the Sinsaran lost the Behru would stop their raids. The leader agreed to the duel, but refused to fight "children" and instead put forward Davros Hex as a surrogate.[[1]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_9-1) During the ensuing fighting Jinn slashed a wound into Hex's chest with [his lightsaber](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn%27s_lightsaber "Qui-Gon Jinn's lightsaber") while his Master tried to stop him.[[6]](#cite_note-MoonofDeath-6)

In the following [decades](/wiki/Standard_decade "Standard decade"), Hex began using the name Corlis Rath and altered his appearance, with [Jedi librarian](/wiki/Jedi_librarian "Jedi librarian") [Jocasta Nu](/wiki/Jocasta_Nu "Jocasta Nu") suspecting that he underwent the highly dangerous Sinsaran ritual known as "[Chrysalis](/wiki/Chrysalis "Chrysalis")", something which would also explain his superior fighting skills.[[1]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_9-1) At some point, a [hologram](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") of Corlis Rath was included together with his name in the [Jedi Archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives").[[2]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_2-2)

### Attempts to assassinate Qui-Gon Jinn[]

    "*Sinsara.*"

 [![Corlis Rath attacks Qui-Gon Jinn on Vekura](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/53/QuiGonJinnAttacked-JediKnights1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250405025901) [![Corlis Rath attacks Qui-Gon Jinn on Vekura](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/53/QuiGonJinnAttacked-JediKnights1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20250405025901)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/53/QuiGonJinnAttacked-JediKnights1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250405025901) 

Corlis Rath attacks Qui-Gon Jinn on Vekura

 

Between [40 BBY](/wiki/40_BBY "40 BBY") and [34 BBY](/wiki/34_BBY "34 BBY")[[7]](#cite_note-Date-7) during the [Invasion of Syrinx Prime](/wiki/Invasion_of_Syrinx_Prime "Invasion of Syrinx Prime"), Rath attacked Jinn – now a Jedi Master – on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") on [Vekura](/wiki/Vekura "Vekura"). Jinn and fellow [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Berem Khana](/wiki/Berem_Khana "Berem Khana") were on their way to their [shuttle](/wiki/T-6_shuttle "T-6 shuttle") to return to [Syrinx Prime](/wiki/Syrinx_Prime "Syrinx Prime") after they had unsuccessfully tried to negotiate a ceasefire with the [Vekuran](/wiki/Vekuran "Vekuran") [general](/wiki/General "General") [Lafco](/wiki/Lafco "Lafco"). Rath jumped at Jinn and tried to stab him[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) with two energy-edged [blades](/wiki/Sword "Sword"),[[6]](#cite_note-MoonofDeath-6) and when Khana tried to assist Jinn, Rath propelled him back against a [tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree") with a small[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) concussive-based weapon[[5]](#cite_note-FCBD_2025-5) on his right upper arm. Eventually Jinn managed to [Force push](/wiki/Force_push "Force push") Rath against a boulder, but before Jinn could remove Rath's mask and question him, he activated a [smoke grenade](/wiki/Smoke_grenade "Smoke grenade") and fled.[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3)

Back in the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), Bhana showed Jinn a microdust transmitter sown into his [robe](/wiki/Jedi_apparel "Jedi apparel") that his attacker must have used to track him. Meanwhile Jinn's [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") got a [Force vision](/wiki/Force_vision "Force vision") of Rath killing Jinn[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) and, after investigation in the Jedi Archives, found out his identity and showed his master the hologram of Rath, who didn't recognize him.[[2]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_2-2) Fellow Padawan [Tensu Run](/wiki/Tensu_Run "Tensu Run") began tracking Rath. Together with Jinn, Run tried [to meet](/wiki/Mission_to_K%27shar "Mission to K'shar") with an informant named [Aquint Garva](/wiki/Aquint_Garva "Aquint Garva") on the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [K'shar](/wiki/K%27shar "K'shar"), but found Garva to have already been murdered by Rath.[[6]](#cite_note-MoonofDeath-6) At some point later, Jinn was on a mission on the planet [Nar Shaddaa](/wiki/Nar_Shaddaa "Nar Shaddaa") together with fellow Jedi Master [Shaak Ti](/wiki/Shaak_Ti "Shaak Ti"), when he [sensed](/wiki/Force_sense "Force sense") the presence of Rath, who was surveilling them from atop the roof of a nearby building, but didn't make a move to attack them.[[8]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_4-8)

To lure out Jinn, Rath [poisoned](/wiki/Poison "Poison") and [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") the Jedi Master [Fondar Etzis](/wiki/Fondar_Etzis "Fondar Etzis") with a fast acting toxin,[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4) [emubara](/wiki/Emubara "Emubara"),[[9]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_7-9) on the [agriworld](/wiki/Agriworld "Agriworld") of [Mina-Rau](/wiki/Mina-Rau "Mina-Rau"). [Arriving](/wiki/Duel_on_Mina-Rau "Duel on Mina-Rau") on Mina-Rau as part of an honor guard with fellow Master [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") and Knight [Aayla Secura](/wiki/Aayla_Secura "Aayla Secura"), Jinn put together that Etzis had been murdered and sensed the assassin's approach when he finally revealed himself, whereas Windu and Secura were caught off guard and knocked out via darts. Rath dueled Jinn's lightsaber with his blades[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4) in a challenging battle[[9]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_7-9) and revealed his vendetta against the Jedi had something to do with Sinsara, something that shocked Jinn and gave Rath and opening to stab him through the chest.[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4) Jinn was left poisoned but was saved from death by Windu and Secura, who made their way to Sinsara to [recover](/wiki/Mission_to_Sinsara "Mission to Sinsara") the antidote.[[9]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_7-9)

After his recovery, Jinn recognized Corlis Rath as Davros Hex. Having researched how he could have altered his appearance in the Jedi Archives, Jinn, accompanied by his Padawan Kenobi, traveled to Sinsara to confront Rath once and for all.[[1]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_9-1)

## Personality and traits[]

Corlis Rath had light-[colored](/wiki/Color "Color") skin[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) and yellow-colored [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye"). He had a personal vendetta against Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4) and tried to kill him, but used only non-lethal weapons against other enemies.[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3)

## Skills and abilities[]

Rath was skilled in the use of dual blades, able to push Qui-Gon Jinn on the defensive during their fight.[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) During their duel on Mina-Rau, Rath showed extraordinary jumping abilities comparable to [these](/wiki/Force_jump "Force jump") of [Force-sensitives](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive").[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4)

## Equipment[]

During his duel with the young Qui-Gon Jinn, Rath wore only dark [pants](/wiki/Clothing "Clothing") and [boots](/wiki/Boot "Boot").[[1]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_9-1) During his later hunt for Jinn, Rath used two two energy-edged blades,[[6]](#cite_note-MoonofDeath-6) as well as smoke grenades,[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) a concussive-based weapon,[[5]](#cite_note-FCBD_2025-5) and [poison darts](/wiki/Poison_dart "Poison dart"),[[4]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_6-4) and wore mostly black clothing going up to his head, with red parts around his upper arms, and he concealed his face with a red [metalic](/wiki/Metal "Metal") mask and a grey [goggles](/wiki/Goggles "Goggles").[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3)

## Behind the scenes[]

Corlis Rath first appeared in [first issue](/wiki/Jedi_Knights_1 "Jedi Knights 1") of the [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025") [comic](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") series *[Star Wars: Jedi Knights](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Jedi_Knights "Star Wars: Jedi Knights")*, written by [Marc Guggenheim](/wiki/Marc_Guggenheim "Marc Guggenheim") and drawn by [Madibek Musabekov](/wiki/Madibek_Musabekov "Madibek Musabekov") and [Luis Guerrero](/wiki/Luis_Guerrero "Luis Guerrero"),[[3]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_1-3) which was released on [March 5](/wiki/March_5 "March 5"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025").[[10]](#cite_note-Penguin_Issue_1-10) He serves as the primary antagonist[[11]](#cite_note-Announcement-11) of the first arc of *Jedi Knight*,[[12]](#cite_note-Jedi_Knights_10-12) tying together the different individual stories of the series. He was first revealed during its announcement in October [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024").[[11]](#cite_note-Announcement-11)
