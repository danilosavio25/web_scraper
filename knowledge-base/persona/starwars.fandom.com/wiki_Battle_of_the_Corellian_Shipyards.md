The **Battle of the Corellian Shipyards**,[[4]](#cite_note-EFV-4) also known as the **battle on Corellia** was a battle fought on the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Corellia](/wiki/Corellia "Corellia") concurrent with the [destruction of Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon") as part of the [Nihil conflict](/wiki/Nihil_conflict "Nihil conflict") in[[1]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-1) [230 BBY](/wiki/230_BBY "230 BBY").[[3]](#cite_note-Timelines-3) [Alys "Crash" Ongwa](/wiki/Alys_Ongwa "Alys Ongwa") and members of [her company](/wiki/Supreme_Coronet_City_Diplomat_Protection "Supreme Coronet City Diplomat Protection") fought alongside Jedi Masters [Kantam Sy](/wiki/Kantam_Sy "Kantam Sy"), [Cohmac Vitus](/wiki/Cohmac_Vitus "Cohmac Vitus"), and [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), Padawans [Reath Silas](/wiki/Reath_Silas "Reath Silas") and [Ram Jomaram](/wiki/Ram_Jomaram "Ram Jomaram"), and [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola") against Nihil forces led by [Sabata Krill](/wiki/Sabata_Krill "Sabata Krill"), who attempted to steal ships from [Coronet City](/wiki/Coronet_City "Coronet City")'s shipyards.[[1]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

The battle on Corellia first appeared in the [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") young-adult novel *[The High Republic: Midnight Horizon](/wiki/The_High_Republic:_Midnight_Horizon "The High Republic: Midnight Horizon")*, written by [Daniel José Older](/wiki/Daniel_Jos%C3%A9_Older "Daniel José Older") and released as part of the *[Star Wars: The High Republic](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic "Star Wars: The High Republic")* publishing initiative.[[1]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-1)
