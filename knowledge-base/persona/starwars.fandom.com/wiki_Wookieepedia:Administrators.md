**Administrators** are experienced and trusted [Wookieepedians](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") who have been elected by the community to enforce [site policies](/wiki/Wookieepedia:About#Policies_and_consensus "Wookieepedia:About") and carry out a wide range of maintenance tasks. Administrators have "[sysop rights](/wiki/Special:ListGroupRights "Special:ListGroupRights")," a set of tools that allow them to [delete](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy") pages and [block](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy") users. Like every Wookieepedian, administrators are required to follow all policies, and as active community leaders, they can be contacted for advice and information about Wookieepedia.

## Responsibilities

Administrators are Wookieepedia users with "sysop rights," which are fully listed on [Special:ListGroupRights](/wiki/Special:ListGroupRights#sysop "Special:ListGroupRights"). Administrators are expected to use these sysop tools to enforce [site policies](/wiki/Wookieepedia:About#Policies_and_consensus "Wookieepedia:About") on Wookieepedia, which are determined through a system of [community consensus](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus"). The administration is responsible for acting in the interests of the Wookieepedia community as active leaders.

While administrators have access to certain features and are therefore responsible for maintaining them via sysop rights, the wider Wookieepedia community is encouraged to request changes, such as updating the site interface and adjusting protected templates; administrators should act on such suggestions as policies permit. The [administrators' noticeboard](/wiki/Forum:Administrators%27_noticeboard "Forum:Administrators' noticeboard") is among various ways of [contacting](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Contact "Wookieepedia:Contact") administrators.

The [Administrators' how-to-guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators%27_how-to_guide "Wookieepedia:Administrators' how-to guide"), [Administrators' reading list](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators%27_reading_list "Wookieepedia:Administrators' reading list"), and [Special:AdminDashboard](/wiki/Special:AdminDashboard "Special:AdminDashboard") are also useful resources in carrying out administrative tasks.

### [AbuseFilter](/wiki/Special:AbuseFilter "Special:AbuseFilter")

* Manage the local [AbuseFilter](/wiki/Special:AbuseFilter "Special:AbuseFilter") to moderate editing, such as preventing policy-breaking edits from being submitted.
* Monitor [Wookieepedia:Spamfilter problems](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Spamfilter_problems "Wookieepedia:Spamfilter problems") and the [Special:AbuseLog](/wiki/Special:AbuseLog "Special:AbuseLog").

### [Blocking](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy")

* Block and unblock IP addresses, IP ranges, and user accounts for a specific time, or indefinitely, in accordance with the [Blocking policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy").
* Check [Wookieepedia:Vandalism in progress](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Vandalism_in_progress "Wookieepedia:Vandalism in progress") and the [Special:AbuseLog](/wiki/Special:AbuseLog "Special:AbuseLog") for situations that may require blocking users.
* Counter [sockpuppetry](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sockpuppetry "Wookieepedia:Sockpuppetry") via [CheckUser](#Becoming_a_CheckUser).

### Chat moderation

Administrators automatically receive server moderator status ("administrators" role) on Wookieepedia's official [Discord server](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord") and have access to moderation tools on the [Discussions](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions") forum. Both platforms have additional rules, as listed on [Discord#Rules](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord"), [Discussions#Rules](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions#Rules "Wookieepedia:Discussions"), and [Discussions Guidelines](https://starwars.fandom.com/f/g). [Bureaucrats](#Becoming_a_bureaucrat) additionally have full Discord server permissions during their tenure, including viewing and managing all private channels, but consultation channels shall be deleted upon archiving.

* Work with [Discussions moderators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions") in moderating both the Discord server and Discussions.
* Manage bots and channel access and information on the Discord server, including use of the #announcements channel, consultation channels, and [WookieeNights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeNights "Wookieepedia:WookieeNights").
* Monitor the Discord server's audit log and #admin-help and #admin-requests channels.

### [Consensus](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus")

* Facilitate the democratic community consensus process by participating in the [Senate Hall](/wiki/Forum:Senate_Hall "Forum:Senate Hall"), [Consensus track](/wiki/Forum:Consensus_track "Forum:Consensus track"), and [Trash compactor](/wiki/Forum:Trash_compactor "Forum:Trash compactor") forums.
* Enforce the [voting eligibility policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Voting_eligibility_policy "Wookieepedia:Voting eligibility policy") on voting pages.
* [Archive](/wiki/Forum:CT:Forum:Senate_Hall_wording "Forum:CT:Forum:Senate Hall wording") Senate Hall threads.
* Close votes in accordance with the [Consensus policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus").
* Maintain the [Policy and consensus updates](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Policy_and_consensus_updates "Wookieepedia:Policy and consensus updates") page.
* Respecting and implementing community consensus and site policies, such as by updating relevant policy pages and adjusting site content as necessary.

### [Deletion](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy")

* Delete and restore content in accordance with the [Deletion policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy").
* Check [Category:Candidates for speedy deletion](/wiki/Category:Candidates_for_speedy_deletion "Category:Candidates for speedy deletion") for pages marked for [speedy deletion](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Speedy_deletions "Wookieepedia:Speedy deletions") and [Category:Articles in need of verification](/wiki/Category:Articles_in_need_of_verification "Category:Articles in need of verification") for pages that, if left unverified, would be marked for speedy deletion as well.
* Hide edit revisions via [RevisionDelete](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy#RevisionDelete "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy") ([bureaucrats](#Becoming_a_bureaucrat) only).
* View deleted content.

### [Interface management](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators%27_how-to_guide#Interface_management "Wookieepedia:Administrators' how-to guide")

Administrators can manage the [appearance and functionality](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators%27_how-to_guide#Interface_management "Wookieepedia:Administrators' how-to guide") of Wookieepedia to the extent allowed by Fandom's [Customization policy](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Customization_policy "w:c:community:Customization policy") by editing [Mediawiki pages](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Special:PrefixIndex?prefix=&namespace=8).

* Maintain the [style and functionality](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Help:CSS_and_JS_customization "w:c:community:Help:CSS and JS customization") of the interface using [CSS](/wiki/MediaWiki:Common.css "MediaWiki:Common.css") and [JavaScript](/wiki/MediaWiki:Common.js "MediaWiki:Common.js") (including [Fandomdesktop.css](/wiki/MediaWiki:Fandomdesktop.css "MediaWiki:Fandomdesktop.css"), [FandomMobile.css](/wiki/MediaWiki:FandomMobile.css "MediaWiki:FandomMobile.css"), and [Gadgets](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Special:PrefixIndex?prefix=Gadget&namespace=8), accessible via [Special:Preferences](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Special:Preferences#mw-prefsection-gadgets)).
* Maintain the [Main Page](/wiki/Main_Page "Main Page") and the [main wiki navigation bar](/wiki/MediaWiki:Wiki-navigation "MediaWiki:Wiki-navigation").
* Maintain the [Community](/wiki/MediaWiki:Community-to-do-list "MediaWiki:Community-to-do-list") and [Welcome, newcomers](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Welcome,_newcomers "Wookieepedia:Welcome, newcomers") portals.
* Change the text of [software messages](/wiki/Special:AllMessages "Special:AllMessages") seen on various pages, including the [page](/wiki/MediaWiki:Blockedtext "MediaWiki:Blockedtext") that blocked users see when attempting to edit, the [file upload form](/wiki/MediaWiki:Uploadtext "MediaWiki:Uploadtext"), and the Fandom platform–wide [site](/wiki/MediaWiki:Wiki-description-site-meta "MediaWiki:Wiki-description-site-meta") [descriptions](/wiki/MediaWiki:Description "MediaWiki:Description").
  + [Site announcements](/wiki/Special:Announcements "Special:Announcements") send notifications to all users who have edited, upvoted, posted on Wookieepedia in the past 90 days.
  + When [site notice](/wiki/MediaWiki:Sitenotice "MediaWiki:Sitenotice") is used, readers would see it in a pop-up box in the lower left corner across Wookieepedia.
  + When [Anonnotice](/wiki/MediaWiki:Anonnotice "MediaWiki:Anonnotice") is used, logged-out readers would see it in the pop-up box instead, restricting the standard site notice (if in use) to logged-in users.

### Managing partnerships

* Responsibly represent the Wookieepedia community and act in its interests, which may include coordinating with Wookieepedia's [Social Media Team](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Social_Media_Team "Wookieepedia:Social Media Team") and collaborating with Fandom staff.
* Establish and promote partnerships with the wider *Star Wars* community, such [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") or its affiliates and charity initiatives.
* Modify the site interface, theme, and specially made pages (e.g. [Wookieepedia:Star Wars: Uprising Super Walkthrough](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Star_Wars:_Uprising_Super_Walkthrough "Wookieepedia:Star Wars: Uprising Super Walkthrough"), [comic previews](https://starwars.fandom.com/f/p/4400000000003780237) on [Discussions](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions"), and [Podathon](https://www.thesswnetwork.com/podathon)) for the purposes of partnerships.

### [Moving pages](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Help:Renaming_pages "Help:Renaming pages")

* Move pages to titles with /Legends suffix in accordance with the [Naming policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Naming_policy "Wookieepedia:Naming policy").
* Automatically move all associated subpages when moving a page.
* Check [Category:Files to be renamed](/wiki/Category:Files_to_be_renamed "Category:Files to be renamed") and move files as appropriate.

### [Page protection](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Protection_policy "Wookieepedia:Protection policy")

* Protect and unprotect pages and files in accordance with the [Protection policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Protection_policy "Wookieepedia:Protection policy").
* Edit and move fully protected (sysop-only) pages and replace upload-protected (sysop-only) files.
* Check user requests for page protection on [Wookieepedia:Requests for protection](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_protection "Wookieepedia:Requests for protection").

### [Rollback](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Rollback "Wookieepedia:Rollback")

* Use the [Rollback](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Rollback "Wookieepedia:Rollback") tool.
* Mark Rollback edits [as bot edits](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators%27_how-to_guide#Bot_rollback "Wookieepedia:Administrators' how-to guide").
* Mentor elected Rollback users and work with them to combat vandalism.

### User rights management

* Grant and remove the [bot flag](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Bots "Wookieepedia:Bots") for users ([bureaucrats](#Becoming_a_bureaucrat) only).
* Promote and demote [Rollback](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Rollback "Wookieepedia:Rollback") users and [Discussions moderators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions") in accordance with [Requests for user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for user rights") and [Requests for removal of user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_removal_of_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for removal of user rights").
* Promote administrators and [bureaucrats](#Becoming_a_bureaucrat) in accordance with [Requests for user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for user rights") (bureaucrats only).
* Demote administrators in accordance with [Requests for removal of user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_removal_of_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for removal of user rights") and the [Activity requirements](#Activity_requirements) ([bureaucrats](#Becoming_a_bureaucrat) only).

## Becoming an administrator

Experienced and trusted Wookieepedians may be elected by the community to become administrators. A [Request for adminship](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_user_rights#Request_for_adminship "Wookieepedia:Requests for user rights") ("RFA") lasts for two weeks and is subject to the voting requirements listed on that page, which include the nominee needing to have contributed to the site for at least one year with regular interactions with fellow users in a fair and constructive manner.

Administrators may choose to resign, be blocked due to policy violations, or be otherwise demoted in accordance with [Requests for removal of user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_removal_of_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for removal of user rights") or the [Activity requirements](#Activity_requirements).

## Becoming a CheckUser

[Shortcut](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Shortcut "Wookieepedia:Shortcut"):  
WP:CU

CheckUsers are administrators with access to the [CheckUser](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Help:CheckUser "Help:CheckUser") tool, which allows them to look up the IP addresses of registered user accounts for the purposes of combating [sockpuppetry](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sockpuppetry "Wookieepedia:Sockpuppetry"). Fandom staff have the CheckUser tool as well.

CheckUsers are nominated through [Requests for CheckUser](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_user_rights#Requests_for_CheckUser "Wookieepedia:Requests for user rights") ("RCU") and then granted the CheckUser tool at the discretion of Fandom. The current CheckUsers are:

## Activity requirements

Administrators should meet a regular level of productivity. Those who are unable should place the {{[Vacation](/wiki/Template:Vacation "Template:Vacation")}} template or a similar message at the top of their userpage.

An administrator who does not make **at least 250 non-userspace, non–talk page edits and use their sysop tools at least once within a six-month span of their adminship** is eligible for removal ([bot](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Bots "Wookieepedia:Bots") edits count toward the edit total if the administrator is actively using a bot). However, the administrator must first be cautioned by another administrator among the administration if the requirements are not being met. They may explain their relative inactivity and provide an expected date for returning to the required activity levels. The administrator shall be warned about their activity levels if they fail to achieve the activity requirements by their specified date; **demotion will occur if they fail to either provide or adhere to another explanation and expected date of return**.

Also, if an administrator has not edited or used their sysop tools for four months, then attempts to communicate should be made, including a warning—which should be repeated at five months of absence—that they shall be **demoted at six months of absence**.

## Administrative autonomy

[Shortcut](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Shortcut "Wookieepedia:Shortcut"):  
WP:AA

An administrator may not unilaterally overturn any administrative action by another administrator without a group consensus from the rest of the administration. The basis of this **administrative autonomy** policy is that administrators are responsible for enforcing site policies as a group, trusting each other with appropriately using their powers, such as [deleting articles](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy") and [blocking users](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy"), to keep Wookieepedia running as smoothly as possible.

### Rules

1. No single member of the administration may overturn any action by another administrator without a group consensus from the rest of the administration. The following exceptions apply:
   * [**Undeletion**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Deletion_policy "Wookieepedia:Deletion policy") – An item may be undeleted if there is [canonical](/wiki/Canon "Canon") evidence to justify its existence, pursuant to the [Notability policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Notability_policy "Wookieepedia:Notability policy"), or if it was clearly deleted erroneously, such as a file being deleted for being unused despite the contrary. A file may also be undeleted by request if it is intended to be used.
   * [**Page protection**](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Protection_policy "Wookieepedia:Protection policy") – Administrative autonomy for permanent protection of any level or type expires six months after the protection's original institution. However, files and templates are partially exempt from the aforementioned requirement and may be temporarily unprotected at any time for routine maintenance and updates.
   * **Partial blocks** — Superseding an existing [partial block](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy#Technical_process "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy") with a full block or a partial block of longer duration and/or wider scope does not count as overturning another administrator's decision under administrative autonomy.
   * **Former administrators** – Administrative autonomy does not apply to former administrators. With the exception of [blocks](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy") (see below), any decision by a former administrator may be overturned by a current administrator at any time.
2. Decisions approved by community consensus, such as page deletions voted through the [Trash compactor](/wiki/Forum:Trash_compactor "Forum:Trash compactor") process, are not affected by administrative autonomy (i.e. they should not be reversed without a community vote) as they are subject to the [Consensus policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus") instead.

### Reversing an administrator's decision

1. In the event an administrator feels that another administrator's decision should be overturned, a good-faith effort should be made to discuss the issue with them.
2. To determine whether to overturn any administrative decision, including blocks issued by a former administrator, the entire administration must be contacted (e.g. a ping on [Discord](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord"))—with each administrator asked to respond and given the opportunity to participate in relevant discussions—and come to a majority agreement. If an administrator is on a hiatus (e.g. using {{[Vacation](/wiki/Template:Vacation "Template:Vacation")}}), the administration need not wait for outstanding responses after a period of 2 days, though a longer waiting time is recommended for non-urgent matters.

## Administrative abuse

If you think an administrator has acted improperly against you or another user or otherwise violated site policies, you may express your concerns directly to the administrator responsible and attempt to resolve the issue with the [Dispute resolution](/wiki/Wookieepedia:User_conduct_policy#Dispute_resolution "Wookieepedia:User conduct policy") guidance if you feel comfortable doing so. Otherwise, contact another administrator through any of the methods listed on the [Contact](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Contact "Wookieepedia:Contact") page, including via Message Wall on Fandom's [Community Central](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/Community_Central), which is regulated by its own staff rather than Wookieepedia administrators.

You may instead reach out to Fandom staff, such as Community Manager [Spongebob456 (Chris)](https://community.fandom.com/wiki/User:Spongebob456), in severe cases.

In extreme circumstances, administrators can be removed from their positions via a [Requests for removal of user rights](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Requests_for_removal_of_user_rights "Wookieepedia:Requests for removal of user rights") (RFRUR) vote.

If an administrator is blocked by another administrator, then, pursuant to administrative autonomy, the Wookieepedia administration shall vote among themselves on whether or not to overturn the block as soon as possible. The blocking administrator must not continue to issue blocks against other administrators while the vote is ongoing; otherwise, any more blocks by the blocking administrator are automatically considered invalid, and that user shall be blocked for violating this policy.

If the administration decides not to overturn the block with immediate effect, then an RFRUR must be immediately opened for the blocked administrator. Regardless of the block length, the blocked administrator shall have their user rights suspended and remain blocked for the vote's duration; if the RFRUR does not pass (i.e. the Wookieepedia community *does not* agree on removing an administrator from their position), then the administrator should have their user rights restored and be unblocked immediately.
