***Resistance*** is a [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") of [Wookieepedians](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") who are dedicated to maintaining all articles related to the *[Star Wars Resistance](/wiki/Star_Wars_Resistance "Star Wars Resistance")* TV series from [2018](/wiki/2018 "2018")–[2020](/wiki/2020 "2020").

If you are considering joining the project, take a look at our mission. If you would like to join, please add your name with three tildes (~~~) at the bottom of the page under the "[Participants](#Participants)" section.

Since [*Star Wars Resistance* Season One](/wiki/Star_Wars_Resistance_Season_One "Star Wars Resistance Season One") and [*Star Wars Resistance* Season Two](/wiki/Star_Wars_Resistance_Season_Two "Star Wars Resistance Season Two") are available on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+"), this WookieeProject will focus on updating articles relating to characters, events, locations, technologies, and vehicles from the series. If you intend to be an active participant in the project, you are welcome to sign up to join the project, as mentioned above.

## Instructions[]

### Writing[]

1. Use acceptable English grammar and spelling.
2. Write from a balanced and fair perspective with a [neutral point-of-view](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style#Perspective "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style").
3. Follow the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style"), [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide"), and all other [policies on Wookieepedia](/wiki/Category:Policies_on_Wookieepedia "Category:Policies on Wookieepedia").
4. Do not add [speculation](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Attribution#No_original_research "Wookieepedia:Attribution") of any kind, whether it is unconfirmed info or trivial information. If it is not [stated](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Canon_policy "Wookieepedia:Canon policy") by a [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm")-affiliated (or official) source, it is not [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and will not be accepted on Wookieepedia.
5. All information in an article must be [sourced](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing").
6. "Behind the scenes" section should include notes on appearances and any developmental material.
7. Don't forget a "Personality and traits" section for characters where applicable, as well as a "Powers and abilities" section, also where applicable.

### Editing[]

1. Check for flawless spelling and grammar, as well as cohesive sentence flow and context.
2. Make sure articles follow the Manual of Style, Layout Guide, and all other Wookieepedia policies.
3. Make sure all information, images, and quotes are properly sourced.
4. Fix templates and check for excessive/incorrect links.
5. Check for any appearances or sources that might have been missed.

## Articles[]

**All articles originating from *Resistance* have a goal to eventually obtain Featured, Good, or Comprehensive status.** Articles that cannot reach the required length can be put up for nomination for Comprehensive status. Even the most minor characters and articles have the potential to be upgraded.

### Choosing an article[]

Many subjects appear in the *Resistance* series and related material—some old, some new. Please note that while the project aims to cover all subjects appearing in the series, we cannot cover every single person, place, or thing that appears. Furthermore, it is improper to try to use *this project* to focus on subjects that extensively appear elsewhere. For all articles that appear in *Resistance*, their related and corresponding sections should be maintained.

In choosing articles to add as projects, the following questions should be asked about the article. **For an article to be added as a planned/future project, the answers to at least one of these questions must be "yes."**

1. Does the subject appear for the **first time** in *Resistance* material **OR** have a **first ID** originate in *Resistance* material? If not,
2. Are the **majority** of the subject's appearances in *Resistance* material? If not,
3. Do(es) the subject's appearance(s) in *Resistance* material have an **important impact**?

If you have questions, do not hesitate to bring the article up on the [project's talk page](/wiki/Wookieepedia_talk:WookieeProject_Resistance "Wookieepedia talk:WookieeProject Resistance").

Once the article is added as a future project, please place the [notice](#Talk_page_template) below on the article's talk page so that others may get involved.

### Current individual projects[]

Please add articles that you are working on and list your name next to them. Once an article has been nominated for Featured/Good/Comprehensive status, move it to the [appropriate section above](#Promoted_articles).

**Inactive projects are subject to removal on a regular basis to keep articles open for others. Please therefore be courteous to others and do not reserve more projects than you can handle.**
