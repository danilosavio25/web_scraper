## Events[]

* [Maz Kanata](/wiki/Maz_Kanata "Maz Kanata") helps [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo") acquire the [Ubese](/wiki/Ubese "Ubese") armor for her [Boushh](/wiki/Boushh "Boushh") disguise.[[8]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-8)
* [Han Solo](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo") is [rescued](/wiki/Rescue_of_Han_Solo "Rescue of Han Solo") from [Jabba the Hutt](/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure "Jabba Desilijic Tiure"). During the rescue, Leia Organa [kills](/wiki/Death "Death") the [Hutt](/wiki/Hutt "Hutt") [crime lord](/wiki/Crime_lord "Crime lord") aboard his own [sail barge](/wiki/Sail_barge "Sail barge").[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-2)
* [Boba Fett](/wiki/Boba_Fett "Boba Fett") is eaten by the Sarlacc Pit of Carkoon, but escapes.
* [Bib Fortuna](/wiki/Bib_Fortuna "Bib Fortuna") takes Jabba the Hutt’s throne.
* Having kept the promise he made to [Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") returned to [Dagobah](/wiki/Dagobah "Dagobah") to complete his [Jedi training](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training"), but Yoda dies of old age before he could do so. Before dying, Yoda told Luke that he must confront [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") to become a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight").[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-2)
* [Gallius Rax](/wiki/Gallius_Rax "Gallius Rax") promoted to the [rank](/wiki/Military_rank "Military rank") of [admiral](/wiki/Admiral "Admiral").[[9]](#cite_note-Empire's_End-9)
* The [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") began its fragmentation[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3) after the first death of [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") [Sheev Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") over [Endor](/wiki/Endor "Endor"). The [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor") also sees the destruction of the [DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station](/wiki/DS-2_Death_Star_II_Mobile_Battle_Station "DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station") and redemption of Vader, with the latter becoming Anakin Skywalker once more after returning to the [light side](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force") and killing Sidious.[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-2)
* Most remaining Imperials surrender or flee, starting the [Imperial Remnant](/wiki/Imperial_Remnant "Imperial Remnant").
* Luke Skywalker holds a [funeral](/wiki/Funeral_of_Anakin_Skywalker "Funeral of Anakin Skywalker") on Endor to honor his father.[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-2)
* [A riot](/wiki/Riot_in_Monument_Plaza "Riot in Monument Plaza") breaks out in [Monument Plaza](/wiki/Monument_Plaza "Monument Plaza"), beginning the [Coruscant civil war](/wiki/Coruscant_civil_war "Coruscant civil war").[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3)
* A power vacuum opens up on [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") as a result of the death of Jabba[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3) and withdrawal of the [Imperial Army](/wiki/Imperial_Army "Imperial Army"). The [Mining Collective](/wiki/Mining_Collective "Mining Collective") seeks to take advantage of the vacuum and occupies [Mos Pelgo](/wiki/Freetown "Freetown"), though they are soon [single-handledly](/wiki/Liberation_of_Mos_Pelgo "Liberation of Mos Pelgo") defeated by[[10]](#cite_note-Chapter_9-10) [Sheriff](/wiki/Sheriff "Sheriff")[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3) [Cobb Vanth](/wiki/Cobb_Vanth "Cobb Vanth").[[10]](#cite_note-Chapter_9-10)
* [Mas Amedda](/wiki/Mas_Amedda "Mas Amedda") acted as Emperor in all but name and politics, while holding little real influence beyond Coruscant.[[1]](#cite_note-Life_Debt-1)
* The [Alliance to Restore the Republic](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic") is [reorganized](/wiki/Declaration_of_a_New_Republic "Declaration of a New Republic") into the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic").[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3)
* As part of his [Contingency](/wiki/Contingency "Contingency") plan, the Empire follow Palpatine's last order via [Operation: Cinder](/wiki/Operation:_Cinder "Operation: Cinder").[[11]](#cite_note-Shattered_Empire_2-11)
* [Mon Mothma](/wiki/Mon_Mothma "Mon Mothma") is elected the first [Chancellor](/wiki/Chancellor_(New_Republic) "Chancellor (New Republic)") of the New Republic.[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3)
* The [Galactic Senate](/wiki/New_Republic_Senate "New Republic Senate") is restored on [Chandrila](/wiki/Chandrila "Chandrila").[[3]](#cite_note-Aftermath-3)
* [Iden Versio](/wiki/Iden_Versio "Iden Versio") and [Del Meeko](/wiki/Del_Meeko "Del Meeko") [defect](/wiki/Skirmish_on_Vardos "Skirmish on Vardos") from the Galactic Empire and join the Rebel Alliance/New Republic.[[12]](#cite_note-Battlefront_II-12)
* With the help of [Lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") [Shara Bey](/wiki/Shara_Bey "Shara Bey") and [R2-D2](/wiki/R2-D2 "R2-D2"), [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") travels to [Vetine](/wiki/Vetine "Vetine") to [retrieve](/wiki/Mission_to_Vetine "Mission to Vetine") the remnants of two [Great Trees](/wiki/Great_Tree_(Coruscant) "Great Tree (Coruscant)") that were stolen from the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") by [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious").[[13]](#cite_note-SE4-13)
