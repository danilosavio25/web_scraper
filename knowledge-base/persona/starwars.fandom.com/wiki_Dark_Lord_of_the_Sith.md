**Dark Lord of the Sith** was a title used by members of the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith"), also known as [Sith Lords](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord"). Under the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two"), it was applied to both the [Sith Master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") and the [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice").

[Darth Plagueis](/wiki/Darth_Plagueis "Darth Plagueis")' apprentice, [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), conspired to restore the Sith to power roughly a [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") after the [death](/wiki/Death "Death") of [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane"), the Dark [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") who reformed the Sith. During the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") and [Savage Opress](/wiki/Savage_Opress "Savage Opress") claimed the mantle of Dark Lord despite the presence of Sidious and [Darth Tyranus](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"). Sidious killed Opress and chastised Maul, reminding his former apprentice that there could only be two Sith Lords at any given [time](/wiki/Time "Time") in the galaxy.

After the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") [fell](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order"), [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") was ruled by two Dark Lords—[Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Sidious and his apprentice, the fallen [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker")—during the [era](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"). Their reign ended when Vader [sacrificed](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor") himself by destroying his master, rendering the Sith extinct through the [deaths](/wiki/Death "Death") of the final Dark Lords.

Despite the Sith's [prophesied destruction](/wiki/Chosen_One "Chosen One"), their legacy endured through Sidious, who was revived through [cloning](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") and the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force"), as well as [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), Vader's [grandson](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family"). During the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order")'s [war](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War") against the [Resistance](/wiki/Resistance "Resistance"), Ren aspired to rule all the [galaxy](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") as the new [Emperor](/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") and Dark Lord of the Sith. Following Ren's [redemption](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force"), Sidious perished along with his [followers](/wiki/Sith_Eternal "Sith Eternal") in the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol") as the very last Dark Lord of the Sith remaining.

The Dark [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") of the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith"), alternatively known as a [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord"), was a title in the Sith Order.[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_II-2) The title "[Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth")" used by all Sith Lords roughly translated into "Dark Lord."[[5]](#cite_note-SWEasy-5)

The [Valley of the Dark Lords](/wiki/Valley_of_the_Dark_Lords "Valley of the Dark Lords") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Korriban](/wiki/Moraband "Moraband") contained crypts of the deceased Dark Lords of the Sith.[[6]](#cite_note-Moraband_DB-6) The planet became a sacred place of ceremonial rituals for the Sith. The [burial](/wiki/Burial "Burial") ceremony of a Dark Lord of the Sith would be accompanied by hundreds of Sith warriors carrying the body of the fallen master. The corpse was stored in the tomb along with precious objects, and an escort of slaves accompanied the deceased on his journey to the spirit realm. The Sith then left the planet until the next burial.[[7]](#cite_note-Mustafar_and_Other_Outer_Rim_Planets-7)

The Sith later fell victim to infighting because of their greed for power, weakening the Sith Order to the point where the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") defeated and nearly [destroyed](/wiki/Fall_of_the_Old_Republic "Fall of the Old Republic") their ancient rivals.[[8]](#cite_note-SOTS-8) However, the ancient Sith Order had one surviving member: [Darth Bane](/wiki/Darth_Bane "Darth Bane"). Bane assumed the mantle of Dark Lord and established a philosophy known as the [Rule of Two](/wiki/Rule_of_Two "Rule of Two"), declaring that only two Sith Lords could exist at any [time](/wiki/Time "Time")—a [Sith Master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") and a [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice").[[9]](#cite_note-Bane_DB-9) After his death, Bane was the last Dark Lord to be buried at Moraband's Valley of the Dark Lords.[[10]](#cite_note-Bane_SWE-10)

### Fall of the Republic[]

 [![Darth Sidious concealed his nature as the Dark Lord of the Sith behind the facade of Sheev Palpatine.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2e/DarthSidious-Villains2024.png/revision/latest?cb=20240913230542) [![Darth Sidious concealed his nature as the Dark Lord of the Sith behind the facade of Sheev Palpatine.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2e/DarthSidious-Villains2024.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20240913230542)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/2e/DarthSidious-Villains2024.png/revision/latest?cb=20240913230542) 

Darth Sidious concealed his nature as the Dark Lord of the Sith behind the facade of Sheev Palpatine.

The Dark Lords that succeeded [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Bane would adhere to his teachings, maintaining the Sith Order's ranks at two members throughout the [millennium](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium")-long [reign](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") of the modern [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"). [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), publicly known as Sheev Palpatine from [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo"), became the Dark Lord of The Sith after betraying and [murdering](/wiki/Death "Death") his Sith Master [Darth Plagueis](/wiki/Darth_Plagueis "Darth Plagueis"). Sidious was the Dark Lord of the Sith during the [last years](/wiki/Republic_Era "Republic Era") of the Republic and the [rise](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[11]](#cite_note-Episode_III-11)

At the time of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), Sidious' former apprentice [Darth Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") sought to reclaim his status as a Sith Lord while training his brother, [Savage Opress](/wiki/Savage_Opress "Savage Opress"). Having replaced Maul with [Darth Tyranus](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), Sidious confronted the [Zabrak](/wiki/Zabrak "Zabrak") Sith claimants on [Mandalore](/wiki/Mandalore "Mandalore"). There, he killed Opress and subdued Maul. The Dark Lord castigated Maul, informing the [Dathomirian](/wiki/Dathomirian "Dathomirian") that he had been replaced in an Order that permitted only two Sith Lords. However, Sidious informed Maul that he still had [uses](/wiki/Talzin "Talzin") for his former apprentice,[[12]](#cite_note-The_Lawless-12) using him to [draw out](/wiki/Campaign_against_the_Shadow_Collective "Campaign against the Shadow Collective") [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Talzin](/wiki/Talzin "Talzin").[[13]](#cite_note-Son_of_Dathomir,_Part_One-13)

### Age of the Empire[]

:   "*I am a lord of the Sith. They are the ones who should be running.*"
:   ―Darth Vader[[14]](#cite_note-Vader_Down_1-14)

 [![The dark side of the Force transformed Anakin Skywalker into the Dark Lord of the Sith known as Darth Vader.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/0a/Battlefront_Vader.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20181211045721) [![The dark side of the Force transformed Anakin Skywalker into the Dark Lord of the Sith known as Darth Vader.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/0a/Battlefront_Vader.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181211045721)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/0a/Battlefront_Vader.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20181211045721) 

The dark side of the Force transformed Anakin Skywalker into the Dark Lord of the Sith known as Darth Vader.

 

Sidious would later betray Tyranus and have him killed by the [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"), as he desired to make Skywalker his apprentice. After the death of Tyranus, Skywalker fell to the dark side and became Darth Vader, Sidious' third apprentice. Sidious [proclaimed](/wiki/Proclamation_of_the_New_Order "Proclamation of the New Order") himself [Galactic Emperor](/wiki/Galactic_Emperor "Galactic Emperor") after [destroying](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") the Jedi Order.[[11]](#cite_note-Episode_III-11) The Dark Lords ruled [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") for a generation[[15]](#cite_note-Sith_DB-15) until the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor")[[16]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-16) in [4 ABY](/wiki/4_ABY "4 ABY"),[[17]](#cite_note-GA-17) when Sidious betrayed Vader and tried to make [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") his new apprentice.[[16]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-16) Previously, Vader had tried to do the same, but he gave up on his quest to make his son his apprentice after the [mission to Exegol](/wiki/Mission_to_Exegol "Mission to Exegol"),[[18]](#cite_note-Darth_Vader_11-18) only to resume during his alliance with the [Schism Imperial](/wiki/Schism_Imperial "Schism Imperial").[[19]](#cite_note-Darth_Vader_48-19)

During the Battle of [Endor](/wiki/Endor "Endor"), Luke refused to kill his [father](/wiki/Parent "Parent"), however, declaring his allegiance to the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi").[[16]](#cite_note-Episode_VI-16) Enraged by Luke's refusal to turn to the dark side, Sidious [tortured](/wiki/Torture "Torture") the Jedi Knight with [Force lightning](/wiki/Force_lightning "Force lightning"). Luke begged Vader to save him; unwilling to see his son die, Vader sacrificed his life[[15]](#cite_note-Sith_DB-15) by destroying the [Emperor](/wiki/Emperor "Emperor").[[20]](#cite_note-TSWB-20)

### Return of the Dark Lord[]

:   "*At last the work of generations is complete. The great error is corrected. The day of victory is at hand. The day of revenge. The day of the Sith.*"
:   ―Darth Sidious — ![20?cb=20250116042720](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/ee/Quote-audio.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/20?cb=20250116042720) [Link](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/12/PalpatinesBroadcast.flac/revision/latest?cb=20250308181525)▶️ ([file info](/wiki/File:PalpatinesBroadcast.flac "File:PalpatinesBroadcast.flac"))[[21]](#cite_note-TROS_EE-21)

The deaths of Sidious and Vader marked the end of the lineage of Dark Lords going back to Darth Bane,[[22]](#cite_note-TLJ_VD-22) and the beginning of the [New Republic Era](/wiki/New_Republic_Era "New Republic Era").[[15]](#cite_note-Sith_DB-15) Vader died as Anakin Skywalker[[23]](#cite_note-Anakin_DB-23) having returned to the light side[[24]](#cite_note-Obi-Wan_DB-24) through an act of selflessness and sacrifice,[[23]](#cite_note-Anakin_DB-23) which allowed Yoda to preserve Anakin's identity when he became one with the Force. As a result, Anakin joined Yoda and [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") in death as a [Force spirit](/wiki/Force_spirit "Force spirit"). With his death, Anakin brought balance to the Force[[25]](#cite_note-Yoda_DB-25) and his destiny as the [Chosen One](/wiki/Chosen_One "Chosen One") was fulfilled.[[26]](#cite_note-Beware_the_Power-26)

 [![Resurrected on Exegol, Darth Sidious endeavored to reclaim the galaxy as Emperor and Dark Lord of the Sith.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/07/Sidious_triumphant.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250205185007) [![Resurrected on Exegol, Darth Sidious endeavored to reclaim the galaxy as Emperor and Dark Lord of the Sith.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/07/Sidious_triumphant.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20250205185007)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/07/Sidious_triumphant.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250205185007) 

Resurrected on Exegol, Darth Sidious endeavored to reclaim the galaxy as Emperor and Dark Lord of the Sith.

 

Unbeknownst to the galaxy at large, Sidious was revived when he used the dark side to move his spirit to a [cloned](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") body.[[20]](#cite_note-TSWB-20) Aided by his [followers](/wiki/Sith_cultist "Sith cultist") on [Exegol](/wiki/Exegol "Exegol"), the [Sith Eternal](/wiki/Sith_Eternal "Sith Eternal") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion"), the fallen Emperor [engineered](/wiki/Bioengineering "Bioengineering") the [strandcast](/wiki/Strand-Cast "Strand-Cast") [Snoke](/wiki/Snoke "Snoke")[[21]](#cite_note-TROS_EE-21) to groom Vader's grandson, [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), into an heir that was worthy of inheriting the Sith legacy.[[27]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-27) Ren, who coveted the power of his Sith grandfather,[[28]](#cite_note-Episode_VII-28) sought to rule all the galaxy as the new Emperor[[29]](#cite_note-Episode_IX-29) and Dark Lord of the Sith.[[30]](#cite_note-TROS_JN-30)

Sidious' plan to restore his Order through the creation of a new [Sith Empire](/wiki/Sith_Empire "Sith Empire")[[27]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-27) ended at the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol"). Kylo Ren had returned to the [light side of the Force](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force"), renouncing the dark side and his claim to the Sith mantle, while Sidious' [granddaughter](/wiki/Palpatine_family "Palpatine family"), the [Jedi apprentice](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Rey](/wiki/Rey_Skywalker "Rey Skywalker") rejected the Emperor's entreaty to succeed him. Ultimately, Rey used the power from all the past deceased Jedi to permanently destroy Sidious and Sith for good.[[29]](#cite_note-Episode_IX-29)

## Behind the scenes[]

In the story of the non-[canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* short "[THE TWINS](/wiki/THE_TWINS "THE TWINS")," the [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [twins](/wiki/Twin "Twin") [Am](/wiki/Am "Am") and [Karre](/wiki/Karre "Karre") both held the title "[Dark Lord](/wiki/Dark_Lord "Dark Lord")."[[53]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-53) In that capacity, both wielded power over a [Galactic Empire](/wiki/TRIGGER_Empire "TRIGGER Empire")[[54]](#cite_note-Twins-54) that emerged after the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol").[[55]](#cite_note-IGN-55) The twins' empire hoped to claim control over the galaxy, only for Karre to rebel and abandon the regime.[[54]](#cite_note-Twins-54)
