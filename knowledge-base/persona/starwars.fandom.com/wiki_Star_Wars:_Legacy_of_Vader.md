***Star Wars: Legacy of Vader***, or ***Star Wars: The Legacy of Vader***, is a [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025") ongoing [comic book](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") series written by [Charles Soule](/wiki/Charles_Soule "Charles Soule") and illustrated by [Luke Ross](/wiki/Luke_Ross "Luke Ross"). The series focuses on [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") and takes place right between the events of [*Star Wars*: Episode VIII *The Last Jedi*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VIII_The_Last_Jedi "Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi") and [*Star Wars*: Episode IX *The Rise of Skywalker*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_IX_The_Rise_of_Skywalker "Star Wars: Episode IX The Rise of Skywalker"). The [first issue](/wiki/Legacy_of_Vader_1 "Legacy of Vader 1") was published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [February 5](/wiki/February_5 "February 5"), 2025.
