## Plot summary[]

[Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") traveled to [Oosalon](/wiki/Oosalon "Oosalon") to investigate the [warlord](/wiki/Warlord "Warlord") [Guattako](/wiki/Guattako "Guattako")'s [child](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") [army](/wiki/Guattako%27s_army "Guattako's army"), and why it was funded by the [Hutts](/wiki/Hutt_Clan "Hutt Clan"). After he was captured by his forces he was led by a [Kyuzo](/wiki/Kyuzo "Kyuzo") called [Kyossk](/wiki/Kyossk "Kyossk") and a teenage-aged individual called [Quia](/wiki/Quia "Quia") to his hideout. After confronted with Guattako, Windu demanded him to drop the operation, giving him a chance to surrender. After Guattako refused, Windu repaired [his](/wiki/Mace_Windu%27s_lightsaber "Mace Windu's lightsaber") broken [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") [using](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") and pulled it to himself, slicing Kyossk's hand off in the process, and Guattako sent his men to attack him. After deflecting their [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") bolts, [Quia](/wiki/Quia "Quia") began to send bolts towards Windu. Guattako then engaged in combat with Mace, swinging his [axe](/wiki/Axe "Axe") at him. Guattako hit Mace with the end of his axe knocking him out of the fight for a couple seconds. Before Guattako was able to swing at Windu again, a scared Pantoran youngling shot Guattako in the chest, [killing](/wiki/Death "Death") him. Afterwards, Jedi Masters [Plo Koon](/wiki/Plo_Koon "Plo Koon"), [Kit Fisto](/wiki/Kit_Fisto "Kit Fisto"), [Depa Billaba](/wiki/Depa_Billaba "Depa Billaba"), and [Qui-Gon Jinn](/wiki/Qui-Gon_Jinn "Qui-Gon Jinn"), who Windu had called with the Force, arrived to the scene and Mace [hologramed](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"), who told him that Quia would be sent to [prison](/wiki/Prison "Prison") by the local authorities, which prompted Windu to ask if they really saved everyone.
