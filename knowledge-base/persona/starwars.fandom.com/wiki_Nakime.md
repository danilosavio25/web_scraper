**Nakime** was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"). At some point, she and [her master](/wiki/Nakime%27s_Jedi_Master "Nakime's Jedi Master") engaged in a [lightsaber duel](/wiki/Lightsaber_duel "Lightsaber duel") with the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") [Aman](/wiki/Aman "Aman"). During the duel she performed a [Force jump](/wiki/Force_jump "Force jump"), with the help of the [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") [Daruma](/wiki/Daruma "Daruma"), while attacking Aman; however, Aman leaped at her and struck her eyes with her [lightsabers](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber"), blinding her and causing her to fall down a cliff and into the chasm.[[1]](#cite_note-Paradise-1)

Afterwards, she underwent a series of spiritual trials in which she overcame the temptation to the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force").[[1]](#cite_note-Paradise-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

:   "*In the story, the main character becomes blind, and in order to depict the scenery that they feel in their hearts while they are blind, I did various artwork in the sense of going back and forth between reality, fantasy, and various worlds.*"
:   ―Tadahiro Yoshihira[[4]](#cite_note-First_Look-4)

Nakime appeared in "[The Bird of Paradise](/wiki/The_Bird_of_Paradise "The Bird of Paradise")," a [non-canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") short film directed by [Tadahiro Yoshihira](/wiki/Tadahiro_Yoshihira "Tadahiro Yoshihira") released as part of the [third volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_3 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 3")  of the *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* series. The animated short, produced by [Polygon Pictures](/wiki/Polygon_Pictures "Polygon Pictures"), released on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") on [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"). The character was first introduced at the *Star Wars: Visions* panel at [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)") on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), 2025.[[3]](#cite_note-Celebration-3) She is voiced by [Tomoyo Kurosawa](/wiki/Tomoyo_Kurosawa "Tomoyo Kurosawa") in the original [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_language "wikipedia:Japanese language") version and by Sonoya Mizuno in the English dub.[[5]](#cite_note-Getty-5) According to Yoshihira, Nakime's acrobatics and fighting style during the lightsaber duel were meant to highlight her hot-headedness and lack of maturity.[[6]](#cite_note-Encount-6)
