**Chita** was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [smuggler](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler"). During the [Imperial Era](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era"), she was hired to bring [Arluu](/wiki/Arluu "Arluu"), a [prince](/wiki/Prince "Prince") being pursued by the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), to safety.[[1]](#cite_note-Smuggler-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

:   "*The main character is a rogue and I wanted to make it an action-packed fun adventure. When we were discussing the project, I thought of Han Solo, and the design became similar.*"
:   ―Masahiko Otsuka[[2]](#cite_note-First_Look-2)

Chita first appeared in "[The Smuggler](/wiki/The_Smuggler_(short_film) "The Smuggler (short film)")," a non-[canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") short film directed by [Masahiko Otsuka](/wiki/Masahiko_Otsuka "Masahiko Otsuka") that released as part of the [third volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_3 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 3") of the *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* series. The animated short, produced by [Studio Trigger](/wiki/Trigger_(animation_studio) "Trigger (animation studio)"), released on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") on [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"). The character was first introduced at the *Star Wars: Visions* panel at [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)") on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), 2025.[[3]](#cite_note-Day_3-3) She is voiced by [Ami Maeshima](/wiki/Ami_Maeshima "Ami Maeshima") in the original [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_language "wikipedia:Japanese language") version and by Emma Myers in the English dub.[[1]](#cite_note-Smuggler-1)
