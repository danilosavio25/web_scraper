**WookieeProject:Comics** is a collaborative effort across [Wookieepedia](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") to improve the quality and status of the thousands of articles relating to [*Star Wars* comic books](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books"). If you are a comic fan and a *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* then this [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") is for you! If you wish to join the project then simply sign your name in the participants section below.

## WookieeProject goal[]

The overall goal of the WookieeProject is to improve many of the comic book related articles on the Wook from both [Canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"). The project will also be overlapping with other WookieeProjects when it comes to status articles as many comic articles will fall under multiple projects.

## WookieeProject scope[]

WookieeProject:Comics will cover articles relating to comics, [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon"), [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"), and out of universe. While articles that show their subject are preferred, this is not a requirement and articles that just mention a subject will be fine. Although there will be requirements, any author, illustrator, editor, company, etc. that has mainly worked on comics would also fall into this scope and can be nominated. If you have any questions feel free to ask.

### Rules for nominations[]

If you wish to nominate an article for the project it must meet one of the following requirements:

* The article subject must've originated in a comic (mentions count)
* OR at least half of the article's appearances must be from comics (depending on the article we can list sources too)
