## Production[]

### Development[]

*Rebuild the Galaxy: Pieces of the Past* was written by [Dan Hernandez](/wiki/Dan_Hernandez "Dan Hernandez") and [Benji Samit](/wiki/Benji_Samit "Benji Samit"), who served as the series' showrunners as the sequel series to the [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024") animated series *[LEGO Star Wars: Rebuild the Galaxy](/wiki/LEGO_Star_Wars:_Rebuild_the_Galaxy "LEGO Star Wars: Rebuild the Galaxy")*.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)[[5]](#cite_note-Giant_Chewbacca-5) The series was directed by [Chris Buckley](/wiki/Chris_Buckley "Chris Buckley").[[5]](#cite_note-Giant_Chewbacca-5)

During a [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)") panel on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"), [Ahmed Best](/wiki/Ahmed_Best "Ahmed Best") said that the second series would explore the [Darth Jar Jar](/wiki/Jar_Jar_Binks "Jar Jar Binks") theory.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)

### Casting[]

On April 19, 2025, Best confirmed that he would be reprising his role as "Darth Jar Jar" in *Pieces of the Past*. The characters [Sig Greebling](/wiki/Sig_Greebling "Sig Greebling"), [Yesi Scala](/wiki/Yesi_Scala "Yesi Scala") and [Bobarian Afol](/wiki/Bobarian_Afol "Bobarian Afol") were also confirmed as returning for the second season. In addition, Best confirmed that [Ben Schwartz](/wiki/Ben_Schwartz "Ben Schwartz") would be voicing the [Lepi](/wiki/Lepi "Lepi") smuggler [Jaxxon T. Tumperakki](/wiki/Jaxxon_T._Tumperakki "Jaxxon T. Tumperakki") and that [Ashley Eckstein](/wiki/Ashley_Eckstein "Ashley Eckstein") would be voicing [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano").[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)

On August 14, 2025, [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") confirmed that [Anthony Daniels](/wiki/Anthony_Daniels "Anthony Daniels") would be reprising his role as [C-3PO](/wiki/C-3PO "C-3PO") and [Kelly Marie Tran](/wiki/Kelly_Marie_Tran "Kelly Marie Tran") as Darth [Rose Tico](/wiki/Rose_Tico "Rose Tico"). Other confirmed cast members included [Sam Witwer](/wiki/Sam_Witwer "Sam Witwer") as [Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul"), [Cameron Monaghan](/wiki/Cameron_Monaghan "Cameron Monaghan") as [Cal Kestis](/wiki/Cal_Kestis "Cal Kestis"), [Nika Futterman](/wiki/Nika_Futterman "Nika Futterman") as [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress") and [James Arnold Taylor](/wiki/James_Arnold_Taylor "James Arnold Taylor") as [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi").[[5]](#cite_note-Giant_Chewbacca-5)

## Release and reception[]

The official trailer was released on [August 14](/wiki/August_14 "August 14"), 2025.[[6]](#cite_note-Official_Trailer-6)[[5]](#cite_note-Giant_Chewbacca-5) On [September 4](/wiki/September_4 "September 4"), a second trailer featuring [Revan](/wiki/Revan "Revan") and [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") was released.[[7]](#cite_note-Darth_Revan-7)

The series premiered on Disney+ on September 19, 2025.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)
