**Fortress Vader**, also known as **Darth Vader's castle**, was a stronghold located on the [volcanic](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") [world](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar"). Once the site of a [Sith temple](/wiki/Sith_temple_(Mustafar) "Sith temple (Mustafar)"), the fortress was built above a [Sith cave](/wiki/Sith_cave "Sith cave") that still remained, as well as the ancient [Corvax Fortress](/wiki/Corvax_Fortress "Corvax Fortress") located underneath. Existing by the time of [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), the Sith cave contained a [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") locus.

The castle was designed by the preserved consciousness of the ancient [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") and [artist](/wiki/Artist "Artist") [Darth Momin](/wiki/Darth_Momin "Darth Momin"), whose haunted [mask](/wiki/Mask_of_Lord_Momin "Mask of Lord Momin") possessed an Imperial [lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") and made him create the fortress's blueprints. Momin meant for it to channel the Force energies from the dark side locus under the castle and open the door to the dark side. The Sith Lord [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") allowed Momin's design to be built using Imperial resources in his possession. Although Vader failed to open the door with the first design, after a trial and error process involving eight more designs, a perfect design was finally built with the ninth. It was able to open the door to the dark side, but when Vader was distracted by a [Mustafarian](/wiki/Mustafarian "Mustafarian") [uprising](/wiki/Battle_of_Fortress_Vader "Battle of Fortress Vader"), Momin opened it for himself and resurrected his original body.

Despite Vader's intervention, the army of Mustafarians were able to wipe out the [Imperial garrison](/wiki/Fortress_Vader_Imperial_garrison "Fortress Vader Imperial garrison"). Vader retreated to the castle and used its power to do the same to the Mustafarians. Momin then confronted Vader and was able to badly damage Vader in their brief duel. Vader crushed him using a stone slab. With Momin, the Mustafarians, and the Imperial garrison dead, Vader was left alone to open the door to the dark side himself. Vader's spirit entered the portal in search of his dead wife, [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala"). He returned unsuccessful and used the fortress' power to destroy the portal. He then contacted his [master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master"), [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), to confirm his planned return to Coruscant. Later, an Imperial Inspector got driven insane by lava fumes by Vader, creating Vader`s servant [Vaneé](/wiki/Vane%C3%A9 "Vaneé"). Ever since then, Vaneé became a devoted servant to Darth Vader.

The castle came to serve as Vader's personal residence and from time to time, he rested inside a [bacta tank](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank"). On one occasion, his stasis was cut short by [Director](/wiki/Director "Director") [Orson Krennic](/wiki/Orson_Callan_Krennic "Orson Callan Krennic") of the [Death Star](/wiki/DS-1_Orbital_Battle_Station "DS-1 Orbital Battle Station") [project](/wiki/Project_Stardust "Project Stardust"). The two discussed the project at hand. Vader also conversed with [Admiral](/wiki/Admiral "Admiral") [Kendal Ozzel](/wiki/Kendal_Ozzel "Kendal Ozzel") and [General](/wiki/General "General") [Maximilian Veers](/wiki/Maximilian_Veers "Maximilian Veers") there about the over stretching efforts to defeat the [Rebel Alliance](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic"). After Vader`s death, Vaneé attempted to resurrect his master, after hearing the seeming ghost of his master, but it turned out to be a hallucination. Vaneé would be trapped by his own nightmares for a time before escaping and continuing lurking beneath the castle. Fortress Vader would later fall into disrepair, and by the time of the [First Order-Resistance War](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War"), the rotting castle would be guarded by the [Alazmec of Winsit](/wiki/Alazmec_of_Winsit "Alazmec of Winsit"). Vader's grandson, [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), would eventually visit the castle grounds in search of Vader's [Sith wayfinder](/wiki/Sith_wayfinder "Sith wayfinder").

## Details[]

The castle's unique shape was based on designs by an ancient Sith Lord, Momin, and was specifically attuned to the dark energies of that world for one purpose: to pierce the veil between life and death.*"

Fortress Vader was built upon a Sith cave in the Gahenn Plains of Mustafar.

 

[Fortress](/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") Vader was a [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") castle designed by the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Darth Momin](/wiki/Darth_Momin "Darth Momin")[[11]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_22-11) and occupied by the Sith [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker").[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) Momin intended for it to tune [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") energies from the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") locus under it and open the door to the dark side of the Force.[[7]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_21-7) It was constructed by Vader [himself](/wiki/Gender "Gender") as a testament to his own might,[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) with the assistance of his [Imperial](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") resources.[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3) It was located on the site of his [greatest defeat](/wiki/Duel_on_Mustafar "Duel on Mustafar"),[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) and was built over an ancient [Sith cave](/wiki/Sith_cave "Sith cave")[[7]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_21-7) in the [Gahenn Plains](/wiki/Gahenn_Plains "Gahenn Plains") of the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar").[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3)

The complex's location and design came as much from the dark side of the Force as it did Vader's personal history.[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) The central tower was constructed out of [obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian"),[[12]](#cite_note-Rogue_One_Visual_Guide-12) and its tuning towers served as conduits for the dark side. Within, Vader maintained a [bacta tank](/wiki/Bacta_tank "Bacta tank") where he would remove his [armor](/wiki/Darth_Vader%27s_armor "Darth Vader's armor") and rest inside.[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) The castle also contained a [throne room](/wiki/Vader%27s_throne_room "Vader's throne room"), from which Vader could take [hologram](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") calls and look out across Mustafar.[[5]](#cite_note-Part_III-5)

## History[]

### Design[]

:   "*You have given me a wondrous opportunity, Lord Vader. I will not throw that away. This will be my masterpiece.*"
:   ―Darth Momin[[11]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_22-11)

 [![The Sith Lord Darth Vader discovered early blueprints for the fortress on Mustafar.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/FortressVaderBlueprints-2017DarthVader21.png/revision/latest?cb=20181129060411) [![The Sith Lord Darth Vader discovered early blueprints for the fortress on Mustafar.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/FortressVaderBlueprints-2017DarthVader21.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181129060411)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/FortressVaderBlueprints-2017DarthVader21.png/revision/latest?cb=20181129060411) 

The Sith Lord Darth Vader discovered early blueprints for the fortress on Mustafar.

 

In [14 BBY](/wiki/14_BBY "14 BBY"), after a [rogue Inquisitor chase](/wiki/Coruscant_Incident "Coruscant Incident"),[[13]](#cite_note-Vader_19_chronology_note-13) which [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Vader successfully dealt with, Vader requested a planet to call his own from his [Sith Master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master"), [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"). Agreeing, Sidious suggested [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine") and [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") for his [Sith apprentice](/wiki/Sith_apprentice "Sith apprentice"), but Vader asked for control over the [volcanic](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") planet of Mustafar,[[14]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_20-14) as it was highly linked to his connection with the dark side.[[8]](#cite_note-Databank_Vader-8) Sidious accepted and sent Vader to the planet, accompanied by [Colonel](/wiki/Colonel "Colonel") [Alva Brenne](/wiki/Alva_Brenne "Alva Brenne") and [Lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") [Roggo](/wiki/Roggo "Roggo"), as well as the [Mask of Lord Momin](/wiki/Mask_of_Lord_Momin "Mask of Lord Momin"), which Sidious had found in a hidden room in the [Jedi Archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives") [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") prior and had already communicated with. Upon arrival, Vader scouted the area and decided to have the fortress to be built over an ancient Sith [cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave"),[[7]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_21-7) which Vader had [meditated](/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") in before.[[15]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_5-15) Vader returned to meditate in the cave, but was interrupted by Brenne, who had created a design for the fortress. Vader rejected it, and Brenne returned to the [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship").[[7]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_21-7)

Upon her return, Momin's mask [possessed](/wiki/Possession "Possession") Lieutenant Roggo who [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") Colonel Brenne, finally giving Momin a corporeal form after many [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century"). During his possession of Roggo, Momin created the first blueprints for the fortress, designing it in such a way to maximize its attunement to the dark side.[[7]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_21-7) Momin then showed these blueprints to Vader upon his return. Vader killed the possessed Roggo and took Momin's mask into the Sith cave, where the mask gave Vader a vision of Momin's past and his love for art, and then attempted to possess Vader, though Vader resisted. Shortly after, a group of [Mustafarians](/wiki/Mustafarian "Mustafarian") attacked, and the mask possessed one of them. In his new body, Momin stated that the design he had for the fortress would act as a tuning fork for the Force, and promised Vader that if it was constructed, Vader would finally be able to achieve a way to meet his lost love, [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala"), again. Vader threatened Momin, stating that Sidious had already lied about this to him prior and that Momin would be killed if he did not live up to his word.[[11]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_22-11)

### Construction[]

:   "*Now, I can understand why you might have lost a bit of faith in me at this point. But I've said all along that this is a* process*. And my* ninth *design, well…I think it's really something special.*"  
    "*One way or another, Momin…there will be no tenth.*"
:   ―Darth Momin and Darth Vader[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3)

 [![The ancient Sith Lord Darth Momin presented the fortress' ninth design to Darth Vader.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/MominPresentsFortressVader-2017DarthVader23.png/revision/latest?cb=20181129061321) [![The ancient Sith Lord Darth Momin presented the fortress' ninth design to Darth Vader.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/MominPresentsFortressVader-2017DarthVader23.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181129061321)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/ce/MominPresentsFortressVader-2017DarthVader23.png/revision/latest?cb=20181129061321) 

The ancient Sith Lord Darth Momin presented the fortress' ninth design to Darth Vader.

 

Construction began for Momin's design. With the aid of Vader's countless Imperial resources, the fortress was soon finished. Vader attempted to use it in opening the door to the dark side, but it failed and the fortress was wrecked. The pair retried with four more of Momin's designs, but they also failed.[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3) In fact, for two years,[[16]](#cite_note-Vader_14_BBY_12_BBY_Chronology_Note-16) the cycle of construction continued,[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3) although Vader did not stay on Mustafar for that entire time. Additionally, the [Inquisitors](/wiki/Inquisitor "Inquisitor") moved to the similarly designed [Fortress Inquisitorius](/wiki/Fortress_Inquisitorius "Fortress Inquisitorius") during the interim.[[17]](#cite_note-Jedi_Fallen_Order-17) Every time Vader tried to open the door to the dark side it affected the rest of Mustafar, causing lava storms all over the planet to wreak havoc on the Mustafarians. [Lava fleas](/wiki/Lava_flea "Lava flea") began swarming the construction site regularly because of this. After the failure of Momin's fifth design, Vader killed the body his spirit possessed as a punishment and had his mask possess a [stormtrooper](/wiki/Stormtrooper "Stormtrooper"). Following the failure of Momin's sixth design, Vader killed his newer body and had the helmet possess an [Imperial officer](/wiki/Imperial_officer "Imperial officer"). Momin had confidence in the seventh design, but it failed as well, and Vader replaced his body with a lava flea's.[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3)

In [12 BBY](/wiki/12_BBY "12 BBY"),[[1]](#cite_note-Vader23Date-1) when Momin's eighth design failed, Vader once again killed his body and had the mask possess an Imperial construction worker. This time, Vader told Momin there would not be another chance. Momin had great faith in the ninth design. Vader began to channel the force from the Sith Cave and the pair saw it was working. However, [Captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Junus](/wiki/Junus "Junus"), the [commander](/wiki/Commander "Commander") of the fortress' [garrison](/wiki/Garrison "Garrison") alerted Vader of another [attack](/wiki/Battle_of_Fortress_Vader "Battle of Fortress Vader"), one the Imperial forces could not defend against. Vader left the Sith cave to deal with this while Momin double crossed him, opening the door to the dark side himself and resurrecting his original body. As his mask greeted it, the possessed body of the Imperial construction worker handed over the mask and Momin was made one again.[[3]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_23-3)

### Ascension[]

:   "*There will be no* evacuation*. There will be no* retreat*.*"
:   ―Darth Vader[[18]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_24-18)

 [![Darth Vader channeled the dark side of the Force through the fortress to destroy the Mustafarian army.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/52/KkktsDeath-2017DarthVader24.png/revision/latest?cb=20181130023900) [![Darth Vader channeled the dark side of the Force through the fortress to destroy the Mustafarian army.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/52/KkktsDeath-2017DarthVader24.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181130023900)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/52/KkktsDeath-2017DarthVader24.png/revision/latest?cb=20181130023900) 

Darth Vader channeled the dark side of the Force through the fortress to destroy the Mustafarian army.

 

The [Imperial garrison](/wiki/Fortress_Vader_Imperial_garrison "Fortress Vader Imperial garrison") attempted to flee until Vader intervened and turned the tide of the battle. From afar, the Mustafarian [leader](/wiki/Father_(title) "Father (title)") [Kkkt](/wiki/Kkkt "Kkkt") and his fellow chieftains then used the Force to bring a flow of lava onto the Imperial forces. The battlefield was wiped clean, and only Vader survived. Kkkt brought his remaining forces to attack the fortress and Vader entered the Sith cave. From there, he channeled the Force through the tower and caused a lava storm to wipe out Kkkt and his army.[[18]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_24-18)

Momin appeared from behind and confronted Vader. Momin claimed that Vader was weak, that he ought to serve the dark side, rather than seek to control it, and that Vader would never be able to open the portal as the dark side would have rejected him. The two dueled for a time, while Momin lamented the apparent regression and dilution of the power of the Sith in the millennia since his death, calling them weak and "Jedi-obsessed." Vader, who had been damaged during the battle and had expended a considerable amount of energy destroying the Mustafarians, received several devastating wounds from Momin during their duel. Unrelenting, Vader reached out with the Force and crushed Momin beneath an enormous stone. Momin proclaimed that he would not die, for the dark side loved him, and Vader replied that if that was indeed the case, then he would be saved by the will of the dark side. However, Momin could not summon enough power to overcome Vader's, and Vader slammed the stone flat against the wall, killing the disbelieving Sith Lord. Vader proclaimed his destiny was his own, before opening the portal himself.[[18]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_24-18)

Vader's spirit left his body and entered the portal, in search of Padmé Amidala. During his search on the other side, Vader's spirit came across a vision of his fortress and its many former designs before it turned into the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace"). Vader's spirit soon returned, unsuccessful of his objective, and merged with his body again. After this, he used the fortress to once again tune the energies of the dark side locus and destroy the portal[[19]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_25-19) because of its failure to resurrect Amidala.[[20]](#cite_note-Encyclopedia_Maul-20) After this, he contacted Sidious, saying he was ready to return to Coruscant.[[19]](#cite_note-Dark_Lord_25-19)

### Vaneé[]

Vaneé lived in Fortress Vader as the personal attendant to the Sith Lord Darth Vader.

 

Not long after its completion, an Imperial Inspector named [Vaneé](/wiki/Vane%C3%A9 "Vaneé") was sent by [Moff](/wiki/Moff "Moff") [Seward](/wiki/Seward "Seward") to Mustafar with his droid, [See-Seven](/wiki/See-Seven "See-Seven"), to determine why several of the garrison's stormtroopers had gone mad. He and See-Seven examined a [trooper](/wiki/Trooper "Trooper") of the castle's Imperial garrison that had been exposed to the [lava fumes](/wiki/Lava_fume "Lava fume") and became a lunatic. During his questioning, the trooper attacked Vaneé and warned him not to go inside of the castle. He saw that the trooper was just weak-minded. Vaneé entered the castle, looked around and searched for the Sith Lord[[22]](#cite_note-ROASWSN-22) Darth Vader. He found Lord Vader in a bacta tank.[[23]](#cite_note-Shadows_of_Vader's_Castle-23)

He told Lord Vader that the castle was not safe, should be evacuated and have environmental filters installed. Although he stated filters could be installed eventually, Vader refused for the time being, and Vaneé argued against it until the fumes affected him. He saw hallucinations and Vader offered him a position as his [attendant](/wiki/Aide "Aide"). Vaneé heard the hallucinations telling him what Vader needed. Vaneé tried to convince himself that the hallucinations were not real and tried to laugh them off. He laughed for days as the laughter eventually devolved into mad cackling. A broken Vaneé agreed to become Lord Vader's attendant at the castle and told him his name.[[23]](#cite_note-Shadows_of_Vader's_Castle-23)

### Raid of Mother Ssl[]

:   "*Into the lava… They dragged her into the lava…*"
:   ―Rersey[[25]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-25)

Later Sidious grew displeased with Vader, and so sent [Rersey](/wiki/Rersey "Rersey") to antagonize Vader. Vaneé stood on the lower level of an audience chamber within Fortress Vader near [Imperial advisor](/wiki/Emperor%27s_adviser "Emperor's adviser") Rersey as his master stood on the upper level looking out the room's window with two [Imperial Royal Guards](/wiki/Emperor%27s_Royal_Guard "Emperor's Royal Guard") standing below. Rersey was demanding an explanation from Vader which angered Vaneé due to the advisor's lack of respect towards his master. Vaneé reminded Rersey that he was a guest in his master's residence and would act accordingly which Rersey rebuffed. Vader halted the pair's squabbling as he felt a disturbance in the force moments before a chilling scream echoed in the chamber, startling all but Vader.[[25]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-25)

 [![Vaneé and Rersey cried out in terror as a seemingly mind-controlled Vader approached the two.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20191030102307) [![Vaneé and Rersey cried out in terror as a seemingly mind-controlled Vader approached the two.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20191030102307)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/87/PseudoInfectedVader-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20191030102307) 

Vaneé and Rersey cried out in terror as a seemingly mind-controlled Vader approached the two.

 

Vaneé along with Rersey followed Vader and the two guards as they went to investigate the source of the scream. Rersey remarked how the fortress was supposed to be secured to which Vaneé quietly suggested that Rersey secure his own mouth. When Rersey asked what he had said, Vaneé drearily replied he had said nothing right before loud heavy booming sounds echoed through the corridor. Arriving at the fortress' entrance, Rersey demanded to know what was going on to which Vaneé answered that the castle was under attack as a horde of [plagued](/wiki/Unidentified_plague_(Mustafar) "Unidentified plague (Mustafar)") infested Mustafarians and a [lava flea](/wiki/Lava_flea "Lava flea") broke through the doors. Vaneé looked on in shock as his master and the guards engaged the rampaging horde which included an infected lava trooper. After Vader had been bitten by the infected trooper, Vaneé cried out in horror to Rersey that they had to escape only to look on in horror, clenching to Rersey as the horde and Vader slowly advanced on them until he was halted by [Mother](/wiki/Mother_(title) "Mother (title)") [Sssl](/wiki/Sssl "Sssl") of [Clan Rrrt](/wiki/Clan_Rrrt "Clan Rrrt").[[25]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-25)

As Rersey confronted Sssl, Vaneé cautiously advised him to be careful as she controlled the infected. After the advisor snidely asked Sssl what secrets she was protecting, Vaneé pointed out to Rersey that Sssl would not be telling them what secrets she was protecting. Vaneé and Rersey recoiled in fear as Sssl commanded Vader to kill the both of them as Vaneé cried out in fear. Rersey then shoved Vaneé to the floor between him and Vader as he told the Dark Lord he needed to resist Sssl's power over him. As Vader advanced towards them, Vaneé cowered in fear on the floor until the Dark Lord revealed he was not under Sssl's power by levitating her off the ground.[[25]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-25)

 [![Vaneé and Rersey watched as Vader willed the Mustafarians to kill themselves in the lavas of their homeworld.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20210807134450) [![Vaneé and Rersey watched as Vader willed the Mustafarians to kill themselves in the lavas of their homeworld.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20210807134450)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/40/IntoTheLava-ReturnToVadersCastle5.png/revision/latest?cb=20210807134450) 

Vaneé and Rersey watched as Vader willed the Mustafarians to kill themselves in the lavas of their homeworld.

 

Getting up off the ground, Vaneé revealed to Sssl that it had all been a trap to lure her into attacking the castle. As Sssl struggled in Vader's grip, Vaneé gleefully told her that she was no match for his master's ingenuity and power. Vaneé watched as Vader willed Sssl to order her horde to drag her into the [lava](/wiki/Magma "Magma") and followed his master and Rersey outside as they watched Sssl and her infected horde burn in the lava. Once the ordeal was over with Vaneé congratulated his master, stating how all Mustafarians would tremble at the very mention of his name. Vader informed Vaneé that it was of no importance before telling Advisor Rersey that his [shuttle](/wiki/Theta-class_T-2c_shuttle "Theta-class T-2c shuttle") was waiting for him to take him back to the Emperor. Vaneé looked at Rersey with an expression of exasperation as the traumatized advisor continually recounted the sight of Mother Sssl being dragged into the lava.[[25]](#cite_note-Return_to_Vader's_Castle_5-25)

In [1 BBY](/wiki/1_BBY "1 BBY"),[[30]](#cite_note-SotE_Date-30) members of the [Rebel Alliance](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic") conducted a [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Mustafar_(Rebel_Alliance) "Mission to Mustafar (Rebel Alliance)") to infiltrate an [Imperial base](/wiki/Imperial_base_(Mustafar) "Imperial base (Mustafar)") near the castle for [Captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Cassian Andor](/wiki/Cassian_Jeron_Andor "Cassian Jeron Andor"). Disguised in [stormtrooper armor](/wiki/Stormtrooper_armor "Stormtrooper armor") the four traveled to Mustafar with their [pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") [Athex](/wiki/Athex "Athex") and the reprogrammed [security droid](/wiki/Security_droid "Security droid") [K-2SO](/wiki/K-2SO "K-2SO") in a stolen Imperial transport. Upon arriving, the Imperial base was not happy to hear of the ship's arrival and demanded the ship land immediately and be ready for inspection. A hover platform docked with the ship and took the [spies](/wiki/Spy "Spy") across to the main base, before taking a lift down to the base. They found a rack of blasters inside and the alarms started going off. K-2SO informed the others of this and instructed them to pick up the blasters and find their target. As the base responded to the threat, stormtroopers filed out onto walkways to try and shoot the rebels. They were ultimately defeated, but then insectoids rose from the lava and spat fire at the infiltrators. A giant insectoid backed them up until the spies killed it as well.[[31]](#cite_note-Secrets-31)

K-2SO unlocked the door for the rebels to enter inside the base. As the rebels moved back into a corridor, K-2SO presented them with a memory puzzle whilst Stormtroopers were shooting the rebels from behind. They then ran down another corridor leading to a room containing the weapons crate the rebels were dispatched to get. Athex opened the crate and found a [proto-saber](/wiki/Proto-saber "Proto-saber"). However, Darth Vader arrived and threw his lightsaber at them, stabbing Athex in the chest. The infiltrators fired at Vader and the Stormtroopers that arrived to reinforce him. Vader used the Force to crush their weapons and began walking towards the party. K-2SO then rammed the Imperial transport into the side of the building, scooping up the spies and the crate. After leaving the atmosphere, they jumped to hyperspace.[[31]](#cite_note-Secrets-31)

After barely escaping the ship as it sunk into the lava, Graf decided to go to the fortress in order to find a way off the planet,[[33]](#cite_note-Haunting_of_the_Ghost-33) much to the protest of Lieutenant [Thom Hudd](/wiki/Thom_Hudd "Thom Hudd"). Suddenly the rest of the crew heard him scream and ran to where he had gone, only to find no trace of him. They split up in search of Hudd and G3 went off with Skritt, telling him a story about the [Gorax](/wiki/Gorax "Gorax") along the way. They found the castle's attendant, Vaneé, in the control room. Vaneé was reporting their presence to Vader when G3 confronted him. After telling Vader they had found him, he watched as the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") arrived and ripped G3 apart in front of Skritt. Skritt reported this to Graf, and she left 8R, only for him to be attacked by Vader straight after.[[36]](#cite_note-Night_of_the_Gorax-36)

Graf and Skritt ran down a hallway where they came under attack by [security droids](/wiki/Unidentified_Fortress_Vader_droid_model "Unidentified Fortress Vader droid model") under Vaneé and Lord Vader. Graf tried to shoot Vader, but he was able to withstand and deflect her shots. After Vader tossed Crater at Graf, she and Skritt made their escape to the castle's hangar. There, a [kubaza beetle](/wiki/Kubaza_beetle "Kubaza beetle") attacked the lava troopers, while Graff and Skritt were able to steal a TIE fighter and escape. However, Hudd got captured.[[24]](#cite_note-Vader's_Castle_5-24)

After finishing the story, Vaneé explained to the Fourth Sister that being a lackey of the Emperor wasn't enough, but that they must earn their respect within the fortress, just before electrocuting the Inquisitor to reveal that it was Lina Graf, who has come to rescue Hudd. While Graf was fighting Vaneé, Hudd managed to reactivate the distorted holograms at Vaneé, causing him to not see for a moment just as Graf and Hudd ran to the ship and escape into hyperspace. Vaneé noticed that Hudd escaped, and said to himself that Vader must not know of this just as Vader appeared before him. Vaneé tried to explain what happened, but Vader had him electrocuted in the Torture Web.[[40]](#cite_note-Return_5-40)

 [![Vader was attacked by DT-series sentry droids controlled by the Scourge in his fortress.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/49/VaderThrown-DarkDroids3.png/revision/latest?cb=20240102135614) [![Vader was attacked by DT-series sentry droids controlled by the Scourge in his fortress.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/49/VaderThrown-DarkDroids3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20240102135614)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/49/VaderThrown-DarkDroids3.png/revision/latest?cb=20240102135614) 

Vader was attacked by DT-series sentry droids controlled by the Scourge in his fortress.

 

When the [Scourge](/wiki/Scourge "Scourge"), an artificial intelligence that aimed to dominate the minds of organic and artificial beings, [attacked](/wiki/Scourge_attack_on_Fortress_Vader "Scourge attack on Fortress Vader") Fortress Vader to try to control Vader, who he believed was the key to controlling the power of [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), some fortress's [DT-series sentry droids](/wiki/DT-series_sentry_droid "DT-series sentry droid") were controlled by the Scourge to attack Vader. Although Vader was attacked at a time when he was very vulnerable, having been ripped from a bacta tank, the Scourge were unable to fully focus on the attack due to a [confrontation](/wiki/Attack_on_Scourge_01 "Attack on Scourge 01") elsewhere, which allowed Vader to recover and win, destroying the droids.[[42]](#cite_note-Dark_Droids_3-42)

### Fortress Vader without Vader[]

#### Fall of the Fortress[]

At some point, Vader grew angry with Vaneé and imprisoned him in a deep pit below the castle.[[43]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Darth_Vader's_Castle-43)
Following the [death](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor") of Vader, the residents of the castle departed, leaving an imprisoned Vaneé behind. Not long after, two young Mustafarian [boys](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") named [Tuttel](/wiki/Tuttel "Tuttel") and [Giggek](/wiki/Giggek "Giggek") attempted to blow up the Fortress to symbolize the demise of Vader. Tuttel's uncle [Lellis](/wiki/Lellis "Lellis") tried to stop the boys from going there, warning of the evil that may be there, however Tuttel and Giggek ignored the old Mustafarian and made their way to Fortress Vader. Upon reaching the castle, the two young Mustafarian boys planned to collapse the fortress by blowing up the bottom. Tuttel became scared and wondered if Lellis was right. Giggek teased Tuttel by telling him of the story of the Sorcerer's Jewel, in which Tuttel said he wouldn't believe in such a thing.[[43]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Darth_Vader's_Castle-43)

 [![Fortress Vader stood abandoned after its namesake, Darth Vader, was killed during the Battle of Endor.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/11/FortressVader-Biomes.png/revision/latest?cb=20230619045442) [![Fortress Vader stood abandoned after its namesake, Darth Vader, was killed during the Battle of Endor.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/11/FortressVader-Biomes.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20230619045442)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/11/FortressVader-Biomes.png/revision/latest?cb=20230619045442) 

Fortress Vader stood abandoned after its namesake, Darth Vader, was killed during the Battle of Endor.

 

After the conversation, the two Mustafarians heard the voice of seemingly-helpless old man. The pair thought it was an illusion set up from the lava fumes, but soon realized that the man was real and was Vader's assistant, Vaneé. The servant said that Vader was angry with him, and was the one responsible for his imprisonment. Giggek replied by saying that Vader was gone, which Vaneé was surprised to learn. He explained of a time when an Imperial scientist named Rersey attempted to kill Vader and recounted Vader thwarting the scientist's plan.[[43]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Darth_Vader's_Castle-43)

After explaining the event, Giggek offered to help Vaneé escape. Vaneé pulled Giggek down, and a shocked Tuttel asked who he was. The servant explained that he was "The one true [Acolyte of the Beyond](/wiki/Acolytes_of_the_Beyond "Acolytes of the Beyond")," and revealed his name, proclaiming that he was death to all who profane the name of the Sith. Giggek was still alive, and told Tuttel to run as fast as he can and tell everybody about they did. Giggek detonated the bombs, and the bottom of Fortress Vader exploded; however, the castle still stood. Giggek's sacrifice was not in vain: Vaneé somehow managed to survive, and as he was limping away from the ruins, a voice came to him.[[43]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Darth_Vader's_Castle-43)

The Voice told Vaneé that he knew him, the one who spoke to him at the bottom of the pit. Vaneé knew that the voice was Vader's, and asked him of what he wanted him to do. The apparent ghost of Vader told his servant that it was not over, and that he would rise out of the lava river and be made whole, becoming more powerful than ever before.[[43]](#cite_note-Shadow_of_Darth_Vader's_Castle-43) In reality, however, the unfiltered lava fumes were merely making Vaneé hear things that were not there.[[44]](#cite_note-Ghosts_of_Vader's_Castle_5-44)

### Legacy[]

#### A remnant of the past[]

:   "*You are the past. Darth Vader is the past. This entire* place *is the past. The past* strangles *me. Its expectations and legacies and vendettas. I will destroy every last vestige of it. I will* free myself*.*"  
    "*You foolish, ignorant* child*. Vader's power* was *his past.*"
:   ―Kylo Ren and Vaneé[[47]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_1-47)

 [![Kylo Ren visited his grandfather's fortress on Mustafar during his reign as Supreme Leader of the First Order.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/LegacyofVader1-VirginVariant-Gist.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250203065858) [![Kylo Ren visited his grandfather's fortress on Mustafar during his reign as Supreme Leader of the First Order.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/LegacyofVader1-VirginVariant-Gist.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20250203065858)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/LegacyofVader1-VirginVariant-Gist.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250203065858) 

Kylo Ren visited his grandfather's fortress on Mustafar during his reign as Supreme Leader of the First Order.

 

The fortress began to slowly decay and was left vacant after the Empire's end, save for Vaneé, who eventually became a cyborg.[[47]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_1-47) Its grounds eventually came to be protected by the [Alazmec of Winsit](/wiki/Alazmec_of_Winsit "Alazmec of Winsit"), a [Sith cult](/wiki/Sith_cult "Sith cult") who colonized Mustafar and protected [Corvax Fen](/wiki/Corvax_Fen "Corvax Fen").[[48]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-48) At some point, [Snoke](/wiki/Snoke "Snoke") acquired a black obsidian stone from the catacombs beneath the castle, which he used to make a [ring](/wiki/Snoke%27s_ring "Snoke's ring") for himself.[[49]](#cite_note-TLJ_VD-49)

[Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo"), the grandson of Darth Vader and [master](/wiki/Ren "Ren") of the [Knights of Ren](/wiki/Knights_of_Ren "Knights of Ren"), visited Fortress Vader during the [First Order-Resistance War](/wiki/First_Order-Resistance_War "First Order-Resistance War"), shortly after he [murdered](/wiki/Battle_of_Oetchi "Battle of Oetchi") Snoke and succeeded him as [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") of the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order"). Determined to sever all ties to his past, Ren traveled to Mustafar with the intention of destroying his grandfather's legacy, hoping to free himself from burden he carried as heir to the [Skywalker bloodline](/wiki/Skywalker_family "Skywalker family"). Ren fought his way through a group of [Alazmec](/wiki/Alazmec "Alazmec") colonists before entering the Fortress, where he was received by Vaneé. The former attendant surmised that Ren was related to Vader, given that the fortress allowed him entry after scanning his [blood](/wiki/Blood "Blood"), to which Ren confirmed that Vader was his grandfather. Although Ren threatened to kill Vaneé, whom he viewed as another vestige of the past that he wished to destroy, Vaneé convinced Ren to reconsider his beliefs by revealing that Vader used his own past to enhance his connection to the dark side of the Force. Ren considered Vaneé's argument and ultimately accepted his offer, ordering his new servant to show him how Vader channeled his pain into power.[[47]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader_1-47)

:   ―Eye of Webbish Bog, to Kylo Ren[[50]](#cite_note-Star_Wars:_The_Rise_of_Skywalker:_A_Junior_Novel-50)

In [35 ABY](/wiki/35_ABY "35 ABY"),[[51]](#cite_note-TROS_Date-51) the Alazmec colonists guarded the [Sith wayfinder](/wiki/Sith_wayfinder "Sith wayfinder") at fortress remnants in [Corvax Fen](/wiki/Corvax_Fen "Corvax Fen"). The colonists were ultimately [cut down](/wiki/Mission_to_Mustafar_(First_Order) "Mission to Mustafar (First Order)") by Supreme Leader Kylo Ren of the First Order and his [stormtroopers](/wiki/First_Order_stormtrooper "First Order stormtrooper"), who had traveled to Mustafar to collect [Vader's wayfinder](/wiki/Vader%27s_wayfinder "Vader's wayfinder") that led to [Exegol](/wiki/Exegol "Exegol").[[9]](#cite_note-Episode_IX-9)

## Behind the scenes[]

### Development[]

:   "*Ralph McQuarrie actually drew quite a few small thumbnail sketches [of Vader's castle] that were very intriguing. They were kind of angular versions of a tower, and I saw the potential of where he was going, and I just exaggerated that quite a bit.*"
:   ―[Doug Chiang](/wiki/Doug_Chiang "Doug Chiang")[[52]](#cite_note-Doug_Chiang-52)

 [![Concept art of Vader's castle for The Empire Strikes Back which inspired Fortress Vader](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/99/VadersCastleConcept.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100826194116) [![Concept art of Vader's castle for The Empire Strikes Back which inspired Fortress Vader](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/99/VadersCastleConcept.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20100826194116)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/99/VadersCastleConcept.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100826194116) 

Concept art of Vader's castle for *The Empire Strikes Back* which inspired Fortress Vader

 

Fortress Vader first appeared in the [*Star Wars* Anthology](/wiki/Star_Wars_Anthology_Series "Star Wars Anthology Series") film *[Rogue One: A Star Wars Story](/wiki/Rogue_One:_A_Star_Wars_Story "Rogue One: A Star Wars Story")*, which was released in [2016](/wiki/2016 "2016").[[2]](#cite_note-Rogue_One-2) Early story meetings between [Leigh Brackett](/wiki/Leigh_Brackett "Leigh Brackett") and [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas") for [*Star Wars*: Episode V *The Empire Strikes Back*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_V_The_Empire_Strikes_Back "Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back") had them discussing Darth Vader having a black castle surrounded by lava, gargoyles and gremlins.[[53]](#cite_note-SW:TAS-53) Inspired by the unused [Ralph McQuarrie](/wiki/Ralph_McQuarrie "Ralph McQuarrie") sketches for Episode V,[[54]](#cite_note-Art_of_Rogue_One-54) the design team for *Rogue One* gave the castle a tuning fork shape, leading director [Gareth Edwards](/wiki/Gareth_Edwards "Gareth Edwards") to suggest that it is "tuning the dark side". The concept was also used for the [Temple of the Kyber](/wiki/Temple_of_the_Kyber "Temple of the Kyber"), which represents a mirror image on the [light side](/wiki/Light_side_of_the_Force "Light side of the Force").[[52]](#cite_note-Doug_Chiang-52)

During the concept phase of the [2015](/wiki/2015 "2015") film [*Star Wars*: Episode VII *The Force Awakens*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VII_The_Force_Awakens "Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens"), concept art titled "Vader Castle" was created by [Erik Tiemens](/wiki/Erik_Tiemens "Erik Tiemens") and [Brett Northcutt](/wiki/Brett_Northcutt "Brett Northcutt"), with the piece depicting [TIE/IN interceptors](/wiki/TIE/IN_interceptor "TIE/IN interceptor") exiting a fortress near a river of [lava](/wiki/Magma "Magma").[[55]](#cite_note-Art_of_TFA-55) In [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"), Darth Vader had similar abodes, including a [palace on Coruscant](/wiki/Darth_Vader%27s_Palace "Darth Vader's Palace")[[56]](#cite_note-Shadows_of_the_Empire-56) and the more well-known [Bast Castle](/wiki/Bast_Castle "Bast Castle") on [Vjun](/wiki/Vjun/Legends "Vjun/Legends").[[57]](#cite_note-Dark_Empire_II-57)

 [![Concept art by  of the castle.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/26/FortressVaderConceptArt-R1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161225102239) [![Concept art by  of the castle.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/26/FortressVaderConceptArt-R1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161225102239)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/2/26/FortressVaderConceptArt-R1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161225102239) 

Concept art by [Christian Alzmann](/wiki/Christian_Alzmann "Christian Alzmann") of the castle.

 

In the *[Rebels Recon](/wiki/Rebels_Recon "Rebels Recon")* behind-the-scenes video for "[Ghosts of Geonosis](/wiki/Ghosts_of_Geonosis "Ghosts of Geonosis")," [Pablo Hidalgo](/wiki/Pablo_Hidalgo "Pablo Hidalgo") directly confirmed that Darth Vader's castle is specifically where Tarkin was taking the captured Kanan Jarrus in the [Season One](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels_Season_One "Star Wars Rebels Season One") [finale](/wiki/Fire_Across_the_Galaxy "Fire Across the Galaxy") of *[Star Wars Rebels](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels "Star Wars Rebels")*. At the time, the series only said that he was being taken to Mustafar, and that Kanan had heard it was "where Jedi go to die." Even in Season One, Hidalgo knew that they were taking him to a castle Vader has on the surface - this wasn't established retroactively but was planned out. Hidalgo also directly established that Vader's castle was definitely constructed on Mustafar by the start of the series, but he stated that he wouldn't reveal exactly when and how it was built.[[58]](#cite_note-Rebel_Recon-58)

This was one of the early designs by concept artist [Erik Tiemens](/wiki/Erik_Tiemens "Erik Tiemens") during the concept phase of *Rogue One*, supplied by [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm")'s art department as noted by [Phil Szostak](/wiki/Phil_Szostak "Phil Szostak").[[59]](#cite_note-Phil's_Twitter-59)

The castle was meant to appear in [*Star Wars*: Episode IX *The Rise of Skywalker*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_IX_The_Rise_of_Skywalker "Star Wars: Episode IX The Rise of Skywalker"), in which Kylo Ren would be seen battling [Alazmec](/wiki/Alazmec "Alazmec") warriors on bridge leading to the fortress while searching for the Sith Wayfinder on Mustafar, but the scene was ultimately cut.[[60]](#cite_note-TROSTheDirect-60)
