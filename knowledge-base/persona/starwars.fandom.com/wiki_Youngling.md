"**Youngling**"[[2]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-2) or "**cubling**"[[3]](#cite_note-CEC-3) was a term for a child that was used by many [species](/wiki/Species "Species").[[2]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-2) [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") younglings who were taken to the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") to be [trained](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") as [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") were known as "Jedi younglings"[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1) but had the specific title of "[Jedi Initiates](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate")."[[4]](#cite_note-DJL-4) Children in the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion") were known as the [Littles](/wiki/Littles "Littles"). The title of "Children" was reserved for specific adults trusted by "[the Mother](/wiki/Elecia_Zeveron "Elecia Zeveron")" to carry out missions.[[5]](#cite_note-POD-5)

[Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") once carried out a massacre on a group of Jedi younglings in the Jedi Temple on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), as part of the [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge").[[6]](#cite_note-Episode_III-6) Most young children needed adult supervision, as they could not take care of themselves on their own. When [Din Djarin](/wiki/Din_Djarin "Din Djarin") left the [Child](/wiki/Din_Grogu "Din Grogu") aboard the *[Razor Crest](/wiki/Razor_Crest "Razor Crest")* all by himself, [Peli Motto](/wiki/Peli_Motto "Peli Motto") admonished the [Mandalorian](/wiki/Mandalorian "Mandalorian"), saying that he couldn't leave a child alone like that and that he had a lot to learn about raising a young one.[[7]](#cite_note-Gunslinger-7) Bringing kids into gladiator matches was considered inappropriate; when Djarin brought Grogu to the [Carnita arena](/wiki/Gor_Koresh%27s_Honky_Tonk "Gor Koresh's Honky Tonk"), a spectator pointed out that this was no place for a child, but the Mandalorian merely replied, "Wherever I go, he goes."[[8]](#cite_note-Marshal-8)

As a child, [Ben Solo](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") coined a new name for himself but later dismissed it as "stupid." [Snoke](/wiki/Snoke "Snoke") encouraged Solo to discard his old identity by taking the name that he invented during his childhood. He believed that children did not practice self-deception; therefore, they had only pure thoughts and were true to their nature.[[9]](#cite_note-Kylo_2-9)

During or after [34 ABY](/wiki/34_ABY "34 ABY"),[[10]](#cite_note-Pals_Date-10) several different species of younglings were stationed onboard the [Youngling Care Space Station](/wiki/Youngling_Care_Space_Station "Youngling Care Space Station"). The [Safari droid](/wiki/Safari_droid "Safari droid") [M1-RE](/wiki/M1-RE "M1-RE"), a specialized member of the [Galactic Society of Creature Enthusiasts](/wiki/Galactic_Society_of_Creature_Enthusiasts "Galactic Society of Creature Enthusiasts"), cared for and studied the younglings.[[11]](#cite_note-Learn_to_care-11) The Safari droid [SF-R3](/wiki/SF-R3 "SF-R3") asked M1-RE to show viewers on the [HoloNet](/wiki/HoloNet "HoloNet") how they could take care of their younglings, and M1-RE did so while being recorded by the [cam droid](/wiki/Cam_droid "Cam droid") [CAM-E](/wiki/CAM-E "CAM-E").[[12]](#cite_note-Wookiee-12)
