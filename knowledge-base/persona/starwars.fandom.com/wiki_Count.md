**Count** was a title of [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [nobility](/wiki/Noble "Noble"). [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), a [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") and the political leader of the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"),[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_II-2) was [the count](/wiki/Count_of_Serenno "Count of Serenno") of the planet [Serenno](/wiki/Serenno "Serenno"). Dooku reclaimed his title of count, along with his [ancestral](/wiki/House_Serenno "House Serenno") lands and wealth on Serenno, after he abandoned the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"),[[3]](#cite_note-Serenno-3) [killing](/wiki/Death "Death") his brother [Ramil](/wiki/Ramil "Ramil"), who had taken the title following the death of their father, [Gora](/wiki/Gora "Gora").[[4]](#cite_note-DJL-4) During the [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), [Chrodber](/wiki/Chrodber "Chrodber") also had the title of count of Serenno.[[5]](#cite_note-TOG-5) The [thorilide](/wiki/Thorilide "Thorilide") mogul [Denetrius Vidian](/wiki/Denetrius_Vidian "Denetrius Vidian") was also a count. As such, he was addressed as "my [lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord")."[[1]](#cite_note-AND-1) [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic") [senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Jogurner](/wiki/Jogurner "Jogurner") held the title of count.[[6]](#cite_note-BL-6)
