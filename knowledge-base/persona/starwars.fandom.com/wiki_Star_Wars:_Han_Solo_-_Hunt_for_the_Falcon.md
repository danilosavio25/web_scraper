***Star Wars: Han Solo - Hunt for the Falcon*** is a [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025") [comic book](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") miniseries written by [Rodney Barnes](/wiki/Rodney_Barnes "Rodney Barnes") and illustrated by [Ramon Rosanas](/wiki/Ramon_Rosanas "Ramon Rosanas"). The [first](/wiki/Han_Solo_-_Hunt_for_the_Falcon_1 "Han Solo - Hunt for the Falcon 1") of five issues was published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [September 3](/wiki/September_3 "September 3"), 2025.
