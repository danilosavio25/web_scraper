## Events[]

* The [Antar 4 schism](/wiki/Antar_4_schism "Antar 4 schism") occurs over whether to support the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") or the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems").[[5]](#cite_note-GA-5)
* A [border dispute](/wiki/Border_dispute_on_Ansion "Border dispute on Ansion") occurs on [Ansion](/wiki/Ansion "Ansion").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)
* [Zam Wesell](/wiki/Zam_Wesell "Zam Wesell") and [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett") [attempt](/wiki/Plot_to_assassinate_Senator_Amidala "Plot to assassinate Senator Amidala") to [assassinate](/wiki/Assassin "Assassin") [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala") on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)
* The [Separatist Crisis](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis") ends and the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") begin with the [First Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)
* [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") and Padmé Amidala are [married](/wiki/Marriage "Marriage") in a [secret ceremony](/wiki/Wedding_of_Anakin_Skywalker_and_Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Wedding of Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala") on [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)
* Anakin Skywalker is promoted to [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight").[[6]](#cite_note-Cat_and_Mouse-6)
* Concurrent to the early period of the Clone Wars, [RA-7 protocol droids](/wiki/RA-7_protocol_droid "RA-7 protocol droid") are created with plating from the [3PO-series protocol droid](/wiki/3PO-series_protocol_droid "3PO-series protocol droid"). One of the [droids](/wiki/Droid "Droid") made during this [time](/wiki/Time "Time") is [4-A7](/wiki/4-A7 "4-A7"),[[7]](#cite_note-4-A7-7) who is actually assembled by Separatist allies within [Arakyd Industries](/wiki/Arakyd_Industries "Arakyd Industries").[[8]](#cite_note-Build_Your_Own_R2-D2_11-8)
* The [Battle of Muunilinst](/wiki/Battle_of_Muunilinst "Battle of Muunilinst") occurs; the Republic defeats the Separatist stronghold. However the victory comes at great cost: [Muun](/wiki/Muun "Muun") [cities](/wiki/City "City") were heavily damaged in the battle, and the engagement will lead the galaxy to face a financial crisis.[[9]](#cite_note-Build_Falcon_45-9)
* Jedi General [Tholme](/wiki/Tholme "Tholme") leads his forces in a [major battle](/wiki/Battle_of_unidentified_planet_(Tholme_and_Ventress) "Battle of unidentified planet (Tholme and Ventress)") against [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress") and her [battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid"). Ventress wins the day and murders Tholme.[[10]](#cite_note-Dark_Disciple-10)[[11]](#cite_note-Timeline-11)
* [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") and his Jedi [squad](/wiki/Mace_Windu%27s_squad "Mace Windu's squad") [battles](/wiki/Operation_on_Hissrich "Operation on Hissrich") with the Separatists and the hired [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") [mercenary](/wiki/Mercenary "Mercenary") [AD-W4](/wiki/AD-W4 "AD-W4") over the planet [Hissrich](/wiki/Hissrich "Hissrich").[[12]](#cite_note-Mace_Windu_1-12)
* Anakin Skywalker destroys [Admiral Trench's](/wiki/Trench "Trench") [flagship](/wiki/Invincible "Invincible") above the [Battle of Christophsis](/wiki/Battle_of_Christophsis "Battle of Christophsis") and delivers needed supplies to senator [Bail Organa](/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa "Bail Prestor Organa") on the ground.[[6]](#cite_note-Cat_and_Mouse-6)
* [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano") is assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") learner.[[13]](#cite_note-TCW_film-13)
* Anakin Skywalker and Ahsoka Tano rescue [Jabba's](/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure "Jabba Desilijic Tiure") son [Rotta](/wiki/Rotta "Rotta") from Asajj Ventress.[[13]](#cite_note-TCW_film-13)
* [Ziro](/wiki/Ziro_Desilijic_Tiure "Ziro Desilijic Tiure") the Hutt is imprisoned after Padmé Amidala exposes his role in the plot to kidnap Rotta.[[13]](#cite_note-TCW_film-13)
* [General](/wiki/General "General") [Grievous](/wiki/Grievous "Grievous") takes the [warship](/wiki/Warship "Warship") *[Malevolence](/wiki/Malevolence "Malevolence")* as his flagship and launches a [campaign](/wiki/Malevolence_Campaign "Malevolence Campaign") in which he brought about the destruction of multiple Republic fleets. The ship is later destroyed by Republic and Jedi forces.[[14]](#cite_note-Destroy-14)
* The [Twi'lek](/wiki/Twi%27lek "Twi'lek") homeworld of [Ryloth](/wiki/Ryloth "Ryloth") is [invaded](/wiki/Invasion_of_Ryloth "Invasion of Ryloth") by Separatist forces led by [Techno Union](/wiki/Techno_Union "Techno Union") [Foreman](/wiki/Foreman "Foreman") [Wat Tambor](/wiki/Wat_Tambor "Wat Tambor"). The Republic's [Outer Rim garrison](/wiki/Outer_Rim_garrison "Outer Rim garrison") is destroyed in the ensuing invasion and the planet is occupied by Separatist forces.[[15]](#cite_note-Supply_Lines-15)
* The planet [Rodia](/wiki/Rodia "Rodia") rejoins the Republic after a successful [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Rodia "Mission to Rodia") by Senator Padmé Amidala, Representative [Jar Jar Binks](/wiki/Jar_Jar_Binks "Jar Jar Binks"), and [protocol droid](/wiki/Protocol_droid "Protocol droid") [C-3PO](/wiki/C-3PO "C-3PO").[[16]](#cite_note-Bombad-16)
