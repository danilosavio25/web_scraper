## Plot summary[]

### The invisible friend[]

 [![B1 battle droids looking for a "secret" on Retta](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/71/Retta.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014532) [![B1 battle droids looking for a "secret" on Retta](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/71/Retta.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/205?cb=20190419014532)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/71/Retta.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014532) 

B1 battle droids looking for a "secret" on Retta

 

On the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Retta](/wiki/Retta "Retta") in [Wild Space](/wiki/Wild_Space "Wild Space"), [B1-series battle droids](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid") were patrolling the [jungles](/wiki/Jungle "Jungle") of the planet when their [commander](/wiki/OOM_command_battle_droid "OOM command battle droid") received a message from their [general](/wiki/General "General"), [Flebek](/wiki/Flebek "Flebek") who ordered them to keep finding the secrets of Retta for [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"). One of the [battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid") thought they had found something. But then [Jedi General](/wiki/Jedi_General "Jedi General") [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"). The battle droids opened fire on the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") but then were attacked from their opposite side by [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda"). Yoda and Skywalker were sent to stop Flebek from discovering a secret on the planet.

But then the Jedi were attacked by a soaring boulder. Yoda and Skywalker used [The Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") to locate the perpetrator. Skywalker knocked an invisible individual into a [tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree"). The individual became revisable and Yoda instantly knew that it was an old friend named [Kreeda](/wiki/Kreeda "Kreeda"). Kreeda was the last remaining of the [Segredo](/wiki/Segredo "Segredo") [species](/wiki/Species "Species") who was able to manipulate the minds of others to hide their appearance. Skywalker thought it was amazing and wanted to take [her](/wiki/Gender "Gender") back to [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") and make her a [spy](/wiki/Spy "Spy") for the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"). Kreeda did not like this, causing her to become invisible once again.

### Discovering the invisible refugees[]

 [![Kreeda was protecting refugees of the Clone Wars](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/05/Segredo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014852) [![Kreeda was protecting refugees of the Clone Wars](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/05/Segredo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/105?cb=20190419014852)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/0/05/Segredo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014852) 

Kreeda was protecting refugees of the Clone Wars

 

A group of battle droids appeared and attacked the Jedi. Skywalker was able to destroy them but when he finished he realized that Master Yoda had disappeared. Yoda was tracking down Kreeda and found her as she was turning an [Ishi Tib](/wiki/Ishi_Tib "Ishi Tib") invisible. They then ran away from Yoda as he continued after them.

Skywalker destroyed the last battle droid as he continued his search for Yoda. He found Yoda meditating in an open area in the jungle. Skywalker asked him what he was doing out on his own but Yoda questioned if he really was alone. Then, members of various species appeared behind Yoda. Kreeda walked forward and told Skywalker that the individuals had come to her to escape the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") and she had been protecting them by turning them invisible when she had to. Skywalker realized that it would be best if she stayed to protect them then leave to become a spy. They were interrupted by the sound of [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") battle droids approaching.

### Protecting the camp[]

 [![Flebek failed to find the "secret"](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/Flebek.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014954) [![Flebek failed to find the "secret"](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/Flebek.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/125?cb=20190419014954)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f6/Flebek.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190419014954) 

Flebek failed to find the "secret"

 

A short distance away, Flebek knelt down, observing the remains of a destroyed battle droid. She realized that there were Jedi nearby. The battle droid asked if the Jedi were also after the secret to invisibility like them.

Back with the Jedi, Kreeda started to panic knowing that the CIS was after her. She also knew her powers did not work on droids but Yoda and Skywalker volunteered to protect her camp. Skywalker had a plan and asked to borrow the [Kyuzo war shield](/wiki/Kyuzo_war_shield "Kyuzo war shield"), to wear on his head ,from one of the refugees.

Shortly after, Skywalker made a fake report to the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") that the [cloaking cap](/wiki/Cloaking_cap "Cloaking cap") was working fine even though it was fake. Flebek and her battle droids appeared behind Skywalker and feel for his trick. She chased after Skywalker but behind her, Yoda dropped a tree on her battle droid. Skywalker then turned around and threatened to [kill](/wiki/Death "Death") Flebek with [his lightsaber](/wiki/Skywalker_lightsaber "Skywalker lightsaber"). Flebek then decided to abort the mission believing Skywalker's trick of there being no secret.

Kreeda questioned what was for them next. Yoda reassured her that she will stay where she was and that her secret was safe with them. Skywalker agreed but didn't know what to tell Jedi Master [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") for their report. Yoda told him to tell Windu that the truth with was they saw nothing on Reeta.
