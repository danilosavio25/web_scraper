**Lah Zhima** was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [sabersmith](/wiki/Sabersmith "Sabersmith") who worked for [Margrave](/wiki/Margrave "Margrave") [Juro](/wiki/Juro "Juro") on the [world](/wiki/Planet "Planet") of [Hy Izlan](/wiki/Hy_Izlan "Hy Izlan"), where he raised a daughter, [Lah Kara](/wiki/Lah_Kara "Lah Kara"). Juro had Zhima produce eight [lightsabers](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") for him as he intended to try and restart the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), but the duo's attempts were discovered by [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") [Jedi hunters](/wiki/Jedi_hunter "Jedi hunter"). The hunters captured Zhima and took him to a distant [star system](/wiki/Star_system "Star system") but not before he managed to get the sabers to Kara, who brought them to Juro's [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") recruits. After Kara helped Juro defeat the recruits that were disguised Sith, Juro promised that he would help save her [father](/wiki/Parent "Parent").

## Biography[]

### A legendary sabersmith[]

:   "*I kept my promise, and I actually finished them on time.*"
:   ―Lah Zhima[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

 [![Lah Zhima in his workspace with Lah Kara](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c5/LahZhimaLightsaber.png/revision/latest?cb=20211018011951) [![Lah Zhima in his workspace with Lah Kara](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c5/LahZhimaLightsaber.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/325?cb=20211018011951)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c5/LahZhimaLightsaber.png/revision/latest?cb=20211018011951) 

Lah Zhima in his workspace with Lah Kara

 

Lah Zhima[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1) was a legendary[[2]](#cite_note-Heroes-2) [sabersmith](/wiki/Sabersmith "Sabersmith") who lived on the [Outer Rim](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Hy Izlan](/wiki/Hy_Izlan "Hy Izlan"), where he used [kyber crystals](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal") to build [lightsabers](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") for the [margrave](/wiki/Margrave "Margrave"), [Juro](/wiki/Juro "Juro"). Zhima's daughter, [Lah Kara](/wiki/Lah_Kara "Lah Kara"), lived with him on the planet. Zhima built eight lightsabers for the margrave and finished them on [time](/wiki/Time "Time") as he had promised, tempering each [crystal](/wiki/Crystal "Crystal") so that the [color](/wiki/Color "Color") and length would be controlled by which [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") wielded it. Zhima explained this to Kara following the eighth saber being completed, noticing her talent and teaching her about [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force").[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

However, that [day](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"), a [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship") of [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") [Jedi hunters](/wiki/Jedi_hunter "Jedi hunter") arrived, which prompted Zhima to give all of the lightsabers to Kara so that she could deliver them to the [Aerial Temple](/wiki/Hy_Izlan_Aerial_Temple "Hy Izlan Aerial Temple") that [orbited](/wiki/Orbit "Orbit") the planet; Juro had recruited Jedi candidates to the temple in the hopes of restoring the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), which was why he required the lightsabers. Kara was surprised that her [father](/wiki/Parent "Parent") was not going to deliver them, but he claimed that he would be looking after the guests, despite the fact that they were not actually his guests. He embraced her, acting as though he would not see his daughter again—surprising her once more. Kara then ran away just as two hunters burst through the door and asked of his identity, which he confirmed. They then accused him of manufacturing illegal lightsabers, which he avoided denying.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

When Zhima was told that he would be taken away, he tried to refuse. He then checked the window to make sure that Kara was far enough away[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1) and deflected the hunters' shots into his oven,[[3]](#cite_note-epguide-3) exploding his [building](/wiki/Kara%27s_House "Kara's House") along with the hunters. The explosion was too small, however, and all three individuals inside survived. Kara also noticed and turned around to find her [father](/wiki/Gender "Gender") being dragged on the ground by the hunters. He begged her to run once he saw her, and she did, escaping on a [speeder](/wiki/Speeder "Speeder") and being chased by [one](/wiki/Unidentified_Jedi_Hunter_(Lah_Kara) "Unidentified Jedi Hunter (Lah Kara)") of the hunters. The [remaining hunter](/wiki/Unidentified_Jedi_Hunter_(Lah_Zhima) "Unidentified Jedi Hunter (Lah Zhima)") took away Zhima.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

### Following capture[]

:   "*I'm the daughter of the sabersmith Lah Zhima.*"
:   ―Kara[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

 [![Zhima was captured and taken away.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c8/ZhimaCaptured.png/revision/latest?cb=20211018012027) [![Zhima was captured and taken away.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c8/ZhimaCaptured.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20211018012027)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c8/ZhimaCaptured.png/revision/latest?cb=20211018012027) 

Zhima was captured and taken away.

 

Kara mentioned her father when meeting the Jedi recruits of the Aerial Temple, which intrigued them, for they were anticipating the lightsabers. Once Kara revealed them, they accepted the sabers eagerly. However, most of the recruits were Sith, not Jedi, their lightsabers turning red as an indication.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

During the subsequent [lightsaber duel](/wiki/Lightsaber_duel "Lightsaber duel"), one of the Sith told Kara that Lah Zhima had been taken to a [star system](/wiki/Unidentified_star_system_(Lah_Zhima) "Unidentified star system (Lah Zhima)") far away and that she would never see him again. She also informed Kara that any rescue attempts would be unsuccessful. Despite the Sith's number, Juro, Kara, and the only two recruits who were indeed Jedi, [Ethan](/wiki/Ethan "Ethan") and [Homen](/wiki/Homen "Homen"), were able to win the duel. Juro explained to Kara that he had only recently learned that Sith were sent in place of several Jedi, which was how they had learned of Zhima's sabersmith abilities. He apologized for not being able to help when he was taken but promised that he would help Kara save him.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1) Kara later went on a journey to search for her father.[[4]](#cite_note-Celebration-4)

## Personality and traits[]

:   "*You do have some natural talent with a lightsaber, Kara, but your connection will grow in time with proper training.*"
:   ―Lah Zhima[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

Zhima had a loving relationship with his daughter; she meant everything to him, and he meant everything to her.[[2]](#cite_note-Heroes-2) He was willing to sacrifice himself for her, begging her to run while he was captured. He was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") male with light skin, black [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"), and gray [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye").[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

## Powers and abilities[]

:   "*I've tempered the kyber crystals on each lightsaber so it reflects the user's connection with the Force.*"
:   ―Lah Zhima[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

A legendary lightsaber-smith,[[2]](#cite_note-Heroes-2) Zhima made all eight lightsabers on time and was able to make each lightsaber react to its user. Additionally, he was able to [deflect](/wiki/Force_deflection "Force deflection") blaster fire using the Force.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

## Equipment[]

When dealing with kyber crystals, Zhima used a [hammer](/wiki/Hammer_(tool) "Hammer (tool)"), and he had many other tools in his workspace. When he ignited one of his lightsabers, the blade turned blue. He wore simple, gray [clothing](/wiki/Clothing "Clothing") with overalls along with a white [bandana](/wiki/Bandana "Bandana").[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1)

## Behind the scenes[]

Lah Zhima was created for the *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* short film "[The Ninth Jedi](/wiki/The_Ninth_Jedi "The Ninth Jedi"),"[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1) which was released on [September 22](/wiki/September_22 "September 22"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021"). Prior to the film's release, Zhima was revealed simultaneously in the original Japanese trailer for the series, which was uploaded to the official *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [YouTube](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube "wikipedia:YouTube") channel on [August 17](/wiki/August_17 "August 17") of that year,[[5]](#cite_note-JP_Trailer-5) and in the English dub trailer, which was uploaded the same day.[[6]](#cite_note-English_Trailer-6) He was identified the same day in a [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") article detailing the series' cast.[[7]](#cite_note-Cast-7) He was voiced by [Shin-ichiro Miki](/wiki/Shin-ichiro_Miki "Shin-ichiro Miki") in the original Japanese audio and by [Simu Liu](/wiki/Simu_Liu "Simu Liu") in the English dub.[[1]](#cite_note-Ninth_Jedi-1) Zhima's name was misspelt as "Lar Zhima" in *[The Art of Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/The_Art_of_Star_Wars:_Visions "The Art of Star Wars: Visions")*, a [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") reference book written by [Zack Davisson](/wiki/Zack_Davisson "Zack Davisson").[[8]](#cite_note-art-8)

Executive producer [Josh Rimes](/wiki/Josh_Rimes "Josh Rimes") called Zhima's profession and lightsabers unique to "The Ninth Jedi," "different from anything […] seen before in *Star Wars*." The film's writer and director, [Kenji Kamiyama](/wiki/Kenji_Kamiyama "Kenji Kamiyama"), wanted the lightsabers to feel new.[[2]](#cite_note-Heroes-2) The idea of including a blacksmith character was one of Kamiyama's first ideas for "The Ninth Jedi," as he was interested in a non-Jedi individual that could make a lightsaber,[[9]](#cite_note-Focus-9) inspired by swords and swordsmiths of Japanese history. To create Zhima's design, Kamiyama and character designer [Tetsuya Nishio](/wiki/Tetsuya_Nishio "Tetsuya Nishio") drew from traditional Japanese clothing, with the exception of his hammer, which was more Western.[[8]](#cite_note-art-8) Although [canonically](/wiki/Canon "Canon"), Jedi usually created their own lightsabers, the presence of Zhima was part of the freedom that the creators of *Visions* had in telling the story that they wanted to.[[10]](#cite_note-Launchpad-10)

At the *Star Wars: Visions* panel at [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)") on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025"), it was announced that "[The Ninth Jedi: Child of Hope](/wiki/The_Ninth_Jedi:_Child_of_Hope "The Ninth Jedi: Child of Hope")", a sequel to "The Ninth Jedi", will release on [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), 2025 as part of the [third volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_3 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 3") of *Visions*. A trailer shown at the panel revealed Zhima will appear in the sequel. It was also announced that a spin-off series titled [*Star Wars: Visions* Presents - The Ninth Jedi](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Presents_-_The_Ninth_Jedi "Star Wars: Visions Presents - The Ninth Jedi") will release in [2026](/wiki/2026 "2026"). Kamiyama later revealed in an interview that the events of the series will include Kara's search for Zhima.[[4]](#cite_note-Celebration-4)
