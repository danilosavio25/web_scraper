***Star Wars: The Last Jedi: A Junior Novel*** is the junior novelization by [Michael Kogge](/wiki/Michael_Kogge "Michael Kogge") that adapts the events of the [2017](/wiki/2017 "2017") [film of the same name](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VIII_The_Last_Jedi "Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi"). The novel was published by [Disney–Lucasfilm Press](/wiki/Disney%E2%80%93Lucasfilm_Press "Disney–Lucasfilm Press") on [March 6](/wiki/March_6 "March 6"), [2018](/wiki/2018 "2018"). It contains original scenes and an eight-page full-color insert. The cover for the junior novelization is derived from the Japanese poster art for the film. The [audiobook](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Last_Jedi:_A_Junior_Novel_(audiobook) "Star Wars: The Last Jedi: A Junior Novel (audiobook)") version was narrated by [Jessica Almasy](/wiki/Jessica_Almasy "Jessica Almasy").

## Publisher's summary[]

*A junior novel retelling of* Star Wars: The Last Jedi*, with deleted and extended scenes, as well as exclusive scenes you won't find anywhere else featuring Rey, Chewbacca, R2-D2, Finn, and more! This book also includes an 8-page full-color insert.*
