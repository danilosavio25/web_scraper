***Star Wars: Visions* Volume 3** is the third season of *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* that premiered on [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025").[[2]](#cite_note-Date-2) It was announced on [November 20](/wiki/November_20 "November 20"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024"),[[3]](#cite_note-Announcement-3) and the titles of the shorts were revealed on [April 19](/wiki/April_19 "April 19"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025") at [Celebration Japan](/wiki/Celebration_Japan_(2025) "Celebration Japan (2025)").[[4]](#cite_note-Day_3-4) Volume 3 consists of nine shorts by various [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "wikipedia:Japan") [anime](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anime "wikipedia:Anime") studios. Volume 3 goes back to the original roots of Volume 1 by focusing on the anime medium with returning studios and new one.[[3]](#cite_note-Announcement-3)
