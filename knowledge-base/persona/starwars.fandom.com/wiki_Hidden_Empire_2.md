***Hidden Empire* 2** is the second issue of the [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") [comic book](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") miniseries *[Star Wars: Hidden Empire](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Hidden_Empire "Star Wars: Hidden Empire")*. The issue was written by [Charles Soule](/wiki/Charles_Soule "Charles Soule"), illustrated by [Steven Cummings](/wiki/Steven_Cummings "Steven Cummings"), [Victor Olazaba](/wiki/Victor_Olazaba "Victor Olazaba"), and [Guru-eFX](/wiki/Guru-eFX "Guru-eFX"), and published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [December 7](/wiki/December_7 "December 7"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"). The issue contains the story **Part 2: "The Dawn Fleet"**.

## Publisher's summary[]

*The plans of LADY QI'RA to use an ancient Dark Side weapon known as the FERMATA CAGE to destroy the Sith are falling apart, and she has lost many of her primary resources and allies, with DARTH VADER drawing ever nearer. Can she salvage victory from certain defeat?*

## Opening crawl[]

| PART 2 "THE DAWN FLEET" |
| --- |
| ``` Dreaming of a freer galaxy, LADY QI'RA and her organization, CRIMSON DAWN, have waged a secret war against the Empire. Qi'ra activated operatives across the galaxy to cause havoc and distract the Emperor while she sought a power capable of deposing the Sith Lord.  Qi'ra learned the means of prevailing against the Sith from her predecessor, Maul. His teachings led her to the FERMATA CAGE, an artifact that sealed away an ancient Sith Lord suspended in time. Qi'ra tasked the Archivist with freeing this Sith to serve as a weapon against the Emperor.  Qi'ra's smoke screen bought her time, but she has earned the Emperor's undivided attention–and wrath. Seeking to eradicate Crimson Dawn, the Emperor ordered the destruction of her flagship, the ''Vermillion''.  Qi'ra escaped aboard Chanath Cha's ship yet urgently needs the Archivist to complete her work. The disturbance in the Force caused by the artifact serves as a beacon to Qi'ra's enemies, and Crimson Dawn cannot hide forever…. ``` |
