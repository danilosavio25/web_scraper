## Development[]

During a presentation at [Disney](/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company "The Walt Disney Company") Investor Day [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"), [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") President [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy") announced that interconnected stories between series such as *[Star Wars: The Mandalorian](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Mandalorian "Star Wars: The Mandalorian")* and [*Ahsoka*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Ahsoka "Star Wars: Ahsoka") would cross over and culminate in a "climactic story event."[[5]](#cite_note-Investor2020-5)

On [April 7](/wiki/April_7 "April 7"), [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023"), at a panel at [Celebration Europe](/wiki/Celebration_Europe_(2023) "Celebration Europe (2023)"), the project was revealed to be a theatrical film helmed by director [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni"). A news post on [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") the same day confirmed that the film would "close out" the stories started in series like *The Mandalorian*.[[1]](#cite_note-New_movies-1) In an interview with [*IGN*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IGN "wikipedia:IGN") at the convention, Kennedy discussed how the genesis for the film had started over a decade prior and evolved organically into the current iteration of the project. She also revealed that Filoni would write the film.[[3]](#cite_note-KK_IGN_Interview-3) Also at Celebration, Filoni was interviewed by [*Variety*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Variety_(magazine) "wikipedia:Variety (magazine)") and revealed that the film would center around a conflict between the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic") and [Imperial Remnants](/wiki/Imperial_Remnants "Imperial Remnants").[[4]](#cite_note-Variety_Celebration-4) When interviewed by [*Empire*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_(magazine) "wikipedia:Empire (magazine)") on [May 5](/wiki/May_5 "May 5"), Filoni discussed how he wants the film to deal with moments that "define an era," in this case the space between [*Star Wars*: Episode VI *Return of the Jedi*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VI_Return_of_the_Jedi "Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi") and [*Star Wars*: Episode VII *The Force Awakens*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VII_The_Force_Awakens "Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens").[[6]](#cite_note-Empire_Interview-6)
