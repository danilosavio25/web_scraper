**F** was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). She [trained](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") under the Jedi [Shad-Rah Varcanella](/wiki/Zero "Zero"), who was [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") in [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY") at the [Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis"). After her master's death, F was traumatized and began wearing a mask to isolate herself. During the [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), F received a message from the [explorer](/wiki/Scout "Scout") [Valco](/wiki/Valco "Valco") and met with him on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Keelia](/wiki/Keelia "Keelia"). There, Valco showed F to where the humans [Haru](/wiki/Haru "Haru") and [Asu](/wiki/Asu "Asu") were performing a [purification ceremony](/wiki/Purification_ceremony "Purification ceremony"), allowing them to [view](/wiki/Force_vision "Force vision") the planet's memories. That [night](/wiki/Local_day "Local day"), F and Valco attended Asu and Haru's [wedding](/wiki/Wedding_of_Asu_and_Haru "Wedding of Asu and Haru") in [Meevai Village](/wiki/Meevai_Village "Meevai Village"). At the [wedding](/wiki/Marriage "Marriage"), F learned that Haru would be giving herself up as a hostage to a [group](/wiki/Izuma%27s_raiders "Izuma's raiders") of [raiders](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate") the next [morning](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"). Haru's younger sister [Saku](/wiki/Saku "Saku") attempted to persuade her to fight back, but Haru refused. After the wedding, F [meditated](/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") in the [forest](/wiki/Forest "Forest") and spoke with Haru, who explained her choice to give herself to the raiders.

The next morning, F cut her [Padawan braid](/wiki/Padawan_braid "Padawan braid") and removed her mask. [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") [Izuma](/wiki/Izuma "Izuma"), the leader of the raiders, [arrived](/wiki/Attack_on_Meevai_Village "Attack on Meevai Village") outside the [village](/wiki/City "City") to take Haru and revealed that his forces had captured Saku after she had tried to resist him. He attempted to execute her, but F [stopped](/wiki/Force_Stasis "Force Stasis") his [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") bolt with [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") and sent it back toward him. After Valco deactivated Izuma's [battle droids](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid"), Izuma grabbed Haru and held a blaster to her. F ignited her [lightsaber](/wiki/F%27s_lightsaber "F's lightsaber") and propelled herself toward Izuma, [cutting off](/wiki/Dismemberment "Dismemberment") his [arm](/wiki/Limb "Limb") and saving Haru. She later departed the planet in her [ship](/wiki/F%27s_ship "F's ship").

Less than a [month](/wiki/Standard_month "Standard month") later, F was present on the planet [Inoli](/wiki/Inoli "Inoli") when a [groundquake](/wiki/Groundquake "Groundquake") occurred. The quake caused the collapse of a building, trapping the humans [Ron](/wiki/Ron "Ron") and [Theo](/wiki/Theo_(Inoli) "Theo (Inoli)") inside. F rescued them, and then joined them on [Ron's shuttle](/wiki/Ron%27s_Sheathipede-class_transport_shuttle "Ron's Sheathipede-class transport shuttle") as clouds of [carbonite](/wiki/Carbonite "Carbonite") released from abandoned [mines](/wiki/Mining "Mining") by the quake engulfed the [town](/wiki/Unidentified_city_(Inoli) "Unidentified city (Inoli)"). The [shuttle](/wiki/Sheathipede-class_transport_shuttle "Sheathipede-class transport shuttle") landed in a [refugee ship](/wiki/Unidentified_refugee_ship "Unidentified refugee ship"), where F visited a [clinic](/wiki/Joona%27s_clinic "Joona's clinic") run by the [Besalisk](/wiki/Besalisk "Besalisk") [Joona](/wiki/Joona "Joona"). F gave advice to a group of [children](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling") asking her questions, and after, Joona repaired her [cybernetic](/wiki/Cybernetics "Cybernetics") leg.

The [*Imperial*-class Star Destroyer](/wiki/Imperial-class_Star_Destroyer "Imperial-class Star Destroyer") [*Oppressor*](/wiki/Oppressor_(Imperial-class) "Oppressor (Imperial-class)") exited [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace") near the [refugee](/wiki/Refugee "Refugee") ship, and its [admiral](/wiki/Unidentified_Imperial_admiral_(Oppressor) "Unidentified Imperial admiral (Oppressor)") demanded F surrender herself. F went with Ron on his shuttle and landed in the [Star Destroyer](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer")'s [hangar](/wiki/Hangar "Hangar"), with Ron pretending he had captured F. F was brought to a [room](/wiki/Zero%27s_room "Zero's room") aboard the *Oppressor* where she was shocked to meet a [resurrected](/wiki/Resurrection "Resurrection") Varcanella, who was serving the Empire using the name Zero. He invited F to join him, and when she refused, he [attacked](/wiki/Duel_on_the_Oppressor "Duel on the Oppressor") her, with F avoiding each of his strikes. F and Zero charged toward each other, and when F leaped above him to attack, Zero cut off her feet and right arm. However, F placed a capsule of carbonite on him, [freezing](/wiki/Carbon-freezing "Carbon-freezing") him moments later. The *Oppressor* began to fall toward Inoli due to explosions set off by Ron, and F escaped with him after he helped her to his shuttle.

## Biography[]

### A traumatizing past[]

:   "*It's just because I'm little. Wait till I'm grown up!*"
:   ―F, to Shad-Rah Varcanella[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

 [![A young F training with Varcanella](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/YoungF-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest?cb=20251105231526) [![A young F training with Varcanella](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/YoungF-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20251105231526)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/YoungF-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest?cb=20251105231526)

F was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human")[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender")[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_Gallery_Slide_2-2) [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") who [trained](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") under the Jedi[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) [Shad-Rah Varcanella](/wiki/Zero "Zero").[[4]](#cite_note-ProductionBrief-4) At one point as a [child](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling"), she[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) idly swung a training stick[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) while waiting at the base of a large [tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree") on a path between two[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) [grain](/wiki/Grain "Grain") fields.[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) She saw Varcanella approach and ran up to greet him, swinging her stick[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) belligerently.[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) Her master offered her a [meiloorun fruit](/wiki/Meiloorun_fruit "Meiloorun fruit"), which she accepted[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) with curiosity.[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) F continued to speak to Varcanella, waving her stick with one hand and the [fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") with the other, causing him to laugh.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) On another occasion, F and Varcanella both faced each other wielding sticks. F was knocked backward, but got up and claimed it was because of her small size. Varcanella responded by reminding her[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master")[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") was both strong and small, and F charged back toward him.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

At one point, F met[[7]](#cite_note-Main_Ep_Guide-7) the [explorer](/wiki/Scout "Scout") [Valco](/wiki/Valco "Valco"), a friend of her master,[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) as well as the [Besalisk](/wiki/Besalisk "Besalisk") [Joona](/wiki/Joona "Joona") and[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) her[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") [Lulu](/wiki/Lulu_(droid) "Lulu (droid)").[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) In [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY"),[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) at the [Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/First_Battle_of_Geonosis "First Battle of Geonosis"),[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) which marked the beginning of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") between the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") and [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic"),[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) F was scarred by the [blade](/wiki/Dooku%27s_lightsaber "Dooku's lightsaber") of[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") [Count](/wiki/Count "Count")[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"),[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) and Varcanella died protecting her.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) After her master's death, F laid on the ground with tears in her [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye") and reached a [hand](/wiki/Limb "Limb") toward his body while other Jedi fought around them.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

After Varcanella's death, F left a red flower at his grave.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) Traumatized by her master's death, she began wearing a mask to isolate herself. Without him, she was left with no purpose or guidance and was unsure of which side of [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") she would fall.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) While recuperating from her injury, the war spread and F began to have doubts about the Jedi and her identity. With the doubts in her mind, she set off on a journey,[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) running from the war and the Jedi.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) During her journey,[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) in [19 BBY](/wiki/19_BBY "19 BBY"), near the [end](/wiki/Mission_to_Mustafar "Mission to Mustafar") of the Clone Wars, the [Sith Lord](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious")[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) issued [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66"),[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) [compelling](/wiki/Executive_Order_Codes_for_the_Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Executive Order Codes for the Grand Army of the Republic") the Republic's [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") to turn against the Jedi and [kill](/wiki/Death "Death") many of them, signaling the beginning of the [Great Jedi Purge](/wiki/Great_Jedi_Purge "Great Jedi Purge") and ushering in his [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6)

During the Empire's [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era"),[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) between 19 [BBY](/wiki/%27ABY-BBY%27_dating_system "'ABY-BBY' dating system") and [1 BBY](/wiki/1_BBY "1 BBY"),[[10]](#cite_note-The_Village_Bride_IU_Date-10) F received a message from Valco and traveled to the [Outer Rim](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet")[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) [Keelia](/wiki/Keelia "Keelia").[[11]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Heroes-11) After arriving on the planet, F found Valco using[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) his[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) [helmet](/wiki/Valco%27s_helmet "Valco's helmet") and other devices to explore a [forest](/wiki/Forest "Forest"). Valco acknowledged her arrival, and F stated her need to be cautious, though Valco assured her that the Empire would not reach her there. After noting his interest in the planet and telling her that he wanted to show her something, Valco commented that F's mask did not suit her. F looked away and noticed the human [Asu](/wiki/Asu "Asu") carrying[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) his[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) [bride](/wiki/Marriage "Marriage"), the human [Haru](/wiki/Haru "Haru"), on a [chair](/wiki/Bridal_chair "Bridal chair") up a nearby [mountain](/wiki/Mountain "Mountain").[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

Valco then showed F to the edge of a cliff, where they observed Asu and Haru perform[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) a [purification ceremony](/wiki/Purification_ceremony "Purification ceremony")[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) in the valley below. A circle of[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) [blue](/wiki/Color "Color") energy[[12]](#cite_note-Bride_AD-12) covered the valley, and Haru and Asu saw themselves playing in a [river](/wiki/Water "Water") as children. F and Valco also witnessed the [vision](/wiki/Force_vision "Force vision"), and F realized they were seeing the planet's memories. As the vision ended, the valley appeared to collapse. Valco told F of the deep respect the people of Keelia had for nature and explained he had shown her the ritual because her master had roots on the planet. Later, F and Valco discussed how the Separatists had exploited Keelia and stripped it of its resources. When Valco commented on the loss that war brought, F remembered her master's death.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

### A wedding of mourning[]

:   "*I understand the grief of the village runs deep. Imagine being imprisoned by invaders to save your grandpa.*"
:   ―Valco[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

 [![F and Valco attend the wedding](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6f/F-the-village-bride.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250401204342) [![F and Valco attend the wedding](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6f/F-the-village-bride.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20250401204342)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/6f/F-the-village-bride.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250401204342) 

F and Valco attend the wedding

 

That [night](/wiki/Local_day "Local day"), F and Valco attended Asu and Haru's [wedding](/wiki/Wedding_of_Asu_and_Haru "Wedding of Asu and Haru") in [Meevai Village](/wiki/Meevai_Village "Meevai Village"), where they sat outside at low tables. F noted that it seemed wrong, but Valco told her celebrations were meant to be shared with a large group. A [villager](/wiki/Unidentified_villager_(Valco) "Unidentified villager (Valco)") offered them both [drinks](/wiki/Alcohol "Alcohol"), which they accepted. Valco and the villager discussed the circumstances surrounding the wedding, explaining that [raiders](/wiki/Izuma%27s_raiders "Izuma's raiders") with reprogramed Separatist [battle droids](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid") had demanded the [village chief](/wiki/Unidentified_village_chief_(Meevai_Village) "Unidentified village chief (Meevai Village)") as collateral, but that Haru had volunteered to go in his place and would be taken the next [morning](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"). Another [villager](/wiki/Unidentified_brown-haired_villager "Unidentified brown-haired villager") apologized for the villager bothering them, though Valco said he understood.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

Haru's younger sister [Saku](/wiki/Saku "Saku") then stood and shouted at Haru, attempting to persuade her to fight back. When Haru refused to allow them to, Saku and a group of other villagers who supported her left the wedding. As they did, Valco commented to F that Saku reminded him of her. F responded she knew what he was trying to do, which Valco replied to by claiming he did not care about her [code](/wiki/Jedi_Code "Jedi Code").[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

### Making a choice[]

:   "*I have a bad feeling about this.*"  
    "*You think I'm being reckless?*"
:   ―F and Haru[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

 [![F freezes Izuma's blaster bolt, saving Saku](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/57/IzumaSaku-VillageBride.png/revision/latest?cb=20250401214326) [![F freezes Izuma's blaster bolt, saving Saku](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/57/IzumaSaku-VillageBride.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20250401214326)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/5/57/IzumaSaku-VillageBride.png/revision/latest?cb=20250401214326) 

F freezes Izuma's blaster bolt, saving Saku

 

Later that night, F stood in the forest and[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) [meditated](/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") by [using](/wiki/Force_power "Force power") the Force to [levitate](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") small rocks.[[13]](#cite_note-Episode_Gallery_Slide_9-13) She was approached by Haru, who noticed F could also feel the Magina, the local name for the Force. F expressed doubts about Haru's decision to give herself to the raiders, but Haru remained resolute and explained that not doing so would put the [village](/wiki/City "City") at risk. Asu arrived, and Haru added that she would not be alone before leaving with her husband. After they departed, F saw a brief vision of a red [lightsaber](/wiki/Darth_Sidious%27_lightsabers "Darth Sidious' lightsabers") and heard Sidious scream.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

As the [sun](/wiki/Star "Star") rose the next morning, F used a [knife](/wiki/Knife "Knife") to cut her [Padawan braid](/wiki/Padawan_braid "Padawan braid") and removed her mask, letting both fall to the ground. Just outside the village, its inhabitants gathered as the raiders [arrived](/wiki/Attack_on_Meevai_Village "Attack on Meevai Village") to take Haru, who approached them along with Asu to give herself up. [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") [Izuma](/wiki/Izuma "Izuma"), the leader of the raiders, then revealed that[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) his[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) forces had captured Saku and other villagers who had attempted to resist. He drew his [blaster pistol](/wiki/KYD-21_blaster_pistol "KYD-21 blaster pistol") and aimed it at Saku, intending to execute her for resisting. Izuma fired at point-blank range, but F, who by that point had arrived behind Asu and Haru, used the Force to [freeze](/wiki/Force_Stasis "Force Stasis") the [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") bolt before it could reach Saku. Izuma, as well as Asu and Haru, looked at F with surprise, and the Jedi sent the bolt back into Izuma's hand, causing him to fall to the ground and drop his [blaster pistol](/wiki/Blaster_pistol "Blaster pistol"). F stepped in front of the couple and spoke about the way the people of Keelia lived in harmony with nature and the Force.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

### Magina rises[]

:   "*What are you? Some kind of a monster?*"  
    "*I am a Jedi.*"
:   ―Izuma and F[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

 [![F faces off against Izuma](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/FvsIzuma-VillageBride.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250401210559) [![F faces off against Izuma](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/FvsIzuma-VillageBride.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/400?cb=20250401210559)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/4/42/FvsIzuma-VillageBride.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20250401210559) 

F faces off against Izuma

 

Izuma ordered his forces to fire, but Valco began shooting at them from atop a nearby cliff. After running out of ammunition, Valco threw his [helmet](/wiki/Armor "Armor") to the ground below. It propelled itself into [Izuma's starship](/wiki/Izuma%27s_starship "Izuma's starship") and caused it to explode, deactivating Izuma's [battle droids](/wiki/Battle_droid "Battle droid") and causing his remaining forces to flee. Izuma scrambled to pick up a blaster from the ground, then grabbed Haru and held the blaster to her while screaming at F. She responded by declaring herself to be a Jedi and igniting her [lightsaber](/wiki/F%27s_lightsaber "F's lightsaber"). After a few moments, F used a device in her shoe to propel herself toward Izuma[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) with super-human speed[[14]](#cite_note-Episode_Gallery_Slide_16-14) and [cut off](/wiki/Dismemberment "Dismemberment") his blaster arm before he could react. The raider collapsed as F remained crouched behind him.[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

Later, a group of villagers gathered as F shook hands with Haru. F then departed in her [ship](/wiki/F%27s_ship "F's ship"), and as she [piloted](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") it away, the villagers waved at her and Valco removed his helmet to look up at it as she flew overhead. After leaving the planet, F jumped to [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace").[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

### Carbonite crisis[]

:   "*What happened to this planet?*"

### Ghost of the past[]

:   "*Hear me, Padawan. No, you're a proper Jedi now, aren't you? I'm giving you a chance to become my apprentice once again. A chance to correct your misguided ways. Your choice. Will you eliminate the Jedi with me? Or will you die as a Jedi?*"
:   ―Zero, to F[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

Awaiting F in the room was a [resurrected](/wiki/Resurrection "Resurrection") Varcanella, who served the Empire while going by the name Zero. F stared at Zero in shock, and he noted the flower he was holding was the same as the one she had left at his grave. Using the Force, Zero removed F's binders, and she asked how he was alive, remembering how she had witnessed his death. Zero responded that although he had died, he had returned after being given a second chance. Between them, a pillar emerged from the ground and revealed F's lightsaber. Zero invited F to rejoin him as his apprentice, explaining his belief that Jedi were misguided and must be destroyed. Repeating his offer, Zero acknowledged that F was no longer a Padawan. He demanded F take her lightsaber and either join him or die as a Jedi, but F refused to do either.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

Zero accused F of running, reminding her of how she had run from the Clone Wars and from being a Jedi. Zero then stated he hated the war and the Jedi who caused it, as well as F, who had run after he had died to save her. He ignited his [lightsaber](/wiki/Shad-Rah%27s_lightsaber "Shad-Rah's lightsaber") and [charged](/wiki/Duel_on_the_Oppressor "Duel on the Oppressor") at F, who pulled her own lightsaber to her in time to block his strike. Zero swung his lightsaber at F several times, but she evaded each strike without attacking back and then [jumped](/wiki/Force_jump "Force jump") backward[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) with the Force, putting distance between them.[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5)

### Path of the Jedi[]

:   "*It is by supporting each other that peace can be born. My master followed the path of the Jedi and lost his life. And so…I will continue to live as that master taught me.*"
:   ―F, to Zero[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

F acknowledged that Zero had saved her life and stated she would continue to protect people as he had taught her. Zero responded by telling her that she was there because of those she protected. As the hologram around the room's windows fell away, revealing the room was surrounded by viewports showing space, F resolved to continue living by the teachings of her master, and assumed a stance with her lightsaber held behind her. F's boot opened, and she and Zero dashed toward each other.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

 [![F leaps to attack Zero](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/79/FJumpAttack-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest?cb=20251105225430) [![F leaps to attack Zero](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/79/FJumpAttack-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/400?cb=20251105225430)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/7/79/FJumpAttack-TheLostOnes.png/revision/latest?cb=20251105225430)

F performed a flip and leaped above Zero's head to strike at him,[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) but he anticipated the attack[[5]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_AD-5) and cut off her right arm at the elbow, then cut off her feet with another slash. She pushed off his shoulder, placing a capsule of carbonite on him, and Zero slashed at her again as she fell to the floor. Zero taunted her, telling F her skills were not enough to defeat him, but the carbonite began to spread across him, [freezing](/wiki/Carbon-freezing "Carbon-freezing") him. F looked at Zero and acknowledged she lacked the lightsaber skills needed to defeat him, but told him she could still stop him. As the carbonite spread across him, Zero complemented F's growth, and she called out to him.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

At the same time, Ron set off explosions aboard the *Oppressor*, which began to fall toward Inoli, allowing the refugee ship to escape into hyperspace. A tear formed in F's eye, and she closed her eyes as the *Oppressor* fell toward Inoli. Ron ran into the room and brought her aboard his shuttle, which flew away from the Star Destroyer. ## Personality and traits[]

:   "*Know something? She reminds me of you.*"
:   ―Valco, comparing Saku to F[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1)

 [![F was a stoic and guarded Jedi.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f0/F-TheLostOnes.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20251105232245) [![F was a stoic and guarded Jedi.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f0/F-TheLostOnes.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20251105232245)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f0/F-TheLostOnes.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20251105232245) 

F was a stoic and guarded Jedi.

 

F was a young[[2]](#cite_note-Episode_Gallery_Slide_2-2) human with light skin, dark blue eyes, and black hair, which was longer when she was a child. She had a scar on her face and a [cybernetic](/wiki/Cybernetics "Cybernetics") left leg.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) F was traumatized after her master's death and was left without guidance or a purpose,[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) losing both her pride and self-confidence.[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) She struggled with her identity and was unsure of which side of the Force she would fall.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) F was cautious[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) and isolated herself behind her mask.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) When F arrived on Keelia, she was drawn into conflict and forced to decide whether to reveal herself.[[11]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Heroes-11) Valco gently guided her without pushing her,[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) and after making her choice she was resolute. She cut her Padawan [braid](/wiki/Braid "Braid"), which marked her as a student, indicating she was ready to become the master of her destiny.[[17]](#cite_note-Highlights-17) Valco compared F to[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) the headstrong[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) Saku,[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) and F saw something of herself in her as well.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8)

Stoic and guarded, F sought out people to help despite the danger involved.[[4]](#cite_note-ProductionBrief-4) When F was asked questions by the children aboard the refugee ship, she did not answer them and instead gave them advice. She did, however, greet Mei's [tooka](/wiki/Tooka "Tooka") [doll](/wiki/Doll "Doll"). F willingly surrendered herself to the Empire to protect the refugees aboard the ship. She refused to join Zero, instead choosing to live by his past teachings.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

She lacked the lightsaber skills to directly defeat Zero in lightsaber combat, but was still able to stop him using a capsule of carbonite.[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)

F first appeared in "[The Village Bride](/wiki/The_Village_Bride "The Village Bride")," a [non-canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") short film directed by [Hitoshi Haga](/wiki/Hitoshi_Haga "Hitoshi Haga"), written by Haga and [Takahito Oonishi](/wiki/Takahito_Oonishi "Takahito Oonishi"), and released as part of the [first volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_1 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 1")  of the *[Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions "Star Wars: Visions")* series. The animated short, produced by [Kinema Citrus](/wiki/Kinema_Citrus_Co. "Kinema Citrus Co."), was released on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+")[[1]](#cite_note-Bride-1) on [September 22](/wiki/September_22 "September 22"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021").[[18]](#cite_note-Stunning_Trailer-18) Prior to the short's release, F was first pictured in a video uploaded to the official *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [YouTube](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube "wikipedia:YouTube") channel on [July 3](/wiki/July_3 "July 3"), 2021,[[19]](#cite_note-Special_Look-19) and she was first mentioned, albeit indirectly, in a [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") article the same day.[[20]](#cite_note-20_Things-20) F was then identified in another StarWars.com article published on [August 17](/wiki/August_17 "August 17") of that year.[[18]](#cite_note-Stunning_Trailer-18) F also appeared in "[The Lost Ones](/wiki/The_Lost_Ones "The Lost Ones")," a sequel to "The Village Bride" written and directed by Haga, produced by Kinema Citrus,[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3) and released on Disney+ [October 29](/wiki/October_29 "October 29"), 2025 as part of the [third volume](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Visions_Volume_3 "Star Wars: Visions Volume 3") of *Visions*.[[16]](#cite_note-Focus-16) F is voiced by [Asami Seto](/wiki/Asami_Seto "Asami Seto") in the original Japanese and by [Karen Fukuhara](/wiki/Karen_Fukuhara "Karen Fukuhara") in the English dub.[[18]](#cite_note-Stunning_Trailer-18)

Haga complimented Seto's performance in "The Village Bride" and was sorry she had so few lines. Rimes also explained that F's mask and cloak both literally and figuratively hid her from her past trauma.[[11]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Heroes-11) Haga explained that when F took off her mask, she separated from the unpleasant memories of her past and removed the sign of her being a Padawan, allowing her to rise up as a Jedi in her own way.[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) In "The Lost Ones," Haga wanted to show the choices F had to make to overcome her past and move forward.[[22]](#cite_note-Key_Tradition-22) Haga said that when Zero acknowledged F as a Jedi, it was in a sense her greatest wish being finally fulfilled.[[23]](#cite_note-Sequel_Directors-23)

### Development[]

:   "*I thought it would be easy to imagine what happens next. The injured Padawan would understand her own purpose and rise up. However, I thought viewers might not accept that, so we brought it to a proper conclusion.*"
:   ―Hitoshi Haga[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9)

 [![Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Village Bride"](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/35/F_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20210927215704) [![Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Village Bride"](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/35/F_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20210927215704)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/35/F_concept_art.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20210927215704) 

Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Village Bride"

 

In "The Village Bride," F became an observer and guest at Asu and Haru's wedding after happening upon them in the forest, which was inspired by [Akira Kurosawa's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akira_Kurosawa "wikipedia:Akira Kurosawa")[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) [1990](/wiki/1990 "1990")[[24]](#cite_note-Dreams-24) film [*Dreams*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dreams_(1990_film) "wikipedia:Dreams (1990 film)").[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) Originally, Haga planned for "The Village Bride" to end when F ignited her lightsaber blade, as he believed it would be easy to imagine what happened next. However, he worried viewers would not accept that and decided to show the confrontation's conclusion. When F landed the blow with her lightsaber, the film cuts to black, as rather than just depicting the action, Haga wanted to symbolically depict the event and F's incredible speed.[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) In "The Lost Ones," Haga wanted the audience to feel that F was unable to defeat Zero, but still had to stop him. According to Haga, the most difficult shot was when F jumped over Zero during their duel and was cut up.[[22]](#cite_note-Key_Tradition-22)

F was originally a temporary name used during production, but it was kept due to Kinema Citrus staff preferring to use the name. In "The Lost Ones," Haga considered having F's real name be revealed. However, this did not occur, and Haga stated that it remained undecided whether F was an alias or the character's real name.[[25]](#cite_note-Instagram_Interview-25) Haga wanted to express a contrast between F and [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano"), another Order 66 survivor. Unlike Tano, F continued to be a Jedi, which Haga thought meant she could play a different role than Tano did in spinoff works.[[26]](#cite_note-Animate_Times-26)

Haga created a backstory for F so that the filmmakers could determine how she would react during the events of "The Village Bride," although only a few details from it actually appeared in the short. In the backstory, F received her scar from Dooku at the Battle of Geonosis, where she also lost her master.[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) *[The Art of Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/The_Art_of_Star_Wars:_Visions "The Art of Star Wars: Visions")*, a [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") reference book by [Zack Davisson](/wiki/Zack_Davisson "Zack Davisson"), instead stated F's master died during Order 66.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) As "The Lost Ones" later referenced elements of Haga's original backstory,[[3]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-3)[[9]](#cite_note-Backstory-9) this article assumes *The Art of Star Wars: Visions* is incorrect.

### Design[]

:   "*I was highly conscious of what F would wear. Is F Japanese or not? Is F a man or a woman? I wanted to keep it ambiguous.*"
:   ―Shiori Tani[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8)

 [![Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Lost Ones"](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/FConcept-TaniSide8.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20251031225314) [![Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Lost Ones"](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/FConcept-TaniSide8.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20251031225314)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/66/FConcept-TaniSide8.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20251031225314) 

Concept art by Shiori Tani for "The Lost Ones"

 

F was designed by [Shiori Tani](/wiki/Shiori_Tani "Shiori Tani").[[21]](#cite_note-Special_Program-21) While visualizing her, Tani was highly conscious of what she would wear, and wanted to keep it ambiguous as to whether F was [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_people "wikipedia:Japanese people") or not, and whether she was a man or woman. F's clothes were based on traditional [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "wikipedia:Japan") [*hakama*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hakama "wikipedia:Hakama"), and her mask was based on [*menpō*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Men-yoroi "wikipedia:Men-yoroi") jaw armor.[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) During the process of creating the character, the staff at Kinema Citrus dressed up like F to test her costume design. Producer [Kanako Shirasaki](/wiki/Kanako_Shirasaki "Kanako Shirasaki") loved the combination of F's *hakama* and boots,[[11]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Heroes-11) and Haga also praised Tani's design.[[21]](#cite_note-Special_Program-21) Concept art of F was created by Tani for both "The Village Bride"[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) and "The Lost Ones,"[[27]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_Gallery_Slide_5-27) and Anna Okamatsu also created a piece of concept art for "The Lost Ones."[[28]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones_Gallery_Slide_6-28)

Early in development of "The Village Bride," Haga wanted to give F a classic send-off in a heroic ship such as an [X-wing](/wiki/X-wing_starfighter "X-wing starfighter"). However, because of the time period the short took place in, traditional X-wings could not be used, and [Matt Martin](/wiki/Matt_Martin "Matt Martin") of the [Lucasfilm Story Group](/wiki/Lucasfilm_Story_Group "Lucasfilm Story Group") instead suggested that F's ship be a Z-95.[[29]](#cite_note-Meet_the_Ships-29) F's lightsaber was designed by [Takeshi Takakura](/wiki/Takeshi_Takakura "Takeshi Takakura") and has a distinct [Japanese sword](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_sword "wikipedia:Japanese sword") motif, similar to the [Mandalorian](/wiki/Mandalorian "Mandalorian") [Darksaber](/wiki/Darksaber "Darksaber"). Unlike traditional lightsabers, it has a thin blade like a [katana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Katana "wikipedia:Katana").[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) Haga thought F was in a similar position to [Jedi Temple Guards](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_Guard "Jedi Temple Guard"),[[30]](#cite_note-IGN_Japan-30) who often used yellow-bladed lightsabers,[[8]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-8) and kept their role as special guardians in mind while designing F, which led to his decision for her to use a yellow lightsaber.

In "The Village Bride," the gimmick of F's boot was made to be deliberately vague so that fans would wonder if she had a prosthetic leg, which was confirmed to be the case in "The Lost Ones". In addition to the scar on her face, Haga wanted F's body to also be wounded, as he felt *Star Wars* has a theme of moving forward despite physical imperfections, which was the case with[[26]](#cite_note-Animate_Times-26) the Jedi[[6]](#cite_note-Timelines-6) [Luke](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker") and [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"). According to Haga, this was also the reason why F continued to wander space and help people.[[26]](#cite_note-Animate_Times-26) Visually, Haga wanted Zero's design to be the complete opposite of F.[[22]](#cite_note-Key_Tradition-22)
