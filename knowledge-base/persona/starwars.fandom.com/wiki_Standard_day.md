A **standard day**,[[2]](#cite_note-Catalyst-2) also known as a **standard-day**,[[3]](#cite_note-TROSVD-3) was a unit of [time](/wiki/Time "Time"). Like other "standard" time units,[[4]](#cite_note-Future_of_the_Force_Recon-4) it was based on the [galactic standard calendar](/wiki/Galactic_standard_calendar "Galactic standard calendar"), which itself was based on the rotation of the [galactic](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant").[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5) As such, a standard day was divided into 24 [standard hours](/wiki/Standard_hour "Standard hour"), and 365 standard days made up a [standard year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year").[[6]](#cite_note-AB_Update-6) [Noryokon](/wiki/Noryokon "Noryokon") days were much longer than a galactic standard day.[[7]](#cite_note-Chewie's_Day_Off-7)

Within a [standard week](/wiki/Standard_week "Standard week") there were five days, which were as follows:

1. [Primeday](/wiki/Primeday "Primeday"), named after Coruscant's [star](/wiki/Star "Star") [Coruscant Prime](/wiki/Coruscant_Prime "Coruscant Prime")[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5)
2. [Centaxday](/wiki/Centaxday "Centaxday") named after the planet's primary [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon"), [Centax-1](/wiki/Centax-1 "Centax-1")[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5)
3. [Taungsday](/wiki/Taungsday "Taungsday"), named after the planet's ancient inhabitants the [Taung](/wiki/Taung "Taung")[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5)
4. [Zhellday](/wiki/Zhellday "Zhellday"), named after the other Coruscanti species the [Zhell](/wiki/Zhell "Zhell")[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5)
5. [Benduday](/wiki/Benduday "Benduday"), named after the native [Dai Bendu](/wiki/Dai_Bendu "Dai Bendu") order[[5]](#cite_note-Falcon61-5)

Within a day, a specific point in time could be expressed in terms of hours and minutes. In written form, there was no separator between the hours and minutes (e.g. 1630 meaning 16 hours and 30 minutes).[[8]](#cite_note-Smuggler's_Guide-8)

The number of days could also be measured in the number of rotations. On [Oba Diah](/wiki/Oba_Diah "Oba Diah"), [Marg Krim](/wiki/Marg_Krim "Marg Krim") gave [Rafa](/wiki/Rafa_Martez "Rafa Martez") and [Trace Martez](/wiki/Trace_Martez "Trace Martez") one rotation to bring back his missing [spice](/wiki/Spice "Spice").[[9]](#cite_note-Together_Again-9)
