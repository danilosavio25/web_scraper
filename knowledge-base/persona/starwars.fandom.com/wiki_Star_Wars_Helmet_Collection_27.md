***Star Wars Helmet Collection* 27** is the twenty-seventh issue of *[Star Wars Helmet Collection](/wiki/Star_Wars_Helmet_Collection "Star Wars Helmet Collection")*, published by [De Agostini](/wiki/De_Agostini "De Agostini"). The issue includes a 1:5 scale replica of [Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") [Gree's](/wiki/Gree_(clone_trooper) "Gree (clone trooper)") [helmet](/wiki/Helmet "Helmet"). It was published on [August 26](/wiki/August_26 "August 26"), [2015](/wiki/2015 "2015") in France by the Altaya brand, and on [January 11](/wiki/January_11 "January 11"), [2017](/wiki/2017 "2017") in the United Kingdom.
