Pirates existed throughout the galaxy.

 

**Pirates**,[[2]](#cite_note-Hunt_on_Celsor_3-2) also known as **space pirates**,[[3]](#cite_note-I229-3) were individuals who engaged in the criminal activity known as **piracy**. Such acts included hunting,[[2]](#cite_note-Hunt_on_Celsor_3-2) hostage taking,[[4]](#cite_note-Dooku_Captured-4) and theft of cargo.[[5]](#cite_note-A_Test_of_Strength-5) Notable pirates include [Hondo Ohnaka](/wiki/Hondo_Ohnaka "Hondo Ohnaka") who had dealings with the highest levels of leadership of both the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") and the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), and those led by [Q'anah](/wiki/Q%27anah_(pirate) "Q'anah (pirate)"), who was thwarted by [Wilhuff Tarkin](/wiki/Wilhuff_Tarkin "Wilhuff Tarkin") during his tenure with the [Outland Regions Security Force](/wiki/Outland_Regions_Security_Force "Outland Regions Security Force") of [Eriadu](/wiki/Eriadu "Eriadu"). Pirates were sometimes associated with [bounty hunters](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter").[[6]](#cite_note-Lethal_Trackdown-6)

Pirates were in a similar class of galactic individuals like [smugglers](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler"), [fringers](/wiki/Fringer "Fringer"), and scoundrels.[[7]](#cite_note-TROSVD-7) Pirates who were legitimized by a [letter of marque](/wiki/Letter_of_marque "Letter of marque")[[8]](#cite_note-Smuggler's_Guide-8) from some government or authority were known as [privateers](/wiki/Privateer "Privateer").[[9]](#cite_note-Tarkin-9) Piracy was often romanticized in media and popular culture, where it was associated with the perceived freedom that came from breaking the rules.[[10]](#cite_note-Scum-10)

Prior to the construction of [starships](/wiki/Starship "Starship") that pirates could prey upon, they hunted a vast array of creatures, such as [purrgil](/wiki/Purrgil "Purrgil") and [space slugs](/wiki/Exogorth "Exogorth").[[2]](#cite_note-Hunt_on_Celsor_3-2)[[12]](#cite_note-Preview-12) The [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") emerged as a major threat to the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"): the marauders were responsible for major atrocities, including the [Great Hyperspace Disaster](/wiki/Great_Hyperspace_Disaster "Great Hyperspace Disaster") through their [unique travel](/wiki/Path "Path") in [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace"),[[13]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-13) the [attack](/wiki/Valo_Atrocity "Valo Atrocity") on the [Republic Fair](/wiki/Republic_Fair "Republic Fair")[[14]](#cite_note-The_Rising_Storm-14) and numerous other raids, and even the [destruction](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon") of the [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon").[[11]](#cite_note-Fallen_Star-11) The [Eye of the Nihil](/wiki/Eye_of_the_Nihil "Eye of the Nihil"), [Marchion Ro](/wiki/Marchion_Ro "Marchion Ro"), would even establish the [Nihil space](/wiki/Occlusion_Zone "Occlusion Zone") and claimed he did so in the name of creating a place free of Republic rule.[[15]](#cite_note-Eye_of_the_Storm_2-15)

Before the [Invasion of Naboo](/wiki/Invasion_of_Naboo "Invasion of Naboo"), [Lord](/wiki/Lord "Lord") [Alorg](/wiki/Alorg "Alorg")'s [Flesh Mongers](/wiki/Flesh_Mongers "Flesh Mongers") kidnapped a child, [Lo](/wiki/Lo "Lo"). However they lost the child when Jedi [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") Yoda [rescued](/wiki/Rescue_of_Lo "Rescue of Lo") Lo.[[16]](#cite_note-SW26-16) Another [pirate faction](/wiki/Unidentified_pirate_faction "Unidentified pirate faction") carried out numerous raids on [Trade Federation](/wiki/Trade_Federation "Trade Federation").[[17]](#cite_note-Darth_Maul_1-17) [Nym](/wiki/Nym "Nym") and his [Lok Revenants](/wiki/Lok_Revenants "Lok Revenants") were pirates who targeted the Trade Federation, [spicerunners](/wiki/Spicerunner "Spicerunner"), and [enslavers](/wiki/Enslaver "Enslaver"), and sometimes worked with the [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Adi Gallia](/wiki/Adi_Gallia "Adi Gallia"). Following the [Battle of Naboo](/wiki/Battle_of_Naboo "Battle of Naboo"), [Hudso Shaku](/wiki/Hudso_Shaku "Hudso Shaku") and his [Krypder Riders](/wiki/Krypder_Riders "Krypder Riders") attempted to steal a [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi_holocron "Jedi holocron") [holocron](/wiki/Dallenor_holocron "Dallenor holocron") from a [excavation](/wiki/Dallenor_Excavation "Dallenor Excavation") on [Dallenor](/wiki/Dallenor "Dallenor"). However, they failed due to intervention from Jedi Master [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") and his [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker").[[18]](#cite_note-AoR-OWK1-18)

#### Clone Wars[]

:   "*Filth...you will pay for your insolence.*""*Insolence? We are pirates! We don't even know what that means!*"
:   ―Maul and Hondo Ohnaka[[19]](#cite_note-Revival-19)

During the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), several Republic supply ships were destroyed by pirates en route to [Rodia](/wiki/Rodia "Rodia").[[20]](#cite_note-Bombad_Jedi-20) Later the pirate [gang](/wiki/Ohnaka_Gang "Ohnaka Gang") of Captain [Hondo Ohnaka](/wiki/Hondo_Ohnaka "Hondo Ohnaka") captured Separatist leader, [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku") on [Vanqor](/wiki/Vanqor "Vanqor"). They attempted to ransom Dooku off to the Republic.[[4]](#cite_note-Dooku_Captured-4) They tried to triple the ransom by capturing Jedi Master Kenobi and Jedi Knight Skywalker. However, Dooku escaped and Kenobi and Skywalker were rescued by Representative [Jar Jar Binks](/wiki/Jar_Jar_Binks "Jar Jar Binks") and [Clone Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander") [Stone's](/wiki/Stone_(clone_trooper) "Stone (clone trooper)") troopers.[[21]](#cite_note-Gungan_General-21) The Ohnaka Gang would continue in their operations such as extorting [nysillin](/wiki/Nysillin "Nysillin") from farmers on [Felucia](/wiki/Felucia "Felucia"),[[22]](#cite_note-Bounty_Hunters-22) smuggling weapons to known resistance movements,[[23]](#cite_note-Tipping_Points-23) and attempted to steal [Kyber crystals](/wiki/Kyber_crystal "Kyber crystal") from a group of [Jedi younglings](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate").[[5]](#cite_note-A_Test_of_Strength-5)

Several members of the Ohnaka gang were approached and bribed by [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") and his brother [Savage Opress](/wiki/Savage_Opress "Savage Opress") into working for them. [Jiro](/wiki/Jiro "Jiro") and several of the pirates joined Maul until Captain Ohnaka was able to hold them at gunpoint. The Ohnaka gang along with Master Kenobi were able to force the two zabraks to retreat. After Maul's ship crashed, the Ohnaka gang found the [credit coffer](/wiki/Credit_coffer "Credit coffer") and assumed that the Zabraks were dead.[[19]](#cite_note-Revival-19) Another group of Weequay pirates of the [*Marauder*](/wiki/Marauder_(Weequay_pirate_ship) "Marauder (Weequay pirate ship)") organized [gladiator](/wiki/Gladiator "Gladiator") games aboard their ship with stolen droids.[[24]](#cite_note-Nomad_Droids-24)

At some unspecified point of [her](/wiki/Gender "Gender") life, [Kiera Swan](/wiki/Kiera_Swan "Kiera Swan") became a pirate and became well known in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy"). However, she later abandoned that occupation and became a [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter"), something which made her more famous than she was before.[[25]](#cite_note-TB-25)

[Lassa Rhayme](/wiki/Lassa_Rhayme "Lassa Rhayme") [Pantoran](/wiki/Pantoran "Pantoran") **pirate** who served as the [captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") of the *[Opportunity](/wiki/Opportunity "Opportunity")* and "[Pirate Queen](/wiki/Pirate_Queen "Pirate Queen")" leader of the [Blood Bone Order](/wiki/Blood_Bone_Order "Blood Bone Order") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"). During the last months of the war, she had an encounter with [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Asajj Ventress](/wiki/Asajj_Ventress "Asajj Ventress"), whom she considered a friend after successfully recovering the *[Steady On](/wiki/Steady_On "Steady On")* and its cargo from Hondo Ohnaka and his gang on their own.[[26]](#cite_note-Kindred_Spirits-26)

### Age of the Empire[]

#### Empire's reign[]

 [![The Cloud Riders interrogating Tobias Beckett](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/EnfysAndBeckett-Beckett1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180816081056) [![The Cloud Riders interrogating Tobias Beckett](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/EnfysAndBeckett-Beckett1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180816081056)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/3/3e/EnfysAndBeckett-Beckett1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180816081056) 

The Cloud Riders interrogating Tobias Beckett

 

According to reports from the [Imperial Security Bureau](/wiki/Imperial_Security_Bureau "Imperial Security Bureau"), pirate groups were likely to shelter in the polar regions. In order to keep control of such regions, [Imperial High Command](/wiki/Imperial_High_Command "Imperial High Command") established the [snowtrooper](/wiki/Cold_weather_assault_trooper "Cold weather assault trooper") division.[[27]](#cite_note-Imperial_Troops-27) [Phee Genoa](/wiki/Phee_Genoa "Phee Genoa") was a pirate operating in the early Imperial Era.[[28]](#cite_note-Spoils_of_War-28) The Empire was able to break up the Ohnaka gang, but Ohnaka continued with his operations on his own.[[29]](#cite_note-Brothers_of_the_Broken_Horn-29) [Enfys Nest](/wiki/Enfys_Nest "Enfys Nest") and her [Cloud-Riders](/wiki/Cloud-Riders "Cloud-Riders") were seen as pirates during their attempted thefts of [Identichips](/wiki/Identichip "Identichip")[[30]](#cite_note-Beckett-30) and [Coaxium](/wiki/Coaxium "Coaxium").[[31]](#cite_note-Solo-31) Gwarm, formerly Ohnaka's lieutenant, started his own [gang](/wiki/Gwarm%27s_gang "Gwarm's gang").[[32]](#cite_note-Droid_Hunters-32) The Empire fought with the [Iska pirates](/wiki/Iska_pirates "Iska pirates") during the Imperial Era.[[33]](#cite_note-The_Secret_Academy-33)

[Angel's](/wiki/Angel_(pirate) "Angel (pirate)") [Culoss](/wiki/Culoss "Culoss") band hijacked the freighter *[Dromedar](/wiki/Dromedar "Dromedar")* to steal its [tibanna](/wiki/Tibanna "Tibanna") [gas](/wiki/Gas "Gas") and captured its crew. The Culoss pretended to be its crew when a boarding party under Lieutenant [Thrawn](/wiki/Mitth%27raw%27nuruodo "Mitth'raw'nuruodo") from the Imperial Navy ship *[Blood Crow](/wiki/Blood_Crow "Blood Crow")*. They were able to capture the boarding party and escape with the gas. However, three of the pirates were captured and questioned by Thrawn at [Ansion](/wiki/Ansion "Ansion")'s [Imperial base](/wiki/Unidentified_Imperial_base_(Ansion) "Unidentified Imperial base (Ansion)"). With Thrawn's information, the Culoss' operations on [Uba](/wiki/Uba "Uba"), [Cartherston](/wiki/Cartherston "Cartherston"), and [Kril'Dor](/wiki/Kril%27Dor "Kril'Dor") were targeted by the Empire.[[34]](#cite_note-Thrawn-34)

Ohnaka was able to briefly take [Cikatro Vizago](/wiki/Cikatro_Vizago "Cikatro Vizago")'s ship, the *[Broken Horn](/wiki/Broken_Horn "Broken Horn")* and take [Azmorigan](/wiki/Azmorigan "Azmorigan")'s credits for the [power generator](/wiki/Power_generator "Power generator"). Ohnaka lost the ship, however, when Bridger helped Vizago in taking back the *Broken Horn*. He was able to keep the credits for himself.[[29]](#cite_note-Brothers_of_the_Broken_Horn-29) Ohnaka helped the [Spectres](/wiki/Spectres "Spectres") of [Phoenix Cell](/wiki/Phoenix_Cell "Phoenix Cell") in rescuing Lasat refugees,[[35]](#cite_note-Legends_of_the_Lasat-35) providing locations of derelict bombers[[36]](#cite_note-Steps_Into_Shadow-36) and acquiring [proton bombs](/wiki/Proton_bomb "Proton bomb"). He also recruited [Melch](/wiki/Melch "Melch") as a partner.[[37]](#cite_note-Wynkahthu_Job-37) The [Seventh](/wiki/Seventh_Fleet_(Galactic_Empire) "Seventh Fleet (Galactic Empire)") and [Third Fleets](/wiki/Third_Fleet "Third Fleet") hunted down pirates that were causing problems for [Director](/wiki/Director "Director") [Orson Callan Krennic](/wiki/Orson_Callan_Krennic "Orson Callan Krennic")'s [Project Stardust](/wiki/Project_Stardust "Project Stardust"). Thrawn discovered that Savit was working with the pirates in stealing supplies Governor [Haveland](/wiki/Haveland "Haveland") and Krennic. Thrawn was able to defeat Savit's fleet and had him tried for treason on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant").[[38]](#cite_note-Treason-38)

#### Galactic Civil War[]

During the [Galactic Civil War](/wiki/Galactic_Civil_War "Galactic Civil War"), Vader dealt with [pirates](/wiki/Crymorah-backed_pirates "Crymorah-backed pirates") backed by the [Crymorah Syndicate](/wiki/Crymorah_Syndicate "Crymorah Syndicate").[[39]](#cite_note-Vader_II-39) The [Krawg pirates](/wiki/Krawg_pirates "Krawg pirates") attempted to buy a crate of Imperial [blasters](/wiki/E-11_medium_blaster_rifle "E-11 medium blaster rifle") from smuggler [Sana Starros](/wiki/Sana_Starros "Sana Starros"). However, Starros tricked them and [Lando Calrissian](/wiki/Landonis_Balthazar_Calrissian "Landonis Balthazar Calrissian") made sure the pirates were arrested by the Empire for the theft.[[40]](#cite_note-13_crates-40) During the [reign](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") of the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire"), Rhayme and her Blood Bone Order became a food and weapons supplier for a subgroup of the mainly extinct [Partisans](/wiki/Partisans "Partisans") known as the [Dreamers](/wiki/Dreamers "Dreamers").[[41]](#cite_note-Inferno_Squad-41)

Following the end of the Civil War, [Eleodie Maracavanya](/wiki/Eleodie_Maracavanya "Eleodie Maracavanya") and zher pirates of the [Sovereign Latitudes of Maracavanya](/wiki/Sovereign_Latitudes_of_Maracavanya "Sovereign Latitudes of Maracavanya") were able to capture the [*Executor*-class Star Dreadnought](/wiki/Executor-class_Star_Dreadnought "Executor-class Star Dreadnought") [*Annihilator*](/wiki/Liberty%27s_Misrule "Liberty's Misrule") and renamed it the *Liberty's Misrule*.[[42]](#cite_note-Life_Debt-42) Together, Maracavany and zher pirates used it to control their small empire in [Wild Space](/wiki/Wild_Space "Wild Space").[[43]](#cite_note-Empire's_End-43) Another pirate, [Viz Moshara](/wiki/Viz_Moshara "Viz Moshara") attempted to protect [Substation Grimdock](/wiki/Substation_Grimdock "Substation Grimdock") from the [New Republic army](/wiki/New_Republic_army "New Republic army"), but was killed.[[44]](#cite_note-Last_Shot-44)

### Age of the New Republic[]

:   ―Din Djarin to Grogu[[45]](#cite_note-Chapter_17-45)

By the time of the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic"), they could not stop the [Pirate Nation](/wiki/Pirate_Nation "Pirate Nation") in the [Mid Rim Territories](/wiki/Mid_Rim_Territories "Mid Rim Territories"). [Gorian Shard](/wiki/Gorian_Shard "Gorian Shard") and his [gang](/wiki/Gorian_Shard%27s_pirate_crew "Gorian Shard's pirate crew") attacked [Nevarro](/wiki/Nevarro "Nevarro"), but eventually were defeated by [Mandalorians](/wiki/Mandalorian "Mandalorian").[[46]](#cite_note-Chapter_21-46)

On the other hand, in the [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories"), many pirates settled at the [Borgo Prime](/wiki/Borgo_Prime "Borgo Prime") base, located in the [Hydian Way](/wiki/Hydian_Way "Hydian Way"). A widespread myth among many pirates from this era is that of [At Attin](/wiki/At_Attin "At Attin"), a lost world where legendary treasure was believed to lie, a pirate's dream. Captain "[Silvo](/wiki/Jod_Na_Nawood "Jod Na Nawood")" and his [pirates](/wiki/Silvo%27s_pirates "Silvo's pirates") were able to find [At Attin](/wiki/At_Attin "At Attin") when he came across a [group](/wiki/Skeleton_Crew "Skeleton Crew") of children at Port Borgo.[[47]](#cite_note-Past_the_Barrier-47) Silvo was able to use the [Onyx Cinder](/wiki/Onyx_Cinder "Onyx Cinder") to land on At Attin[[48]](#cite_note-We're_Gonna_Be_In_So_Much_Trouble-48) and force Under Secretary [Fara](/wiki/Fara "Fara") to allow his [frigate](/wiki/Silvo%27s_pirate_frigate "Silvo's pirate frigate") through the barrier. However, [SM-33](/wiki/SM-33 "SM-33") and [KB](/wiki/KB "KB") were able to contact [Kh'ymm](/wiki/Kh%27ymm "Kh'ymm") and she was able to send Commander [Kent](/wiki/Kent "Kent") and his Squadron of [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic") X-wings. Silvo was left behind and watched as the New Republic destroyed his frigate.[[49]](#cite_note-Real_Good_Guys-49)

### Age of Resistance[]

 [![Sidon Ithano's crew raiding a Separatist holdout.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/14/CrimsonCorsairCrewRaid-Ashcan.png/revision/latest?cb=20210920074819) [![Sidon Ithano's crew raiding a Separatist holdout.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/14/CrimsonCorsairCrewRaid-Ashcan.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20210920074819)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/1/14/CrimsonCorsairCrewRaid-Ashcan.png/revision/latest?cb=20210920074819)

They later boarded the *Colossus* as it entered hyperspace towards the [Resistance base](/wiki/Resistance_base "Resistance base") on [D'Qar](/wiki/D%27Qar "D'Qar").[[56]](#cite_note-No_Escape_2-56)
