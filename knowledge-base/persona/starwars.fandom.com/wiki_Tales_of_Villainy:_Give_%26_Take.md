"**Tales of Villainy: Give & Take**" is a comic story that was published as the back up story in the [twelfth issue](/wiki/Star_Wars_Adventures_(2020)_12 "Star Wars Adventures (2020) 12") of the [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020") [*Star Wars Adventures*](/wiki/Star_Wars_Adventures_(2020) "Star Wars Adventures (2020)") comic series. It was written by [Andrew Lee Griffith](/wiki/Andrew_Griffith "Andrew Griffith"), illustrated by Andrew Lee Griffith, and published by [IDW Publishing](/wiki/IDW_Publishing "IDW Publishing") on [November 24](/wiki/November_24 "November 24"), [2021](/wiki/2021 "2021").

The story was dedicated to [Katie Johnson](/wiki/Katie_Johnson "Katie Johnson").
