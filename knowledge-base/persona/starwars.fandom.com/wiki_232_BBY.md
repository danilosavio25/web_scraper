## Events[]

* The [Kublop Springs festival](/wiki/Kublop_Springs_festival "Kublop Springs festival") is held by the local residents and invited the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") to celebrate.[[7]](#cite_note-Yoda's_Mission-7)
* [Team *Crimson Bolt*](/wiki/Team_Crimson_Bolt "Team Crimson Bolt") wins the [Kublop Classic](/wiki/Kublop_Classic "Kublop Classic") on [Tenoo](/wiki/Tenoo "Tenoo").[[8]](#cite_note-Nash's_Race_Day-8)
* The residents in [Kublop Springs](/wiki/Kublop_Springs "Kublop Springs") hold the [Jellyfruit Festival](/wiki/Jellyfruit_Festival "Jellyfruit Festival").[[9]](#cite_note-The_Jellyfruit_Pursuit-9)
* [Kai Brightstar](/wiki/Kai_Brightstar "Kai Brightstar"), [Lys Solay](/wiki/Lys_Solay "Lys Solay"), [Nubs](/wiki/Nubs "Nubs"), [SF-R3](/wiki/SF-R3 "SF-R3"), and [CAM](/wiki/CAM "CAM") discover a new [bird](/wiki/Bird "Bird") species, the [burrowberry bird](/wiki/Burrowberry_bird "Burrowberry bird") on [Yamradi](/wiki/Yamradi "Yamradi").[[10]](#cite_note-Creature_Safari-10)
* To commemorate the launch of the new [space station](/wiki/Space_station "Space station") [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon"), the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") and [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") host a [conclave](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon_conclave "Starlight Beacon conclave") at the station.[[2]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-2)
* Lys Solay, SR-R3 and CAM discover a new [reptile](/wiki/Reptile "Reptile") species, the [Mardorsh](/wiki/Mardorsh "Mardorsh").[[11]](#cite_note-Creature_Comforts-11)
* In the midst of the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"), the [Great Hyperspace Disaster](/wiki/Great_Hyperspace_Disaster "Great Hyperspace Disaster") occurs, causing many [starships](/wiki/Starship "Starship") across [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") to be abruptly knocked out of [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace").[[2]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-2)[[12]](#cite_note-Into_the_Dark-12)
* The Nihil [raid](/wiki/Raid_on_Elphrona "Raid on Elphrona") the [Blythe family](/wiki/Blythe_family "Blythe family") [homestead](/wiki/Blythe_homestead "Blythe homestead"), with the secret goal of capturing, for [Marchion Ro](/wiki/Marchion_Ro "Marchion Ro"), a Jedi who would come to the rescue.[[2]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-2)
* The *[Vessel](/wiki/Vessel "Vessel")*, carrying a group of Jedi, was stranded at the [Amaxine space station](/wiki/Amaxine_Space_Station "Amaxine Space Station") in the aftermath of the Great Hyperspace Disaster along with many other galactic citizens. There, they encounter and unknowingly [unleash](/wiki/Drengir_crisis "Drengir crisis") the [Drengir](/wiki/Drengir "Drengir") across the galaxy.[[12]](#cite_note-Into_the_Dark-12)
* The [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [attack](/wiki/Attack_on_Trymant_IV "Attack on Trymant IV") [Trymant IV](/wiki/Trymant_IV "Trymant IV").[[13]](#cite_note-HRA_1-13)
* [Keeve Trennis](/wiki/Keeve_Trennis "Keeve Trennis") is [knighted](/wiki/Knighting_Ceremony "Knighting Ceremony") and begins serving on the Jedi front-lines fighting the Drengir.[[14]](#cite_note-HR_2-14)
* The [padawans](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of the [*Star Hopper*](/wiki/Star_Hopper "Star Hopper") welcome [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola"), a refugee from Trymant IV, into their ranks, but not as a padawan.[[15]](#cite_note-HRA_2-15)[[16]](#cite_note-HRA_3-16)
* The Galactic Republic starship *[Steady Wing](/wiki/Steady_Wing "Steady Wing")* is [bombed](/wiki/Bomb "Bomb") by undercover Nihil [saboteurs](/wiki/Saboteur "Saboteur"), causing the party onboard to be stranded on the [jungle](/wiki/Jungle "Jungle") [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Wevo](/wiki/Wevo "Wevo").[[17]](#cite_note-AToC-17)
* [Krix Kamerat](/wiki/Krix_Kamerat "Krix Kamerat"), another Trymant IV refugee, joins the Nihil and is taken on as the protege of Marchion Ro as the [Evereni](/wiki/Evereni "Evereni") [Eye](/wiki/Eye_of_the_Nihil "Eye of the Nihil") searches for the [Great Leveler](/wiki/Great_Leveler "Great Leveler").[[18]](#cite_note-HRA_5-18)
* The Nihil [Tempest](/wiki/Kassav%27s_Tempest "Kassav's Tempest") of [Kassav Milliko](/wiki/Kassav_Milliko "Kassav Milliko") battles the Galactic Republic and Jedi Order at the [Kur Nebula](/wiki/Kur_Nebula "Kur Nebula"), the Tempest being decimated.[[2]](#cite_note-Light_of_the_Jedi-2)
* A Drengir arrives on [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon") in the [corpse](/wiki/Death "Death") of a [Hutt trader](/wiki/Unidentified_Hutt_trader "Unidentified Hutt trader"), and [attacks](/wiki/Assault_on_Starlight_Beacon "Assault on Starlight Beacon") the station when awakened.[[19]](#cite_note-HR_4-19)[[20]](#cite_note-HR_5-20)
