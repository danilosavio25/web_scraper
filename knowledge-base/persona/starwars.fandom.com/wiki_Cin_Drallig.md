**Cin Drallig** was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") who served the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") as [Battlemaster](/wiki/Battlemaster "Battlemaster") and [Chief of Security](/wiki/Chief_of_Security "Chief of Security") of the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"). He was the head of the [Jedi Temple Guard](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_Guard "Jedi Temple Guard") and a [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") instructor who was disrespectfully nicknamed "**the Troll**" by members of the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi"). Drallig was killed by [Darth Vader](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker") during his [attack](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall") on the Jedi Temple.

## Biography[]

### Early life and Jedi career[]

[Hailing](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") from [Lavisar](/wiki/Lavisar "Lavisar"),[[1]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-1) Cin Drallig, a gifted [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender"), was [apprenticed](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") to the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council")'s [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda").[[4]](#cite_note-CVDNE-4) Faithfully honing his skills and attunement to the Force, Drallig garnered many years of knowledge and experience, and eventually became the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order")'s [Battlemaster](/wiki/Battlemaster "Battlemaster").[[6]](#cite_note-6) Drallig also achieved the rank of [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master"), and led the [Jedi Temple Guard](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_Guard "Jedi Temple Guard") during the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars") as the temple [Chief of Security](/wiki/Chief_of_Security "Chief of Security"). As a Master, he was a teacher of [lightsaber](/wiki/Lightsaber "Lightsaber") combat who passed his skills on to new generations of [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi"),[[7]](#cite_note-Databank-7) such as [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") and [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker").[[4]](#cite_note-CVDNE-4) Although he was regarded as perhaps the finest duelist in the Order,[[1]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-1) he earned the unflattering nickname "the [Troll](/wiki/Troll "Troll")" from other members of the Jedi Order.[[7]](#cite_note-Databank-7)

#### Guarding the Temple[]

Drallig guards the Jedi Temple during a rally.

 

In [21 BBY](/wiki/21_BBY "21 BBY"),[[8]](#cite_note-Date-8) [Cad Bane](/wiki/Cad_Bane "Cad Bane") slipped past Drallig and the Temple's security forces using specialized technology and his intelligence and managed to slip into the [Holocron Vault](/wiki/Holocron_Vault "Holocron Vault"). A Jedi [holocron](/wiki/Holocron "Holocron") was stolen.[[3]](#cite_note-SWE-3)

During the [Clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") Wars, the [Jedi Temple was bombed](/wiki/Bombing_of_the_Jedi_Temple_hangar "Bombing of the Jedi Temple hangar") by [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") [Barriss Offee](/wiki/Barriss_Offee "Barriss Offee"). Before the perpetrator had been caught, the Jedi led an investigation to determine who was behind the attack.[[9]](#cite_note-Sabotage-9) As the bombing caused great alarm, Drallig being the head of Temple security, was most concerned at how vulnerable the Jedi Order had become.[[10]](#cite_note-NoLive_Sabotage_Ep_Guide-10) Skywalker approached Drallig, who was standing guard with the Temple Guard at the gates of the Jedi Temple, where protesters were demonstrating against the war and the Jedi Order's involvement in it. Skywalker showed Drallig a [holographic](/wiki/Hologram "Hologram") image of their then-suspect, [Jackar Bowmani](/wiki/Jackar_Bowmani "Jackar Bowmani"), and asked the Jedi Master to be on alert for him. This led to the suspect's wife, [Letta Turmond](/wiki/Letta_Turmond "Letta Turmond"), being taken into custody after spotting the holographic image,[[9]](#cite_note-Sabotage-9) and the Jedi ultimately learned that Turmond was part of the plot to attack the Jedi Temple.[[11]](#cite_note-Knew_Too_Much-11)

 [![Drallig defends younglings from Separatist explosives, aided by two Temple Guards.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/85/CinDrallig-Kanan8.png/revision/latest?cb=20180129063932) [![Drallig defends younglings from Separatist explosives, aided by two Temple Guards.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/85/CinDrallig-Kanan8.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180129063932)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/85/CinDrallig-Kanan8.png/revision/latest?cb=20180129063932) 

Drallig defends younglings from Separatist explosives, aided by two Temple Guards.

 

The Temple was later [attacked](/wiki/Bombing_at_the_Jedi_Temple "Bombing at the Jedi Temple") by [Captain](/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Rackham Sear](/wiki/Rackham_Sear "Rackham Sear") of the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems"). During the attack, Drallig led two Temple Guards in securing the quarters of the Jedi [younglings](/wiki/Youngling "Youngling"). He arrived just in time to cut a small explosive drone in half before it detonated in a room with several younglings. He ordered the guards to stay with the younglings and destroy any more explosives that entered the area. Drallig then joined Masters [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") and Kenobi, reporting to them that the younglings were safe, but that there were explosions throughout the Temple and that the source was unknown. Master [Depa Billaba](/wiki/Depa_Billaba "Depa Billaba") and [Initiate](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") [Caleb Dume](/wiki/Kanan_Jarrus "Kanan Jarrus") ended the attack by confronting Sear on the roof of the Temple, where he committed suicide by leaping off the building.[[5]](#cite_note-Kanan_8-5)

#### Fall of the Jedi Temple[]

 [![Drallig dueled Darth Vader alongside Padawans Whie and Bene.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e6/CinDrallig.png/revision/latest?cb=20130210221819) [![Drallig dueled Darth Vader alongside Padawans Whie and Bene.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e6/CinDrallig.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/264?cb=20130210221819)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/e/e6/CinDrallig.png/revision/latest?cb=20130210221819) 

Drallig dueled Darth Vader alongside Padawans Whie and Bene.

 

At the end of the war, Skywalker turned to the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") and became Darth Vader, apprentice to [Darth Sidious](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious"), the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith"). Vader [attacked the Jedi Temple](/wiki/Operation:_Knightfall "Operation: Knightfall") with the [501st Legion](/wiki/501st_Legion "501st Legion") of [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper"), while Sidious issued [Order 66](/wiki/Order_66 "Order 66") to force the clones to turn against the Jedi Order. Drallig fought to defend the Jedi Temple[[12]](#cite_note-Episode_III-12) alongside Padawans [Whie Malreaux](/wiki/Whie_Malreaux "Whie Malreaux") and [Bene](/wiki/Bene "Bene"), both of whom perished in [lightsaber combat](/wiki/Lightsaber_duel "Lightsaber duel") with an enraged Darth Vader.[[3]](#cite_note-SWE-3) Drallig confronted Vader himself, but proved unable to put an end to the Sith Lord's rampage.[[12]](#cite_note-Episode_III-12)

### Legacy[]

Shortly after Drallig's death, Masters Yoda and Kenobi returned to the Temple, where they witnessed a security hologram recording depicting Drallig's final moments dueling Darth Vader alongside Padawans Bene and Whie.[[12]](#cite_note-Episode_III-12)

Sometime after his death, the [holocron of Cin Drallig](/wiki/Holocron_of_Cin_Drallig "Holocron of Cin Drallig") was sold in an auction on [Denon](/wiki/Denon "Denon") to the [noble](/wiki/Noble "Noble") [Rin Praji](/wiki/Rin_Praji "Rin Praji"), who kept the holocron safe at [his estate](/wiki/Rin_Praji%27s_estate "Rin Praji's estate") on [Kaikielius](/wiki/Kaikielius "Kaikielius").[[13]](#cite_note-Knights_of_Fate-13)

## Personality and traits[]

 [![Cin Drallig takes a stand against Darth Vader, his former comrade Anakin Skywalker.](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c6/VaderDrallig-TheSecretsOfCloneTroopers.png/revision/latest?cb=20250701035917) [![Cin Drallig takes a stand against Darth Vader, his former comrade Anakin Skywalker.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c6/VaderDrallig-TheSecretsOfCloneTroopers.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/275?cb=20250701035917)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/c/c6/VaderDrallig-TheSecretsOfCloneTroopers.png/revision/latest?cb=20250701035917) 

Cin Drallig takes a stand against Darth Vader, his former comrade Anakin Skywalker.

 

Cin Drallig was a stoic and resolved Jedi Master, a noted leader who presided over the Temple Guard at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. He stood at 1.74 [meters](/wiki/Meter "Meter") tall, or 5 [feet](/wiki/Foot "Foot") 9 inches. He had [dark blond](/wiki/Color "Color") hair, blue eyes, and light skin.[[1]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-1)

## Equipment[]

Drallig wore classic Jedi robes and boots and kept his hair long. From time to time he had protective gauntlets on his arms and hands.[[1]](#cite_note-Join_the_Battle-1) Drallig wielded a single green lightsaber.[[5]](#cite_note-Kanan_8-5)

## Behind the scenes[]

For his brief appearance in [*Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_III_Revenge_of_the_Sith "Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith"), Cin Drallig was portrayed by [prequel trilogy](/wiki/Prequel_trilogy "Prequel trilogy") stunt coordinator [Nick Gillard](/wiki/Nick_Gillard "Nick Gillard"), whose name was the [basis for the character's name](/wiki/Tuckerization "Tuckerization") (Cin is Nick backwards without the K, and Drallig is Gillard backwards).[[12]](#cite_note-Episode_III-12) Drallig was later voiced by [Robin Atkin Downes](/wiki/Robin_Atkin_Downes "Robin Atkin Downes") in "[Sabotage](/wiki/Sabotage_(episode) "Sabotage (episode)")," the seventeenth episode of the [fifth season](/wiki/The_Clone_Wars:_Season_Five "The Clone Wars: Season Five") of the animated television series *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")*.[[9]](#cite_note-Sabotage-9)
