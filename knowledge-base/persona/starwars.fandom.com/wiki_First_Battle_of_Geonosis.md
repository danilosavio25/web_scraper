The **First Battle of Geonosis**, also referred to as the **Battle of Geonosis** or the **Battle on Geonosis**, was the first major battle fought in [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY") between the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") and the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") on [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis"), marking the beginning of the three-[year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"). It would be the first major combat of the [Grand Army of the Republic](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic"), as well as the first major battle the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") had fought in years.

## Prelude[]

:   "*I've got a bad feeling about this.*"
:   ―Anakin Skywalker[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

Prior to the outbreak of the battle on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Geonosis](/wiki/Geonosis "Geonosis"), the [Galactic Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") had endured for several [centuries](/wiki/Standard_century "Standard century") as it expanded and prospered. However, despite its foundation as a democratic governmental body, with a [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") of hundreds of [senators](/wiki/Senator "Senator") representing thousands of [star systems](/wiki/Star_system "Star system"), the Republic had begun to develop indications of corruption and neglect for worlds beyond the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds") within its democratic foundation. As a result of its corruption and ineffectiveness, several star systems had developed a dissatisfaction for the Republic, which led to [political](/wiki/Politics "Politics") tensions, establishment of coalitions and alliances, and the accumulation of resources, as well as the inception of a [secessionist movement](/wiki/Separatist_Crisis "Separatist Crisis") under the direction of the fallen [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") and [Sith Lord](/wiki/Sith_Lord "Sith Lord") [Count](/wiki/Count "Count") [Dooku](/wiki/Dooku "Dooku"), who founded the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems "Confederacy of Independent Systems") as a new government supported by thousands of seceded star systems and trade and commercial groups, such as the [Trade Federation](/wiki/Trade_Federation "Trade Federation"), [InterGalactic Banking Clan](/wiki/InterGalactic_Banking_Clan "InterGalactic Banking Clan"), [Techno Union](/wiki/Techno_Union "Techno Union"), [Commerce Guild](/wiki/Commerce_Guild "Commerce Guild") and [Corporate Alliance](/wiki/Corporate_Alliance "Corporate Alliance"), following his [address](/wiki/Raxus_Address "Raxus Address") on the planet [Raxus Secundus](/wiki/Raxus_Secundus "Raxus Secundus").[[14]](#cite_note-Rise-14)

In [22 BBY](/wiki/22_BBY "22 BBY"),[[4]](#cite_note-SWGA-4) following the Confederacy's establishment, with Dooku as its [Head of State](/wiki/Head_of_State "Head of State"), as [terrorist](/wiki/Terrorist "Terrorist") strikes were conducted on the Republic's [capital](/wiki/Capital "Capital") world of [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), the democratic government's senate and [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor"), [Sheev Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious")—secretly the [Dark Lord of the Sith](/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith "Dark Lord of the Sith") Darth Sidious—had prepared to discuss the consideration of the [Military Creation Act](/wiki/Military_Creation_Act "Military Creation Act"). However, on the [day](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day") of the vote, Senator [Padmé Amidala](/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Padmé Amidala") of the planet [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") had traveled to Coruscant in an attempt to oppose the proposed bill, was prevented from denouncing the Military Creation Act as a result of a series of [assassination attempts](/wiki/Plot_to_assassinate_Senator_Amidala "Plot to assassinate Senator Amidala") orchestrated by the Federation's [Viceroy](/wiki/Viceroy "Viceroy"), [Nute Gunray](/wiki/Nute_Gunray "Nute Gunray"), which led to the assignment of [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") [Obi-Wan Kenobi](/wiki/Obi-Wan_Kenobi "Obi-Wan Kenobi") and his [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"), [Anakin Skywalker](/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker "Anakin Skywalker"), as her security detail at the [Chancellor](/wiki/Chancellor "Chancellor")'s request. While Amidala was under protection by the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), the two Jedi managed to discover the identity of the Senator's assailant as the [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Zam Wesell](/wiki/Zam_Wesell "Zam Wesell"), whose [death](/wiki/Death "Death") by [saberdart](/wiki/Kamino_saberdart "Kamino saberdart") by her employer, the [bounty](/wiki/Bounty "Bounty") hunter [Jango Fett](/wiki/Jango_Fett "Jango Fett"), resulted in Amidala's return to Naboo accompanied by Skywalker and a wider investigation by Kenobi.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

As Kenobi investigated the unidentified bounty hunter, the Jedi Knight, who determined the saberdart to be of [Kaminoan](/wiki/Kaminoan "Kaminoan") origins, [traveled](/wiki/Mission_to_Kamino_(Separatist_Crisis) "Mission to Kamino (Separatist Crisis)") to the planet [Kamino](/wiki/Kamino "Kamino"), which had unknowingly been removed from the [archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives") in the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") by an anonymous force, in an attempt to locate the assailant. While on Kamino, Kenobi had discovered the foundation of an [army](/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic "Grand Army of the Republic") of [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") for the Republic, whose creation by the Kaminoans had been arranged by deceased Jedi Master [Sifo-Dyas](/wiki/Sifo-Dyas "Sifo-Dyas") [ten years](/wiki/32_BBY "32 BBY") prior to Kenobi's arrival. After his discovery of the [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") [army](/wiki/Army "Army")'s benefactor as the bounty hunter Jango Fett, Kenobi requested to meet with the [clone template](/wiki/Clone_template "Clone template"), where he began to question Fett about his travels and the army, who explained that he was recruited by a man known as Tyranus to be the army's template.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

Following their meeting, the bounty hunter, after a brief skirmish with Kenobi, made his departure from Kamino to the planet Geonosis, but was pursued by the Jedi Knight, who landed on the surface, where he managed to intercept a meeting of the Separatist Council members and discover that Viceroy Gunray was responsible for Amidala's assassination attempts, as well as the Confederacy's creation of a massive [droid army](/wiki/Separatist_Droid_Army "Separatist Droid Army") on the planet. However, as the Jedi Knight contacted Coruscant, whose transmission was transferred to the Republic and Jedi by Amidala and Skywalker on the planet [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine"), Kenobi explained that the Federation was to produce a [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") army and the conspirator of Amidala's attempted assassinations but was instead taken captive by the Confederacy.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

In response to Kenobi's capture, the Republic Senate, at the proposal of [Junior Representative](/wiki/Junior_Representative "Junior Representative") [Jar Jar Binks](/wiki/Jar_Jar_Binks "Jar Jar Binks") of Naboo, granted Chancellor Palpatine the authority to form a Grand Army of the Republic in order to combat the rising Separatist Droid Army as decreed by the government's [Emergency Powers Act](/wiki/Emergency_Powers_Act "Emergency Powers Act"). As a result, Jedi [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") proclaimed that we would be departing for Kamino to investigate the clone army, while Jedi Master [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu") would lead a [strike team](/wiki/Jedi_assault_team "Jedi assault team") to Geonosis in an attempt to rescue Kenobi and prevent Dooku from proceeding with his plans. Although ordered by Windu to remain on Tatooine, Amidala and Skywalker had departed for Geonosis in order to rescue the Jedi Knight, where they, following a prolonged struggle in the droid manufacturing facilities, were also taken prisoner by the [Geonosians](/wiki/Geonosian "Geonosian") and sentenced to [execution](/wiki/Death_penalty "Death penalty") in the [Petranaki Arena](/wiki/Petranaki_Arena "Petranaki Arena") along with Kenobi. However, the three managed to narrowly escape their executions as they outmaneuvered their animal executioners, while Windu and his strike team arrived on the planet, erupting the chaotic First Battle of Geonosis.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

The [Zilkin](/wiki/Zilkin "Zilkin") [Meebur Gascon](/wiki/Meebur_Gascon "Meebur Gascon") participated in the battle, as part of [Republic Strategic Command](/wiki/Republic_High_Command "Republic High Command"), by analyzing Geonosis's hostile terrain and recommending an effective attack plan.[[38]](#cite_note-enc-38) A first wave of Jedi were deployed by clones on shuttles to the planet. One group, consisting of Jedi Masters [Klefan Opus](/wiki/Klefan_Opus "Klefan Opus"), [Sember Vey](/wiki/Sember_Vey "Sember Vey"), [Bavoc Ansho](/wiki/Bavoc_Ansho "Bavoc Ansho"), Gobi, and [Theca](/wiki/Theca "Theca"), as well as Padawans [Iskat Akaris](/wiki/Thirteenth_Sister "Thirteenth Sister"), [Charlin](/wiki/Charlin_Plaka "Charlin Plaka"), [Onielle](/wiki/Onielle "Onielle"), Fvorn, Zeeth, and [Tualon](/wiki/Tualon_Yaluna "Tualon Yaluna") were ordered by Mace Windu to take out any nearby long-ranged weapons and cannons that could be used against the Jedi in the arena. Once the shuttle landed, Klefan led the group underground, guided by the light of their lightsabers.[[7]](#cite_note-Inquisitor:_Rise_of_the_Red_Blade-7)

As the team moved underground, a Geonosian caught the group by surprise and electrocuted Fvorn. Klefan stuck down the Geonosian, but was too late, with Fvorn becoming the first casualty. Zeeth cried out in pain, having been close friends with Fvorn, only for more Geonosians to ambush the group. Iskat’s instincts kicked in and she immediately began to cut the Geonosians down. As the Jedi became surrounded by Geonosians in the tunnel, they sprang into action. Soon after, Onielle became injured by a [sonic blaster](/wiki/Sonic_blaster "Sonic blaster"), causing the Jedi to retreat, except Zeeth, who still clung to his fallen friend. While Zeeth mourned, Klefan defended Zeeth but became overwhelmed, causing Iskat to return and help defend the grieving Zeeth. During combat, Iskat became enchanted and began to lose composure, to the point where Klefan interfered to stop her from cutting down the Geonosians who were already killed. The two took Zeeth away from his friend's body, and Klefan convinced Zeeth to return to the mission. They rejoined the rest of the group and moved around the arena, having split up to disarm the area.[[7]](#cite_note-Inquisitor:_Rise_of_the_Red_Blade-7)

Klefan comes across the situation and pulls Iskat away and they retreat from the arena and back to their much emptier shuttle.[[7]](#cite_note-Inquisitor:_Rise_of_the_Red_Blade-7)

Following the Grand Army's arrival, the [Neimoidians](/wiki/Neimoidian "Neimoidian") on Geonosis fled to their Federation [core ships](/wiki/Core_ship "Core ship"),[[32]](#cite_note-Complete_Vehicles-32) while Dooku and the other CIS leaders left to a [Geonosian Command Center](/wiki/Geonosian_War_Room "Geonosian War Room") to oversee the battle.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1) Jedi [Sidn-ee](/wiki/Sidn-ee "Sidn-ee") and [Till-ee](/wiki/Till-ee "Till-ee") were the first to reach the outside reaches of the [Trade Federation landing zones](/wiki/Trade_Federation_landing_zones "Trade Federation landing zones").[[37]](#cite_note-Battles_that_Changed_the_Galaxy-37) After the rescue of the Jedi, the droid and clone armies battled one another in an intricate series of confrontations on a number of different fronts. Much of the Separatists' heavy equipment had already been loaded onto their transports, but with their communications being jammed the rest was hastily thrown at the clones in order to buy the [Confederacy navy](/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems_Navy "Confederacy of Independent Systems Navy") enough [time](/wiki/Time "Time") to escape. Meanwhile, the Republic army's goal was to capture the Separatists leadership and as much of the Geonosian droid foundries intact as possible in order to uncover the depths of the Separatist conspiracy, precluding the indiscriminate use of [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship") [weapons](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)[[18]](#cite_note-SWCC2016-18)

The resulting battle would mostly consist of ground combat then, but, although the clone troopers were greatly outnumbered by battle droids, the Republic enjoyed air dominance thanks to their preemptive strikes on the launch hives.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)[[18]](#cite_note-SWCC2016-18) Nevertheless, at some point during the battle, the Geonosians did launch thousands of standby [*Nantex*-class territorial defense starfighters](/wiki/Nantex-class_territorial_defense_starfighter "Nantex-class territorial defense starfighter") to break the Republic's [orbital](/wiki/Orbit "Orbit") cordon, which was preventing Separatist ground reinforcements from the Corporate Alliance.[[32]](#cite_note-Complete_Vehicles-32) Corporate Alliance [NR-N99 *Persuader*-class droid enforcers](/wiki/NR-N99_Persuader-class_droid_enforcer "NR-N99 Persuader-class droid enforcer") did, nonetheless, see active use in the battle.[[30]](#cite_note-The_Separatists-30) Meanwhile, Obi-Wan, Anakin, and Senator Amidala took a [gunship](/wiki/Gunship "Gunship") to attack the lightly armored Techno Union transports while the gunship carrying Yoda and Mace Windu proceeded on to a nearby assembly area.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

One of their shots knocked Padmé and a clone trooper out of the gunship, but despite Anakin's protestations, they did not go back for her. Upon arriving at Dooku's hangar, Kenobi and Skywalker confronted the Count. As Kenobi began to strategize, Skywalker impatiently charged at the Count. Dooku and Kenobi then began to circle one another, and Dooku remarked that his powers were far beyond those of his opponent. Ordering Kenobi to back down, he unleashed a blast of [Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") lightning at the Jedi Knight, but Kenobi simply used his lightsaber to absorb the incoming energy and calmly rebuffed Dooku's demand. Dooku then ignited his [own lightsaber](/wiki/Dooku%27s_lightsaber "Dooku's lightsaber") and awaited Kenobi.

Kenobi initiated the [lightsaber duel](/wiki/Lightsaber_duel "Lightsaber duel") by aggressively charging the Count, but Dooku deflected Kenobi's opening attack and his subsequent jab. After a quick flurry of bladework, Kenobi sliced at Dooku's [legs](/wiki/Limb "Limb"), but the Count leapt over the strike and flourished his blade to drive Kenobi back as he landed. As Kenobi retreated, Dooku remarked upon his disappointment, noting that he had expected a better performance from someone who was held in such high esteem by Yoda.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

#### Yoda vs. Dooku[]

:   "*Fought well you have, my old Padawan.*"  
    "*This is just the beginning.*"

As Dooku caught his breath, Yoda entered the hangar and confronted his former student. Dooku asserted that his diminutive former master would not interfere with his affairs again, and immediately attempted to kill Yoda by [telekinetically](/wiki/Telekinesis "Telekinesis") flinging a pair of wall fixtures at him. Yoda repelled the fixtures with his own powers but launched no counteroffensive. Dooku then used the Force to break apart a small portion of the hangar ceiling, which he dropped onto Yoda. Again however, Yoda was able to toss the improvised [missiles](/wiki/Missile "Missile") aside. Yoda noted that Dooku had become powerful but lamented that he could sense the [dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") in his former student. Recognizing the futility of continuing his Force-based assault, Dooku challenged Yoda to [lightsaber combat](/wiki/Lightsaber_combat "Lightsaber combat") and ignited his blade. A brief but very intense duel then ensued in which Yoda was able to gain a slight but visible advantage over the Count. Recognizing that he could not defeat Yoda, and forced to act due to incoming Republic reinforcements, Dooku diverted Yoda's focus by using the Force to collapse one of the hangar's power columns onto Kenobi and Skywalker. Yoda broke off the duel in order to telekinetically catch the power column, preventing it from crushing the two Jedi. Seizing the opportunity, Dooku quickly boarded [his ship](/wiki/Count_Dooku%27s_solar_sailer "Count Dooku's solar sailer") and fled.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

Count Dooku, who was secretly a Sith Lord called [Darth](/wiki/Darth "Darth") Tyranus, traveled to Coruscant, to convene with his [master](/wiki/Sith_Master "Sith Master") Darth Sidious, who was settled in a [deserted building](/wiki/Inquisitorius_Headquarters "Inquisitorius Headquarters") in [The Works](/wiki/The_Works "The Works"). Pleased by the news of war, Sidious commanded his apprentice to arrange a second battlefront against the Republic. Meanwhile, the Grand Army of the Republic was assembled throughout [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") as the Clone Wars began.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1) 1,000 more [*Acclamator I*-class Assault Ships](/wiki/Acclamator_I-class_Assault_Ship "Acclamator I-class Assault Ship") were ordered following the battle.[[44]](#cite_note-Encyclopedia_RS-44) Jedi injured in the battle were treated at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, with Jedi Master [Depa Billaba](/wiki/Depa_Billaba "Depa Billaba") later thanking Padawan [Barriss Offee](/wiki/Barriss_Offee "Barriss Offee")'s special attention to the injured as the reason for their quick recovery. Offee tried to avoid the war for as long as possible, continuing to work as a healer at the Temple.[[3]](#cite_note-Queen's_Hope-3) However, Offee would eventually return to the battlefield and even return to the planet where the conflict began when war returned to Geonosis.[[45]](#cite_note-45)

At the Jedi Temple, Kenobi and Windu both declared their shared belief that the battle would not have been won without the clones. However, Master Yoda was quick to point out the irony in such a pyrrhic victory, as the Republic had only fought their first of many such battles, in what became known as the Clone Wars. The Council also agreed to investigate the Sith Lord that Dooku had mentioned to Obi-Wan Kenobi during the latter's capture, Darth Sidious, in case Dooku was telling the truth. It would not be until three [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") later, nearing the end of the Clone Wars, that they would finally identify Sidious as being none other than Supreme Chancellor Palpatine. Skywalker, meanwhile, accompanied by Senator Amidala, headed back to Naboo, where they [married](/wiki/Wedding_of_Anakin_Skywalker_and_Padm%C3%A9_Amidala "Wedding of Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala") in a secret ceremony.[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

With the Clone Wars being declared, members of the Jedi Order became [Generals](/wiki/General "General") and [Commanders](/wiki/Jedi_Commander "Jedi Commander") in the Grand Army of the Republic.[[46]](#cite_note-The_Clone_Wars_(film)-46) However, the loss of so many Jedi greatly depleted their ranks, with Iskat noting how much emptier the temple felt following the battle. To reinforce their ranks, many Padawans had their trials skipped and were rushed into knighthood, including Iskat, Anakin, Onielle, Zeeth, Tualon, and Charlin.[[7]](#cite_note-Inquisitor:_Rise_of_the_Red_Blade-7) Clone trooper "[Slick](/wiki/Slick "Slick")" participated in the Battle of Geonosis, but he came to the conclusion that the Jedi were not competent [commanders](/wiki/Commander "Commander"). Slick began to feel anger at the Jedi Order and felt that their defeat would end the war faster, and that this would free his fellow clone troopers from the service that he saw as [slavery](/wiki/Slavery "Slavery").[[22]](#cite_note-Slick_Front_Lines-22)

When Republic [engineers](/wiki/Engineer "Engineer") announced that they were unable to reconfigure Geonosian [factories](/wiki/Factory "Factory") for Republic needs, its strategic value fell.[[2]](#cite_note-Front_Lines-2) This eventually led to the [Republic occupation force](/wiki/Geonosis_garrison "Geonosis garrison") stationed on the planet being overrun by Separatist and Geonosian forces, ending the [occupation of Geonosis](/wiki/Occupation_of_Geonosis "Occupation of Geonosis") and leading to the [Second Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Geonosis "Second Battle of Geonosis").[[47]](#cite_note-Landing_at_Point_Rain-47)

The First Battle of Geonosis was remembered during the [Imperial Era](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era") by some as Jedi Master Mace Windu and a criminal gang interfering with legal executions, which sparked the Clone Wars.[[48]](#cite_note-Lost_Stars-48)

## Behind the scenes[]

### Climax of *Attack of the Clones*[]

The First Battle of Geonosis first appeared in the [2002](/wiki/2002 "2002") film [*Star Wars*: Episode II *Attack of the Clones*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_II_Attack_of_the_Clones "Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones"), the second installment of the *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* [prequel trilogy](/wiki/Prequel_trilogy "Prequel trilogy").[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1)

[Samuel L. Jackson](/wiki/Samuel_L._Jackson "Samuel L. Jackson"), the actor who played [Mace Windu](/wiki/Mace_Windu "Mace Windu"), requested to[[49]](#cite_note-Graham_Norton_Show-49) director[[1]](#cite_note-Episode_II-1) [George Lucas](/wiki/George_Lucas "George Lucas") that his character should have a purple lightsaber because he wanted to be able to find himself in the Geonosis battle scene in *Attack of the Clones*. He reasoned that, since Windu is the second most powerful Jedi after Yoda, Windu should have a lightsaber of a different color from the customary red, blue, and green.[[49]](#cite_note-Graham_Norton_Show-49)

### Yoda and Count Dooku's duel[]

:   "*Finally, I got the script, sat down, and my memory of the first draft was, 'In a fight that defies description, Yoda and Count Dooku battle.' That was it. That was just one line.*"
:   ―Rob Coleman[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50)

From the start of the creation of Yoda and Count Dooku's lightsaber duel, most of the work would fall to animation director [Rob Coleman](/wiki/Rob_Coleman "Rob Coleman")'s team. He didn't get the script until he had been in [Sydney](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sydney "wikipedia:Sydney"), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "wikipedia:Australia") for one or two [weeks](/wiki/Standard_week "Standard week"). The first draft described the fight as "'In a fight that defies description, Yoda and Count Dooku battle." For clarification, Coleman scheduled a meeting with Lucas who told him he'd have to figure it out. To do this, Coleman went to stunt coordinator [Nick Gillard](/wiki/Nick_Gillard "Nick Gillard") and they talked about how a combatant would fight with small [weapons](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon"), and they looked at real-world examples. Also, [Ahmed Best](/wiki/Ahmed_Best "Ahmed Best"), the actor who played Jar Jar, introduced Coleman to some [anime](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anime "wikipedia:Anime")[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50) and [martial arts](/wiki/Unarmed_combat "Unarmed combat")[[51]](#cite_note-Ahmed_Best_help-51) that he thought could be helpful. However, Coleman still wasn't sure how to depict Yoda's speed and style in a fight.[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50)

He attended a screening of the [1992](/wiki/1992 "1992") film [Swordsman II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swordsman_II "wikipedia:Swordsman II") and one of the scenes showed [ninjas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ninja "wikipedia:Ninja") leaping between [trees](/wiki/Tree "Tree") and bouncing around.[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50) Coleman studied the action in the film,[[51]](#cite_note-Ahmed_Best_help-51) and this inspired him to give Yoda the ability to jump around.[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50) While working on the duel, Coleman referred to [Glen McIntosh](/wiki/Glen_McIntosh "Glen McIntosh")'s dynamic drawings.[[51]](#cite_note-Ahmed_Best_help-51) In the duel, Lucas wanted a shot of Yoda pulling back his duster to reveal his lightsaber as a reference to the iconic shot of the [gunfighter at the O.K Corral](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gunfight_at_the_O.K._Corral "wikipedia:Gunfight at the O.K. Corral"). After finishing the duel, Lucas and Coleman felt happy with it. Despite Coleman's concerns, Lucas wanted Yoda to jump, leap, and flip during the duel. The duel is now considered to be a highlight of the prequel trilogy.[[50]](#cite_note-Clones_at_20-50)

### Discrepancies[]

The [2016](/wiki/2016 "2016") reference book [*Star Wars: Complete Locations*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Complete_Locations_(2016) "Star Wars: Complete Locations (2016)") establishes that the Republic was outnumbered by the Separatist Army by giving the number of [troops](/wiki/Troop "Troop") each side used.[[18]](#cite_note-SWCC2016-18) A later reference book, [2017](/wiki/2017 "2017")'s *[Star Wars: On the Front Lines](/wiki/Star_Wars:_On_the_Front_Lines "Star Wars: On the Front Lines")*, claimed that the Republic held the numerical advantage.[[2]](#cite_note-Front_Lines-2) "[Prelude to the Clone Wars and the First Battle of Geonosis](/wiki/Prologue_to_the_Clone_Wars_and_the_First_Battle_of_Geonosis "Prologue to the Clone Wars and the First Battle of Geonosis")" later affirmed the numbers given by[[16]](#cite_note-Prelude_to_the_Clone_Wars-16) *Complete Locations*.[[18]](#cite_note-SWCC2016-18) This article assumes *Complete Locations* and "Prelude to the Clone Wars and the First Battle of Geonosis" are correct.

In the [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022") *Visions* reference book *[The Art of Star Wars: Visions](/wiki/The_Art_of_Star_Wars:_Visions "The Art of Star Wars: Visions")* claims that Shad-Rah Varcanella was killed during the events of Order 66[[57]](#cite_note-Art_of_Visions-57) as opposed to the First Battle of Geonosis, as was envisioned by "The Village Bridge" crew.[[52]](#cite_note-Backstory-52) "The Lost Ones," however, went onto affirm the Battle of Geonosis background for F and Varcanella.[[54]](#cite_note-Lost_Ones-54)
