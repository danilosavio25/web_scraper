***Lando***, marketed as ***Star Wars: Lando***, is an upcoming film. It will focus on [Lando Calrissian](/wiki/Landonis_Balthazar_Calrissian "Landonis Balthazar Calrissian"). The story will be developed[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) by Lando actor [Donald Glover](/wiki/Donald_Glover "Donald Glover") and his brother Stephen Glover.[[2]](#cite_note-ATL-2) Announced on [December 10](/wiki/December_10 "December 10"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"), the project was originally planned to be a series on [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+")[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) before being pivoted to a movie format.[[4]](#cite_note-VarietyLando-4)

## Publisher's summary[]

*Everyone's favorite scoundrel Lando Calrissian will return in a brand-new event series for Disney+. Justin Simien, creator of the critically-acclaimed* Dear White People *and a huge* Star Wars *fan, is developing the story.*[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1)

## Development[]

On [December 10](/wiki/December_10 "December 10"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"), [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") announced that a *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* series centering on [Landonis Balthazar Calrissian](/wiki/Landonis_Balthazar_Calrissian "Landonis Balthazar Calrissian") was in development for [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+"), with [Justin Simien](/wiki/Justin_Simien "Justin Simien") writing and serving as showrunner.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) On [May 26](/wiki/May_26 "May 26"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"), during an interview with [*Cinemablend*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Future_plc#Brands "wikipedia:Future plc") at [Celebration Anaheim](/wiki/Celebration_Anaheim_(2022) "Celebration Anaheim (2022)"), [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy") said that there was no movement on the series, and they were waiting for [Donald Glover](/wiki/Donald_Glover "Donald Glover") to finish other projects first.[[5]](#cite_note-Cinemablend-5) On [July 18](/wiki/July_18 "July 18"), [2023](/wiki/2023 "2023"), *The Direct* published an interview with Simien, in which he revealed he had not received any updates about the series since 2020. He stated "I have no idea what's going on" with the series.[[6]](#cite_note-The_Direct-6)

On [July 27](/wiki/July_27 "July 27"), 2023, it was reported that Simien had exited the project due to his commitments with *[Haunted Mansion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haunted_Mansion_(2023_film) "wikipedia:Haunted Mansion (2023 film)")*, and that Donald Glover and his brother Stephen Glover would write and serve as showrunners for the series, with Glover reprising his role as Lando.[[2]](#cite_note-ATL-2) In an interview published earlier the same day, Simien had told *[The Hollywood Reporter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hollywood_Reporter "wikipedia:The Hollywood Reporter")* that he was "still attached, I think" to the series. He stated he had been told the higher ups "loved" what he wrote, but they had needed to halt production as they worked on availability. However, he did ponder if he had been quietly fired, asking "Am I too Black? Am I too queer? And people just don't want to say that?"[[7]](#cite_note-THRSimien-7)

On [September 14](/wiki/September_14 "September 14"), 2023, Lucasfilm confirmed that the project had officially shifted development into a film, rather than a television series. Stephen Glover had previously stated that its status as a film, rather than a series, was in discussion.[[4]](#cite_note-VarietyLando-4) On [February 7](/wiki/February_7 "February 7"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024"), Donald Glover stated that taking on *Lando* was not an "easy yes," and that he had done so due to liking the characters and his children's love for *Star Wars*. He also stated that he felt the degree of creative control that he had allowed for a more "singular" vision for the film, which he believed would make it more desirable for audiences.[[8]](#cite_note-THRGlover-8) On [August 2](/wiki/August_2 "August 2"), 2024, Simien reflected on his time with the project in an interview with [*Collider*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Collider_(website) "wikipedia:Collider (website)"), stating that the iteration involving him had been shelved; however, he also mentioned that he had not been directly informed of such a decision.[[9]](#cite_note-Grieving-9) On [September 12](/wiki/September_12 "September 12"), 2024, Donald Glover stated that he hopes that everyone can enjoy his film, saying that he wants it to be more fun and less serious than other *Star Wars* movies.[[10]](#cite_note-DG-10)
