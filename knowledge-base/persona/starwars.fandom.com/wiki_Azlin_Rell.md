**Azlin Rell** was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight") who served the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order") during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"). A naturally humble and self-effacing individual, Rell deeply trusted in [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") and cared about undertaking his duties as a [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi"). He was also a longtime friend of the [Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee") Jedi [Arkoff](/wiki/Arkoff "Arkoff"), who had once been his master. He was assigned on a service tour of the [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories") and the [frontier](/wiki/Galactic_Frontier "Galactic Frontier"), accompanied by his [astromech droid](/wiki/Astromech_droid "Astromech droid"), [C-9](/wiki/C-9 "C-9"). During the tour, Rell went on various missions, once visiting the [Relik moons](/wiki/Relik_moons "Relik moons").

In [382 BBY](/wiki/382_BBY "382 BBY"), Rell came to the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Tiikae](/wiki/Tiikae "Tiikae") to assist a trade outpost threatened by a large herd of migrating [grass sharks](/wiki/Grass_shark "Grass shark"). Finding the task too large for himself and C-9 to handle, he made a [distress call](/wiki/Distress_signal "Distress signal") which was answered by [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Zallah Macri](/wiki/Zallah_Macri "Zallah Macri") and her [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"), [Kevmo Zink](/wiki/Kevmo_Zink "Kevmo Zink"). Together, the group was able to successfully divert the [creatures](/wiki/Species "Species"). Afterward, Macri and Zink were sent on a [search](/wiki/Mission_to_Dalna_(Macri_and_Zink) "Mission to Dalna (Macri and Zink)") for a [stolen](/wiki/Theft_of_the_Rod_of_Seasons "Theft of the Rod of Seasons") [Force artifact](/wiki/Force_artifact "Force artifact") called the [Rod of Seasons](/wiki/Rod_of_Seasons "Rod of Seasons"), ending up going to investigate the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand") [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion") on the [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [Dalna](/wiki/Dalna "Dalna") as a result, while Rell wrote some reports and made some repairs, before planning to return to the [Jedi outpost](/wiki/Jedha_outpost "Jedha outpost") on the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Jedha](/wiki/Jedha "Jedha"). However, while en route, Rell was asked to search for the pair on [Dalna](/wiki/Dalna "Dalna") as the Jedi had lost contact with them. Rell's search led him to find only the Master and apprentice's mysteriously calcified [remains](/wiki/Death "Death") in the Path's [compound](/wiki/Path_compound "Path compound").

After reporting his discovery on [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"), Rell discovered while researching in the [Jedi Archives](/wiki/Jedi_Archives "Jedi Archives") that the Rod of Seasons was connected to the [Echo Stone](/wiki/Echo_Stone "Echo Stone"). Rell eventually joined a party of Jedi sent by and containing members of the [Jedi High Council](/wiki/Jedi_High_Council "Jedi High Council") to investigate the Path of the Open Hand on Dalna because of the bodies he found and in the aftermath of the [Battle of Jedha](/wiki/Battle_of_Jedha "Battle of Jedha"). However, the Path attacked them and many other Jedi there, sparking a devastating battle known as the [Night of Sorrow](/wiki/Night_of_Sorrow "Night of Sorrow"). During the battle, Rell encountered [Nameless](/wiki/Shrikarai "Shrikarai") creatures deployed by the Path against the Jedi, and was around them to such a degree that he was driven insane and plagued with [hallucinations](/wiki/Hallucination "Hallucination") as a result. Once the battle was over, Rell began creating a [journal](/wiki/Azlin%27s_journal "Azlin's journal") in a crazed frenzy that contained information about the creatures. He was eventually checked in to the [Gravan Monastery](/wiki/Gravan_Monastery "Gravan Monastery") sanatorium as a result, his journal eventually hidden away by his friend Arkoff. Rell then disappeared and abandoned the Jedi, falling to the dark side of the Force.

Rell reappeared a [century and a half later](/wiki/230_BBY "230 BBY") when Grand Master Yoda went in search of him shortly after the [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [destroyed](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon") [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon"), hoping that his many years of obsessive research on the Nameless could help the order combat the beasts.

## Biography[]

### Jedi Knight[]

Azlin Rell was a [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender") who was once the [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of the [Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee") Jedi [Arkoff](/wiki/Arkoff "Arkoff").[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) Rell achieved the rank of [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight")[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1) by [382 BBY](/wiki/382_BBY "382 BBY"),[[8]](#cite_note-Date-8) during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"). He was sent on a tour of duty to the [Outer Rim Territories](/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories "Outer Rim Territories"), accompanied by his [astromech droid](/wiki/Astromech_droid "Astromech droid"), [C-9](/wiki/C-9 "C-9"). Two [months](/wiki/Standard_month "Standard month") into the tour, Rell and C-9 visited one of the [Relik moons](/wiki/Relik_moons "Relik moons"), where the [droid](/wiki/Droid "Droid") found a strangely [charged alloy](/wiki/Charged_alloy "Charged alloy") which it used to fashion itself a new arm, which allowed it to detect [electromagnetic fields](/wiki/Electromagnetic_field "Electromagnetic field"). When Rell pointed out that this made C-9 more sensitive to people approaching than its master was at times, the droid cheekily suggested they call the arm its [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") [hat](/wiki/Hat "Hat"). Rell spent several [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") on the [frontier](/wiki/Galactic_Frontier "Galactic Frontier").[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

In 382 [BBY](/wiki/%27ABY-BBY%27_dating_system "'ABY-BBY' dating system"),[[8]](#cite_note-Date-8) Rell ventured to the planet [Tiikae](/wiki/Tiikae "Tiikae") to assist in deflecting a large herd of migrating [grass sharks](/wiki/Grass_shark "Grass shark") away from a trade outpost. Finding himself unable to complete the task on his own, he sent out a short-range [distress call](/wiki/Distress_signal "Distress signal") which was received by [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Zallah Macri](/wiki/Zallah_Macri "Zallah Macri") and her [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan"), [Kevmo Zink](/wiki/Kevmo_Zink "Kevmo Zink"), who were operating out of the [Port Haileap](/wiki/Port_Haileap "Port Haileap") [Jedi outpost](/wiki/Temple_Outpost_(Port_Haileap) "Temple Outpost (Port Haileap)") and had just completed a rescue mission on the [ocean moon of Amloch](/wiki/Ocean_moon_of_Amloch "Ocean moon of Amloch"). They had been due to return to [Haileap](/wiki/Haileap "Haileap"), but Macri had decided to stay on the [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") for one more [day](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"), allowing them to hear Rell's call and arrive in time to help the settlement.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

While Macri and Zink rode [skimboards](/wiki/Skimboard "Skimboard") and diverted the [sharks](/wiki/Shark "Shark") away from the outpost, Rell and C-9 worked on a way to encourage the [creatures](/wiki/Species "Species") to take a different route, while the outpost itself was evacuated. Rell and C-9 discovered that the sharks used Tiikae's [magnetic field](/wiki/Magnetic_field "Magnetic field") to choose their migration path, and since the last migration five [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year") before, the field had shifted enough that the sharks' new chosen migration path took them too close to the settlement. With his droid's assistance, Rell repeated the trick the two had performed on[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1) the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds") [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet")[[9]](#cite_note-USW_NE-9) [Alderaan](/wiki/Alderaan "Alderaan") to keep flocks of [night-lethers](/wiki/Night-lether "Night-lether") away from tall buildings, using a comm beacon to make an artificial signal which would lead the sharks down a different canyon, away from the outpost.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Zink and Macri spent some time rerouting the herd while Rell searched for a solution, and as sunset approached, the Padawan contacted Rell to ask how much longer the operation would take. Rell responded that he and C-9 needed just a few more [minutes](/wiki/Standard_minute "Standard minute") just as Zink's skimboard failed, causing the [Pantoran](/wiki/Pantoran "Pantoran") to leap away from the vehicle, leaving him on the ground near the herd. Macri moved to protect her apprentice by diverting the grass sharks, which shortly moved away of their own accord when Rell and C-9 activated their beacon to reroute them. Zink told Rell that whatever he had done was working, and Macri asked him to come pick up Zink while she tracked the herd to ensure the solution continued to work. Rell acknowledged that he was on his way.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell arrived in his shuttle to pick up Zink from the grassland, lowering the [ramp](/wiki/Entry_ramp "Entry ramp") for the Padawan to board. Zink made his way to the [cockpit](/wiki/Cockpit "Cockpit") to meet Rell in person for the first time, and Rell thanked him for his help. He informed Zink that Macri had messaged him that she would meet them back at the [spaceport](/wiki/Spaceport "Spaceport"), where Macri and Zink's [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship") was located. Sitting in the [copilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") chair, Zink expressed how impressed he was with how Rell had diverted the herd, asking how it had been done. When Rell explained, Zink briefly thought that the Jedi Knight had changed Tiikae's entire magnetic field. Rell, appearing slightly embarrassed, elaborated on how he and C-9 had reconfigured a comms beacon. Zink was impressed, and C-9 was proud, but Rell demurred that he had simply done his job. Zink insisted that Rell's feat had still been incredible, and Rell mentioned how he had used the trick on Alderaan. As Rell piloted his shuttle to the settlement, Zink asked him what the best thing to [eat](/wiki/Diet "Diet") there was.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell said he had been doing much the same as Zink had, and the Padawan took a moment to discern if Rell was teasing him before agreeing and raising his mug in a salute.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

### In search of the missing[]

:   "*Stay here. Be ready to send that emergency burst back to the ship if anything happens. I'll keep in touch with the comlink.*"
:   ―Azlin Rell instructs C-9[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Macri and Zink's [mission](/wiki/Mission_to_Dalna_(Macri_and_Zink) "Mission to Dalna (Macri and Zink)") took them to the planet [Dalna](/wiki/Dalna "Dalna") to investigate the [Path of the Open Hand](/wiki/Path_of_the_Open_Hand "Path of the Open Hand"), a [cult](/wiki/Religion "Religion") based there which they identified as likely suspects in the case. During the course of their investigation, the Jedi pair discovered that the cult were indeed responsible for the theft, and eventually snuck into the [Path's compound](/wiki/Path_compound "Path compound") to retrieve the stolen Rod of Seasons and other artifacts the cult had taken. However, they were confronted by the Path's leader, [the Mother](/wiki/Elecia_Zeveron "Elecia Zeveron"), who unleashed a bizarre [creature](/wiki/Shrikarai "Shrikarai") she named [the Leveler](/wiki/Great_Leveler "Great Leveler") on them, which overwhelmed and [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") them. In the aftermath, the Path of the Open Hand swiftly abandoned Dalna.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

 [![Xo Lahru](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bc/XoLahru-SWcom.png/revision/latest?cb=20250106042005) [![Xo Lahru](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bc/XoLahru-SWcom.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20250106042005)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/b/bc/XoLahru-SWcom.png/revision/latest?cb=20250106042005) 

Xo Lahru

 

When days had passed with no contact from the stricken master and apprentice, Rell was contacted by [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") [Xo Lahru](/wiki/Xo_Lahru "Xo Lahru") while he was en route to Jedha, the emergency message asking him to stop at Dalna to check in on them. Upon receiving the message, which mentioned the Path of the Open Hand, Rell refreshed himself on what he knew about the cult due to the extensive research he had performed during his time on the frontier. Aware that the Path preached non-interference with [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force"), Rell became concerned about the trouble Macri and especially Zink could have gotten into with them.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell found that there had been no contact from them in days, and [Jinx Pickwick](/wiki/Jinx_Pickwick "Jinx Pickwick"), Ferdan's [sheriff](/wiki/Sheriff "Sheriff"), had notified the outpost of the Jedi's abandoned [skiff](/wiki/Skiff "Skiff"), with some locals wanting to confiscate it. Breaking into the [starship](/wiki/Starship "Starship"), Rell and C-9 downloaded the information on its [computer](/wiki/Computer "Computer") onto a [datapad](/wiki/Datapad "Datapad"). When Rell spoke to Pickwick, although the sheriff was reluctant to involve herself with the situation, she informed him that the Path had departed Dalna two days before, in a [large starship](/wiki/Gaze_Electric "Gaze Electric") they had been constructing for months. She also told him about Zink's role in assisting members of the cult to survive during recent heavy [flooding](/wiki/Water "Water"). Rell then spoke with [Lady](/wiki/Lady "Lady") [Jara](/wiki/Jara "Jara"), the owner of the [boarding house](/wiki/Lady_Jara%27s_boarding_house "Lady Jara's boarding house") where Macri and Zink had stayed, who was quick to blame the Path for their disappearance.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

### The horror underground[]

:   "*Kevmo.*"
:   ―Azlin Rell makes a grim discovery[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Taking out his [glow rod](/wiki/Glowrod "Glowrod"), Rell ordered C-9 to stay there and be ready to send an emergency burst back to their ship if anything were to happen, promising to use his [comlink](/wiki/Comlink "Comlink") to keep in touch. Venturing into the caverns, Rell called out but received no reply. He reached out with his Force senses, but detected very little. Upon reaching an intersection at the bottom of the first staircase, Rell pushed out with the Force again and moved instinctively towards where he sensed an unusual sensation that felt almost like a yearning of sorts. Walking with his [eyes](/wiki/Eye "Eye") shut, Rell went on a twisting route through the tunnels until he nearly stumbled upon hitting a wall that he discovered was not physical, but something mirroring the Force back at him, reflecting his growing dread.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Examining the [large chamber](/wiki/Nesting_chamber "Nesting chamber") he found himself in, Rell saw an odd scatter of what looked like pulverized stone on the floor. Rell knelt down to examine the pile, but flung himself away upon touching what he immediately recognized as a [Jedi cloak](/wiki/Jedi_apparel "Jedi apparel"), not stopping until he reached the wall. Shining his light upon the remains, Rell was horrified to discover the pile was the remains of a Jedi whose body had been calcified and broken into pieces. Rell recognized that the remains were those of Kevmo Zink. Despite his horror, Rell steeled himself and walked past the remains, promising to return, and reached into the Force again, sensing the dread which permeated the room. Taking it step by step, Rell followed the strange Force thread while trying to remain confident, determined to locate Macri's remains while trying to ignore his memories of her and her Padawan.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell stopped, reaching out to connect with the [Living Force](/wiki/Living_Force "Living Force") to reassure himself, before pressing on. He found Macri's remains in [another chamber](/wiki/The_Mother%27s_listening_room "The Mother's listening room") with automatic lighting, sensing a hidden door beyond which powerful Force artifacts had formerly been stored. As he knelt beside Macri's remains, which consisted of her robes, [belt](/wiki/Belt "Belt"), [boots](/wiki/Boot "Boot") and utility items in addition to what was left of her body, which had become stone-like just like Zink's, Rell thought about how to collect them for funerary rites, knowing that both of the fallen pair's robes could be brought back to [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant"). He also realized that both of the lightsabers belonging to the fallen Jedi were missing. Rell was filled with dread over how Macri and Zink had been destroyed, identifying that he was afraid.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1) Through the Force, he felt an overwhelming feeling of fear clinging to their bodies.

Rell explained that he had turned to the dark side for the Jedi, and that they needed him more than he needed them. Yoda explained to the council that they would work to unlock the secrets of the Nameless that Rell learned throughout his life and were now lost within Rell's mind because of his madness, fear, and turn to the dark side.[[4]](#cite_note-Shadows_of_Starlight_1-4)

Azlin Rell was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [human](/wiki/Human "Human")[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1) [male](/wiki/Gender "Gender")[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) with [tan](/wiki/Color "Color") skin and short-cropped dark [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair"). Not a naturally fearful person, he trusted in the Force and accepted it as part of him, but he was also careful and cautious. He preferred the company of droids to other people. Rell avoided taking pride in his accomplishments, remaining humble and stating that he had simply been doing his job. Taking credit for being a hero embarrassed him, as he felt that he was simply doing what was required of him and all any Jedi could do. He enjoyed Kevmo Zink's company, finding him easy to laugh with. Rell was serious and did not mind doing reports, leading the enthusiastic Zink to be confused about whether or not the older Jedi was teasing him. He considered a solid research background to be essential to completing missions with satisfactory outcomes, a philosophy which saved him on several occasions.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell's first thought upon hearing of missing Jedi was that something terrible had happened to them, a guess which would prove to be correct. He had a gesture of brushing his fingers against his lightsaber to comfort himself, which he performed only when alone. Rell was deeply unsettled by the sensation of the aftermath of the Leveler's [birth](/wiki/Reproduction "Reproduction") and subsequent killing of Zink and Zallah Macri, which turned to horror when he discovered they were dead. He took pieces of the pair's robes to be returned to the Jedi for their funerals, and was grieved by their deaths, especially Zink's.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

After finding their bodies, Rell began to be more afraid and did not even want to continue investigating what had happened and instead pass off the job to someone else.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) He also became quite frustrated, but also curious, which lead him to investigate at the Jedi Archives on Coruscant.[[10]](#cite_note-Quest-10) He did not wish to do more than what was asked of him and advised Matthea Cathley to keep her head down when he heard her arguing for a bigger role in a mission. He also revealed to her that he was terrified to return to the caves where he had found Macri and Zink's bodies.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

After coming into contact with the Nameless, Rell was even more overcome by fear and did not want to continue fighting or trying to save lives. He was especially terrified by the prospect that the Force was not with him as he had not been able to feel it when the Nameless were nearby, and struggled with feeling powerless in general. He also started to ignore what the Jedi accepted as right and wrong and aggresively forced himself into Werth Plouth's mind without remorse. The encounter with the Nameless also caused him to continually mutter that they were coming back out of fear.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) When he descended into madness after being affected by the creatures three times,[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)[[11]](#cite_note-Precedent-11) he became obsessed with the Nameless and the song "Shrii ka rai ka rai" and desperately tried to make Cathley understand the danger the creatures posed.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2)

## Powers and abilities[]

:   "*We determined they use the magnetic field of the planet to choose their migration path, and the field has changed enough since the last migration that they shifted too close to the settlement.*"

Rell could sense the Force around him and align himself with it, in addition to using it to sense things around him. He sensed emotions tied to locations, and upon entering the Mother's former listening room on Dalna, could sense the vault where her stolen artifacts had once been kept. He knew how to pilot starships, and could calibrate communication beacons.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1) He had the power to reach into someone's mind with the Force, a power he used on Werth Plouth, which allowed him to see what the man was thinking.[[2]](#cite_note-Path_of_Vengeance-2) He possessed strong telekinetic abilities and he was once able to level a building with a [Force repulse](/wiki/Force_repulse "Force repulse") while suffering an hallucination.[[11]](#cite_note-Precedent-11) After his fall to the [Dark side of the Force](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") he displayed enough telekinetic power to [Force choke](/wiki/Force_choke "Force choke") a [Jedi Temple Guard](/wiki/Jedi_Temple_Guard "Jedi Temple Guard") and steal his lightsaber, he [took control](/wiki/Mind_trick "Mind trick") of a man's mind and made him crash his starship in a building and was able to [see through the Force](/wiki/Farsight "Farsight") to compensate for the loss of his eyes.[[4]](#cite_note-Shadows_of_Starlight_1-4) After confronting his fears, his powers greatly increased and he was able to detonate a Nameless through telekinesis.[[14]](#cite_note-Edge_of_Balance_3-14)

## Equipment[]

:   "*Thanks, Azlin! I'm on board!*"
:   ―Kevmo Zink, boarding Azlin Rell's shuttle[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)

Rell was typically accompanied by his personal astromech droid, C-9, and owned a comlink to communicate with it. On his tour of the Outer Rim, Rell travelled in a standard single shuttle. He wielded a [lightsaber](/wiki/Azlin%27s_lightsaber "Azlin's lightsaber") with a pale blue blade, which he carried in a [holster](/wiki/Holster "Holster"). Rell used a glow rod in dark areas.[[1]](#cite_note-POD-1)
