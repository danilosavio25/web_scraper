***Crimson Reign* 3** is the third issue of the [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") [comic](/wiki/Star_Wars_comic_books "Star Wars comic books") miniseries *[Star Wars: Crimson Reign](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Crimson_Reign "Star Wars: Crimson Reign")*. The issue was written by [Charles Soule](/wiki/Charles_Soule "Charles Soule"), illustrated by [Steven Cummings](/wiki/Steven_Cummings "Steven Cummings"), [Victor Olazaba](/wiki/Victor_Olazaba "Victor Olazaba"), and [Guru-eFX](/wiki/Guru-eFX "Guru-eFX"), and published by [Marvel Comics](/wiki/Marvel_Comics "Marvel Comics") on [March 9](/wiki/March_9 "March 9"), [2022](/wiki/2022 "2022"). The issue contains the story **Part 3: "The Archivist"**.

## Publisher's summary[]

*ODYSSEY OF THE ARCHIVIST! LADY QI'RA'S ultimate plan to reshape the galaxy continues as we focus on one of her most key operatives – the mysterious SAVA MADELIN SUN, former expert in the DARK SIDE OF THE FORCE, now known only as THE ARCHIVIST. Qi'ra has given this woman a crucial task and put all the resources of CRIMSON DAWN at her disposal. The Archivist's search will find answers to mysteries at the very heart of the EMPIRE'S formation…and the ultimate fall of THE JEDI ORDER.*
