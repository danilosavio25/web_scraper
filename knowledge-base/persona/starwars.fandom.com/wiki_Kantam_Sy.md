**Kantam Sy** was a [non-binary](/wiki/Gender "Gender") [human](/wiki/Human "Human") [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master") during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era") who was once the [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and later taught their own apprentice, [Lula Talisola](/wiki/Lula_Mrala-Talisola "Lula Mrala-Talisola"), whom they rescued on [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo") while on a walkabout from the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"). When the [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola") was brought to the [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon") after a [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [attack](/wiki/Attack_on_Trymant_IV "Attack on Trymant IV") on [Trymant IV](/wiki/Trymant_IV "Trymant IV"), Sy argued that Mrala should be allowed to stay despite being too old for [Jedi training](/wiki/Jedi_training "Jedi training") because she had befriended Talisola and the other apprentices. Later, Sy participated in a [skirmish](/wiki/Battle_in_the_Bright_Jewel_system "Battle in the Bright Jewel system") in the [Bright Jewel system](/wiki/Bright_Jewel_system "Bright Jewel system") against Nihil forces alongside [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda") and [Torban Buck](/wiki/Torban_Buck "Torban Buck").

## Biography[]

### Early life[]

:   "*Did you do that? Did you stop your friend from falling?* How *did you do that?*"  
    "*Is what* you *did. So I did. I did a bad?*"  
    "*No. No, you did a very, very good.*"  
    "*Then why are you crying?*"  
    "*Because I can finally listen again.*"
:   ―Kantam Sy and Lula Talisola's first meeting[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

Kantam Sy was a [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive"), [nonbinary](/wiki/Gender "Gender")[[2]](#cite_note-Art_of_THR-2) [human](/wiki/Human "Human") who lived during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"). A member of the [Jedi Order](/wiki/Jedi_Order "Jedi Order"), they were once the [Padawan](/wiki/Padawan "Padawan") of [Grand Master](/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") [Yoda](/wiki/Yoda "Yoda").[[1]](#cite_note-THREn-1) As a young adult, Sy achieved higher ranks in combat, [meditation](/wiki/Meditation "Meditation"), and other [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") abilities than many of their elders, but fellow Jedi speculated about why the skilled Padawan had not yet been [knighted](/wiki/Knighting_Ceremony "Knighting Ceremony") by Yoda. Sy was not in a hurry to progress; they knew it was not only about skill, but about something deeper that they and Yoda were waiting to happen.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

On [Endovar](/wiki/Endovar "Endovar"), Sy met [Aytar](/wiki/Aytar "Aytar"), an eighteen-[year](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year")-old male circus performer, whose arrival in Sy's life felt to them like a storm. The pair quickly developed a mutual attraction, and after knowing each other for four [days](/wiki/Standard_day "Standard day"), Sy realized that the unpredictability and complexities of life away from the [Jedi Temple](/wiki/Imperial_Palace "Imperial Palace") was something they needed to experience. They received Yoda's permission to go on a walkabout away from the Order.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

For a time, Sy and Aytar were lovers who lived and traveled together, both working in the [circus](/wiki/Circus "Circus"). When the couple eventually ended their relationship, it was on good terms. Sy let go of their attachment to Aytar and continued to travel. They worked in many jobs, including as a messenger, a mechanic, a [smuggler](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler"), a [waiter](/wiki/Waiter "Waiter"), and a [bodyguard](/wiki/Bodyguard "Bodyguard"). One of those jobs was as a person-to-person courier between diplomats on [Alderaan](/wiki/Alderaan "Alderaan") and [Naboo](/wiki/Naboo "Naboo"), requiring Sy to travel back and forth many times.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

One day on Naboo, Sy felt a stirring through the [the Force](/wiki/The_Force "The Force") and saw a [kolvor](/wiki/Kolvor "Kolvor"), a large [bird](/wiki/Bird "Bird") known among mountain villagers as *vangartraks* (vanishers) for their ability to carry away children. The kolvor snatched up a boy and a girl who were playing in a park under the supervision of an orphanage matron. Sy quickly made a decision that saving the children was more important than delivering the message. The former Jedi pursued the kolvor halfway up a mountain before the bird dropped both children. Using the Force, Sy extended a hand and successfully slowed the fall of the girl—[Lula Talisola](/wiki/Lula_Mrala-Talisola "Lula Mrala-Talisola")—but the boy disappeared into the trees. As Sy rescued Talisola, they realized she had imitated Sy's actions, stretching her hand out to save the boy from falling.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

After more than a year away from the Jedi, meeting Talisola affirmed to Sy that their place in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy") was among the Order. The little girl and the wayward Jedi found Yoda and assisted him with the [happabore](/wiki/Happabore "Happabore") [Monsalmo](/wiki/Monsalmo "Monsalmo") as she [gave birth](/wiki/Reproduction "Reproduction") to her litter of pups. Yoda was pleased and pronounced that Sy had left as a Padawan and returned as a [Jedi Knight](/wiki/Jedi_Knight "Jedi Knight"). Sy was formally granted the rank in a modest [Knighting Ceremony](/wiki/Knighting_Ceremony "Knighting Ceremony") and became Talisola's Jedi Master.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

### As a Jedi Master[]

#### Trymant IV battle[]

 [![Sy and several other Jedi Masters met with Mrala and Talisola](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/91/MastersMeetZeen-2021THRA3.png/revision/latest?cb=20250208164415) [![Sy and several other Jedi Masters met with Mrala and Talisola](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/91/MastersMeetZeen-2021THRA3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20250208164415)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/9/91/MastersMeetZeen-2021THRA3.png/revision/latest?cb=20250208164415) 

Sy and several other Jedi Masters met with Mrala and Talisola

 

Sy eventually attained the rank of [Jedi Master](/wiki/Jedi_Master "Jedi Master"). During a [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") [attack](/wiki/Attack_on_Trymant_IV "Attack on Trymant IV") on [Trymant IV](/wiki/Trymant_IV "Trymant IV"), Talisola and her friends met and befriended the [Force-sensitive](/wiki/Force-sensitive "Force-sensitive") [Mikkian](/wiki/Mikkian "Mikkian") [Zeen Mrala](/wiki/Zeen_Mrala-Talisola "Zeen Mrala-Talisola"), who was invited to come to the [Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Starlight_Beacon "Starlight Beacon") in the aftermath. One [week](/wiki/Standard_week "Standard week") after Mrala's arrival, Sy, along with Masters Yoda, [Torban Buck](/wiki/Torban_Buck "Torban Buck"), [Avar Kriss](/wiki/Avar_Kriss "Avar Kriss"), and [Estala Maru](/wiki/Estala_Maru "Estala Maru"), came to see her to decide whether she would stay at the Beacon. During the Masters' discussion, in response to Maru noting that Mrala was too old to begin training, Sy countered that she had gotten along well with Talisola and the other Padawans, and Yoda noted that Sy had made a good point. The apprentices, including Talisola and [Farzala Tarabal](/wiki/Farzala_Tarabal "Farzala Tarabal"), pointed out that Mrala's opinion, as well as theirs, should be taken into account.[[3]](#cite_note-HRA_3-3)

When the Padawans and [younglings](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") regaled the Masters with the tale of the events on Trymant IV, Sy watched along with the other Masters. Eventually, it was agreed that Mrala could stay at the Beacon for a time, as the status of Trymant IV was still unknown and she could help them locate the Nihil since she knew [Krix Kamerat](/wiki/Krix_Kamerat "Krix Kamerat") and [Tromak](/wiki/Tromak "Tromak"), who had both been taken by the pirates. After Yoda revealed that he had given Mrala and Kamerat [holoprojectors](/wiki/Holoprojector "Holoprojector") which allowed them to communicate, during the ensuing discussion, Sy noted that it was imperative the Jedi find out about the [strange object](/wiki/Nameless_control_rods "Nameless control rods") Yoda had encountered on the [pirates](/wiki/Pirate "Pirate")' [Spider Cruiser](/wiki/Spider_Cruiser "Spider Cruiser"). Mrala, however, was uncertain about whether she would help stop the Nihil.[[3]](#cite_note-HRA_3-3)

#### Yoda's departure[]

Sy later travelled to the [Republic Outpost](/wiki/Republic_Outpost "Republic Outpost") space station in the [Bright Jewel system](/wiki/Bright_Jewel_system "Bright Jewel system"), along with Yoda, Buck, Talisola, Tarabal, Qort, and Mrala. While Talisola kept Mrala company, the three Masters worked with the Republic personnel at the station to watch for Nihil activity. Eventually, the Masters headed out in their [Jedi Vectors](/wiki/Jedi_Vector "Jedi Vector"), accompanied by a force of [Z-28 Skywings](/wiki/Z-28_Skywing "Z-28 Skywing"), after a credible report was received about the Nihil. Talisola was [meditating](/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") with Mrala when she heard the news, and finding out from Tarabal what was going on, was disappointed to be left behind. However, Sy insisted over the comms that there was no good reason for Talisola and her friends to endanger themselves by going along. The ensuing [battle](/wiki/Battle_in_the_Bright_Jewel_system "Battle in the Bright Jewel system") was swift and decisive, with the Nihil forces easily being routed. However, Yoda was suspicious, believing that the victory had been too easy. Sy picked up a [distress signal](/wiki/Distress_signal "Distress signal") in their [starfighter](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") which the Masters quickly determined was coming from the junk [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") [Quantxi](/wiki/Quantxi "Quantxi"), and the Masters set off in that direction to investigate.[[6]](#cite_note-HRA_4-6) Yoda would go missing afterward as he embarked on an effort to learn about the threats facing the Jedi.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

#### Mission to Corellia[]

In [230 BBY](/wiki/230_BBY "230 BBY"),[[7]](#cite_note-Timelines-7) Sy joined a mission to the planet [Corellia](/wiki/Corellia "Corellia"), where they told fellow Jedi Master [Cohmac Vitus](/wiki/Cohmac_Vitus "Cohmac Vitus") about their old decision to spend time away from the Order. During the [Battle of the Corellian Shipyards](/wiki/Battle_of_the_Corellian_Shipyards "Battle of the Corellian Shipyards"), Sy attempted to hold up a trawler but struggled until Yoda revealed himself, making his return to the Jedi and locking eyes with Sy, who was brought to tears by the return of their old master. Afterward, Sy helped in [Reath Silas](/wiki/Reath_Silas "Reath Silas")'s [Knighting Ceremony](/wiki/Knighting_Ceremony "Knighting Ceremony") but was surprised by Vitus's decision to then leave the Jedi, which Sy feared was inspired by their story. Vitus, however, revealed he was not simply taking a break but leaving the Order forever and then departed.[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

## Personality and traits[]

:   "*I'm ahead of everyone else in my class. I'm higher ranked in combat and meditation and basically everything else than most of my elders. Even Master Yoda has said I'm the most disciplined Padawan he's ever trained and one of the most connected to the Force.*"
:   ―Kantam Sy, as a Padawan[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4)

Kantam Sy was a human with tan skin and [black](/wiki/Color "Color") [hair](/wiki/Hair "Hair").[[3]](#cite_note-HRA_3-3) Before becoming a Jedi Knight, their hair was short;[[4]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-4) as a Jedi Master, their hair was long and worn in a topknot. Sy was welcoming to Zeen Mrala, believing that she deserved a chance to stay with the Jedi as she got on well with the apprentices, including Talisola.[[3]](#cite_note-HRA_3-3)

## Behind the scenes[]

Kantam Sy was first mentioned in the second episode of *[Star Wars: The High Republic Show](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic_Show "Star Wars: The High Republic Show")*, in a [flowchart](/wiki/File:THRCharacterChart-THRShow.png "File:THRCharacterChart-THRShow.png") which depicted relationships between various [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") of the era.[[9]](#cite_note-THR_Show_2-9) Sy first appeared in the [third issue](/wiki/The_High_Republic_Adventures_(2021)_3 "The High Republic Adventures (2021) 3") of [*Star Wars: The High Republic Adventures*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_High_Republic_Adventures_(2021) "Star Wars: The High Republic Adventures (2021)").[[3]](#cite_note-HRA_3-3)

During development, Kantam Sy was initially named Kantam Bo. Sy's look was inspired by Japanese culture, with early concepts by [Harvey Tolibao](/wiki/Harvey_Tolibao "Harvey Tolibao") depicting the Jedi with a samurai-like bun and sandaled feet. The character of Kantam Sy holds special meaning for Tolibao, whose grandmother was Japanese and married his Filipino grandfather.[[2]](#cite_note-Art_of_THR-2)
