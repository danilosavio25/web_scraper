:   *This is a general resource page for the Wookieepedia project. There is also an article about it at [Wookieepedia](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia").*

**Wookieepedia** is a volunteer-maintained wiki community that aims to comprehensively and accurately document official *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* media, creators, and their stories, including both the [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") and current [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") continuities. The Wookieepedia community strives to create a friendly and welcoming environment for all readers and contributors and includes the [Discussions](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discussions "Wookieepedia:Discussions") forum and official [Discord server](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord").

Since our founding in [2005](/wiki/2005 "2005"), the wiki has been hosted by the [Fandom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fandom_(website) "wikipedia:Fandom (website)") company on its Fandom platform. Our policies are decided by [community consensus](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus") and enforced by elected [administrators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators"). The text of the wiki is licensed under the [Creative Commons Attribution-Share Alike License 3.0 (Unported) (CC BY-SA)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0), and our [copyright page](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Copyrights "Wookieepedia:Copyrights") contains more information about copyright on Wookieepedia.

Wookieepedia's name is a parody of "[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia "wikipedia:Wikipedia")," referencing the word "[Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee")"—the *Star Wars* character [Chewbacca](/wiki/Chewbacca "Chewbacca")'s [species](/wiki/Species "Species")—but Wookieepedia is neither affiliated with Wikipedia nor the [Wikimedia Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_Foundation "wikipedia:Wikimedia Foundation"), which hosts Wikipedia and the [MediaWiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaWiki "wikipedia:MediaWiki") software used by Wookieepedia. Wookieepedia is likewise not affiliated in any way with [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm"), [The Walt Disney Company](/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company "The Walt Disney Company"), or any of their affiliates or subsidiaries.

As a free-to-edit online encyclopedia maintained by a volunteer community of *Star Wars* fans, the Wookieepedia project is an ever-evolving repository of information that may be incomplete and inaccurate. Wookieepedia is built on collaboration and [community consensus](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Consensus "Wookieepedia:Consensus") to establish article standards and other site policies, such as those on [user conduct](/wiki/Wookieepedia:User_page_and_profile_policy "Wookieepedia:User page and profile policy"). All users are expected to follow Wookieepedia policies, which are enforced by elected [administrators](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Administrators "Wookieepedia:Administrators"), and comply with Fandom policies, such as the [Fandom Terms of Use](https://www.fandom.com/terms-of-use).

Discussions on site matters are held on relevant [talk pages](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Talk_page_policy "Wookieepedia:Talk page policy") and the [Senate Hall](/wiki/Forum:Senate_Hall "Forum:Senate Hall") forum, while official votes that [update](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Policy_and_consensus_updates "Wookieepedia:Policy and consensus updates") policies occur via the [Consensus track](/wiki/Forum:Consensus_track "Forum:Consensus track").
