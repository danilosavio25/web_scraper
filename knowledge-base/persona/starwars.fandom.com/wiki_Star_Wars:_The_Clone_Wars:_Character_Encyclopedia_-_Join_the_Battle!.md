## Publisher's summary[]

***Meet more than 350 incredible clones and droids from* Star Wars: The Clone Wars*.***

*What is Clone Force 99? Who inducted Ahsoka Tano into the Jedi Order? Where does General Grievous keep a secret lair? Why does Captain Rex mutiny against his Jedi General on Umbara?*

*Have to know the answers to these questions? Look no further than* Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Character Encyclopedia - Join the Battle! *Written by* Star Wars *expert Jason Fry, this illustrated encyclopedia features more than 350 incredible characters and teams, including Anakin Skywalker, Maul, Bo-Katan Kryze, and Clone Force 99.*

*Perfect for fans of all ages,* Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Character Encyclopedia - Join the Battle! *will enthrall readers for hours on end.*

## Continuity[]

* [Rex](/wiki/Rex "Rex")'s number of CT-7567 is mistakenly identified as CC-7567. He is additionally referred to as a [Clone Captain](/wiki/Clone_Captain "Clone Captain") during the events of the [Siege of Mandalore](/wiki/Siege_of_Mandalore "Siege of Mandalore") and during the [skirmish](/wiki/Skirmish_aboard_the_Tribunal "Skirmish aboard the Tribunal") aboard the *[Tribunal](/wiki/Tribunal "Tribunal")* before his [inhibitor chip](/wiki/Behavioral_modification_biochip "Behavioral modification biochip") was removed, when *[Star Wars: The Clone Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars "Star Wars: The Clone Wars")* established he was a [Clone Commander](/wiki/Clone_Commander "Clone Commander").
* [Ponds](/wiki/Ponds "Ponds")'s [DC-15A blaster rifle](/wiki/DC-15A_blaster_rifle "DC-15A blaster rifle") is incorrectly labeled as a "DCA-15 rifle."
* [R2-D2's battle droid squadron](/wiki/R2-D2%27s_battle_droid_squadron "R2-D2's battle droid squadron") is mistakenly described as being made up of only two [B1-series battle droids](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid") instead of three.
* [Ziro Desilijic Tiure](/wiki/Ziro_Desilijic_Tiure "Ziro Desilijic Tiure")'s homeworld is incorrectly stated to be [Coruscant](/wiki/Coruscant "Coruscant") instead of [Sleheyron](/wiki/Sleheyron "Sleheyron").
* [Lott Dod](/wiki/Lott_Dod "Lott Dod")'s homeworld is incorrectly stated to be [Cato Neimoidia](/wiki/Cato_Neimoidia "Cato Neimoidia") instead of [Neimoidia](/wiki/Neimoidia "Neimoidia").
* [Shahan Alama](/wiki/Shahan_Alama "Shahan Alama") is incorrectly stated to be [female](/wiki/Gender "Gender").
* [Pantora](/wiki/Pantora "Pantora") is mistakenly referred to as a [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") instead of a [moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon") in [Chi Cho](/wiki/Chi_Cho "Chi Cho")'s entry.
* The standard [B1-series battle droid](/wiki/B1-series_battle_droid "B1-series battle droid") is incorrectly described as 1.91 m (6 ft, 3 in) tall when all of its variants are given the correct height of 1.93 m (6 ft, 4 in).
* On Page 89, [Maul](/wiki/Maul "Maul") is shown with the legs that [Death Watch](/wiki/Death_Watch "Death Watch") created for him, but a caption states that this set of cybernetic legs was created by [Mother Talzin](/wiki/Talzin "Talzin").
* In the description of [Krismo Sodi](/wiki/Krismo_Sodi "Krismo Sodi"), [electro swords](/wiki/Electro_sword "Electro sword") are mistakenly stated to be capable of withstanding a lightsaber blade.
