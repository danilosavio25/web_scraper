**Corellia** was a [planet](/wiki/Planet "Planet") located in [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy")'s [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds") known for its ace [pilots](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot") and large [starships](/wiki/Starship "Starship"). The planet's vast [shipyards](/wiki/Shipyard "Shipyard") produced many vessels over hundreds of years, including [starfighters](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") and [Star Destroyers](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer") for the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire") as well as other famous models, such as the [YT-1300 light freighter](/wiki/YT-1300_light_freighter "YT-1300 light freighter").

It was the [homeworld](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") of [Han Solo](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo"), [Qi'ra](/wiki/Qi%27ra "Qi'ra"),[[5]](#cite_note-Most_Wanted-5) [Wedge Antilles](/wiki/Wedge_Antilles "Wedge Antilles"), [Crix Madine](/wiki/Crix_Madine "Crix Madine"), [BoShek](/wiki/BoShek "BoShek"),[[25]](#cite_note-Ultimate_SW-25) the [Besalisk](/wiki/Besalisk "Besalisk") [Gadren](/wiki/Gadren "Gadren"),[[21]](#cite_note-Twilight_Company-21) the [Executive Records Officer](/wiki/Executive_Records_Officer "Executive Records Officer") [Winshur Bratt](/wiki/Winshur_Bratt "Winshur Bratt"),[[20]](#cite_note-Resistance_Reborn-20) the [bounty hunters](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Dengar](/wiki/Dengar "Dengar")[[26]](#cite_note-Galactic_Defense-26) and [Mercurial Swift](/wiki/Mercurial_Swift "Mercurial Swift"),[[27]](#cite_note-Empire's_End-27) and the [Jedi younglings](/wiki/Jedi_Initiate "Jedi Initiate") [Kalifa](/wiki/Kalifa "Kalifa")[[28]](#cite_note-SWE-28) and [Petro](/wiki/Petro "Petro").[[29]](#cite_note-Episode_Gallery-29)

## Description[]

 [![Flag of Corellia](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f2/Corellia_Flag.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180916083502) [![Flag of Corellia](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f2/Corellia_Flag.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180916083502)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f2/Corellia_Flag.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180916083502) 

Flag of Corellia

 

Corellia was located in the [Corellian sector](/wiki/Corellian_sector "Corellian sector")[[2]](#cite_note-Ultimate_Star_Wars-2) in the [Core Worlds](/wiki/Core_Worlds "Core Worlds"). It had a temperate climate and was covered in [forests](/wiki/Forest "Forest"), [jungles](/wiki/Jungle "Jungle"), and urban centers.[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) The official [planetary](/wiki/Planet "Planet") [colors](/wiki/Color "Color") of Corellia were blue and gold.[[7]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-7) One major [city](/wiki/City "City") was [Coronet City](/wiki/Coronet_City "Coronet City") (also the planet's capital), which was home to [shipyards](/wiki/Santhe_Shipyards "Santhe Shipyards") that, during the [Imperial Era](/wiki/Imperial_Era "Imperial Era"), produced [TIE fighters](/wiki/TIE/ln_space_superiority_starfighter "TIE/ln space superiority starfighter") and [Star Destroyers](/wiki/Star_Destroyer "Star Destroyer") for the [Galactic Empire](/wiki/Galactic_Empire "Galactic Empire").[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10) The shipyards were one of Corellia's most prominent characteristics in addition to being known for producing capable [pilots](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot").[[30]](#cite_note-BioGal1-30) Corellia also manufactured the [YT-1300 light freighter](/wiki/YT-1300_light_freighter "YT-1300 light freighter"), including the *[Millennium Falcon](/wiki/Millennium_Falcon "Millennium Falcon")*.[[31]](#cite_note-MillenniumFalconSWE-31)

The planet's [human](/wiki/Human "Human") inhabitants were called [Corellians](/wiki/Corellian "Corellian")[[21]](#cite_note-Twilight_Company-21) and were known for their wanderlust.[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) Besides humans, Corellia was also inhabited by several [alien](/wiki/Alien "Alien") [species](/wiki/Species "Species") including [Barbadelans](/wiki/Barbadelan "Barbadelan"),[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10) [Besalisks](/wiki/Besalisk "Besalisk"),[[21]](#cite_note-Twilight_Company-21) [Czerialans](/wiki/Czerialan "Czerialan"), [Grindalids](/wiki/Grindalid "Grindalid"),[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10) [Kel Dor](/wiki/Kel_Dor "Kel Dor"), [Pa'lowicks](/wiki/Pa%27lowick "Pa'lowick"), [Rodians](/wiki/Rodian "Rodian"), [Sullustans](/wiki/Sullustan "Sullustan"),[[5]](#cite_note-Most_Wanted-5) [Twi'leks](/wiki/Twi%27lek "Twi'lek"), and [Ugors](/wiki/Ugor "Ugor").[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10) In addition, Corellia was also home to several native creatures including [Corellian hounds](/wiki/Corellian_hound "Corellian hound")[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) and [fleek eels](/wiki/Fleek_eel "Fleek eel").[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18) The [politics](/wiki/Politics "Politics") of Corellia were often dangerous and sometimes drew blood, with different politicians vying for new positions and engaging in shady deals.[[7]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-7)

## History[]

### Early history[]

:   "*There's so much history on Corellia! Too bad it's such an awful place.*"
:   ―[DK-RA-43](/wiki/DK-RA-43 "DK-RA-43") in the *[Galactic Explorer's Guide](/wiki/Galactic_Explorer%27s_Guide "Galactic Explorer's Guide")*[[8]](#cite_note-Explorer's_Guide-8)

An ancient Core World,[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) Corellia was the [homeworld](/wiki/Homeworld "Homeworld") of natives known for their wanderlust. In the time before they explored the [stars](/wiki/Star "Star"), Corellians embarked on [ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") voyages to satisfy that wanderlust,[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18) traversing the seas to discover new continents and catch vast quantities of [fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish").[[2]](#cite_note-Ultimate_Star_Wars-2) After the advent of space travel, Corellian royalty sponsored exploration and colonization efforts that helped expand galactic civilization by furthering the frontiers of the [Old Republic](/wiki/Galactic_Republic "Galactic Republic") in its youth.[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18) Corellian colonists settled on thousands of worlds, and its [hyperspace](/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace") [scouts](/wiki/Scout "Scout") paved many of [the galaxy](/wiki/The_galaxy "The galaxy")'s major [hyperspace routes](/wiki/Hyperspace_route "Hyperspace route"), making Corellia an important planet in the Galactic Republic.[[8]](#cite_note-Explorer's_Guide-8)

The planet became known through the [galaxy](/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") for the Corellian people's wanderlust and its massive [shipyards](/wiki/Shipyard "Shipyard").[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) Eventually, the ancient Corellian royalty, which included [Prince-Admiral](/wiki/Prince-Admiral "Prince-Admiral") [Jonashe Solo](/wiki/Jonashe_Solo "Jonashe Solo"), faded away and the throne stood vacant for many [years](/wiki/Standard_year "Standard year").[[4]](#cite_note-Tales_from_Vandor-4) In the [millennia](/wiki/Millennium "Millennium") leading up to the Imperial Era, Corellia's key role in the expansion of galactic civilization[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) decreased and power shifted to younger, more vibrant planets. The once influential world gained a reputation as a polluted planet with extensive poverty and crime.[[8]](#cite_note-Explorer's_Guide-8) With its importance in galactic affairs reduced, Corellia was nonetheless recognized for its historic significance.[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18)

### High Republic Era[]

:   "*Help us stop the Nihil. Help us save Corellia. If this planet falls to the Nihil, who knows what planet will be next, and you may have your gripes with the Republic, but that's nothing compared to the brutality and horror that life will become under the Nihil. Come to the shipyard! Come now! Fight for Corellia! Fight for the galaxy! Fight for light and life!*"
:   ―[Alys Ongwa](/wiki/Alys_Ongwa "Alys Ongwa")[[7]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-7)

The population came to show isolationist tendencies during the [High Republic Era](/wiki/High_Republic_Era "High Republic Era"), leading to the creation of the satirical song *[Corellia for Corellians](/wiki/Corellia_for_Corellians "Corellia for Corellians")*. The [Nihil](/wiki/Nihil "Nihil") launched an [infiltration](/wiki/Infiltration_on_Corellia "Infiltration on Corellia") of the Corellian government to help with the [destruction of Starlight Beacon](/wiki/Destruction_of_Starlight_Beacon "Destruction of Starlight Beacon"), but the infiltrators were defeated by a force of [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi").[[7]](#cite_note-Midnight_Horizon-7) Nevertheless, the infiltration set the stage for a [Corellian civil war](/wiki/War_on_Corellia "War on Corellia") in [229 BBY](/wiki/229_BBY "229 BBY").[[32]](#cite_note-Timelines-32) A [hostage situation in Coronet City](/wiki/Hostage_situation_in_Coronet_City "Hostage situation in Coronet City") dragged on for several weeks.[[33]](#cite_note-Adventures_3-33) The [massacre at the Greens](/wiki/Massacre_at_the_Greens "Massacre at the Greens") effectively wiped out the Nihil presence on Corellia.[[34]](#cite_note-Adventures_7-34)

### Twilight of the Republic[]

The [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Cad Bane](/wiki/Cad_Bane "Cad Bane") was involved in an incident on Corellia that he later said was humiliating.[[35]](#cite_note-Bane's_story-35) [Senator](/wiki/Senator "Senator") [Garm Bel Iblis](/wiki/Garm_Bel_Iblis "Garm Bel Iblis") represented Corellia in the [Galactic Senate](/wiki/Galactic_Senate "Galactic Senate") by the end of the [Clone Wars](/wiki/Clone_Wars "Clone Wars"), but he was one of several senators who grew opposed to [Supreme Chancellor](/wiki/Supreme_Chancellor "Supreme Chancellor") [Sheev Palpatine's](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") governance.[[36]](#cite_note-The_End_of_the_Clone_Wars-36) The obscure [Republic Group](/wiki/Republic_Group "Republic Group") allegedly had ties to holding companies on Corellia, as well as some on Coruscant.[[37]](#cite_note-YTManual-37)

 [![Coronet City in 13 BBY](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f8/Coronet_City.png/revision/latest?cb=20181001233856) [![Coronet City in 13 BBY](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f8/Coronet_City.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20181001233856)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/f8/Coronet_City.png/revision/latest?cb=20181001233856) 

Coronet City in 13 BBY

 

When the Galactic Empire came to power, Corellia's shipyards were transformed into Imperial facilities,[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18) and though Corellia was a faded industrial world, it was used to build [TIE](/wiki/TIE_series "TIE series") [fighters](/wiki/Starfighter "Starfighter") and Star Destroyers for the [Imperial Navy](/wiki/Imperial_Navy "Imperial Navy"). Many impoverished Corellian commoners sought to escape their homeworld for a better life.[[1]](#cite_note-Corellia-1) In the immediate aftermath of the Clone Wars, the [Barsha family](/wiki/Barsha_family "Barsha family") was set to take the lead in Imperial [destroyer](/wiki/Destroyer "Destroyer")-production on Corellia through its factories, with family heir [Jaylen Barsha](/wiki/Jaylen_Vrax "Jaylen Vrax") expecting them to become the richest family on Corellia thanks to these contracts and for thousands of destroyers to be built in their facilities. However, the family "mistake" [Sliro Barsha](/wiki/Sliro_Barsha "Sliro Barsha") spotted an opportunity to make a name for himself and accused his family of plotting against Palpatine, destroying the Barsha family's standing and leading to his own rise through the Imperial ranks. Additionally, Sliro dispatched the reprogrammed [BX-series droid commando](/wiki/BX-series_droid_commando "BX-series droid commando") [ND-5](/wiki/ND-5 "ND-5") to wipe out his fellow Barshas, although Jaylen would manage to subdue and twist the droid to his own ends.[[38]](#cite_note-Outlaws-38)

By [13 BBY](/wiki/13_BBY "13 BBY"),[[39]](#cite_note-Solo_ate-39) the [Corellian Security Forces](/wiki/Corellian_Security_Forces "Corellian Security Forces") including its [emigration officers](/wiki/Imperial_Emigration_Officer "Imperial Emigration Officer") had become an extension of the Imperial security forces on Corellia with [Patrol Troopers](/wiki/Patrol_trooper "Patrol trooper") assisting in policing matters.[[18]](#cite_note-The_Official_Guide-18) Several crime syndicates including [Lady](/wiki/Lady "Lady") [Proxima](/wiki/Proxima "Proxima")'s [White Worms](/wiki/White_Worms "White Worms") operated in the coastal Coronet City.[[40]](#cite_note-White_Worms-40) The White Worms used local street urchins called [scrumrats](/wiki/Scrumrat "Scrumrat") as vermin-catchers, pick-pockets, errand runners, and crime operatives.[[41]](#cite_note-Scrumrats-41) Two notable scrumrats were [Han](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo") and [Qi'ra](/wiki/Qi%27ra "Qi'ra") who sought to escape the White Worms and Corellia by going offworld.[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10) The [Unbroken Clan](/wiki/Unbroken_Clan "Unbroken Clan") had a [palace](/wiki/Unbroken_Clan_Palace "Unbroken Clan Palace") located on Corellia where the [Grand Leader](/wiki/Grand_Leader "Grand Leader") resided.[[15]](#cite_note-Bounty_Hunters_20-15)

After escaping the [Den of the White Worms](/wiki/Den_of_the_White_Worms "Den of the White Worms"), Han and Qi'ra tried to escape through [Coronet City Spaceport](/wiki/Coronet_City_Spaceport "Coronet City Spaceport") by bribing an [emigration officer](/wiki/Falthina_Sharest "Falthina Sharest"). However, Qi'ra was recaptured and sold to [Sarkin Enneb](/wiki/Sarkin_Enneb "Sarkin Enneb"). Han managed to travel offworld and join the [Carida Academy](/wiki/Carida_Academy "Carida Academy") as an [Imperial cadet](/wiki/Imperial_cadet "Imperial cadet") with the hopes of becoming a [pilot](/wiki/Pilot "Pilot"). Due to [his](/wiki/Gender "Gender") insubordination, however, Solo was reassigned to the [Imperial Army](/wiki/Imperial_Army "Imperial Army") and became a [Swamp trooper](/wiki/Swamp_trooper "Swamp trooper") on [Mimban](/wiki/Mimban "Mimban"). Meanwhile, Qi'ra became the chief [lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") to [Dryden Vos](/wiki/Dryden_Vos "Dryden Vos") in the [Crimson Dawn](/wiki/Crimson_Dawn "Crimson Dawn") crime syndicate.[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10)

Around [10 BBY](/wiki/10_BBY "10 BBY"), [Nakano Lash's crew](/wiki/Nakano_Lash%27s_crew "Nakano Lash's crew") undertook a [mission to Corellia](/wiki/Mission_to_Corellia_(Nakano_Lash%27s_crew) "Mission to Corellia (Nakano Lash's crew)"),[[32]](#cite_note-Timelines-32) as part of a war between the Unbroken Clan and the Mourner's Wail crime syndicates.[[42]](#cite_note-BH2-42) Also around ten years before the [Battle of Yavin](/wiki/Battle_of_Yavin "Battle of Yavin"),[[43]](#cite_note-Date_Tweet-43) an organization known as the [Corellian Resistance](/wiki/Corellian_Resistance "Corellian Resistance") operated on the planet, [fighting](/wiki/Corellian_insurgency "Corellian insurgency") against the Empire. One of their members, [Miru Nadrinakar](/wiki/Miru_Nadrinakar "Miru Nadrinakar"), attempted to warn them of an upcoming crackdown.[[44]](#cite_note-TeoH-44) [Smuggler](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler") [Sana Starros](/wiki/Sana_Starros "Sana Starros") made use of a [crash pad](/wiki/Sana_Starros%27_crash_pad "Sana Starros' crash pad") on Corellia that she stocked with supplies, including [tea](/wiki/Tea "Tea"),[[45]](#cite_note-Aphra_7-45) in [3 ABY](/wiki/3_ABY "3 ABY").[[46]](#cite_note-3_ABY_Date-46)

Around that same time, Jaylen Barsha—by that time going by the name Jaylen Vrax—embarked on a [campaign](/wiki/Jaylen_Vrax%27s_campaign "Jaylen Vrax's campaign") to finally unseat his brother Sliro, who by that time was a [director](/wiki/Director "Director") in the [Imperial Security Bureau](/wiki/Imperial_Security_Bureau "Imperial Security Bureau") and leader of the undercover crime syndicate [Zerek Besh](/wiki/Zerek_Besh "Zerek Besh"), as revenge for their family and himself losing the fortune their Corellian industrial might would have offered. In the end, Vrax succeeded and was granted control of Zerek Besh by the Empire, only for Vrax himself to be killed shortly after by the freed ND-5.[[38]](#cite_note-Outlaws-38)

### Rise of the New Republic[]

Following the [Battle of Endor](/wiki/Battle_of_Endor "Battle of Endor") in [4 ABY](/wiki/4_ABY "4 ABY"), the [bounty hunter](/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") [Dengar](/wiki/Dengar "Dengar") tried to offer [Mercurial Swift](/wiki/Mercurial_Swift "Mercurial Swift") the opportunity to work in a partnership. However, the younger [bounty](/wiki/Bounty "Bounty") hunter refused and the two men fought. Dengar warned Swift that the [New Republic](/wiki/New_Republic "New Republic"), the successor to the [Alliance to Restore the Republic](/wiki/Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic "Alliance to Restore the Republic"), would be imposing bounties on bounty hunters and that bounty hunters needed to form a union to work together. However, Swift ignored his advice.[[9]](#cite_note-Aftermath-9)

In [5 ABY](/wiki/5_ABY "5 ABY"), the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") cult [Acolytes of the Beyond](/wiki/Acolytes_of_the_Beyond "Acolytes of the Beyond") recruited a low-class Corellian [Pantoran](/wiki/Pantoran "Pantoran") girl named [Kiza](/wiki/Kiza "Kiza"). The Acolytes worshiped the [Sith](/wiki/Sith "Sith") and the dark side.[[27]](#cite_note-Empire's_End-27) That year, the Acolytes staged an [attack](/wiki/Coronet_City_revolution "Coronet City revolution") on Coronet City so that Kiza and a boy named [Oblivion](/wiki/Remi "Remi") could infiltrate the [Peace and Security Headquarters](/wiki/Peace_and_Security_Headquarters "Peace and Security Headquarters") and steal a [lightsaber](/wiki/Kiza%27s_lightsaber "Kiza's lightsaber") from the archives beneath the prison. In the process, Kiza [killed](/wiki/Death "Death") numerous [Peace and Security](/wiki/Peace_and_Security "Peace and Security") officers while Oblivion [severed](/wiki/Dismemberment "Dismemberment") [detective](/wiki/Detective "Detective") [Erno](/wiki/Erno "Erno")'s [blaster](/wiki/Blaster "Blaster") hand.[[47]](#cite_note-Life_Debt-47)

In the [months](/wiki/Standard_month "Standard month") leading up to the [Battle of Jakku](/wiki/Battle_of_Jakku "Battle of Jakku"), there was closer cooperation between the New Republic and Corellian [law enforcement](/wiki/Law_enforcement_agency "Law enforcement agency"). Coronet City Peace and Security began linking its records to the growing New Republic database. This allowed [Norra Wexley](/wiki/Norra_Wexley "Norra Wexley") and her [team](/wiki/Norra_Wexley%27s_team "Norra Wexley's team") to [track down](/wiki/Capture_of_Mercurial_Swift "Capture of Mercurial Swift") the bounty hunter Swift.[[27]](#cite_note-Empire's_End-27)

In [9 ABY](/wiki/9_ABY "9 ABY"), the [Santhe Shipyards](/wiki/Santhe_Shipyards "Santhe Shipyards") were visited by [Ahsoka Tano](/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano "Ahsoka Tano") and General [Hera Syndulla](/wiki/Hera_Syndulla "Hera Syndulla") while investigating [Imperial Remnant](/wiki/Imperial_Remnant "Imperial Remnant") activities, directed there by an [HK-87 assassin droid](/wiki/HK-87_assassin_droid "HK-87 assassin droid"). On Corellia, the two met with [Regional Supervisor](/wiki/Regional_Supervisor "Regional Supervisor") [Myn Weaver](/wiki/Myn_Weaver "Myn Weaver"), who showed them the facilities of his New Republic shipyard, [Facility 301G](/wiki/Facility_301G "Facility 301G"), where Imperial naval assets are being refitted for the [New Republic Defense Fleet](/wiki/New_Republic_Defense_Fleet "New Republic Defense Fleet"). Inside the shipyard's command center, Tano and Syndulla were attacked by former-Imperial workers while they questioned Weaver's actions.[[14]](#cite_note-Part_Two-14)

Tano broke out of the command center's window, descending to the factory floor, where she was challenged by the former [Inquisitor](/wiki/Inquisitor "Inquisitor") [Marrok](/wiki/Marrok "Marrok"), and the two dueled. Meanwhile, General Syndulla returned to the [Phantom II](/wiki/Phantom_II "Phantom II") and chased a [Super Star Destroyer](/wiki/Super_Star_Destroyer "Super Star Destroyer") [hyperdrive](/wiki/Hyperdrive "Hyperdrive") being ferried into orbit. The confrontations ended with the hyperdrive escaping, and Marrok departing, while [New Republic soldiers](/wiki/New_Republic_soldier "New Republic soldier") arrested several shipyard workers, including Weaver.[[14]](#cite_note-Part_Two-14)

The [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") [took over](/wiki/Conquest_of_Corellia "Conquest of Corellia") Corellia to claim its shipyards.[[48]](#cite_note-Legacy_of_Vader-48) Afterwards, they established a presence on Corellia, beginning a [planetary occupation](/wiki/Occupation_of_Corellia "Occupation of Corellia") and nationalizing the Corellian Engineering Corporation. Soon after the [Battle of Crait](/wiki/Battle_of_Crait "Battle of Crait"), the [Resistance](/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") launched a [mission to Corellia](/wiki/Mission_to_Corellia "Mission to Corellia") to free Resistance sympathizers from the First Order. [Poe Dameron](/wiki/Poe_Dameron "Poe Dameron") and [Finn](/wiki/Finn "Finn") went undercover to the birthday party of [Nifera Shu](/wiki/Nifera_Shu "Nifera Shu") to steal a list of Resistance members, but [stormtroopers](/wiki/First_Order_stormtrooper "First Order stormtrooper") raided the party, forcing the Resistance to flee. As they escaped the planet, they were able to free [Ransolm Casterfo](/wiki/Ransolm_Casterfo "Ransolm Casterfo") and several other prisoners, and stole more ships for the fledgling [Resistance navy](/wiki/Resistance_navy "Resistance navy").[[20]](#cite_note-Resistance_Reborn-20)

Around the [time](/wiki/Time "Time") of the [mission to Batuu](/wiki/Battle_of_Batuu "Battle of Batuu"), the [smuggler](/wiki/Smuggler "Smuggler") [Hondo Ohnaka](/wiki/Hondo_Ohnaka "Hondo Ohnaka") led a [raid](/wiki/Heist_on_a_First_Order_train "Heist on a First Order train") against a First Order [cargo train](/wiki/Hovertrain "Hovertrain"), using the *[Millennium Falcon](/wiki/Millennium_Falcon "Millennium Falcon")* to steal a shipment of [coaxium](/wiki/Coaxium "Coaxium") from the [train](/wiki/Train "Train") and deliver it to the Resistance.[[24]](#cite_note-Smuggler's_Run-24)

By [35 ABY](/wiki/35_ABY "35 ABY"),[[49]](#cite_note-Date-49) the [Corellian Merchants' Guild](/wiki/Corellian_Merchants%27_Guild "Corellian Merchants' Guild") was an influential guild in [Thieves' Quarter](/wiki/Thieves%27_Quarter "Thieves' Quarter") on the planet [Kijimi](/wiki/Kijimi "Kijimi").[[50]](#cite_note-TROS_VD-50) Following the [Battle of Exegol](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol"), Corellia was in [open rebellion](/wiki/Uprising_on_Corellia "Uprising on Corellia") against the First Order.[[51]](#cite_note-TROS_Junior_Novel-51)

## Behind the scenes[]

Corellia has appeared in several spin-off media, including both [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") and [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") works, before making its first onscreen appearance in the [2018](/wiki/2018 "2018") [*Star Wars* Anthology](/wiki/Star_Wars_Anthology_Series "Star Wars Anthology Series") film *[Solo: A Star Wars Story](/wiki/Solo:_A_Star_Wars_Story "Solo: A Star Wars Story")*.[[10]](#cite_note-Solo-10)

In the DVD commentary of *Solo*, set decorate [Lee Sandales](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Sandales) revealed that the film's crew pictured Corellia as the *Star Wars* version of [Venice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venice "wikipedia:Venice"), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy "wikipedia:Italy"), specifically an "industrial" Venice. Filming of Corellia scenes took place in [Southampton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southampton "wikipedia:Southampton"), [England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/England "wikipedia:England"), most notably at Fawley Power Station[[52]](#cite_note-52). Additionally, during the film's development, [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm "Lucasfilm") design supervisor [James Clyne](/wiki/James_Clyne "James Clyne") was asked by the filmmakers to design a complete map of Corellia for the chase scene. Clyne did so, and he drew designs for other locations too, such as the [Death Star](/wiki/DS-1_Orbital_Battle_Station "DS-1 Orbital Battle Station") and [Cloud City](/wiki/Cloud_City "Cloud City").[[53]](#cite_note-Solo_DVD_Commentary-53)
