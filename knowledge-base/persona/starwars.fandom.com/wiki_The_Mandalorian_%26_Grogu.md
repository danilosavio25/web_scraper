***The Mandalorian and Grogu*** is an upcoming live-action *[Star Wars](/wiki/Star_Wars "Star Wars")* film, scheduled for release in theaters on [May 22](/wiki/May_22 "May 22"), [2026](/wiki/2026 "2026"). The film, directed by [Jon Favreau](/wiki/Jon_Favreau "Jon Favreau") and produced by Favreau, [Kathleen Kennedy](/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy "Kathleen Kennedy"), [Dave Filoni](/wiki/Dave_Filoni "Dave Filoni"), and [Ian Bryce](/wiki/Ian_Bryce "Ian Bryce"), takes place between the events of [*Star Wars*: Episode VI *Return of the Jedi*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VI_Return_of_the_Jedi "Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi") and [*Star Wars*: Episode VII *The Force Awakens*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_VII_The_Force_Awakens "Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens"). It continues and is a spinoff of the [Disney+](/wiki/Disney%2B "Disney+") series [*The Mandalorian*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Mandalorian "Star Wars: The Mandalorian").

In *The Mandalorian and Grogu*, the characters of [Din Djarin](/wiki/Din_Djarin "Din Djarin") (the titular "[Mandalorian](/wiki/Mandalorian "Mandalorian")") and [Grogu](/wiki/Din_Grogu "Din Grogu") embark on a new adventure. The film stars [Pedro Pascal](/wiki/Pedro_Pascal "Pedro Pascal"), reprising his role as Din Djarin, and [Sigourney Weaver](/wiki/Sigourney_Weaver "Sigourney Weaver"). [Ludwig Göransson](/wiki/Ludwig_G%C3%B6ransson "Ludwig Göransson"), the original composer of *The Mandalorian*, returns to score the film. The film is the first in a new slate of *Star Wars* films and takes place in the same timeline as the ongoing Disney+ series *The Mandalorian*, a [fourth season](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_Season_Four "The Mandalorian Season Four") of which is still in development, and *[Star Wars: Ahsoka](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Ahsoka "Star Wars: Ahsoka")*.

## Publisher's summary

*The Mandalorian, a lone bounty hunter, is tasked with capturing Grogu, a mysterious and adorable creature. Instead, he forms a strong bond with Grogu and strives to protect him from various threats, exploring his evolving purpose in a post-Empire galaxy.*[[4]](#cite_note-ProductionList-4)

## Development

:   "*Dave Filoni and Jon Favreau have a new vision, continuing on with a Mandalorian movie.*"
:   ―Giancarlo Esposito[[5]](#cite_note-CultureSlate-5)

*The Mandalorian and Grogu* was announced, originally as *The Mandalorian & Grogu*, on [January 9](/wiki/January_9 "January 9"), [2024](/wiki/2024 "2024") on [StarWars.com](/wiki/StarWars.com "StarWars.com") with a conceptual sketch by veteran illustrator [Ryan Church](/wiki/Ryan_Church "Ryan Church"), along with an announcement of a [second season](/wiki/Ahsoka_Season_2 "Ahsoka Season 2") of the television series *[Star Wars: Ahsoka](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Ahsoka "Star Wars: Ahsoka")*.[[1]](#cite_note-Announcement-1) A [fourth season of *The Mandalorian*](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_Season_Four "The Mandalorian Season Four") is also reportedly still in development.[[6]](#cite_note-Deadline_S4-6) However, series actor [Giancarlo Esposito](/wiki/Giancarlo_Esposito "Giancarlo Esposito") suggested the film was part of "a new vision" for *The Mandalorian*.[[5]](#cite_note-CultureSlate-5)

On [May 7](/wiki/May_7 "May 7"), [2019](/wiki/2019 "2019"), [Disney](/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company "The Walt Disney Company") revealed their future movie release schedule, including a set of three untitled new *Star Wars* films, one of which had a scheduled release date of [December 20](/wiki/December_20 "December 20"), 2024.[[7]](#cite_note-Variety_Disney_2019-7) *[The Star Wars Show](/wiki/The_Star_Wars_Show "The Star Wars Show")* also reported on the news on [May 15](/wiki/May_15 "May 15").[[8]](#cite_note-SW_Show-8) On [July 23](/wiki/July_23 "July 23"), [2020](/wiki/2020 "2020"), after the onset of the [COVID-19 pandemic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic "wikipedia:COVID-19 pandemic"), the film was delayed to [December 19](/wiki/December_19 "December 19"), [2025](/wiki/2025 "2025").[[9]](#cite_note-Variety_2020-9) After the start of the [2023 Writers Guild of America strike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2023_Writers_Guild_of_America_strike "wikipedia:2023 Writers Guild of America strike"), Disney shifted their theatrical film release slate, with the film being pushed to its current release date of [May 22](/wiki/May_22 "May 22"), [2026](/wiki/2026 "2026").[[10]](#cite_note-D23_starwarscom-10) A [film](/wiki/Untitled_Star_Wars_film_(Sharmeen_Obaid-Chinoy) "Untitled Star Wars film (Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy)") directed by [Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy](/wiki/Sharmeen_Obaid-Chinoy "Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy") occupied this release slot until *The Mandalorian & Grogu* was announced.[[11]](#cite_note-Sharmeen-11)

[*Star Wars Insider* 224](/wiki/Star_Wars_Insider_224 "Star Wars Insider 224") revealed the initial film logo was the same as the one for [*The Mandalorian* Season Three](/wiki/The_Mandalorian_Season_Three "The Mandalorian Season Three").[[12]](#cite_note-Insider-12) In [August](/wiki/August "August") 2024, during D23 2024, it was revealed that the film had entered production,[[10]](#cite_note-D23_starwarscom-10) and a new logo was revealed, in addition to a teaser that was shown to the audience.[[13]](#cite_note-D23_SWNN-13) In [August](/wiki/August "August") 2024, [Sigourney Weaver](/wiki/Sigourney_Weaver "Sigourney Weaver") revealed that she had joined the cast of *The Mandalorian & Grogu*.[[14]](#cite_note-Deadline_Weaver-14)

Alongside the release of the movie, [*Millennium Falcon*: Smuggler's Run](/wiki/Millennium_Falcon:_Smuggler%27s_Run "Millennium Falcon: Smuggler's Run") will be updated to include a new mission featuring Djarin, Grogu, [Tatooine](/wiki/Tatooine "Tatooine"), [Endor](/wiki/Endor "Endor") and [Cloud City](/wiki/Cloud_City "Cloud City"), which takes place off camera from the film.[[15]](#cite_note-SXSW_2025-15)
