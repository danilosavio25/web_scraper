## Publisher's summary[]

*Witness the epic final chapter of the Skywalker saga with the official novelization of* Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker*, including expanded scenes and additional content not seen in theaters!*

*The Resistance has been reborn. The spark of rebellion is rekindling across the galaxy. But although Rey and her fellow heroes are back in the fight, the war against the First Order, now led by Supreme Leader Kylo Ren, is far from over.*

*Rey, Finn, Poe, and the Resistance must embark on the most perilous adventure they have ever faced. And this time, they're facing it together. With the help of old friends, new allies, and the mysterious guidance of the Force, the story that began in* Star Wars: The Force Awakens *and continued in* Star Wars: The Last Jedi *reaches an astounding conclusion.*

## Continuity[]

The novel contains several differences to the film and scenes that don't appear in the final film.

* The novel opens with [General](/wiki/General "General") [Leia Organa](/wiki/Leia_Skywalker_Organa_Solo "Leia Skywalker Organa Solo") training Rey on [Ajan Kloss](/wiki/Ajan_Kloss "Ajan Kloss"). There is extensive dialogue between the two, with Leia discussing her [Jedi](/wiki/Jedi "Jedi") training with [Luke Skywalker](/wiki/Luke_Skywalker "Luke Skywalker"). Leia hears a familiar voice telling her "it's time." After experiencing her haunting [Force vision](/wiki/Force_vision "Force vision"), Rey talks with [Rose Tico](/wiki/Rose_Tico "Rose Tico") and [Temmin Wexley](/wiki/Temmin_Wexley "Temmin Wexley").
* The opening sequence on [Mustafar](/wiki/Mustafar "Mustafar") is extended, with a scene between [General](/wiki/General "General") [Armitage Hux](/wiki/Armitage_Hux "Armitage Hux") and [Allegiant General](/wiki/Allegiant_General "Allegiant General") [Enric Pryde](/wiki/Enric_Pryde "Enric Pryde"). After killing the [cultists](/wiki/Alazmec_of_Winsit "Alazmec of Winsit"), [Supreme Leader](/wiki/Supreme_Leader "Supreme Leader") [Kylo Ren](/wiki/Ben_Solo "Ben Solo") encounters the [Eye of Webbish Bog](/wiki/Eye_of_Webbish_Bog "Eye of Webbish Bog"), an oracle-like being that emerges from a black lake. The creature directs him to the [Sith Wayfinder](/wiki/Vader%27s_wayfinder "Vader's wayfinder").
* When Kylo Ren finds [Palpatine](/wiki/Darth_Sidious "Darth Sidious") in the [Sith Citadel](/wiki/Sith_Citadel "Sith Citadel") on [Exegol](/wiki/Exegol "Exegol"), Palpatine elaborates on [Snoke](/wiki/Snoke "Snoke"); he explains that killing Snoke was Ren's final test. In this scene, the novel explains that the being in front of Ren is a [clone](/wiki/Cloning "Cloning") body housing Palpatine's damaged spirit. When the [Sith fleet](/wiki/Final_Order "Final Order") emerges from the underground launch bays, [Sith troopers](/wiki/Sith_trooper "Sith trooper") are seen standing at attention in marching formation, similar to the [clone troopers](/wiki/Clone_trooper "Clone trooper") at the end of *[Attack of the Clones](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_II_Attack_of_the_Clones "Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones")*.
* When [Poe Dameron](/wiki/Poe_Dameron "Poe Dameron"), [Finn](/wiki/Finn "Finn"), [Chewbacca](/wiki/Chewbacca "Chewbacca"), [Klaud](/wiki/Klaud "Klaud"), and [R2-D2](/wiki/R2-D2 "R2-D2") retrieve the information from [Boolio](/wiki/Boolio "Boolio") at the [Sinta Glacier Colony](/wiki/Sinta_Glacier_Colony "Sinta Glacier Colony"), Boolio explains that the [First Order](/wiki/First_Order "First Order") tracked the spy's transmission to his location, which is why the [TIE](/wiki/TIE/sf_space_superiority_fighter "TIE/sf space superiority fighter") [Fighters](/wiki/TIE/wi_Interceptor "TIE/wi Interceptor") appear and chase the *[Millennium Falcon](/wiki/Millennium_Falcon "Millennium Falcon")*.
* Before the group leaves for [Pasaana](/wiki/Pasaana "Pasaana"), Leia shares a moment with [Maz Kanata](/wiki/Maz_Kanata "Maz Kanata"). They discuss the fragility of Leia's physical state and what Rey may encounter on her journey. Rose bids Rey farewell with a hug, and Finn does the same with Rose.
* During the reforging of Kylo Ren's [helmet](/wiki/Kylo_Ren%27s_helmet "Kylo Ren's helmet"), there is dialogue between Ren and [Albrekh](/wiki/Albrekh "Albrekh") that is not present in the film.
* Onboard the [*Steadfast*](/wiki/Steadfast_(Resurgent-class) "Steadfast (Resurgent-class)"), [stormtroopers](/wiki/First_Order_stormtrooper "First Order stormtrooper") bring a captured Boolio before the newly masked Kylo Ren and his [Knights of Ren](/wiki/Knights_of_Ren "Knights of Ren"). Boolio defiantly stares down the Supreme Leader as he is beheaded by Ren's [crossguard](/wiki/Crossguard_lightsaber "Crossguard lightsaber") [lightsaber](/wiki/Kylo_Ren%27s_lightsaber "Kylo Ren's lightsaber").
* When the group meets [Lando Calrissian](/wiki/Landonis_Balthazar_Calrissian "Landonis Balthazar Calrissian") in his tank-crawler at the [Festival of Ancestors](/wiki/Festival_of_the_Ancestors "Festival of the Ancestors"), Lando briefly mentions [his kidnapped daughter](/wiki/Kadara_Calrissian "Kadara Calrissian").
* After the group escapes Pasaana in the *[Bestoon Legacy](/wiki/Bestoon_Legacy "Bestoon Legacy")*, Lando watches the *Falcon* get captured by stormtroopers, motivating him to get back in the fight.
* After being brought to [Interrogation Six](/wiki/Interrogation_Six "Interrogation Six"), Chewbacca is visited by Kylo Ren. Ren probes the [Wookiee](/wiki/Wookiee "Wookiee")'s mind for information about the group's mission, but instead sees memories of his childhood and teenage years with Chewbacca and [Han Solo](/wiki/Han_Solo "Han Solo").
* At the [Resistance base](/wiki/Resistance_base_(Ajan_Kloss) "Resistance base (Ajan Kloss)"), Leia gives a rousing speech to the [Resistance](/wiki/Resistance "Resistance"), praising the efforts of Rose and the [Engineering Corps](/wiki/Engineering_Corps "Engineering Corps"). Leia once again hears a familiar voice telling her, "it's time."
* The *Falcon* was being dismantled while in possession of the First Order, which damaged the landing gear and forced the group to make a crash landing on [Kef Bir](/wiki/Kef_Bir "Kef Bir").
* After the [spirit of Luke](/wiki/Force_ghost/Legends "Force ghost/Legends") raised [*Red Five*](/wiki/Red_Five_(T-65B_X-wing_starfighter) "Red Five (T-65B X-wing starfighter)") from the water on [Ahch-To](/wiki/Ahch-To "Ahch-To"), Rey salvaged the remaining functioning components of the destroyed [TIE whisper](/wiki/TIE/wi_modified_interceptor "TIE/wi modified interceptor") to get it flight-worthy.
* [Zorii Bliss](/wiki/Zorii_Bliss "Zorii Bliss") tries to get many of [her crew](/wiki/Spice_Runners_of_Kijimi "Spice Runners of Kijimi") to escape [Kijimi](/wiki/Kijimi "Kijimi"), but only succeeds in escaping with [Babu Frik](/wiki/Babu_Frik "Babu Frik") in her [Y-wing](/wiki/BTA-NR2_Y-wing_starfighter "BTA-NR2 Y-wing starfighter"), the *[Comeuppance](/wiki/Comeuppance "Comeuppance")*.
* The *[Derriphan](/wiki/Derriphan "Derriphan")* returns to Exegol after it [destroyed](/wiki/Destruction_of_Kijimi "Destruction of Kijimi") Kijimi, and it is subsequently destroyed in the [battle](/wiki/Battle_of_Exegol "Battle of Exegol") that follows.
* Before leaving for the [Core Systems](/wiki/Deep_Core "Deep Core"), Lando has an emotional reunion with the *Falcon*, watching a hologram of a young Ben Solo and Chewbacca. He regains his composure, telling his old friend, "You said it, Chewie. One last time."
* As Rey is about to strike Palpatine down, she looks into his mind. In it, she sees Palpatine falling to his [death](/wiki/Death "Death") on the [Death Star II](/wiki/DS-2_Death_Star_II_Mobile_Battle_Station "DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station"), conjuring all the power of the [dark side](/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force "Dark side of the Force") to transport his spirit somewhere "far, far away." She also sees how [her father](/wiki/Dathan "Dathan") was created using strandcasts from Palpatine's [DNA](/wiki/DNA "DNA"), and that he was regarded as a failure, only being useful in furthering the [bloodline](/wiki/Palpatine_family "Palpatine family") through more natural methods.
* When Rose asks why Finn is staying behind on the *Steadfast*, Finn responds that he is "[saving what he loves](/wiki/Battle_of_Crait "Battle of Crait")."
* As Rey is mourning the death of Ben Solo, she hears his voice speak to her.
* When Finn, Rey, and Poe reunite after the battle is won, Finn tearfully begins to tell Rey and Poe what he tried to tell Rey earlier, but they cut him off, saying they know.
