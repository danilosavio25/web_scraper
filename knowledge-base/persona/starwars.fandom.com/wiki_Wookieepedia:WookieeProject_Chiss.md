**WookieeProject: Chiss** is a collaborative effort across [Wookieepedia](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia") dedicated to improving the quality and status of the thousands of articles relating to the [Chiss](/wiki/Chiss "Chiss") [species](/wiki/Species "Species"), their culture, as well as [Timothy Zahn](/wiki/Timothy_Zahn "Timothy Zahn")'s works. If you are interested in [Thrawn](/wiki/Mitth%27raw%27nuruodo "Mitth'raw'nuruodo"), [Thrass](/wiki/Mitth%27ras%27safis "Mitth'ras'safis"), [Eli Vanto](/wiki/Eli_N._Vanto "Eli N. Vanto"), or the [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") or [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends") Thrawn novels, then this [WookieeProject](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects "Wookieepedia:WookieeProjects") is for you! If you wish to join the project then simply add your name under the "[Participants](#Participants)" section.

## WookieeProject goal[]

The main goal of the WookieeProject is to improve many of the [Chiss Ascendancy](/wiki/Chiss_Ascendancy "Chiss Ascendancy")–related articles on Wookieepedia from both [Canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and [Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"). This includes (but is not limited to) articles from the Legends *[Thrawn Trilogy](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Thrawn_Trilogy "Star Wars: The Thrawn Trilogy")*, the canon *[Thrawn Trilogy](/wiki/Star_Wars:_Thrawn_(novel_series) "Star Wars: Thrawn (novel series)")*, [*The Ascendancy Trilogy*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Ascendancy_Trilogy "Star Wars: The Ascendancy Trilogy"), and all other works written by [Timothy Zahn](/wiki/Timothy_Zahn "Timothy Zahn"). The project will often overlap with other WookieeProjects when it comes to status articles as many Chiss articles will fall under multiple projects.

## WookieeProject scope[]

WookieeProject:Chiss covers articles relating to the Chiss and Chiss Ascendancy, whether [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon"), [*Star Wars* Legends](/wiki/Star_Wars_Legends "Star Wars Legends"), or out-of-universe. The Chiss have appeared prominently in canon and Legends novels, but also in *[Star Wars Rebels](/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels "Star Wars Rebels")* and *[Star Wars: The Old Republic](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Old_Republic "Star Wars: The Old Republic")*. The project also covers any subject from Zahn's works.

### Rules for nominations[]

If you wish to nominate an article for the project it must meet one of the following requirements:

* The article subject must be related to a Chiss individual, Chiss culture, Chiss government/society, Chiss history, Chiss tactics, or individuals closely associated with the Chiss Ascendancy (even if of non-Chiss species).
* The article subject must be related to Zahn's works.

## Instructions[]

### Writing[]

1. Use acceptable English grammar and spelling.
2. Write from a balanced and fair perspective with a [neutral point-of-view](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style#Perspective "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style").
3. Follow the [Manual of Style](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Manual_of_Style "Wookieepedia:Manual of Style"), [Layout Guide](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Layout_Guide "Wookieepedia:Layout Guide"), and all other [policies on Wookieepedia](/wiki/Category:Policies_on_Wookieepedia "Category:Policies on Wookieepedia").
4. Do not add [speculation](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Attribution#No_original_research "Wookieepedia:Attribution") of any kind, whether it is unconfirmed info or trivial information. If it is not [stated](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Canon_policy "Wookieepedia:Canon policy") by a [Lucasfilm](/wiki/Lucasfilm_Ltd. "Lucasfilm Ltd.")-affiliated (or official) source, it is not [canon](/wiki/Canon "Canon") and will not be accepted on Wookieepedia.
5. All information in an article must be [sourced](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Sourcing "Wookieepedia:Sourcing").
6. "Behind the scenes" section should include notes on appearances and any developmental material.
7. Don't forget "Personality and traits" and "Skills and abilities" or "Powers and abilities" sections for characters where applicable.

### Editing[]

1. Check for flawless spelling and grammar, as well as cohesive sentence flow and context.
2. Make sure articles follow the Manual of Style, Layout Guide, and all other Wookieepedia policies.
3. Make sure all information, images, and quotes are properly sourced.
4. Fix templates and check for excessive/incorrect links.
5. Check for any appearances or sources that might have been missed.
