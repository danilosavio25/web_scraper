**WookieeProjects** are collaborative efforts by Wookieepedians to improve a certain aspect of [Wookieepedia](/wiki/Wookieepedia "Wookieepedia"). WookieeProjects are open to everyone and registered users can choose to join as many WookieeProjects as they desire. Many WookieeProjects aim to promote articles within their scope to [Comprehensive](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Comprehensive_articles "Wookieepedia:Comprehensive articles"), [Good](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Good_articles "Wookieepedia:Good articles"), or [Featured status](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Featured_articles "Wookieepedia:Featured articles").

However, other types of WookieeProjects focus on maintenance or sitewide improvement: [WookieeProject Web](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Web "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Web"), which focuses on proper citation and archivals of web content; [Wookieepedia:Project Stardust](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Project_Stardust "Wookieepedia:Project Stardust"), which is a collaborative effort to maintain large, popular articles; or [WookieeProject Collaboration](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Collaboration "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Collaboration"), which works on sitewide maintenance and policy proposals.

## Existing WookieeProjects

All registered Wookieepedians are free to join as many WookieeProjects as they like. This is usually done by signing your name on a participants list on the WookieeProject main page, and you can add the {{[UserWookieeProject](/wiki/Template:UserWookieeProject "Template:UserWookieeProject")}} userbox to your userpage to highlight your membership. Pursuant to the [blocking policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Blocking_policy "Wookieepedia:Blocking policy"), banned users may be removed from the WookieeProject leadership and participant listings at the time of blocking. ## How to start a WookieeProject

Any Wookieepedian can start a WookieeProject, but you should read this page, particularly the next section, carefully.

1. **Pick a scope for the project**
   * All WookieeProjects aim to improve a certain aspect of the wiki, but the typical project is one of the following:
     + **Articles with a shared/related source topic(s)**: a large media project like [*The Clone Wars*](/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(multimedia_project) "Star Wars: The Clone Wars (multimedia project)") is a common scope for a WookieeProject. Examples include [WookieeProject Galaxy's Edge](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Galaxy%27s_Edge "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Galaxy's Edge") and [WookieeProject *The High Republic*](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_The_High_Republic "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject The High Republic").
     + **Articles sharing common attributes**: some WookieeProjects aim to improve articles of a certain type of subject rather than using source material as the anchor. Examples include [WookieeProject Astrography](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Astrography "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Astrography") and [WookieeProject Pride](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Pride "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Pride").
   * This list is by no means comprehensive and other types of projects have been successful in the past.
   * The scope should be well-defined and not too narrow or broad. One novel or comic series is probably too narrow whereas a project that encompasses [all 115,978 Legends articles](/wiki/Category:Legends_articles "Category:Legends articles") is too broad.
   * It is common that scopes of different projects overlap in articles, but the scope of a proposed project should not be covered by an existing one. For example, [planets](/wiki/Planet "Planet") are covered by [Project Astrography](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Astrography "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Astrography"). Therefore, a new project that focuses on planets would not be useful since all planets are already within the scope of Astrography.
   * Smaller projects that can be realistically completed within months to a year may be a better fit for a sub-initiative within [WookieeProject Collaboration's](/wiki/Wookieepedia:WookieeProject_Collaboration "Wookieepedia:WookieeProject Collaboration") efforts. Examples of now-completed efforts on smaller projects [archive link project of 2020](/wiki/Forum:SH:Pages_with_missing_archive_links "Forum:SH:Pages with missing archive links") or the [obsolete quote formatting drive of 2023—2024](/wiki/Forum:SH:Collaboration:Quotes_with_obsolete_sourcing "Forum:SH:Collaboration:Quotes with obsolete sourcing").
2. **Find other Wookieepedians who are interested in the topic**
   * Finding other Wookieepedians who are interested in the topic is central to starting a WookieeProject. It is recommended you ask around on [Discord](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord") and start a thread in the [Senate Hall](/wiki/Forum:Senate_Hall "Forum:Senate Hall") before creating a project. There is no set number of editors required to start a WookieeProject, but one or two is probably not enough for a fully fledged WookieeProject.
3. **Create the project**
   * Once you have gone through the steps listed above, you can go ahead and create the project page. There is no standard layout for WookieeProject pages, but most projects have at least the following features:
     + **Infobox and introduction**: details project scope and organization.
     + **Potential articles/projects**: a list of articles or other projects that members can sign up for.
     + **Current status article nominations**: a dynamic list of current nominations that other members are encouraged to review.
     + **Portfolio**: list of status articles promoted by the project and other status articles that fall within the project scope.
     + **Membership**: usually a numbered list of participants where anyone can sign their name.
   * Most WookieeProjects insert WookieeProject notice templates on talk pages of articles within their scope. See [here](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Templates/Talk_namespace#WookieeProject_notices "Wookieepedia:Templates/Talk namespace") for examples. Keep in mind item 6 of the [Talk page policy](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Talk_page_policy "Wookieepedia:Talk page policy").
   * The [Project Data](/wiki/User:JocastaBot/Project_Data "User:JocastaBot/Project Data") for [JocastaBot](/wiki/User:JocastaBot "User:JocastaBot") should be updated to include the new project so that its portfolio and its list of current status article nominations are automated.

## Chat channels

WookieeProjects can request the creation of a subchannel on the Wookieepedia [Discord](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord "Wookieepedia:Discord") server. These channels are opt-in and can be joined by anyone with the Wookieepedians role on the server. A list of WookieeProject subchannels is available in the [Server structure](/wiki/Wookieepedia:Discord#Server_structure "Wookieepedia:Discord") section of the Discord page. For more information regarding the eventual creation of a project subchannel, please contact an administrator.
